About the lease tracker template:
---------------------------------

Tracking your leases can be an arduous task, especially with the new standards. We want to make your life easier, so we developed this free lease asset tracker template. We know that starting an Excel spreadsheet from scratch can be cumbersome, so the tracker is already pre-populated with everything you need in order to accurately record equipment and commercial leases.

In addition to capturing your comprehensive list of properties and leases, the tool will help you simplify your transition. Sharing the tracker with each member of your team helps ensure that everyone is recording your organization’s leases in the same format. Whether you rely on the tool for your property management or lease management, our tool can help you.
