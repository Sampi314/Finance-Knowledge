2021 Infographic: A LeaseQuery Year in Review
=============================================

by [FinQuery](https://finquery.com/author/leasequery/ "Posts by FinQuery")
 | Jan 11, 2022

Much like the previous year, one of our top priorities for 2021 was maintaining the health of both our LeaseQuery employees and our customers. Despite the uncertainty the past year, LeaseQuery continued to work hard throughout the challenges of navigating a pandemic, working from home, and coping with unpredictable situations in order to tackle and accomplish major company goals.

LeaseQuery released two new exciting products over the course of the year, [LeaseQuery Connect](https://finquery.com/connect/)
 and [GASB 96](https://finquery.com/gasb-96-software/)
. We published 54 blogs, 11 podcast episodes, and 3 new industry reports. Attendees of our 40 educational webinars and 4 summits earned over 11,479 CPE credits. We added 203 new employees to the team, including some new accountants, giving us 35 accountants on staff. With 0 FASB delays this year, it was critical that we conducted over 10,000 calls with our 2,000+ customers, helping us maintain our 99% retention rate. Finally, we couldn’t wrap up the year without mentioning the World Series Champs, the Atlanta Braves. Around 150 employees were seen repping the team’s logo on Zoom calls in 2021.

Thank you to everyone who helped make a great year for LeaseQuery in 2021. We can’t wait to see how we grow in 2022.
