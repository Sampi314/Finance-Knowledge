Features
--------

*   Amortization Schedules
*   Journal Entries
*   Disclosure Reports
*   Compliance
*   Dashboard
*   CPA-Approved Calculations

### Amortization Schedules

Auto-generate error-free amortization schedules with the ability to export to excel.

![Amortization Schedules](https://finquery.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/features-amortization-schedules.webp "Amortization Schedules")

### Journal Entries

Instantly create monthly journal entries and export to excel. Available for your total organization and by individual lease.

![Journal Entries](https://finquery.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/features-journal-entries.webp "Journal Entries")

### Disclosure Reports

Populate pre-built, quantitative disclosure reports to ensure compliance. Our system will generate line items for lease cost, cash flow and weighted average.

![Disclosure Reports](https://finquery.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/features-disclosure-reports.webp "Disclosure Reports")

### Compliance

Automate compliance with ASC 842 or IFRS 16 with our CPA-approved calculations.

![Compliance](https://finquery.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/features-compliance.webp "Compliance")

### Dashboard

User-friendly dashboard providing an overview of your entire lease portfolio. Easily identify upcoming and remaining payments, lease status, important dates and more. All on one dashboard.

![Dashboard](https://finquery.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/features-dashboard.webp "Dashboard")

### CPA-Approved Calculations

LeaseGuru is powered by FinQuery’s CPA- approved calculations which have been reviewed and approved by our team of in-house, Certified Public Accountants (CPAs).

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

##### How do I get started?

LeaseGuru is a self-service lease accounting software which means you can get started on your own. [Create your free account](https://secure.leaseguru.com/get-started)
, and add your leases to start.

##### What payment options does LeaseGuru accept?

LeaseGuru only accepts payment via credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express). Checks are not accepted at this time. For billing questions, please email [payments@leaseguru.com](mailto:payments@leaseguru.com)

##### What kind of reports are available?

LeaseGuru provides amortization schedules, journal entries, and quantitative disclosures for your lease portfolio under ASC 842 or IFRS 16.

##### Can I export amortization schedules in LeaseGuru?

Yes, if you upgrade to LeaseGuru Premium you are able to export amortization schedules and journal entries.

##### How will I know that my leases will be fully ASC 842 or IFRS 16 compliant?

Our software has been thoroughly tested and approved by accountants for financial accuracy and compliance.

##### Does LeaseGuru offer support?

No, LeaseGuru is self-service, lease accounting software designed for you to complete your lease accounting on your own. For questions, please reference the Knowledge Base that contains answers to common topics. For software issues, please contact [info@leaseguru.com](mailto:info@leaseguru.com)
. For billing questions, please email [payments@leaseguru.com](mailto:payments@leaseguru.com)

##### Is LeaseGuru really free?

Yes! LeaseGuru is completely free for up to 2 leases.

##### How many leases am I able to account for with LeaseGuru?

For single companies using LeaseGuru, you can account for up to 15 leases under IFRS 16 or ASC 842. For multi-client plans, you can account for up to 5 clients with up to 10 leases each. If you need to account for more leases, you can request pricing for our larger solutions – LeaseQuery Essential or LeaseQuery Advanced.

##### What if I have more than 15 leases?

LeaseGuru is designed to account for small lease portfolios of 15 or less. If you need to account for more leases, you can request pricing for our larger solutions – LeaseQuery Essential or LeaseQuery Advanced.

##### Will I be able to make mid-lease changes, terminations or renewals?

Yes, you will have the ability to amend your entered lease to make modifications, early terminations, and extend your lease term.
