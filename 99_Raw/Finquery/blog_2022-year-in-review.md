2022 Year In Review
===================

by [FinQuery](https://finquery.com/author/leasequery/ "Posts by FinQuery")
 | Feb 6, 2023

LeaseQuery was busy in 2022! After two years of uncertainty due to the pandemic, we are thrilled to be hosting in-office events again. With 138 new employees on the team, we are ready to hit new milestones as we continue to grow into 2023.

Thanks to the hard work of our colleagues and customers, LeaseQuery earned five major awards last year, including The Atlanta Business Chronicle’s Best Places to Work award and an [SIIA CODiE](https://finquery.com/press-releases/siia-best-fintech-solution/)
 award. We published nearly [50 blogs](https://finquery.com/resources/?_sft_post_tag=blog)
 and [seven podcast episodes](https://finquery.com/finquery-focus-podcast/)
, as well as hosted over [80 webinars](https://finquery.com/webinars-events/)
 and five summits. Our accounting experts helped publish the [Ultimate Lease Accounting Guide](https://finquery.com/download-the-ultimate-lease-accounting-guide/)
, offering tips for lease accounting under [ASC 842](https://finquery.com/blog/asc-842-summary-new-lease-accounting-standards/)
, along with access to a free lease accounting masterclass certification. Additionally, our annual survey of 200 accountants resulted in the [Top Challenges & Tech Wishlist](https://finquery.com/download-the-annual-accountants-survey-top-challenges-tech-wishlist/)
, exploring the top priorities for tech investment in the industry. Finally, we can’t forget the amount of time spent discussing FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 games during meetings.

We are grateful to everyone who made 2022 a successful year for LeaseQuery. As we continue to grow in 2023, we hope to crush all of our company goals.
