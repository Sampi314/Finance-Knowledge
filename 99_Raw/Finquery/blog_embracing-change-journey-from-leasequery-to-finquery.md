Embracing Change: A Journey from LeaseQuery to FinQuery
=======================================================

by [George Azih, Founder](https://finquery.com/author/george-azih/ "Posts by George Azih, Founder")
 | Feb 26, 2024

I wanted to take a moment to share with you a significant milestone in our journey together. As you may have already heard, [LeaseQuery has now evolved into FinQuery](https://finquery.com/press-releases/leasequery-rebrands-to-finquery/)
, marking a new chapter in our story.

[_**Read our story here**_](https://finquery.com/why-finquery/)

Why now?
--------

This rebranding represents not just a change in name, but a transformation in our identity and vision. Over the years, LeaseQuery has been synonymous with excellence in [lease accounting](https://finquery.com/blog/lease-accounting-explained/)
, empowering countless businesses with robust solutions and unwavering support. However, as our company has grown and diversified, we realized the need for a name that better reflects the comprehensive suite of services we offer.

The transition to FinQuery mirrors our expanded vision and dedication to empowering businesses with comprehensive financial solutions. While [lease accounting software](https://finquery.com/lease-accounting-software/)
 remains a core part of our offering, FinQuery represents a more holistic approach to financial management, encompassing [contract management](https://finquery.com/blog/contract-management/)
, spend intelligence, and beyond.

What does FinQuery represent?
-----------------------------

We believe that the name FinQuery captures the essence of what we stand for: a trusted partner that provides intelligent financial solutions to help businesses thrive in today’s dynamic and competitive landscape. Our goal is to be more than just a vendor; we strive to be a strategic partner, guiding our clients toward greater efficiency, transparency, and success.

Thank you!
----------

I want to take this opportunity to express my sincere gratitude to each and every one of you who has been a part of this journey. Whether you are a client, partner, employee, or friend of the company, your support and trust have been instrumental in our success.

As we embark on this new chapter as FinQuery, I am filled with excitement and optimism for the future. Together, we will continue to innovate, collaborate, and push the boundaries of what is possible in financial management.

Thank you for being a part of our journey.

About the author
----------------

George Azih, Founder

George Azih is the Founder of FinQuery. A leading expert on lease accounting, he holds a Bachelor's degree in Accounting from the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia. He is a member of the American Institute of CPAs and the Georgia Society of CPAs. During his career, George served as a senior auditor at a CPA form and conducted accounting research and technical accounting for a Fortune 500 company. He founded LeaseQuery in 2011 to help companies of all sizes and across industries manage their leases efficiently under the new lease accounting standards.
