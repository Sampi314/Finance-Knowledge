### **About the Software Contract Tracker  
**

Organizations now have tens to hundreds of software applications to operate smoothly and communicate efficiently. Each department likely has its own list of tools specific to its needs and functions, with little insight into overlap with other departments. For many of the agreements, the number of licenses purchased rather than those being used drives the cost.

We developed this free contract tracker to help ease the burden of accounting for each software subscription. Use our Software Contract Tracker to list all of your software applications in one place. A comprehensive listing is the first step to effectively managing costs and renewals, providing the information needed to recognize and stop unnecessary spending within your organization.
