New Self-Study CPE Offering: Access Premium Educational Content at Your Convenience, Free of Charge
===================================================================================================

by [Kiley Arnold, CPA](https://finquery.com/author/kiley-arnold/ "Posts by Kiley Arnold, CPA")
 | May 20, 2024

We are excited to announce the expansion of our free Continuing Professional Education (CPE) program. This enhancement includes a highly anticipated self-study offering, designed to drive professional growth and keep accounting professionals up-to-date.

Why this matters
----------------

The recent approval from the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA) marks a significant milestone for the company. NASBA’s rigorous review process ensures that FinQuery’s CPE program maintains the highest educational standards, including our new self-study courses available [here](https://finquery.com/webinars-events/)
.

Dedicated to providing free, high-quality education for all accounting professionals
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The addition of self-study courses complements FinQuery’s extensive lineup of live and free CPE courses. CEO and Founder George Azih shares, “Last year, FinQuery awarded more than 10,000 CPE credit hours through our live webinars and training sessions. We’ve now broadened our services to introduce a self-study option that now allows accounting professionals to access our premium educational content at their convenience, free of charge.”

Features of the self-study program
----------------------------------

FinQuery’s self-study program includes a fully developed course with review questions and a final assessment, ensuring that participants grasp the material thoroughly. The program’s policies and promotional materials have also been updated to meet NASBA’s stringent requirements.

The new self-study programs feature review questions and qualified assessments. Participants need to score 80% or higher to pass, with opportunities to retake assessments if necessary. Moreover, participants have one year to complete each self-study course, offering flexibility and convenience.

Course topics and accessibility
-------------------------------

FinQuery’s CPE program offers live and on-demand webinars, virtual summits, and the occasional live event covering several technical and non-technical topics, including training and information on our products, general accounting and finance subjects, and in-depth lease accounting courses.

These courses cater to various knowledge levels, from overview and basic to intermediate and advanced. This makes the program accessible to professionals at all career stages. Each course undergoes a thorough review to guarantee top-quality content.

Get started today
-----------------

For more information about FinQuery’s CPE program and to register, please visit [FinQuery’s Events and Webinars page](https://finquery.com/webinars-events/)
.

This is a fantastic opportunity for accounting professionals to advance their careers and stay informed about industry developments. Don’t miss out!
