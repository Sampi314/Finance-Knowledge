2020 Infographic: A LeaseQuery Year in Review
=============================================

by [FinQuery](https://finquery.com/author/leasequery/ "Posts by FinQuery")
 | Jan 11, 2021

Although 2020 was a scary, unprecedented year for so many, LeaseQuery stayed on the course, putting our best foot forward for the integrity of ourselves, our customers, our partners, and our business.

Our top priority in 2020 quickly shifted to ensuring the health and well-being of our LeaseQuery team. However, being forced to deviate our focus and our in-office interactions early on didn’t stop us from coming together as a team and accomplishing several exciting goals.

Thanks to the dedication of our colleagues and customers, LeaseQuery won seven awards including [#29 on the Inc. 5000 list](https://finquery.com/press-releases/leasequery-recognized-on-inc-5000-list-of-americas-fastest-growing-private-companies/)
. We [published 50 blogs](https://finquery.com/blog/)
, hosted [nearly 60 educational webinars](https://finquery.com/resources/?_sft_post_tag=webinars)
, and offered 1,139 CPE credit hours. We shared a more in-depth look at various parts of lease accounting with [three new industry reports](https://finquery.com/resources/?_sft_post_tag=guides)
. The LeaseQuery team (now with 112 new members) answered more than 11,000 customer calls and maintained a 99% retention rate throughout the year. Finally, this year wouldn’t be complete without a few too many Zoom call interruptions (34% to be “exact”) from our furry friends and pint-sized family members.

Thank you to all who helped make 2020 a great year for LeaseQuery. We’re looking forward to exciting and hopeful days ahead as we embark on our 2021 journey.
