Spreadsheets vs. Software
=========================

by [FinQuery](https://finquery.com/author/leasequery/ "Posts by FinQuery")
 | Jul 27, 2023

We all know Excel is an accountant’s best friend. The unfortunate truth is that relying on this software just won’t cut it when it comes to managing your leased and [fixed assets](https://finquery.com/blog/what-are-fixed-assets/)
 and leaves you at risk for non-compliance. Before you invest time and resources into building spreadsheets, consider miscalculations and other risks.
