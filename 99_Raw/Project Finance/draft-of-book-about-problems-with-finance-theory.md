# My Book New: Rethinking Finance

**Source:** https://edbodmer.com/draft-of-book-about-problems-with-finance-theory

---

I am in the mist of writing a book about the problems with finance. In the book I suggest that there are a whole lot of problems with finance related to measuring returns, evaluating risks, attempting to compute the cost of capital and calculating value. I am nasty to financial academics, financial consultants and others. Unlike other finance books I begin with some philosophy, what growth, IRR and returns really mean and the implications of making overstating the cost of capital. I use financial models to prove some of my ideas such as computing earned risk premiums, evaluating returns with economic depreciation, measuring risk with and without mean reversion, understanding why the essence of project finance should be a starting point in finance analysis, how of projects and corporations can dramatically change over time, how terminal value and use of multiples should be a philosophic issue, how cost of capital can be measured objectively, how country risk premia are equivalent to corruption and how the CAPM can produce inaccurate results.

I have posted videos where I discuss the book and I work through different chapters in the videos. In the videos I promise that I will put the latest version of the book on my website.
