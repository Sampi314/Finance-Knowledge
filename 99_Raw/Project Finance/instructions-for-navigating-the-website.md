Where to Go If You Have No Background and You Want to Learn Project Finance
===========================================================================

,

I suggest you first go the section of my website on courses. Underneath the course menu you will find a menu on corporate finance. There you can find a course on foundations of project finance. My suggestion is to open the power point slides and then the associated documents to understand project finance. I will make a video on this course and include questions to make sure you understand the concepts. The link to this page is below.

.

> [Project Finance Analysis and Theory](https://edbodmer.com/project-finance-theory-and-contracts/)

.

If you want to learn basic modelling, I suggest you then go to the page that explains how to build a model from scratch. I am also in the process of adding quizzes and questions to this section to make sure that you understand the fundamentals of a project finance model. The link to this page that starts with timelines, input structuring, financial model organisation is include with a couple of videos and simple financial model examples.

.

> [Step by Step Process to Complete Exams](https://edbodmer.com/step-by-step-process-to-complete-exams/)

.

After you have practiced some of the basic equations and the instructions, you can move to specific subjects such as hydrogen modelling. I will let you look for this yourself.

.
