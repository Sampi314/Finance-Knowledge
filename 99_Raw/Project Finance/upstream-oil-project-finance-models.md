# Upstream Oil Project Finance Models

**Source:** https://edbodmer.com/upstream-oil-project-finance-models

---

I have included a few models of upstream oil projects on this page.  These projects sometimes involve complex production sharing agreements and/or loan terms that involve production payment covenants, or cash sweeps derived from reserve estimates. In addition to the financial models, I have created some power point slides that describe how to create a model of a reserve based loan that includes excerpts of a term sheet.

**[Power Point Slides that Explain Modelling and Debt Terms of Reserve Based Lending for Oil Projects](https://edbodmer.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/Reserve-Based-Lending-for-Oil-and-Gas-Model.pptx)
