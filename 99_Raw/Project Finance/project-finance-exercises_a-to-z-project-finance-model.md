# A to Z Project Finance Model

**Source:** https://edbodmer.com/project-finance-exercises/a-to-z-project-finance-model

---

This page has videos files and explanations for creating a complete project finance model from A-Z.  If you want to become a really efficient and advanced modeller for project finance modelling you can watch the videos and try to work through the exercies.  A warning though.  These project finance modelling videos are not five minute videos that you can watch on your phone while riding the metro. I have tried to cover both basic and advanced concepts all the way from timelines to resolving circular references associated with DSRA accounts.  I have also tried to include a bit of theory in the videos and also demonstrated what are bad practices that you should avoid.

[Power Point File for Videos](https://edbodmer.com/project-finance-exercises/a-to-z-project-finance-model#)

[Excel File for Videos](https://edbodmer.com/project-finance-exercises/a-to-z-project-finance-model#)

[Circular Reference File](https://edbodmer.com/project-finance-exercises/a-to-z-project-finance-model#)

If you are starting out in modelling and have not yet made a project finance model, the files and videos below are intended to allow you to see the project finance structure, the importance of timing assumptions, the essential nature of separating operating cash flows from financing cash flows and other issues including cash flow waterfalls. The set of videos and files also introduce you to some difficult financing issues including circularity associated with funding during construction.
