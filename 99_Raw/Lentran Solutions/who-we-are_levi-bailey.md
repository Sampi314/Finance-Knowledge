##### Career Biography

Levi started his career as a mechanical engineer, and transitioned into financial services in 2007. Within financial services, he initially worked in Corporate Finance for a global power company, now Engie, where he has involved in multiple refinancings and development of a cogeneration plant. He has subsequently worked in a variety of management consulting roles with a strong focus on financial modelling and advisory. His previous consulting employers range from a large global accounting firm to a boutique London modelling house. Levi is now LMS’s lead resource in London, and is additionally founding director of White Box Financial.

Levi gained a deep understanding of business, transactions and financial modelling considerations by working on a wide range of projects. His sector experience includes energy, natural resources, transport, technology, real estate and social infrastructure, with the majority based in the EMEA and APAC regions. Levi holds a first in mechanical engineering from the University of Canterbury and is a CFA charter holder.
