##### Career Biography

Theo’s consulting experience though focused towards infrastructure retains a broad scope. It includes providing model build and analytical support to the winning bid of Egypt’s first healthcare PPP, a multi-billion EGP transaction, and management of a EUR 500m infrastructure fund including assets such as Thames Water, ROSCO Angel Trains and the Spanish hydrocarbons pipeline company, CLH.

Additionally, Theo has built a large number of best practice models for the ‘non-infrastructure’ project finance space, including a suite of renewable energy models for South Africa’s RFP for IPPs and a variety of mining projects (e.g. uranium, coal, copper, gold) in Australia, Canada and Asia.

More diversified projects include producing a tool for Lend Lease to make feasibility decisions for projects valued in hundreds of millions of AUD, feasibility analysis for Transport for NSW and the delivery of a yearlong process improvement engagement at Maersk Oil and Gas in Copenhagen.

Theo has also delivered over 50 financial model training courses worldwide to clients including US Government, UniCredit and the Development Bank of Japan.
