Training

### **Giving you the skills to be a best practice modeller**

**LMS trainers have delivered tailored financial modelling training worldwide.**

#### **Our Training Courses**

LMS provides focused in-house training courses. Whether, you are looking to up-skill a team in the basics of financial modelling or have an advanced group who need to refine industry specific modelling skills LMS are well-placed to develop a tailored solution for your needs.

LMS’s team have delivered dozens of tailored courses worldwide to multinational corporations, banks and government institutions, but also to small entities and to individuals in one-on-one sessions.

An in-house course is an excellent solution to your training needs as it allows us to:

*   Work closely with you to tailor a course to your precise needs, including notes, case studies and take away financial model or exercises
*   Deliver the tailored course on a date and for a duration that meets your specific availability
*   Pass on the significant cost saving of hosting a course at your offices rather than at a public venue
