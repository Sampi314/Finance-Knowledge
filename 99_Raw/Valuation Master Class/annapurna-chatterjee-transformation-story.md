[ByBo Pedersen](https://valuationmasterclass.com/author/bo-pedersen/)

She was a finance professional when she joined the Valuation Master Class, with a Master’s Degree in Finance. She found where she wanted to go through the Boot Camp.

### Right after the Boot Camp, she was hired by our corporate sponsor, AWR Lloyd, where she’s now an Additional Director, Board Member, and Manager.

### Which Program Is Right for You?

The Valuation Master Class Boot Camp serves finance professionals at every stage. Find your path below.

#### Early-Career Finance Professional

Just starting out in finance or still studying? Build real analyst-level skills, create a defensible research portfolio, and stand out to top employers from day one.

[**Learn more about the Starter program →**](https://valuationmasterclass.com/starters/)

#### Working Finance Professional

Already in finance but want to sharpen your edge? Accelerate your career with advanced valuation techniques and the confidence to back your recommendations.

[**Learn more about the Accelerator program →**](https://valuationmasterclass.com/accelerator/)

#### Career Transitioning into Finance

Coming from a different field? Our program is designed to bridge the gap — giving career changers the technical skills and credibility needed to break into finance.

[**Learn more about the Switcher program →**](https://valuationmasterclass.com/switchers/)
