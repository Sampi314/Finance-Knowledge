Alter Domus wins Best Fund Administrator – Private Credit award
===============================================================

The Private Equity Wire European Credit Awards ceremony took place on 26th October in London.

* * *

Alter Domus

3 November 2023

![Hedgeweek and Private Equitywire European Credit Awards 2023](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%201504%201060%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

We are delighted to have won **“Best Fund Administrator – Private Credit”** at the [Private Equity Wire Private Credit European Awards 2023](https://awards.privateequitywire.co.uk/european-credit-awards)
 in London. This award is the culmination of our efforts over the last ten years and is demonstrated by the fact that today we administer $200bn of European private debt assets.

With our highly experienced European team, we are uniquely positioned to support clients as they look to address the complexities of private debt strategies. The expertise of our people, our [integrated service offering](https://alterdomus.com/services/private-debt-solutions/)
, plus our [market-leading technology solutions](https://alterdomus.com/about-us/our-technology/)
 mean we are truly able to offer the advantage in alternatives.

A big thank you to the Bloomberg-selected credit fund managers who voted for us. And a big congratulations to our team!
