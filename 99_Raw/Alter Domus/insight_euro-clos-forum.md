When

Organizer

FT Live

Venue

Resilient by Design.  
  
As wider credit markets face mounting pressure, why do European CLOs remain the “choice strategy” for global investors?  
  
[Amit Varma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amit-varma-1636029/)
, [Juliana Ritchie](https://www.linkedin.com/in/juliana-ritchie-49702b8/)
, and [Tim Ruxton](https://www.linkedin.com/in/timruxton/)
 join the industry’s most influential voices at [FT Live](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ft-live/)
‘s Euro CLOs Forum in London.  
  
Ask them how our specialized structured finance solutions provide the transparency needed to navigate today’s uneven credit cycle.
