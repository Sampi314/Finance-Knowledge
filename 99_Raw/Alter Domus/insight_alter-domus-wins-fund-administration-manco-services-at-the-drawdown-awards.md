Alter Domus wins Fund Administration: ManCo Services
====================================================

The Drawdown Awards ceremony took place on June 7th in London

* * *

Alter Domus

13 June 2023

![Alter Domus team at Drawdown Awards 2023](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%201405%20937%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

We are delighted to announce that Alter Domus has won the award for Fund Administration: ManCo Services at [The Drawdown Awards 2023](https://the-drawdown.com/article/the-drawdown-awards-2023-winners-announced)
. Held in London on June 7th, the awards were judged by a highly experienced panel of leading industry experts and we faced strong competition in our category.

The award was accepted by Matthew Molton— Country Executive UK— on the night, with Andy Clark, Tim Trott and Sam Wade also present to celebrate our achievement. We are particularly proud that this award was given by a panel of leading GP and LP judges.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20400%20266.666666667%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

> We are delighted to win this prestigious award, which reflects the quality of the services we provide and the trust our clients have in Alter Domus as their chosen ManCo provider.
> 
> Matthew Molton, Country Executive UK

This is the second consecutive year that Alter Domus has won the award for [ManCo Services](https://alterdomus.com/services/aifm-services)
 at The Drawdown Awards, following our previous win in 2022.

This news follows hot on the heels of Alter Domus being ranked one of the top-5 third-party AIFMs, and a top-10 third-party ManCo in [PwC’s 2023 Observatory for Management Companies Barometer](https://www.pwc.lu/en/asset-management/management-company.html)
.
