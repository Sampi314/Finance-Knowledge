Episode Description
-------------------

In this episode, Sean Smith (Managing Partner of Search Fund Ventures &amp; Founder of SMB Investor Network) sits down with Pascal Wagner, founder of Grow Your Cash Flow and seasoned LP investor. Pascal shares his journey from working with the investment team at Techstars, one of the world’s top accelerators, to building a multi-asset-class cash flow strategy. From helping his family secure financial independence to reviewing 2,000+ deals and investing across real estate, private credit, self-storage, oil &amp; gas, and retail development, Pascal lays out his playbook for LP investing. Key topics include: - Why every LP needs a \*\*macro plan before reviewing deals\*\*. - How to vet operators and spot \*\*red flags that others miss\*\*. - The trade-offs between \*\*emerging managers and institutional funds\*\*. - Tax strategies and incentives that boost returns. - Why operator consistency beats flashy pro formas.
