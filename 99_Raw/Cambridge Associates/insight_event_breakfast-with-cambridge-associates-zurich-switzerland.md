Breakfast with Cambridge Associates, Zurich, Switzerland
========================================================

Cambridge Associates  

Oct 01, 2025

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - October 1, 2025  
_8:30 am - 11:00 am_

  

This event will focus on Private Equity and Sustainable Infrastructure Investments. Attendees can look forward to practical insights and engaging discussions with our specialists. **[Andrea Auerbach](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/people/andrea-auerbach/)
**, Global Head of Private Investments at Cambridge Associates, will deliver the keynote address, sharing her perspectives and experiences on private equity in today’s market environment. Followed by a dynamic panel discussion on sustainable infrastructure investments featuring  **[Ratana Tra](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/people/ratana-tra/)
** and [**Dr. Christoph Gort**](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/en-eu/people/dr-christoph-gort/)
, moderated by **[Siti Dawson](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/en-eu/people/siti-dawson/)
**. The team will explore current developments, challenges, and opportunities in this exciting asset class.

This event is for clients and future clients of Cambridge Associates. For more information, please contact **[cvnicholson@cambridgeassociates.com](mailto:cvnicholson@cambridgeassociates.com)
