Regional Event Series
=====================

Cambridge Associates  

May 04, 2022

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - May 4, 2022 - May 12, 2022  
_3:00 pm - 6:00 pm_

  

###### **Chicago, May 4 | Houston, May 5 | New York, May 11 | San Francisco, May 12**

In a period of extreme market volatility and global unrest, private investments continue to be an evolving and attractive option for investors, providing the potential for greater returns relative to other investment strategies. We kicked off each event with a capital markets update to address the current market conditions.

Cambridge Associates’ Andrea Auerbach, Global Head of Private Investments, shared insights and had an interactive discussion on the private investment landscape in this market environment. Andrea was joined by Jasmine Richards, Head of Diverse Manager Research, to discuss our recent work and progress within diversity, equity, and inclusion in the private markets.

These events provided a valuable forum to connect with local investors, including endowments, foundations, private investors, pension plans, and Cambridge Associates’ thought leaders. To see our latest research on private investments, [click here.](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/insights/private-investments/)
