NextGen Leaders Connect
=======================

Cambridge Associates  

Oct 22, 2025

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - October 22, 2025 - October 23, 2025  
_All Day_

  

Join us for the NextGen Leaders Connect on October 22-23, 2025 in Santa Monica, CA. Designed for family members ages 25–40 who are preparing to take a more engaged and active role in managing their family wealth, business, or investment portfolio, this event offers a unique opportunity to develop and deepen relationships with peers. Our goal is to educate and inspire next-generation family members while fostering meaningful connections among participants.

This event is for clients and future clients of Cambridge Associates. For more information, please contact [**events@cambridgeassociates.com**](mailto:events@cambridgeassociates.com)
