An Evening in Milan with Cambridge Associates
=============================================

Oct 18, 2023

![An Evening in Milan with Cambridge Associates](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/EventGraphic-1.png)

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - October 18, 2023  
_4:30 pm - 7:30 pm_

  

Cambridge Associates’ Francesca Signorelli and Francesco Agnes, along with venture capitalists from Lux Capital, FJ Labs, and Balderton Capital, discussed the current opportunities presented by [artificial intelligence](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/insight/podcast-fashion/)
 and machine learning technology. They delved into how these technologies are being applied to transform core industries, providing exciting opportunities for early-stage investors.

Panelists included Grace Isford, Partner at Lux Capital; Jose Marin, Co-Founder at FJ Labs; and James Wise, Partner at Balderton Capital.

After the remarks, attendees networked with other institutional and family office investors. Please note this event is for Cambridge Associates’ clients and prospective clients only. To learn more, click [here](mailto:events@cambridgeassociates.com?subject=An%20Evening%20in%20Milan)
.
