==============================

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - November 19, 2024  
_6:00 pm - 8:00 pm_

  

Join Cambridge Associates for a relaxed evening of conversations over drinks and canapés at our networking reception in Hong Kong on November 19, 2024.

Hosted by Cambridge Associates’ investment leaders, enjoy the opportunity to connect with industry leaders and peers, including trustees, investment committee members, CIOs, family principals, senior investment staff, and fund managers.
