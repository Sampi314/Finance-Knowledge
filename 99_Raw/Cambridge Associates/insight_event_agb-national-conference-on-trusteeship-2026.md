===========================================

Cambridge Associates  

Mar 28, 2026

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - March 28, 2026 - March 30, 2026  
_1:15 pm - 12:15 pm_

**Location**  
[Gaylord Rockies Resort & Convention Center](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/insight/event-locations/gaylord-rockies-resort-convention-center-4/)

**Categories**

*   [Industry Events](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/events/categories/industry-events/)
    

  

Cambridge Associates experts will speak at the AGB National Conference on Trusteeship, sharing insights on how private investments—including private equity, venture capital, real assets, growth equity, secondaries, and private credit—can strengthen college and university portfolios. The session will offer practical guidance for trustees on enhancing returns, diversifying portfolios, and aligning investment strategies with institutional goals. Attendees will gain actionable tips on governance, manager selection, and effective collaboration among trustees, investment committees, and university leadership.

*   **Moderator:** Ben Buttrick, Managing Director for Client Solutions, Endowment & Foundation Practice, Cambridge Associates
*   **Speakers:** Tapan V. Gandhi, Managing Director and Private Investment Specialist, Endowment & Foundation Practice, Cambridge Associates  
    Christopher U. Light, Chair, Investment Committee, Kalamazoo College Board of Trustees
