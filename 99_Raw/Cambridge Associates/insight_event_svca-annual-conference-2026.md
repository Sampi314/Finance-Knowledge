Aaron Costello, Head of Asia, and Scolet Ma, Senior Investment Director, will be speaking at the Singapore Venture and Private Capital Association’s (SVCA) Annual Conference 2026: Building Bridges in a Volatile World, taking place in Singapore on February 5, 2026.

Aaron will be on the opening panel, “Private Capital Investing in an Era of Unknown Unknowns”, while Scolet will lead one of the deep dive roundtables, “China Comeback”.

To hear their views on these topics, visit [the website](https://svca.glueup.com/event/svca-annual-conference-2026-building-bridges-in-a-volatile-world-161374/)
 for more information.
