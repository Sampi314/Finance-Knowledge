PEI Women in Private Markets Summit
===================================

Cambridge Associates  

May 15, 2024

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - May 15, 2024 - May 16, 2024  
_All Day_

**Categories**

*   [Industry Events](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/events/categories/industry-events/)
    

  

Andrea Auerbach and Keirsten Lawton will be speaking at the Private Equity International Women in Private Markets Summit in New York City, May 15-16. Andrea will deliver the keynote address on the 15th, where she’ll discuss managing private markets in a fluid environment. Keirsten will join a panel of private credit specialists to discuss the current private credit landscape and how debt compared to other strategies in the current climate. For more information and to register, [click here](https://www.peievents.com/en/event/women-in-private-markets-summit-north-america/home/)
