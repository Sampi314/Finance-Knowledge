Navigating Private Investments During Volatility: A Conversation with Cambridge Associates’ Global Head of Private Investments
==============================================================================================================================

Cambridge Associates  

Jun 20, 2023

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - June 20, 2023  
_All Day_

  

Long-time allocators, top-performing institutions, and families have benefitted from the outperformance of private investment strategies, particularly private equity and venture capital. Looking ahead, how can investors navigate today’s headwinds of high inflation, recession fears, restless equity markets, and geopolitical turbulence to achieve long-term success?

On June 20, Cambridge Associates’ Andrea Auerbach, Global Head of Private Investments, held an interactive discussion in Seattle, Washington. Andrea shared perspectives on the current private investment landscape and explored the opportunities and challenges in this volatile environment.
