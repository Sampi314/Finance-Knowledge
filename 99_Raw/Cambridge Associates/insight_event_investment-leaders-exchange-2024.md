Investment Leaders Exchange
===========================

Apr 29, 2024

![Investment Leaders Exchange](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Event-InvestmentLeadersExchange-TILE-042924.png)

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - April 29, 2024 - April 30, 2024  
_1:30 pm - 4:15 pm_

  

In April, our extensive global network of asset allocators, fund managers, and industry leaders discussed all aspects of portfolio management to maximize the chances of portfolio outperformance.

Sessions covered a range of topics, including the energy transition, macro environment, broad asset allocation, manager implementation, governance, each major portfolio sleeve, and unconventional investment ideas. Attendees also had several opportunities to network with peers to share investment views.

Please note that this event was for Cambridge Associates’ clients and prospective clients only.
