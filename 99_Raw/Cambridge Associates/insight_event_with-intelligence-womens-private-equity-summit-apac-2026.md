Audrey The, Partner, and Scolet Ma, Senior Investment Director, will be speaking at the With Intelligence Women’s Private Equity Summit APAC 2026, taking place in Singapore from February 2-4, 2026.

Audrey will join the panel, “Family Offices Coming to the Fore,” on February 3, exploring how family offices are moving beyond traditional asset allocations in pursuit of enhanced returns and long-term resilience. On February 4, Scolet will contribute her insights on the “China: Will It Get Renewed Focus from Investors in ’26?” panel, discussing investor sentiment, opportunities, and challenges in the evolving Chinese market.

If you are keen to hear their perspectives on these topics, visit [the website](https://events.withintelligence.com/womensprivateequitysummitapac)
 for more information.
