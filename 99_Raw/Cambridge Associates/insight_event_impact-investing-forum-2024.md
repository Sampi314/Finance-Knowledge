Impact Investing Forum 2024
===========================

Oct 22, 2024

![Impact Investing Forum 2024](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/A-New-Approach.jpg)

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - October 22, 2024 - October 23, 2024  
_1:30 pm - 3:30 pm_

  

In October, we brought together forward-thinking allocators and other industry stakeholders to engage in timely discussions, peer-to-peer dialogue, and interactive thematic spotlight sessions focused on the latest innovations, case studies, and emerging best practices around [sustainable and impact investing](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/impact-investing/)
 (SII).

To commemorate our 10th year hosting the Impact Investing Forum, we reflected on the evolution of sustainable and impact investing over the past decade, provided tangible and action-oriented insights from learnings over the years, and made connections to the current rapidly changing landscape.

Please note this event was for clients and future clients of Cambridge Associates. For more information, please contact [events@cambridgeassociates.com](mailto:events@cambridgeassociates.com)
.
