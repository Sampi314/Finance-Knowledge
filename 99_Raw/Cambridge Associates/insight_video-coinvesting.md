Co-investments allow investors to make opportunistic investments that can enhance and complement their total portfolio. In this video series, our co-investment team discusses the opportunity set, the role of [co-investments in a portfolio](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/coinvestments/)
, guidelines for implementation, and what we believe are [keys to success](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/insight/six-things-to-know-about-co-investments/)
.
