Cambridge Associates

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - May 29, 2024  
_All Day_

**Categories**

*   [Industry Events](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/events/categories/industry-events/)
    

  

Managing Directors [Ming Yan](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/en-as/people/ming-yan/)
 and [Damien Tan](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/en-as/people/damien-tan/)
 will be participating in Institutional Investor’s 2024 Asia Single Family Office Roundtable in Singapore on May 29.

Ming will discuss how single family offices should approach portfolio construction and Damien will share his thoughts on managing the challenges of allocating to the US from Asia on their respective panels. They will also lead breakout discussions on conducting due diligence. For more information and to register, [click here](https://www.iinow.com/Asia-Single-Family-Office-Roundtable)
