Net Zero Investment Consultants Initiative Progress Report, September 2023
==========================================================================

Cambridge Associates  

Sep 30, 2023

![Net Zero Investment Consultants Initiative Progress Report, September 2023](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/AdobeStock_265488462-e1731448035165.jpeg)

The 2023 Net Zero Investment Consultants Initiative Progress Report underscores our commitment to achieve net zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050 or sooner.

This first progress report, published in September 2023, details initiatives in client education, investment manager assessments, and portfolio alignment with net zero targets to drive real-world emissions reductions.

Achieving net zero is a collective journey that requires ongoing industry engagement and innovation. Read the steps we’ve taken so far to integrate [net zero](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/climate-change-and-net-zero/)
 and where we’re aiming to make further progress.
