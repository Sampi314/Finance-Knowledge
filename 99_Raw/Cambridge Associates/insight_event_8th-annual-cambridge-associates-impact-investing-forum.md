8th Annual Cambridge Associates Impact Investing Forum
======================================================

Cambridge Associates  

Nov 09, 2022

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - November 9, 2022 - November 10, 2022  
_12:00 am_

**Location**  
[Four Seasons Denver](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/insight/event-locations/four-seasons-denver/)

  

Over a day and a half, Cambridge Associates, along with practitioners, forward-thinking allocators, and mission-driven innovators, engaged in discussions and interactive breakout sessions on the latest trends, action plans, case studies, and pioneering perspectives on sustainable and impact investing. We also explored the complementary themes of resonance and dissonance as they relate to sustainable and impact investing.
