Join Prabhat Ojha, Partner and Head of Asia Client Business, at the Milken Asia Summit 2025 taking place on October 1-3, 2025 in Singapore.

Prabhat will be a speaker on the “From Potential to Powerhouse: Navigating India’s Growth Journey” panel on October 2, where he will share his insights on how global investors can adapt their strategies in light of India’s rise as a global growth powerhouse.
