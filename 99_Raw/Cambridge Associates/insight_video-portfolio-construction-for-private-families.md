![Video: Portfolio Construction for Private Families](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/AdobeStock_198135664_s.jpeg)

Portfolio management for families of significant wealth is distinctly different than for institutions, or those with traditional wealth management needs. For these families, the wealth typically exists in a much more complex ecosystem—among real estate investments, operating companies, or multiple generations, by way of example. These factors should have a meaningful impact on investment strategy, yet critical inputs are often not fully considered, resulting in a portfolio that isn’t doing its best for the people it’s intended to serve.
