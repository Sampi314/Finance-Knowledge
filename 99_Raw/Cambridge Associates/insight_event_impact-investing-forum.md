Impact Investing Forum – London
===============================

Mar 07, 2023

![Impact Investing Forum – London](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/AdobeStock_3444160.jpg)

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - March 7, 2023  
_11:30 am - 7:00 pm_

  

On Tuesday, 7 March 2023 Cambridge Associates held a London-based **Impact Investing Forum**. This event brought together industry thought leaders and investors for interactive sessions and themes related to sustainable and impact investing.

It included a client panel showcasing their impact journeys, as well as sessions that focused on active shareholder engagement, the energy transition, and biodiversity. We engaged in discussions on what we, as investors and fiduciaries, can do with the opportunities and risks ahead.
