Video: The Future of Cambridge Associates
=========================================

Cambridge Associates  

Aug 11, 2023

![Video: The Future of Cambridge Associates](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/GettyImages-1180187740_1920.jpg)

In celebration of our first 50 years, CEO [David Druley](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/people/david-druley/)
 remarks on the future of the firm and how we will continue to build on our legacy of helping clients achieve their long-term investment goals.

When our co-founders started the firm in 1973, they had two objectives: look for ways to generate outperformance and minimize the conflicts that create misalignment between advisors and their clients. Those two touchstones are still at the core of everything we do, and we’ll continue to use our networks and intellectual capital to build custom portfolios and generate outperformance.
