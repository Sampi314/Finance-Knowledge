2024 Outlook Webinar
====================

Dec 07, 2023

![2024 Outlook Webinar](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/blue-building.png)

**Date/Time**  
Date(s) - December 7, 2023  
_9:00 am - 10:00 am_

  

We held a live webinar on Thursday, December 7, from 9-10am ET where Cambridge Associates’ thought leaders shared our 2024 investment outlook.

We discussed key questions confronting investors in the coming year, including:

*   How will global economic growth and inflation evolve next year?
*   What does that mean for interest rates and as a result, equity and credit investments, both public and private?
*   Where is the market wrongly pricing risks?

We also answered questions about the many risks in today’s challenging market environment.
