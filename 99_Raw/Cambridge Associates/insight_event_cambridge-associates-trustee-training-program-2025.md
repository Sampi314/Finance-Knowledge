Cambridge Associates Trustee Training Program 2025
==================================================

Cambridge Associates  

Oct 30, 2025

**Categories**

*   [Virtual Event](https://www.cambridgeassociates.com/events/categories/virtual-event/)
    

  

The Trustee Training Program is virtual and offered free of charge to both clients and non-clients of Cambridge Associates. It is specifically designed to help new, current, or potential trustees learn about the role of trustees in the investment process. **It is an entry-level course for those seeking to learn the basics or wanting a refresher on the fundamentals of the job and how to do it well.** The program will provide practical tools, examples, and case studies to orient fiduciaries to the role of investment committee member. The goal is to expand participation in endowment governance and access to information about effective practices.

The program will take place over **two 90-minute virtual sessions**. If you are interested in attending, please contact [Emma Thompson](mailto:ethompson@cambridgeassociates.com)
