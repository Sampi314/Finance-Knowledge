Yearbook and Proceedings
========================

Since its inception in 1914, the CAS has recorded the workings of the organization and the professional lives of its members via the Yearbook. Often printed as part of the [_Proceedings of the Casualty Actuarial Society_](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/library)
 (_PCAS_) the Yearbook served as a membership directory and housed all of the Society's important governance and policy documents.

Rendered obsolete by technology and transformed into other publications, the CAS Yearbook and _PCAS_ are historical records of a vibrant organization committed to advancing actuarial science and the profession.
