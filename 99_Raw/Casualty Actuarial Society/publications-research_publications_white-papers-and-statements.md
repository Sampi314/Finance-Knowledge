Insurance Rating Variables: What They Are and Why They Matter

By CAS and the Insurance Information Institute (I.I.I.)

This educative statement is recommended reading for anyone looking for an accessible primer on insurance rating variables, including federal lawmakers, regulators and the general public. The paper aims to improve public understanding of how rating variables are used and regulated, and the important role they play in pricing insurance.

[Download Paper](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2021-06/Insurance_rating_variables_white_paper.pdf)
