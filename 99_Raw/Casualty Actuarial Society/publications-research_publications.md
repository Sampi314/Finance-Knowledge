Publications
============

Our cutting-edge publications provide practical information to members and advance the body of actuarial knowledge.

![Publications Research](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/styles/feature/public/2021-01/publications-research-02.png?itok=-HWEWwIG)

Get Your Work Published

The CAS welcomes submissions to its publications from members and nonmembers through its online manuscript tracking system, ScholarOne. See individual publications for submission guidelines.

[Submit Your Work](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/submit-your-work)

Flagship Publications
---------------------

![CAS Actuarial Review Logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2021-02/AR%20app%20icon%201024x1024.png)

Actuarial Review

Actuarial Review is the bimonthly magazine of the Casualty Actuarial Society.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/actuarial-review)

![Variance Journal logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2021-02/Variance%20564x304.jpg)

Variance

Variance, the peer-reviewed journal of the Casualty Actuarial Society, disseminates original practical and theoretical research in casualty actuarial science to casualty actuaries worldwide.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/variance)

![CAS Monographs Book Cover](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2021-02/Monograph%20Book%20Cover%201-5-2015.jpg)

CAS Monographs

CAS Monographs are comprehensive, peer-reviewed works on single aspects of property casualty actuarial science. They may be a compendium or original research or both.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/flagship-publications/cas-monographs)

![](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/2021-04/Future_Fellows_featured_image.jpg)

Future Fellows

Future Fellows, the CAS quarterly newsletter for those sitting for CAS Exams, is written by members of the Candidate Liaison Committee and Candidate Representatives.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/membership/candidates/future-fellows-newsletter)

CAS Papers
----------

E-Forum

E-Forum is a nonrefereed, electronic publication of the Casualty Actuarial Society. The CAS is not responsible for statements or opinions expressed in E-Forum papers

[Learn More](https://eforum.casact.org/)

Research Papers and Briefs

CAS Research Papers are funded, peer-reviewed, in-depth works on important topics in property-casualty actuarial practice. CAS member dues, individual grants and other sources provide funding.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/cas-research-papers-and-briefs)

Working Papers

Working Papers reflect ideas or concepts being developed on which authors invite discussion. Discussions are encouraged and will be posted on the website.

[Learn More](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/cas-papers/cas-working-papers)
