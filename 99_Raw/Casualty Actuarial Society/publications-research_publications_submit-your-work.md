Publications
------------

Actuarial Review

![Actuarial Review logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/inline-images/AR.gif)

The [Actuarial Review](https://www.casact.org/cas-family-sites/actuarial-review)
 is the bimonthly magazine of the Casualty Actuarial Society. Articles and opinions are copy edited by the AR Working Group and CAS staff. Opinions are reviewed by a subgroup of the ARWG.

**Submitting Your Work**

*   Submit your article in a Word document via email to [AR@casact.org](mailto:AR@casact.org)
    .
*   **Submission Type:** Feature articles, columns, letters to the editor, career news, obituaries.
*   **Word Count**: 700-3,000 for feature articles and most department columns; 700 to 1,500 for opinion pieces (ViewPoint department); no more than 250 words for letters to the editor and career news. The CAS reserves the right to edit items for length.

See [_Actuarial Review_ Statement of Purpose](https://ar.casact.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/AR_Statement_of_Purpose.pdf)
 for more information.

**Timeline**

*   **Submission to Receipt Acknowledgment**: 1–7 days
*   **Response to author**: 2–8 days
*   **Review/Copyediting**: 1–7 days
*   **Author Review/Approval**: 1–3 days

![e-forum logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/inline-images/e-forum.gif)

The [_E-Forum_](https://eforum.casact.org/)

Monographs

![Monographs logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/inline-images/monographs.gif)

A [**CAS _Monograph_**](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/cas-monographs)
 is a comprehensive, long-form, peer-reviewed work on a single aspect of property-casualty actuarial science that is primarily educational in nature. CAS Monographs contribute to the body of literature that forms the foundational knowledge of actuarial science. Several CAS Monographs have been adopted into the syllabi of CAS Exams.

**Submitting Your Work**

*   [_Monograph_ Submission Guidelines](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/flagship-publications/cas-monographs/guidelines-submission)
    

**Contacts**

*   **Submissions Inquiries:** [Greg Guthrie](mailto:gguthrie@casact.org)
    

Variance

![Variance Logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/inline-images/Variance.gif)

[**_Variance_**](https://www.casact.org/variance-journal)
 is the peer-reviewed journal of the Casualty Actuarial Society. _Variance_ publishes papers that explore original practical and theoretical research in property-casualty actuarial science. Membership in the Casualty Actuarial Society is not required to submit papers to the journal, and submissions by nonmembers are encouraged.

**Submitting Your Work**

*   [Variance Submission Guidelines](https://variancejournal.org/for-authors)

*   **Word Count:** Under 10,000

**Contacts**

Working Papers

![Working Papers Logo](https://www.casact.org/sites/default/files/inline-images/working-papers.gif)

[Working Papers](https://www.casact.org/about-working-papers)
 reflect ideas or concepts that are still being developed and for which authors invite discussion. As such, Working Papers are considered works in progress. Discussions of Working Papers are encouraged and will be posted on the website along with the papers. With feedback on the papers, authors can modify and prepare papers for submission to publications. Both CAS members and nonmembers may submit Working Papers.

**Submitting Your Work**

*   [Working Papers Submission Guidelines](https://www.casact.org/publications-research/publications/cas-working-papers-faqs)
