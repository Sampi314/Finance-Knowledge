What You Get
------------

*   Chapter 1: 2026 Market Landscape — Recruiting timeline forecast, market structure analysis, on-cycle vs. off-cycle dynamics, and how the 2026 cycle differs from prior years
*   Chapter 2: The Headhunter Ecosystem — The 7 firms that control PE access (CPI, Henkel, SG Partners, Ratio, Amity, DSP, Oxbridge), their specialties, and how to approach each
*   Chapter 3: Bank-to-Fund Pipeline — Which banks and groups place into which funds, placement data by group, and how to position yourself within your bank's pipeline
*   Chapter 4: Non-Traditional Paths — MBB consulting, Big 4 TAS, corporate development, veterans, and other paths into PE with realistic timelines and requirements
*   Chapter 5: Undergrad Direct Programs — Blackstone, KKR, Warburg Pincus, Apollo, Carlyle analyst programs, application timelines, and what makes candidates stand out
*   Chapter 6: LBO Mechanics — Sources & uses from first principles, debt schedules, cash flow waterfall, returns analysis, and the intuition behind every calculation
*   Chapter 7: Paper LBO Framework — The 10-minute method with full worked example, mental math shortcuts, and the presentation format that impresses interviewers
*   Chapter 8: Modeling Tests — Time management framework, fatal errors to avoid, and how to handle the 3-hour take-home vs. 1-hour in-office format
*   Chapter 9: Technical Deep Dive — Accounting, credit concepts, EV/equity bridge, and the follow-up questions that separate offers from dings
*   Chapter 10: Behavioral & Fit — The 'Why PE' framework, deal discussion structure (60-second walkthrough), and culture signals for megafund vs. middle-market
*   Chapter 11: Firm Selection — Megafund profiles (BX, KKR, Apollo, TPG, Carlyle, H&F), practitioner rankings, and optimizing for learning, comp, exits, or work-life balance
*   Chapter 12: Compensation Data — 2025-2026 comp by level (Associate through Partner), megafund to LMM, base, bonus, carry, and co-invest breakdowns
*   Chapter 13: Career Progression — Promotion rates, what distinguishes people who make Partner, and exit opportunities at each level
*   Chapter 14: Networking & Applications — Email templates with high response rates, resume tailoring for PE, and the channel-by-channel effectiveness data
*   Chapter 15: Contrarian Insights — 5 myths debunked (megafund obsession, on-cycle panic, technical overemphasis) with data showing why conventional wisdom fails
*   Appendix: Mental math rules, IRR approximation tables, common LBO assumptions by sector, and quick-reference cheat sheets
