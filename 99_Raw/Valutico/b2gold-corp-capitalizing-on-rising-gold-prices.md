**Leading Miner**
-----------------

B2Gold is a low-cost gold producer with operations in Mali, Namibia, and the Philippines. It’s developing the Goose Project in northern Canada and exploring additional sites in Mali, Colombia, and Finland. The company forecasts a gold production between 800,000 and 870,000 ounces for 2024.

**Gold price surge**
--------------------

B2Gold stands to benefit from soaring gold prices, which recently hit record highs above USD 2,500 per ounce. This price surge is driven by ongoing geopolitical tensions, which have increased demand for safe-haven assets like gold. Additionally, the Federal Reserve recently announced a rate cut of 50 bps, which is expected to give a further boost to the gold prices.

**Production Challenges**
-------------------------

B2Gold has encountered production setbacks this year, particularly due to a delay in securing an exploration license from the Malian government for the Fekola mine. However, higher realized gold prices have helped mitigate the impact, supporting revenue growth in 2024 and a strong financial outlook​.

**Goose Project on Track**
--------------------------

The Goose Project in Nunavut, Canada, is set to become a major growth catalyst for B2Gold, with 3.6 million ounces in reserves and expected production of 310,000 ounces annually from 2026. The project is progressing on schedule for first gold production by mid-2025, and exploration in nearby regions promises long-term upside potential​.

**Valutico’s View**
-------------------

Valutico’s analysis suggests that B2Gold is currently undervalued. The discounted cash flow (DCF) model estimates the company’s equity value at CAD 8.9 billion, while the Trading Comparables place its valuation between CAD 8 to 9 billion. Despite this, B2Gold is trading at a notable discount compared to peers like Agnico Eagle Mines Limited and Barrick Gold Corporation, likely due to its operations being concentrated in higher-risk geographic regions.

**Long term outlook**
---------------------

With rising gold prices, the expansion of the Fekola mine, and the upcoming launch of the Goose Project, B2Gold is well-positioned for long-term growth. However, moving forward, it will be crucial to monitor key growth drivers, including managing geographical risks, keeping production costs low, and expanding gold reserves.
