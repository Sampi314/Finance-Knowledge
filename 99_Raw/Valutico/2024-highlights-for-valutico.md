2024 Year-End Recap: Valutico Highlights
========================================

_Valutico | Dec 17 2024_

  
As the year draws to a close, we’re taking a moment to reflect on what made 2024 such a great year for Valutico. From global events and innovative product releases to sustainability milestones and team connections, this year has been one for the books. Here’s a recap of our favorite moments.

![](https://valutico.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Group-35501.png)

**🌍 Connecting Around the Globe**
----------------------------------

This year, we traveled far and wide to meet with clients, partners, and industry leaders. From London to Singapore, Paris, Milan, and more, we’ve had the privilege of sharing insights and discussing the future of valuation at some incredible events. Notable highlights include attending **AccountEx in London** and the **Business and Accounting Show in Singapore**. These opportunities to connect face-to-face are what fuel our passion for innovation and collaboration.

![](https://valutico.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Group-35502.png)

**🏆 Celebrating Significant Achievements**
-------------------------------------------

2024 brought us plenty of milestones to celebrate, and we couldn’t be prouder:

*   We won the **Crozdesk Quality Choice Award,** recognizing the excellence of our platform.
*   Our community grew to over **10,000 followers on LinkedIn.**
*   Through our **ValutECO initiative,** we planted over **900 trees** in partnership with the World Land Trust, helping to offset carbon emissions and support reforestation.

![](https://valutico.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Group-35500.png)

**🚀 Launching a Record Number of New Features**
------------------------------------------------

2024 was a banner year for innovation at Valutico. We released over thirty significant upgrades to make valuations faster, smarter, and more tailored to your needs. Some highlights in 2024 include:

*   A new way to do business forecasts with ValuPlan
*   Multiple ways to conduct automated cash flow projections
*   New AI tool for M&A transactions recommendations
*   New AI discount rates calculator assistant
*   Tax loss carryforward added to ValuPlan Plus
*   New advanced search for peers selection
*   New rating analysis tab in ValuPlan
*   Completely new custom transaction management
*   Adjusted Net Asset Value (ANAV) method
*   Expanded customizable valuation report templates

These features are a testament to our commitment to empowering finance professionals with the best tools in the industry.

![](https://valutico.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Group-35503.png)

**🤝 Building Stronger Connections**
------------------------------------

Although we’re a remote-first team, our in-person meetups are at the heart of what makes Valutico special. This year, we made the most of our time together, sharing ideas, celebrating wins, and strengthening the bonds that drive our collaborative spirit.

![](https://valutico.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Group-35504-1.png)

**🦆** **Adventures of the Valutico Duck**
------------------------------------------

What began as a playful team mascot has transformed into a globe-trotting sensation! The Valutico Duck has made appearances everywhere this year—from festivals to scenic waterfalls and more. Wherever it went, it brought smiles and showcased the fun-loving spirit of Valutico.

**Looking Ahead to 2025**
-------------------------

As we reflect on everything 2024 brought us, we’re filled with gratitude for our clients, partners, and community. Your support has been instrumental in making this year such a success, and we’re excited to see what’s next.

From all of us at Valutico, thank you for being part of our journey. Here’s to an even brighter 2025!
