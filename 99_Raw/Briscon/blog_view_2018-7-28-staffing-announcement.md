Announcement from Brisbane Consulting
=====================================

Posted on August 28, 2018

![ Benjamin Schuver, CPA, MBA ](https://briscon.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Schuver2CBenjaminBrisCon.jpg)

[Benjamin Schuver, CPA, MBA](https://briscon.com/people/view/benjamin-schuver/)
 joined Brisbane Consulting Group as a Senior Analyst in August 2018. As Senior Analyst, Ben’s responsibilities include the valuation of businesses, business interests, and intangible assets for purposes of financial reporting, transaction advisory, and tax planning and compliance.  As a Certified Public Accountant, Ben’s prior experience includes performing assurance services for publicly traded companies and large private companies within the aerospace, manufacturing, and gaming industries.  He also has experience in evaluating internal controls over financial reporting. Ben is a graduate of Canisius College with his MBA in Accounting and a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Finance.
