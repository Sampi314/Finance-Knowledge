Law Changes Deductibility of Meals and Entertainment
====================================================

Posted on March 15, 2018

The Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA) commonly referred to as tax reform has changed the deductibility of certain meals and entertainment expenses.  Documentation is especially important in 2018 and now is the time to correctly allocate these expenses and budget accordingly.  **[Print one-page PDF](https://briscon.com/s/Brisbane-Meals-and-Entertainment-New-Rules-2018.pdf)
