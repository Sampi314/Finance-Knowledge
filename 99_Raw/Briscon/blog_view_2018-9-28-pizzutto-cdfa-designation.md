Announcement from Brisbane Consulting - Audrey Pizzuto, CDFA®
=============================================================

Posted on September 28, 2018

\[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2400.0"\]![  Audrey Pizzuto, CDFA®  ](https://briscon.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/Pizutto2CAudreyBrisCon.jpg)

  Audrey Pizzuto, CDFA®  \[/caption\]  
 

Congratulations to [Audrey Pizzuto, CDFA®](https://briscon.squarespace.com/audrey-bio)
 on earning the Certified Divorce Financial Analyst (CDFA®) designation from the Institute for Divorce Financial Analysts™.

Audrey joined Brisbane as a senior analyst responsible for valuation, forensic accounting, and litigation support services. Prior to joining Brisbane Consulting Group, Audrey worked for a commercial enterprise and as an auditor in a public accounting firm. Audrey is a graduate of John Wiley Jones School of Business at the State University of New York at Geneseo where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting.
