Announcements from Brisbane Consulting
======================================

Posted on February 27, 2018

![ Jenna Palczynski ](https://briscon.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Palczynski_JennawithLogo.jpg)    
 

[**Jenna Palczynski**](https://briscon.com/people/view/jenna-palczynski/)
 joined Brisbane Consulting Group as a Staff Analyst in January 2018. Her responsibilities include valuation, forensic accounting, and litigation support services. Jenna is a graduate from Canisius College with a Bachelor of Science degree in Professional Accounting. She joins the Firm with audit experience previously serving in municipal government.  Jenna is a resident of Lakeview.
