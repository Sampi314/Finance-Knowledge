Miles Promoted to Senior Analyst
================================

Posted on February 05, 2016

Brisbane Consulting Group, LLC is pleased to announce that **Johnathon Miles, CPA** has been promoted to senior analyst. 

As senior analyst, Johnathon is responsible for valuation, forensic accounting, and litigation support services.  He has prior public accounting experience working in the tax department of a Syracuse CPA firm and has commercial sales experience working for an automotive firm for more than five years.  John earned his masters of business administration in accounting and finance and a bachelor’s degree in accounting from Niagara University.  He is a board member with the Literacy Empowerment Action Plan (LEAP) of WNY.
