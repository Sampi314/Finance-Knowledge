Burford commissioned independent research investigating the impact of macroeconomic trends on commercial litigation and what GCs expect from their law firm partners. The research was conducted through extensive one-on-one interviews with 66 GCs, heads of litigation and other senior in-house lawyers responsible for litigation in the US, Europe, Asia and Australia.  

In this webcast, Co-Chief Operating Officers Aviva Will and David Perla discuss key findings from the research and their implications for businesses and law firms, including:  

*   Expected increase in commercial disputes in the next 2 years 
    
*   How tighter budgets will impact businesses’ ability to pursue recoveries 
    
*   Innovation and risk-sharing GCs expect from their firms 
    
*   The growing role of legal finance for businesses and law firms
