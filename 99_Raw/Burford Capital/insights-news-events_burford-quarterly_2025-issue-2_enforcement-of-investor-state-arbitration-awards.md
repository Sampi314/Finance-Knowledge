Compliance with and enforcement of investor-state arbitration awards: Testing the datasets
==========================================================================================

*   International arbitration

April 30, 2025

**Jeffery Commission & Hannah Howlett**

![](https://www.burfordcapital.com/media/agebmajh/quarterly-no2-2025-headshots-01.png?width=520&height=390&v=1dbb92c22860670)

Summary
-------

Compliance with ICSID awards was once seen as a cornerstone of state credibility, with most states fulfilling their obligations. Today, however, a growing number of unpaid awards—over 100 involving 20 states—suggests a troubling shift in the enforcement landscape.
