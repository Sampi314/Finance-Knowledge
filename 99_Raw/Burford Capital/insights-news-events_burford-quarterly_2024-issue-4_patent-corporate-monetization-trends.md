The intellectual property revolution: Trends in corporate IP monetization
=========================================================================

September 26, 2024

**Katharine Wolanyk & Christopher Freeman**

![](https://www.burfordcapital.com/media/13uldsq4/katharine_chris-f.jpg?width=520&height=390&v=1db0f2cb814a520)

Summary
-------

Corporate intellectual property monetization is far from new, but recent years have witnessed numerous noteworthy transactions involving respected and sophisticated IP owners—largely operating companies with global footprints—suggesting growing recognition of patents as valuable financial assets.
