As the lead author of one of the industry’s most highly anticipated annual reviews of law firm economics, Hildebrandt is uniquely equipped to provide insights into what the numbers say about the legal market and industry performance in 2023, the impact of legal tech investment and key factors influencing the industry in 2024 and beyond.  

Topics include: 

*   How technology and AI is being adopted throughout the legal industry  
    
*   Key factors impacting law firm performance in 2023  
    
*   Growth opportunities for law firms in 2024  
    
*   Key trends to anticipate in 2024 
    

The webcast took place at **12:00PM EST | 5:00PM GMT**.
