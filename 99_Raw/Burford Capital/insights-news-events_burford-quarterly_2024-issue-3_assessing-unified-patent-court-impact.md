European lawyers weigh in on the Unified Patent Court
=====================================================

June 19, 2024

**Eric Carlson & Katharine Wolanyk**

![](https://www.burfordcapital.com/media/ciukkhzu/susan-wilkinson-nepensqs93y-unsplash_sm.jpg?width=520&height=390&v=1d9f307e32b59d0)

Summary
-------

The 2023 launch of the Unified Patent Court (UPC) is a significant development in global patent litigation. To understand its impact, members of Burford’s top-ranked patent team directed questions to a panel of European patent litigation experts on the trends and opportunities resulting from the UPC and the growth of corporate IP monetization.
