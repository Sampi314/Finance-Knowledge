Trends in commercial arbitration: Perspective from global arbitral centers
==========================================================================

*   International arbitration

April 17, 2024

**Christiane Deniger & Quentin Pak**

![](https://www.burfordcapital.com/media/3f1fxed2/adobestock_411810562_sm.jpg?width=520&height=390&v=1d9f307e2270930)

Summary
-------

Burford convened a panel of arbitration experts from arbitral centers around the world to shed light on current trends shaping the global commercial disputes landscape, including the use of AI, the impact of current conflicts and geopolitical tensions and the increasing importance of legal finance in arbitration. Leigh Mallon, Sara Nadeau-Seguin and Paul Tan are based, respectively, in London, Paris and Singapore.
