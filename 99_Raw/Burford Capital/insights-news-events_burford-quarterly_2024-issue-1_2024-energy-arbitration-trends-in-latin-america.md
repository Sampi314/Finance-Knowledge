As energy and mining arbitration heats up in Latin America, so does legal finance
=================================================================================

*   International arbitration

January 11, 2024

**Florencia Villaggi**

![](https://www.burfordcapital.com/media/ljyoowtt/latin-america-article-florencia.jpg?width=520&height=390&v=1dad84af81a3ea0)

Summary
-------

Of all pending ICSID claims (322), nearly 40% seated in Latin America. Across just five countries—Peru, Mexico, Venezuela, Colombia and Honduras—82 claims are pending, many of which relate to energy. Indeed, nearly half of the 115 LatAm-based claims relate to energy, oil, gas, mining and construction.
