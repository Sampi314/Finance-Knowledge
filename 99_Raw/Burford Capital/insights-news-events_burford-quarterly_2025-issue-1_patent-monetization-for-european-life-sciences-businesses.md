==============================================================================================================

*   Patent & IP

Summary
-------

Innovation in life sciences is vital to advancing global healthcare, but it requires immense investment — of ideas, time, and capital. From navigating Europe’s complex IP frameworks to facing high-stakes R&D cycles, life sciences firms must strategically manage and monetize their intellectual property to sustain innovation, and are increasingly using legal finance to do so.
