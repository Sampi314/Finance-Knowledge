**The Challenge**
-----------------

Manually updating PowerPoint presentations with data from Excel was tedious, error-prone, and inefficient. Analysts spent hours copy-pasting information, double-checking figures, and chasing down the latest version of reports—slowing down delivery and introducing unnecessary risk.

Updating PowerPoint presentations with data from Excel was a manual, error-prone process that consumed hours of analyst time, slowing down delivery and introducing unnecessary risk. Despite investing in a third-party tool, the solution proved unreliable—frequent crashes, broken links, and limited scalability left the team frustrated and workflows fragmented.

**Key challenges faced:**

*   Time-consuming, manual Excel-to-PowerPoint linking.
*   Increased risk of data inconsistencies and human error due to manual processes.
*   Inefficient workflows and repetitive formatting tasks.
*   Limited scalability as reporting demands grew.
*   Version control issues that created inconsistencies, confusion, and rework.

**The Solution**
----------------

Macabacus was selected over the incumbent for its powerful Excel-to-PowerPoint linking capabilities, robust audit tools, and smooth integration with Microsoft 365. The implementation process was straightforward — Macabacus’ intuitive interface and accessible training resources made it easy for the team to get up and running quickly.

Since implementing Macabacus, the firm has seen measurable improvements in reporting speed, accuracy, and collaboration.

*   **Scalable Workflows**: Standardized Templates, automations, and auditing tools have made it easy to scale reporting as the team grows, while improving accuracy and consistency.
