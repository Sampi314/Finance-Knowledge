Version Control – Stay Brand Compliant With Up-to-Date Content
==============================================================

Introducing **Version Control** — a powerful, new feature that’s designed to keep your entire organization aligned and brand-compliant. Version Control replaces the old slide-linking method with a more robust solution, ensuring that everyone in your team or organization is using the latest firm-approved content and brand standards across all their presentations and documents. Version Control ensures:

*   **Brand Compliance:** Ensure that all team members are consistently using the latest approved content, keeping your organization aligned and on brand.
*   **Streamlined Management:** The Version Control pane simplifies the process of updating content, making it easy to maintain consistency.

**Stay On-Brand with Version Control**
--------------------------------------

### ![version control screenshot](https://macabacus.com/assets/2024/08/Version-Control-Screenshot.png)

### **Consistent Content Management**

Macabacus will automatically track and manage newer versions of your firm-approved Office content, including slides, shapes, and images, making them available to your organization’s Macabacus users. This feature provides your team with the most up-to-date and brand-compliant materials, helping you ensure that consistent standards are met across your organization.

### **Centralized Library Content Pane**

The new Library Content pane makes it easy for users to quickly identify and update outdated items, ensuring that everyone in your organization is using the latest approved content. Whether it’s a slide, shape, or image, Version Control helps keep your entire team on the same page. Macabacus will also automatically check for newer versions upon opening a presentation or document.

### **Cross-Platform Support**

Version Control is currently supported in both PowerPoint and Word. This ensures brand consistency not only in presentations but also across documents.

**Conclusion**
--------------

Maintaining brand consistency, and ensuring that your presentations and documents are using the latest and greatest slides, shapes, and images, is essential in today’s fast-paced business world. Macabacus’s new Version Control feature ensures that your entire team always uses the latest firm-approved content, whether in PowerPoint or Word. The centralized Library Content pane simplifies managing and updating materials, making brand compliance easier than ever.
