**What’s New in Super Find?**
-----------------------------

### **Intuitive Task Pane**

When you select Super Find, a streamlined task pane appears on the right-hand side, categorizing search features into intuitive groups. Whether you’re looking for values, dates, or formulas, everything is neatly organized for easy access.

![superfind image task pane](https://macabacus.com/assets/2024/07/Superfind-image-task-pane.jpg)

### **Advanced Search Capabilities**

*   **Formula Searches:** Locate specific formulas, such as margin ratio calculations or growth projections (beta), hidden within your workbook. Super Find makes it simple to identify these key financial model drivers.
*   **Range Searches:** Discover print areas, used ranges, named ranges, and Macabacus-linked cells effortlessly.
*   **Format Searches:** Easily identify different types of formats like merged cells.
*   **Data Searches:** Locate specific data types, such as pivot tables.

### **Integrated Explore Mode**

The previous Explorer Pane has been phased out and consolidated into the current Super Find tool. Now, by selecting Explore Mode, you can navigate key features of any workbook, worksheet, or all open workbooks. View used ranges, graphics, images, charts, and numeric inputs with ease.

**Advantages of Super Find Over Excel’s Native Find**
-----------------------------------------------------

*   Seamlessly navigate between multiple worksheets or workbooks to find content that meets your criteria.
*   Quickly select all results to perform follow-up actions like formatting.
*   Search within a selection, selected sheets, the active workbook, or all open workbooks.
*   Display search results in a non-intrusive, convenient task pane.
*   Simultaneously search within values, formulas, comments, hyperlinks, and more.

**Conclusion**
--------------

Super Find is your go-to solution for efficient and effective searches for practically anything in your workbook. Unlike the native Find capabilities which are limited to text and specific formats, Super Find allows you to search for text, formulas, dates, formats, charts, and much more.
