Pitchly and Macabacus Announce Strategic Partnership to Bring Data Generated Content Directly to Microsoft Office
=================================================================================================================

3 minutes read

Last updated: April 11, 2025

**August 9, New York, NY** - Pitchly, a leading provider of innovative data and content management solutions, is delighted to announce a strategic integration with Macabacus, the award-winning Microsoft Office add-in for finance professionals. ![macabacus pitchly partnership](https://macabacus.com/assets/2023/08/macabacus-pitchly-partnership.svg) This partnership brings together two industry-leading solutions to streamline workflows and enhance productivity across professional services teams. By integrating Pitchly's leading-edge data and content management capabilities with Macabacus' advanced financial modeling and presentation tools, this partnership will empower teams to achieve unparalleled efficiency, build better deliverables, and make informed decisions.

### Key Integration Highlights

*   **Tombstone and Pitch Deck Automation:** With the integration of Pitchly and Macabacus, users can seamlessly access, filter and insert branded content, such as tombstones or bio slides, directly into their PowerPoint pitch decks. This integration reduces time spent on manually entering data and creating branded digital assets.
*   **Effortless Collaboration:** The partnership enables content and data collaboration like never before. Operations and Marketing teams have the ability to set templates and automate the creation of data-driven content, ensuring all users have accurate data in their pitch materials without manual data entry.
*   **Dynamic Content:** Users will experience an unprecedented level of automation by leveraging Pitchly’s Data and Content APIs within Macabacus, to ensure pitch materials are never stale. Up-to-date content can be created in just a few clicks.
*   **Improved Data Governance:** The integration adheres to the highest data security and governance standards, protecting data incompliance with industry regulations.

"We are excited about this integration with Macabacus, as it aligns with our mission to help professionals effectively activate the data inside of their organization," said Vikram Nair, Director and Head of Partnerships at Pitchly. "By joining forces, we are enabling seamless content and data management, simplifying complex workflows for our customers to achieve unparalleled efficiency." "Pitchly's state-of-the-art data and content management platform complements our digital asset management tools perfectly," said Ryan MacGregor, Executive Vice President and Head of Product at Macabacus. "This integration will undoubtedly benefit our users, as they can now leverage dynamic content with up-to-date assets directly from Macabacus."

### About Pitchly

Pitchly was founded on the belief that the future of work is data-driven, not task-driven. With a focus on solving the last-mile dilemma for business data, Pitchly has created a place where people, data, and content come together to generate real business outcomes. Pitchly's data enablement suite of applications, extensions, and integrations empowers collaboration, automation, and bottom-line value for marquee-level clients around the globe.

### About Macabacus

Macabacus, a Corporate Finance Institute company, is the leading Microsoft 365 add-in solution for finance and banking. It’s the fastest way to get more done, from complex financial models to branded presentations. With Macabacus, tens of thousands of finance & banking professionals worldwide get hours back in their day and deliver accurate analysis & presentations every time. The partnership between Pitchly and Macabacus marks an exciting milestone in data-driven innovation.
