Your Firm’s Single Source of Truth Is a Shared Library
------------------------------------------------------

Every banker has had that moment: you know a market overview slide or industry chart exists somewhere, but finding it means digging through previous decks or frantically sending messages to your colleagues. Multiply that by dozens of slides a week, and you’ve got a serious drag on productivity. 

[Shared libraries](https://macabacus.com/features/shared-libraries)
 solve that problem. Imagine what your teams could accomplish if they could access a single central repository for your approved slides, charts, disclaimers, and bios. Team members just pull what they need, and it’s ready to drop into your pitchbook. 

*   **Efficiency:** Analysts spend less time searching and doing what they do best 
*   **Consistency**: Every office, whether it’s in New York or London, works from the same set of materials 
*   **Speed:** Client-ready decks built with fewer, faster review cycles 

Here’s what that might look like in practice. When a Managing Director calls for a deck update by tomorrow morning, you don’t have to start over from the beginning. Instead, you assemble a polished presentation from pre-approved building blocks. 

Streamlining Deal Credentials with Tombstones
---------------------------------------------

[Tombstones](https://macabacus.com/features/tombstones)
 are proof that your firm can do what it claims. They highlight real-world achievements and outcomes for potential clients or existing clients about to start a new project. The problem is that tombstone management is rarely simple. Without a structured system, tombstones get buried in past presentations, inconsistently formatted, or, worse, end up out of date. 

A tombstone library changes that. With a DAM platform, you can: 

*   Store all deal credentials in one centralized, presentation-ready database 
*   Filter tombstones however you need (by sector, deal size, geography, or time period) 
*   Insert them into a deck in just a few clicks, perfectly formatted every time 

Let’s say you need to highlight all software M&A deals closed in the past 18 months. Instead of scrambling through folders, you filter, select, and drop them in.

Brand Compliance at Scale with Dynamic Presentation Templates 
--------------------------------------------------------------

Every pitchbook has an agenda slide, disclaimers, standardized layouts, and branded formatting. The challenge is in keeping them consistent while working under the pressure of a deadline. Manually fixing margins, numbering, or fonts wastes time and increases the chance that you’ll make a mistake. 

[Presentation templates](https://macabacus.com/features/presentation-templates)

*   Dynamic agendas update automatically as slides move around 
*   Pre-populated slides make sure disclaimers and standard sections are never missed 
*   Formatting controls set standards for layout/design, fonts, and more in just a few clicks 

DAM Tools in the Real World
---------------------------

The value of these tools shows up in daily workflows, like the following: 

*   **Pitchbook creation:** Instead of piecing together slides from old decks, analysts assemble a pitchbook fast with pre-approved slides, tombstones, and logos from shared libraries 
*   **Quarterly reporting:** A shared library makes sure that every team pulls from the same templates and charts, so reports are right every single time 
*   **Client updates:** When a client needs an updated deck overnight, a DAM library lets you deliver fast and with confidence 

The result is measurable: hours shaved off every week, fewer last-minute errors, and decks that reflect the professionalism of your firm every time.
