**From Pitch Decks to charts to financial models, Macabacus Has You Covered**
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

### **Streamlined Document Generation with Shared Libraries**

Shared libraries are a core feature of Macabacus, centralizing and standardizing your firm’s content. Users can access firm-approved assets like templates, logos, and images in one place. For example, when building a financial model, the library ensures everyone uses the latest standardized templates, maintaining consistency across your team.

### **Enhanced Charting Functionality via Quick Charts**

Macabacus excels at creating charts, offering a suite of pre-installed Quick Charts that are easily accessible with just a click. Among these is the Stacked Waterfall Chart, a sophisticated chart type that would otherwise be onerous to create manually. What sets Macabacus apart is that it is fully compatible with native Office 365 functionality on Excel, PowerPoint, and Word, ensuring that you face no errors while transferring data across different applications. 

### **Seamless Integration with PowerPoint**

Macabacus makes brand-compliant presentations effortless by integrating with PowerPoint and providing firm-approved templates. Users can link Excel charts to PowerPoint, ensuring updates sync automatically. The shared library stores ready-made tombstones with intuitive filters for quick insertion. Macabacus can deploy branded templates, like firm-approved letterhead, in Word for seamless document creation and publishing.

### **Effortless Document Creation and Customization with DocBuilder**

Macabacus utilizes the DocBuilder utility to create documents, even for complex use cases quickly. All users need to do is populate some key information to the template, such as the company name, type of entity, and jurisdiction, and with one click, your document gets built. For example, to draft an NDA using DocBuilder, the user indicates options around language and IP rights, and the document will automatically be updated to reflect your choices. This saves a considerable amount of time that would otherwise have been used in manual editing and ensures consistency and accuracy across your organization.
