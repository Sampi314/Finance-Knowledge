**What Does the Arrange Shapes Tool Do?**
-----------------------------------------

Whether you’re working with just a handful of shapes or many, aligning them can often be a challenge. [Macabacus Shape Tools](https://macabacus.com/features/presentation-shape-tools)
 like  new Arrange Shapes tool simplifies this process. To arrange multiple shapes in a specific layout—such as a **grid**—all it takes is a few clicks:

1.  **Select the shapes** you want to arrange on your PowerPoint slide.
2.  Navigate to the **Macabacus ribbon** and click on **Arrange Shapes**.

From here, you’ll see a variety of layout options available. The grid option is one of the most popular choices, but there are other configurations to explore as well.

**Simple, Efficient, and Versatile**
------------------------------------

What makes this tool stand out is how easy it is to use. You can arrange shapes quickly and precisely, whether they come from your library of shapes or are newly added elements in your presentation. The Arrange Shapes tool was previously limited to library shapes, but now it’s available for all shapes in PowerPoint, making it a versatile addition to your toolkit.
