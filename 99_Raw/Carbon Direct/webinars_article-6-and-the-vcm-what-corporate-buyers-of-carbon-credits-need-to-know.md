Webinar: Article 6 and the VCM, what corporate buyers of carbon credits need to know
====================================================================================

The voluntary carbon market, vital for addressing emissions challenges and aiding climate efforts, could undergo significant change with Article 6 of the Paris Climate Agreement, aiming to establish an internationally regulated carbon credit market. However, concerns over credit quality and comprehension of Article 6 implications have hindered some companies from participating in carbon removal credit transactions.

Join [Carbon Direct Chief Scientist Dr. Julio Friedmann](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-julio-friedmann)
 and [Senior Forest Scientist Dr. Bodie Cabiyo](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-bodie-cabiyo)
 for a webinar to learn about the future of Article 6 and what it means for your carbon removal strategy, including:

*   How high-profile carbon credit quality issues may affect future credit supply and oversight
    
*   How emerging voluntary standards affect project quality and buyer risk, and how those might affect Article 6 structure and progress
    
*   Best practices for corporate buyers to evaluate carbon projects to mitigate risk and maximize impact
