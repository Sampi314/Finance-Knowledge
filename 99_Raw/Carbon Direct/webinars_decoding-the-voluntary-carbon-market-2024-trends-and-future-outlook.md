Decoding the Voluntary Carbon Market: 2024 Trends and Future Outlook
====================================================================

As the urgency to combat climate change intensifies, understanding the dynamics of the Voluntary Carbon Market (VCM) is increasingly important. This webinar will explore 2024 trends and developments shaping the VCM, with a particular emphasis on carbon dioxide removal (CDR) and its implications for stakeholders, including carbon credit purchasers, project developers, and policymakers.

What you can expect to learn:

*   Key trends influencing the Voluntary Carbon Market in 2024.
    
*   An analysis of supply and demand-side developments in the CDR market.
    
*   Strategies for navigating opportunities within the evolving VCM landscape.
