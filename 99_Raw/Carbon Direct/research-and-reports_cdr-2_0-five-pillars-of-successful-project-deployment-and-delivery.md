White paper

CDR 2.0: Five Pillars of Successful Project Deployment and Delivery
===================================================================

Why do CDR projects backed by forward commitments sometimes still fail to deliver credits? And what can savvy buyers and suppliers do to mitigate risk and ensure performance?

What's inside:

*   Why CDR 1.0 stalled: How compressed front-end loading, misaligned contracting, and the absence of binding offtakes leave projects stranded before a final investment decision
    
*   CDR 2.0: Five pillars—technical readiness, project assurance, standardization, bankable contracting, and transactional ease—that move buyers from the purchase of promises to the procurement of performance
    
*   An action agenda: How to fund project assurance, structure risk-matched capital tranches, and secure the take-or-pay agreements that underwrite project finance
    

Published: Feb 2026
