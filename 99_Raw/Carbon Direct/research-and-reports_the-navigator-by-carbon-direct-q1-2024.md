Q1 2024

The Navigator by Carbon Direct
==============================

The proliferation of news is a good sign of progress on climate action but it can also be overwhelming. In this first issue of _The Navigator_, we distill key carbon market, science, and policy developments from Q1 2024, paired with insights from our team of science and policy experts. Our goal is to help you better navigate this complex landscape.
