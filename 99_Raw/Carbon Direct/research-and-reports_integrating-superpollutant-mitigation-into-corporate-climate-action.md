Integrating Superpollutant Mitigation into Corporate Climate Action
===================================================================

Superpollutants are responsible for roughly 35-50% of today’s climate warming, yet they are missing from many corporate climate strategies. This report outlines existing and emerging opportunities to change that. 

**Key takeaways:**

*   Pairing superpollutant abatement with durable carbon removal delivers both immediate and lasting climate impact.
    
*   The voluntary carbon market already supports credible superpollutant abatement projects; the opportunity now is scaling them. 
    
*   Annual warming matching is a more robust accounting framework than GWP100 alone for achieving a consistently planet-cooling outcome over time.
    
*   Assessing how to include superpollutants in your corporate climate strategy is hard, but Carbon Direct can help.
