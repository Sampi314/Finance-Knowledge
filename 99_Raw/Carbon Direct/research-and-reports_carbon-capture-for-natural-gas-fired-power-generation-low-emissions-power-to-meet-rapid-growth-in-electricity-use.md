Carbon Capture for Natural Gas-Fired Power Generation
=====================================================

**Low-emissions power to meet rapid growth in electricity use**

As electricity demand rises with AI data centers, industrial expansion, and electrification, natural gas remains a key energy source. Carbon capture technology can reduce emissions by up to 95 percent, enabling scalable, low-carbon power. With new power plants facing multi-year delays, capture-ready facilities provide a faster path to decarbonization. This white paper explores the opportunities and challenges of deploying carbon capture for natural gas-fired power, including sequestration, transportation, and regulatory considerations for reliable, low-carbon energy.

Published: Feb 2025
