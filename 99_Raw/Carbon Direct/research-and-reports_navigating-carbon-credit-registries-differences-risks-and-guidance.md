White paper

Navigating Carbon Credit Registries: Differences, risks, and guidance
=====================================================================

Choosing which registry to source carbon credits from is an important decision. This white paper analyzes the seven registries behind 90% of 2024 retirements, highlighting key differences in credit quality, cost, and delivery, and offers guidance to help buyers make informed decisions.

Key takeaways:

*   Registry protocols shape credit quality, cost, and delivery risk.
    
*   Fee timing and structure influence buyer exposure and outcomes.
    
*   Credible purchases require science-led diligence and assurance.
    

Published: Jul 2025
