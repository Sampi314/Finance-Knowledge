AI for climate: Scaling solutions, reducing impact
==================================================

AI plays a transformative role in climate mitigation by optimizing energy systems, reducing emissions in key sectors, and driving innovation. At the same time, its own environmental footprint must be managed.

In this webinar, co-authors of the ICEF Artificial Intelligence for Climate Change Mitigation Roadmap (Second Edition) explore the intersection of AI and climate action, covering:

*   How AI scales sustainable solutions in energy, transport, manufacturing, and buildings.
    
*   The carbon footprint of AI and the need for responsible practices.
    
*   Actionable strategies for policy, collaboration, and regulations to maximize AI’s impact.
