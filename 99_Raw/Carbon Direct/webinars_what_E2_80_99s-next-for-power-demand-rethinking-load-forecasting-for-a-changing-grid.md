What’s next for power demand? Rethinking load forecasting for a changing grid
=============================================================================

As the demand for power continues to accelerate rapidly, accurate load forecasting is essential for grid reliability and smart capital investment. But current forecasts are highly uncertain, at both the grid-scale and nodal levels.

Some uncertainty comes from the commercial sensitivity around large load siting (i.e., data centers), which requires a market-wide perspective to address. Other gaps can be narrowed with better weather forecasting, more granular grid data, and AI/machine learning.

Attendees will leave this webinar with a clearer understanding of:

*   Why forecast accuracy matters for grid investment
    
*   The economic and operational cost of incorrect forecasting for individual companies and the industry at large
    
*   Which commercial and technical barriers are driving today’s uncertainty and how to combat them
    
*   Which emerging techniques are showing the most practical promise
