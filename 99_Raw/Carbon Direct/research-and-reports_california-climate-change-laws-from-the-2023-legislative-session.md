California climate change laws from the 2023 legislative session
================================================================

In 2023, California introduced several new laws that help address climate change. From promoting the expansion of clean energy to mandating climate-related disclosures for large businesses, new California laws are part of more widespread climate policy trends that support the transition to a sustainable economy. 

In this report, Carbon Direct policy experts offer a summary and commentary of the most notable climate laws from last year. Download to learn the implications and benefits of California’s most recent climate laws.
