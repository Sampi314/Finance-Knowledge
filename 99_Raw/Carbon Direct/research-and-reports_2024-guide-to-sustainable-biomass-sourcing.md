2024 edition

2024 Guide to Sustainable Biomass Sourcing for Carbon Removal
=============================================================

As biomass-based carbon dioxide removal capacity continues to scale at a rapid pace, it’s important to balance timely climate action with thoughtful caution. The 2024 edition provides practical guidance for biomass sourcing in carbon dioxide removal contracts to reduce the risk of adverse outcomes in the emerging offtake agreements needed to scale the biomass-based carbon dioxide removal industry.  
  
**Looking for the latest insights?** [**Read the updated 2025 guide**](https://www.carbon-direct.com/research-and-reports/2025-guide-to-sustainable-biomass-sourcing)
 **for the most up-to-date recommendations.**
