Reducing Scope 2 emissions in changing power markets
====================================================

To achieve near-term targets, companies must contend with Scope 2 rules that could shift to hourly and regional matching, federal incentive phase downs, and tightened REC supply. Join Carbon Direct and Ever.green for a strategy-forward session on navigating these shifts to keep 2030 goals credible and achievable.

**Key takeaways:**

*   How to stay resilient as tax credits shrink and projects grow more scarce post-2026
    
*   When to apply today’s vs. proposed advanced Scope 2 calculation methods
    
*   How to secure credible high-impact RECs and forward contracts to reduce power emissions
