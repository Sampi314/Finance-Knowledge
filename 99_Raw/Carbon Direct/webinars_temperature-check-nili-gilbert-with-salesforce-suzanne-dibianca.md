EPISODE 4

Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: Salesforce's Suzanne DiBianca
==================================================================

**Candid conversations about the state of climate action**

Policy, finance, corporate commitment, and communities are the four critical forces that must come together to drive rapid, large-scale decarbonization.

In this webinar series, [Nili Gilbert](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/nili-gilbert)
, Vice Chairwoman of Carbon Direct and an industry thought leader in climate finance is asking leaders from each of these sectors how their decisions accelerate climate action, and what they really believe it will take to reach climate goals.
