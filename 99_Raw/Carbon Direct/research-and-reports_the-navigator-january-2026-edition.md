The Navigator Special Policy Edition
====================================

**Navigate a fragmented climate policy landscape**

Essential analysis for companies, investors, and institutions balancing strategies across diverging state, federal, and international policies.

**What’s inside:**

*   Details on state-led energy infrastructure policy - How states are driving data center policy and advancing carbon storage and pipeline permitting 
    
*   Decoding federal policy shifts - What you need to know about OBBB restructured tax credits with FEOC requirements, DOE terminated financial assistance awards, and proposed EPA regulatory rollbacks
    

Understanding global markets advancements - From the operationalization of Article 6 to country-level program launches to the EU opening the door to international carbon credits, what you need to know

Published: Jan 2026
