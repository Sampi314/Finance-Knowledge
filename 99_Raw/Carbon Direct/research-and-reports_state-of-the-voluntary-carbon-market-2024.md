2024 EDITION

2024 State of the Voluntary Carbon Market
=========================================

The Carbon Direct State of the Voluntary Carbon Market report examines the key trends and developments shaping the voluntary carbon market, with a focus on carbon dioxide removal.

Key insights:

*   Changes in policies, buyer preferences, and supply constraints are transforming the voluntary carbon market.
    
*   Understanding the significance of quality in addressing residual and historical emissions.
    
*   Mature market structures are needed to effectively scale carbon removal solutions.
    

Published: Nov 2024
