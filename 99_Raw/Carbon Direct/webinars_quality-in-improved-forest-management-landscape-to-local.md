Quality in Improved Forest Management: Landscape to Local Impact
================================================================

An in-depth look at how Improved Forest Management (IFM) is transforming land stewardship—from America’s largest private landholdings to community forests in Mexico. This session brings together voices from across the carbon project value chain to explore how high-integrity IFM projects support sustainable forest management, scale climate impact, and bring tangible benefits to biodiversity, local economies, and frontline communities.
