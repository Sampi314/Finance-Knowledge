Webinar: A Guide to Climate Innovation Tax Credits in the Inflation Reduction Act
=================================================================================

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/pHNiD2GTWi2UdRa63s9s9B5mc.png?width=655&height=147)

Watch the webinar to learn more about how the IRA is boosting climate investment. The Carbon Direct Policy Team and Chief Scientist [Julio Friedmann](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-julio-friedmann?utm_source=livestorm&utm_medium=webinar&utm_campaign=q4-ira-webinar)
 will offer insights and updates on the most important climate tax credits and their impact, including:

*   A breakdown of available tax credits, including stackability, timelines, and transferability
    
*   A look at recent tax credit policy updates, including eligibility and payment structures
    
*   Insight into both the business and climate impacts and implications of the IRA
