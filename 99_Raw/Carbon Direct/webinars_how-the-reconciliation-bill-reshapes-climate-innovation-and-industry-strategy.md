How the Reconciliation Bill Reshapes Climate, Innovation, and Industry Strategy
===============================================================================

Join us for an in-depth look at the reconciliation bill moving toward final passage—and with it, a major transformation in climate, energy, and innovation policy.

Carbon Direct’s policy experts break down what the legislation includes, what it leaves out, and how it will affect your business and the broader climate ecosystem. Panelists will also cover strategic actions to help your company prepare for what comes next.

**We’ll cover:**

*   How this impacts existing policy structures like the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) and clean energy tax credits.
    
*   Key implications for data centers, artificial intelligence (AI) deployment, electrification, and emissions reduction pathways.
    
*   How cities, states, and global partners may respond—and what steps your organization can take now to align with the coming wave of policy shifts and market signals.
