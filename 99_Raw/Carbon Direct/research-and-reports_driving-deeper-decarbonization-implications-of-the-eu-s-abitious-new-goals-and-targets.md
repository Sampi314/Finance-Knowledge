Driving deeper decarbonization: Implications of the EU's ambitious new goals and targets
========================================================================================

In February 2024, the European Commission announced two keystone policies that accelerate carbon reduction timelines; the EU 2040 Climate Target Communication and the Industrial Carbon Management (ICM) strategy. In this Carbon Direct deep dive, you will learn the implications of the EU’s ambitious new goals and gain insights into the solutions required to meet them. 

  

*   Learn why effective carbon management is critical in achieving the EU’s accelerated timelines 
    
*   Understand which sectors will be most affected by these ambitious new goals
    
*   Gain insights into reduction solutions including carbon dioxide removal (CDR), clean fuels, and carbon capture, utilization, and storage (CCUS) 
    
*   Discover the need for equity and climate justice considerations moving forward
