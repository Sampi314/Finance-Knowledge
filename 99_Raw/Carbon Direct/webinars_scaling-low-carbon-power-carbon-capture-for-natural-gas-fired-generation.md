Scaling Low-Carbon Power: Carbon Capture for Natural Gas-Fired Generation
=========================================================================

Join Carbon Direct and Calpine for a webinar on [carbon capture and storage (CCS)](https://www.carbon-direct.com/research-and-reports/meeting-data-center-electricity-demand)
 solutions for natural gas-fired power. As electricity demand surges, CCS offers a path to firm, low-emissions electricity for the digital economy.

**What you’ll learn:**

*   How CCS enables scalable, reliable, low-carbon power
    
*   Key steps for implementation: capture, transport, storage
    
*   How CCS can reduce CO₂ emissions by 90–95% and compete in the race for firm, clean electricity
