Webinar: Navigating Carbon Credit Communication and Climate Disclosure Laws
===========================================================================

Laws such as California's AB 1305: Voluntary Carbon Market Disclosures and the EU’s recently approved Green Claims and Empowering Consumers Directives, along with growing consumer and investor activism, make it more critical than ever to prioritize science-based strategies and transparent sustainability reporting. 

  
In this webinar, Carbon Direct Director of Policy, [Dr. Zara Ahmed](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-zara-ahmed)
, and Senior Science and Strategy Manager, [Sanna O'Connor-Morberg](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/sanna-oconnor-morberg)
 will discuss the implications of climate disclosure laws, and explain how you can leverage carbon removal to demonstrate your climate leadership and avoid greenwashing claims.
