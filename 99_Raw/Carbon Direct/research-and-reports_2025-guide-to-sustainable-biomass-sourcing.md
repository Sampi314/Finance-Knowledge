2025 EDITION

Sustainable Forest Biomass Sourcing for CDR: A Buyer's Guide
============================================================

**Mitigating the risks of biomass-based carbon dioxide removal contracting**

Building on insights from the [2024 edition](https://www.carbon-direct.com/research-and-reports/a-buyer-s-guide-sustainable-biomass-sourcing-for-carbon-dioxide-removal)
, this latest report offers actionable, evidence-based guidance for biomass-based carbon dioxide removal offtake agreements, promoting sustainable sourcing practices that reduce risks and support global climate goals.

Key takeaways

*   Insights from nearly 20 biomass certification schemes to guide sourcing. 
    
*   Ready-to-use draft contract language and flexible implementation options.
    
*   Practical tools and guardrails to support projects launching by 2026.
    

Published: Jan 2025
