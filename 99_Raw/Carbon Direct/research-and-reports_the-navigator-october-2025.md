The Navigator, October 2025 Edition
===================================

**Insights to navigate rapidly evolving carbon markets**

This quarter: US policy changes disrupt clean energy, Germany launches national carbon dioxide removal (CDR) procurement, and Europe advances its emissions trading system (ETS) and scope 2 rules, major shifts that together are reshaping global markets.

**Key takeaways:**

*   FEOC rules may slow US project timelines and raise costs
    
*   Germany’s procurement could set a global precedent for CDR
    
*   Europe’s ETS expansion signals convergence with voluntary markets
    
*   Biodiversity is core to nature-based CDR but needs better measurement
    

Published: Oct 2025
