Sustainable Aviation Fuel Primer
================================

Sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) has emerged as the primary means of emissions abatement for the aviation industry. Get the latest report from Carbon Direct, in partnership with Apple, to learn about the full range of fuel types, carbon intensities, sustainability criteria, and costs associated with this landscape of options.

Download the report for a primer on how promising sustainable aviation fuels production pathways and opportunities scale to lead to reduced greenhouse gas emissions.
