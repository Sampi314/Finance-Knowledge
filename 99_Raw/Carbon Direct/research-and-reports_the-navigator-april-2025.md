The Navigator, April 2025 Edition
=================================

**Quarterly analysis of climate policy, carbon science, and market trends**

This edition examines how shifting federal priorities are giving rise to state-level policies, voluntary markets, and international momentum. It also highlights new questions emerging in carbon accounting and explores evolving strategies for scaling clean energy and carbon removal.

Key takeaways:

*   California and New York are advancing new bills to support carbon removal, clean construction, and climate-smart agriculture.
    
*   Japan and Switzerland are setting new benchmarks for regional carbon markets and national climate targets.
    

Published: Apr 2025
