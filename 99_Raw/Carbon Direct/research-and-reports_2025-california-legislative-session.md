2025 California Legislative Session
===================================

**Summary and commentary on new laws addressing climate change**

California's governor signed 794 bills and vetoed 123 during the 2025 legislative session. This report examines the climate laws that passed, those that didn't, and what both outcomes mean for those navigating California's evolving policy landscape.

**Key takeaways:**

*   Analysis of signed climate bills and vetoed proposals.
    
*   Implications for various sectors and stakeholders.
    
*   Strategic insights for policy implementation and planning.
    

Published: Nov 2025
