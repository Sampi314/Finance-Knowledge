Challenges and Opportunities in Soil Carbon Credits
===================================================

**Addressing obstacles in soil carbon measurement and durability**

Soil carbon credits have the potential to drive gigatonne-scale carbon removal, but barriers in measurement, verification, and long-term durability have slowed market growth. This report explores the key challenges facing soil carbon projects and the solutions that can strengthen buyer confidence, improve credit quality, and accelerate investment in regenerative agriculture.
