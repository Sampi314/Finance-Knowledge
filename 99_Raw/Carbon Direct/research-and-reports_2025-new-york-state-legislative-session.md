2025 EDITION

2025 New York State Legislative Session
=======================================

Governor Kathy Hochul signed 713 bills and vetoed 140 during the 2025 legislative session. This report examines the climate and energy laws that were passed, those that were not, and what both outcomes mean for navigating New York's evolving policy landscape.

**What’s inside:**

*   Analysis of major climate, energy, and environmental legislation.
    
*   Strategic insights on transparency requirements, adaptation funding, and infrastructure transitions.
    
*   Implications for utilities, developers, and businesses operating in New York.
