Staying Ahead of Updates to Global Reporting Frameworks
=======================================================

As climate disclosure standards evolve, companies must act now to stay compliant and credible. Join Carbon Direct experts for insights on upcoming changes to key frameworks and how to adapt your strategy.

In this webinar, you’ll learn:

*   What to expect from updates to the SBTi Corporate Net-Zero Standard and GHG Protocol Scope 2 and 3 Guidance.
    
*   How these changes could impact your emissions accounting, target setting, and climate planning.
    
*   Practical steps to future-proof your sustainability strategy.
    
*   How Carbon Direct’s science and policy expertise supports businesses navigating these evolving standards.
