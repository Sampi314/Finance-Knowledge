Advanced Power Emission Accounting Methodologies for Large Electricity Loads
============================================================================

**Using power data to optimize emissions, operations, and compliance**

Traditional methods for measuring greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions, including those in the Greenhouse Gas Protocol, lack the precision large power consumers need for energy procurement decisions for today’s rapidly changing power grid. This report examines advanced power emission methodologies that enable large power consumers to optimize power strategies for cost, emissions reduction, and regulatory compliance.
