Whitepaper

Journey to the Center of Hydrogen
=================================

**Simplifying complexity with Carbon Direct's LCCA tool**

Carbon Direct’s Levelized Cost of Carbon Abatement (LCCA) tool helps organizations maximize emissions reductions per dollar spent by identifying cost-effective decarbonization options. This report explores its applications in hydrogen, offering insights to streamline decision-making and optimize decarbonization strategies.

Published: Feb 2025
