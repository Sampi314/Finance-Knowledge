white paper

Carbon Dioxide Removal: Global Goals and Regional Realities
===========================================================

This white paper examines how integrated assessment models (IAMs) represent carbon removal and outlines implications for policy, investment, and the design of credible net-zero strategies in global and regional contexts.

**Key takeaways:**

*   IAMs inform climate policy but have historically underrepresented the diversity of carbon removal approaches
    
*   This narrower representation of carbon removal risks distorting pathways and weakening policy commitments
    
*   Expanding carbon removal representation and grounding models in real-world data is critical to designing impactful net-zero plans
    

Published: Sep 2025
