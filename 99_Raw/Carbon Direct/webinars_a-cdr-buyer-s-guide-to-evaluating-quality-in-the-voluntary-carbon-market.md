A CDR Buyer’s Guide to Evaluating Quality in the Voluntary Carbon Market
========================================================================

In 2024, quality-oriented, removals-focused purchases of carbon credits have more than doubled. Yet, the carbon removal market is still in its infancy relative to the billions of tonnes needed to minimize climate impacts by 2050. Companies are playing a foundational role in supporting this emerging industry.  

Take climate action by hearing carbon removal diligence and procurement insights, informed by new climate research, by attending the Microsoft and Carbon Direct webinar. Experts will share guidance and lessons learned, relevant to both longtime and first-time carbon credit purchasers.  

The discussion will include:

*   The latest, science-based criteria for high-quality carbon removal
    
*   Practical considerations for buyers to navigate different types of carbon credits offered in the voluntary carbon market
    
*   The role of longer-term offtake contracts for companies to scale high-quality carbon removal projects
