Criteria for High-Quality Environmental Attribute Certificates
==============================================================

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/NCyCDnOUyu7VuDwajghJU0zMN8.png?width=1144&height=138)

**Defining integrity for EACs in the concrete and steel sectors**

Microsoft and Carbon Direct released a first-of-its-kind guide to criteria for high-quality Environmental Attribute Certificates (EACs) in low-carbon concrete and steel. This resource describes key quality criteria for the concrete and steel sectors, and supports procurement, market development, and climate impact, helping buyers and suppliers decarbonize complex supply chains.

Published: May 2025
