Driving Transportation Decarbonization: Shopify and Carbon Direct White Paper
=============================================================================

The transportation sector is currently responsible for about a quarter of carbon dioxide emissions from fossil fuels. The industry must reduce demand, improve efficiency, and transition to low- or zero-emission transportation technologies. In this White Paper, Driving Transportation Decarbonization, Shopify and Carbon Direct explore challenges and opportunities in decarbonizing various modes of transportation and emphasize the importance of considering the entire life cycle of transportation fuels.
