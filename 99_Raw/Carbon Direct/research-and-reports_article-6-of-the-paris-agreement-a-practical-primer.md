White paper

Article 6 of the Paris Agreement: A Practical Primer
====================================================

As Article 6 shifts from negotiation to implementation, this primer equips developers, investors, policymakers, and buyers with practical insights for navigating international carbon credit trading.

**What's inside:**

*   Article 6 fundamentals: What it is, why it matters now, and how it enables countries to cooperate on climate targets through standardized carbon credit trading
    
*   Two pathways for market participants: Bilateral national-level trading (Article 6.2) vs. UN-administered crediting (Article 6.4)—and how each mechanism works in practice
    
*   Strategic implications for carbon removal: How evolving standards and regulatory expectations shape procurement decisions and long-term climate strategies
