Webinar: Carbon Removal in a Beyond Value Chain Mitigation Strategy
===================================================================

The Science Based Targets Initiative (SBTi) recently released its long-awaited [guidance on beyond value chain mitigation](https://sciencebasedtargets.org/resources/files/Above-and-Beyond-Report-on-BVCM.pdf?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8ZKhawNVZdVH2ilqr0FGRNbg9nSvaTCwGyB4Ogzrl9T_huWYpG9G4o9XkJ841Q3NC_ZJ9D)
, calling on the private sector to take immediate climate action above and beyond reduction efforts—including carbon removal—to contribute to global net zero. But the guidance leaves room for more than one approach, which makes it hard to know how to get started.

In this webinar, Carbon Direct’s Director of EMEA Advisory & Senior Scientist [Dr. Meera Atreya](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-meera-atreya?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8ZKhawNVZdVH2ilqr0FGRNbg9nSvaTCwGyB4Ogzrl9T_huWYpG9G4o9XkJ841Q3NC_ZJ9D)
 is joined by Senior Research Associate [Mila Stieglitz-Courtney](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/mila-stieglitz-courtney?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8ZKhawNVZdVH2ilqr0FGRNbg9nSvaTCwGyB4Ogzrl9T_huWYpG9G4o9XkJ841Q3NC_ZJ9D)
 to discuss beyond value chain mitigation strategies that will work for your organization.

Watch to learn:

*   How to invest in carbon removal in line with the latest SBTi guidance
    
*   Tips for determining the right approach, including the money-for-tonne versus a tonne-for-tonne strategy
    
*   Recommendations for communicating your strategy and impacts to customers and stakeholders
