Optimizing for Biodiversity with Nature-Based Projects in the Voluntary Carbon Market
=====================================================================================

Join Carbon Direct and JPMorganChase for a webinar exploring how to design and select voluntary carbon market (VCM) projects that deliver on both climate and biodiversity goals. Drawing from a new whitepaper, Optimizing for Biodiversity within Nature-based projects in the Voluntary Carbon Market: Principles for Pursuing Dual Outcomes, our experts will share a science- and data-driven framework for building high-integrity projects that align with organizational priorities.

We’ll dive into lessons from an analysis of 1,639 global projects, providing practical guidance for evaluating claims, setting credible reporting commitments, and scaling investments that deliver nature-positive outcomes.

**What you’ll learn:**

*   Principles for selecting and designing projects that deliver dual carbon and biodiversity outcomes
    
*   How to align carbon removal investments with organizational climate and nature goals
    
*   Approaches to setting credible, transparent reporting commitments
    
*   Insights into the readiness of the VCM to deliver both climate and biodiversity outcomes today
