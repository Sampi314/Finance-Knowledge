EPISODE 2

Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: United Airlines' Lauren Riley
==================================================================

In the second installment of the "Temperature Check" webinar series, Nili Gilbert, will engage in a dynamic conversation with [Lauren Riley](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lauren-riley2/)
, Chief Sustainability Officer at United Airlines. Lauren leads United’s environmental sustainability strategy, including its progress towards net zero, scaling sustainable aviation fuel (SAF), and implementing decarbonization technologies across United’s global operations.

This session will explore the future of sustainable aviation and provide key insights into how the aviation industry is tackling the challenges of decarbonization while connecting people and cultures across the globe.

**Together they’ll discuss:**

*   United Airlines’ sustainability strategy and long-term climate goals.
    
*   The role of policies and partnerships in accelerating SAF adoption.
    
*   How aviation can balance reducing its carbon footprint with connecting people worldwide.
