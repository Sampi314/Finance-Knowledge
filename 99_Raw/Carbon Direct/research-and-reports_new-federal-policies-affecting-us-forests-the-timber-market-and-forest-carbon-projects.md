New Federal Policies Affecting US Forest Carbon Projects
========================================================

Two new federal executive orders aim to expand US timber production and restrict imports. This report explores how these changes may affect forest carbon credit projects, altering baselines, leakage risks, and market dynamics. Learn what these shifts mean for improved forest management (IFM) and afforestation, reforestation, and revegetation (ARR) projects, and how buyers can navigate a changing policy landscape.
