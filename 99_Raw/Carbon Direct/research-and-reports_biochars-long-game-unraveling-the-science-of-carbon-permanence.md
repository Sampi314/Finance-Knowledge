2025 WHITEPAPER

Biochar's long game: Unraveling the science of carbon permanence
================================================================

Accurately assessing biochar’s durability in soil is essential for credible carbon removal. This white paper challenges recent comparisons to inertinite, a coal component, highlighting key scientific flaws and offering guidance on conservative, evidence-based crediting.

Key takeaways:

*   Inertinite comparisons overlook critical differences between coal and biochar in soil.
    
*   Current protocols vary widely, creating a risk of overcrediting carbon removal.
    
*   Conservative, evidence-based methods are essential for credible biochar carbon removal.
