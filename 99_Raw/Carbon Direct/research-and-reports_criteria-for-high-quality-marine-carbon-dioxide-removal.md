ADDENDUM TO THE 2024 EDITION

Criteria for High-Quality Marine Carbon Dioxide Removal
=======================================================

Carbon Direct has released new science-based standards, developed in collaboration with Microsoft, to ensure marine carbon dioxide removal (mCDR) projects are transparent, durable, and environmentally responsible. These criteria build on Carbon Direct’s annual updates for [high-quality carbon dioxide removal](https://www.carbon-direct.com/research-and-reports/criteria-for-high-quality-carbon-dioxide-removal)
, providing additional guidance tailored specifically for ocean-based solutions.

**Key takeaways:**

*   Provides clear guidelines for ensuring environmental integrity, long-term durability, and rigorous monitoring in mCDR projects.
    
*   Helps buyers and developers use these standards to evaluate and scale high-quality mCDR solutions.
    
*   Supports safe, effective, and transparent deployment as investment in mCDR grows.
    

Published: Mar 2025
