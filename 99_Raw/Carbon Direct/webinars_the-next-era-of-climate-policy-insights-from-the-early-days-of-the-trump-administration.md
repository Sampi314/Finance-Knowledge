The next era of climate policy: Insights from the early days of the Trump Administration
========================================================================================

The next era of US climate policy is taking shape as the Trump Administration begins its second term, raising critical questions about the future of climate action at the federal, state, and global levels. With shifts in policy direction, what lies ahead for the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA), the voluntary carbon market, and international climate commitments?

Join climate policy experts Zara Ahmed, DrPH, and Julio Friedmann, PhD, as they explore the evolving landscape of US climate policy and its implications for businesses, policymakers, and global cooperation.
