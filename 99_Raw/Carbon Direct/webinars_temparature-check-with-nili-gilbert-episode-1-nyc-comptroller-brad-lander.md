EPISODE 1

Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: NYC Comptroller Brad Lander
================================================================

In a new webinar series from Carbon Direct, Vice Chairwoman [Nili Gilbert](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/nili-gilbert?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz--SQ6duS5Sn2ZIj9l9hIwjqdXGwHgY0OYNqJ1m82kOXg871Yd3mCTgzsdIDjArJZX5WPPGF)
, a leading voice in climate and finance, speaks with climate leaders from business, government, and local communities about what they think it will take to reach climate goals.

In her first installment, Nili talks to New York City Comptroller [Brad Lander](https://comptroller.nyc.gov/about/about-brad-lander/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz--SQ6duS5Sn2ZIj9l9hIwjqdXGwHgY0OYNqJ1m82kOXg871Yd3mCTgzsdIDjArJZX5WPPGF)
 about how his decisions as a financial leader affect carbon emissions and social outcomes, and why climate aware investing is a better long-term financial strategy.

Together they discuss:

*   The relationship between financial strategy and climate decisions
    
*   The connection between climate action and social goals
    
*   What it will take to broaden the base of support for accelerating climate action locally and nationally
