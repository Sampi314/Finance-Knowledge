What’s next for US climate policy? Insights from inside the government
======================================================================

Join us for an exclusive insider’s discussion on the evolving dynamics of US climate and energy policy in the face of political change. Hear from two former federal officials and climate policy experts as they share firsthand experience inside the government and offer insight into what to expect in President Trump’s second term. 

Key takeaways: 

*   What the Biden administration achieved on climate, and what’s at stake moving forward. 
    
*   Key regulatory rollbacks, agency shifts, and their domestic and international implications. 
    
*   What businesses and advocates should watch for in a changing policy landscape.
