Guidebook

Integrating Meta’s Canopy Height Model into Forest Carbon Methodologies
=======================================================================

Discover how Meta’s open-source canopy height model and AI tools can support more accurate, cost-effective forest carbon project development. This guidebook gives developers and registries practical tips on applying high-resolution remote sensing (RS) data across key project activities.

**Key takeaways**

*   How high-resolution RS improves planning and monitoring 
    
*   Why data validation supports accurate and credible crediting
    
*   What technical needs must be addressed to scale RS in carbon markets
    

Published: Aug 2025
