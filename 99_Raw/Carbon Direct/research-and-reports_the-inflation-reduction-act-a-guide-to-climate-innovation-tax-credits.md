The Inflation Reduction Act: A guide to climate innovation tax credits
======================================================================

The Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) incentivizes companies to engage in the budding, low-carbon economy, including a range of tax credits for clean technology and renewable energy. To help guide a climate and sustainable technology innovation strategy, the Carbon Direct policy team analyzed the top eight tax credits with the highest potential for large-scale climate and business impact.
