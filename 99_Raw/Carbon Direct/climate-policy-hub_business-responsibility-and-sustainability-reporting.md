Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting
----------------------------------------------------

industry

Finance

Location

India

Status

In effect

Overview

Starting in fiscal year 2023, the ​​Securities and Exchange Board of India mandates the top 1,000 Indian companies by market capitalization to provide quantifiable environment, social, and governance (ESG) metrics on an annual basis. The rules for this mandate are defined by the country’s [Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting](https://www.mca.gov.in/Ministry/pdf/BRR_11082020.pdf)
 (BRSR).

Requirements

Report scope 1 and scope 2 emissions

Annual reporting is required, beginning in fiscal year 2023.
