# Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: H.E. Majid Al Suwaidi | Carbon Direct

EPISODE 3

Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: H.E. Majid Al Suwaidi
==========================================================

In the third installment of the Temperature Check webinar series, Nili Gilbert, Vice Chairwoman of Carbon Direct, engages in a compelling discussion with H.E. Majid Al Suwaidi, CEO of ALTÉRRA. As the leader of the largest private climate investment vehicle, H.E. Majid brings a unique perspective on advancing innovative climate finance solutions to unlock the potential to deliver a net-zero, climate-resilient economy for all.

**Key takeaways include:**

*   Climate transition as one of the biggest wealth investment opportunities of this century
    
*   ALTÉRRA’s 2024 milestones and the journey from vision to execution
    
*   Using catalytic capital to unlock climate investments in emerging economies
    
*   The UAE’s role as a central hub for climate finance and innovation
