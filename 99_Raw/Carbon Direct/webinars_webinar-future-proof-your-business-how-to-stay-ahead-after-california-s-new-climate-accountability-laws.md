Webinar: Future-Proof Your Business: How to Stay Ahead After California’s New Climate Accountability Laws
=========================================================================================================

The landmark California Climate Accountability Package will require both public and private companies to disclose their carbon emissions, as well as climate-related financial risks.

Join this webinar to learn:

*   How your company may be affected by the new laws 
    
*   How the California legislation differs in scope and approach from other proposed SEC and FAR regulations 
    
*   How you can prepare to meet current and future climate disclosure requirements
    

Policy experts [Dr. Zara Ahmed](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/dr-zara-ahmed?utm_source=livestorm&utm_medium=webinar&utm_campaign=q3-ca-emissions-law-webinar)
 and [Dr. Colin McCormick](https://www.carbon-direct.com/team/colin-mccormick?utm_source=livestorm&utm_medium=webinar&utm_campaign=q3-ca-emissions-law-webinar)
 will also talk about the downstream implications for smaller companies not directly subject to disclosure requirements, and how current and future legislation is likely to impact ESG and other reporting requirements.
