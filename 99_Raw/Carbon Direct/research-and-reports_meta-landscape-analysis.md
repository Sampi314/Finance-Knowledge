Remote Sensing for Forest Carbon: Challenges and Opportunities
==============================================================

Remote sensing (RS) can improve forest carbon measurement and monitoring, but key barriers remain. Based on insights from 40+ stakeholders, this report outlines challenges to RS adoption and presents seven pathways to strengthen transparency, access, and reliability in MMRV.

**Key takeaways**

*   Understand governance, data, and capacity gaps limiting RS adoption
    
*   Explore stakeholder-backed priorities to improve RS-based MMRV
    
*   Learn how a shared consortium could drive standards, trust, and progress
    

Published: Aug 2025
