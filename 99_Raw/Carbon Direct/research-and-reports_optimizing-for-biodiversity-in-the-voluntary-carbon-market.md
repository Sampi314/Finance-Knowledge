Optimizing for Biodiversity in the Voluntary Carbon Market
==========================================================

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/3J1CLdPfID89xMHRK0zqNMvTdA.png?width=631&height=46)

Carbon Direct and JPMorganChase outline six principles for carbon removal projects that deliver both carbon and biodiversity outcomes. The principles provide a framework to guide buyers and developers to achieve measurable results that support dual carbon and nature-positive outcomes. 

**Key takeaways:**

*   Reporting should be grounded in science-based objectives
    
*   Biodiversity outcomes are context-specific and non-fungible
    
*   Carbon and biodiversity goals may need separate timelines
    

Published: Oct 2025
