2025 Session of the Virginia General Assembly
=============================================

**Expert insight into Virginia’s evolving climate laws**

In this report, Carbon Direct policy experts analyze the most notable climate-related bills from the 2025 Session of the Virginia General Assembly. The legislation covers a range of issues including data center energy use, grid reliability, renewable energy development, and emissions reduction strategies.

What’s inside:

*   Key legislation that passed and bills that failed.
    
*   The role of data centers in shaping energy policy.
    
*   Impacts on clean energy, grid resilience, and regulatory frameworks.
    

Published: Apr 2025
