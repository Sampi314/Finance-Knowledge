Lessons from the biggest carbon removal purchases this year
===========================================================

2024 marked the biggest year yet for carbon dioxide removal (CDR), with more tonnes and offtake agreements inked than ever before. Join Microsoft and Carbon Direct as they unveil new criteria for Criteria for High-Quality CDR, designed to make science-backed CDR more accessible to buyers and developers everywhere. 

We’ll unpack:

*   How the biggest deals get done
    
*   What it takes to build a robust portfolio
    
*   How we can crowd in new demand to lay the foundation for this critical climate technology
