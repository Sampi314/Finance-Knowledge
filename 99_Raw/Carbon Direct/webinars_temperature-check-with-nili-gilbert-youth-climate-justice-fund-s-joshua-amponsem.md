EPISODE 5

Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert: Youth Climate Justice Fund's Joshua Amponsem
=================================================================================

_Temperature Check with Nili Gilbert_ returns with another thought-provoking conversation on the intersection of sustainability, technology, and business leadership.

In this episode, Nili Gilbert, Vice Chairwoman of Carbon Direct, sits down with Joshua Amponsem, Co-Director of the Youth Climate Justice Fund to explore how investing in youth-led climate leadership can unlock global, intergenerational impact.

**Together they discuss:**

*   The urgent need to invest in youth climate leadership—especially in the Global South
    
*   The value of intergenerational and intersectional collaboration built on mutual respect
    
*   The critical role of reforming global academic systems to fund locally-led research and education
