White paper

The IRA, Three Years Later: Navigating OBBB Tax Credit Changes
==============================================================

The One Big Beautiful Bill Act reshapes IRA clean energy tax incentives, speeding phaseouts, adding strict supply chain rules, and ending key programs. Carbon Direct’s policy team analyzed the most critical changes, providing developers and investors with a clear guide to timelines, eligibility, stackability, and accessibility.

**Key takeaways**

*   How to align project schedules with new tax credit deadlines
    
*   Why FEOC rules could disrupt clean energy supply chains and financing
    
*   Which strategies optimize credit stackability under the revised law
    

Published: Aug 2025
