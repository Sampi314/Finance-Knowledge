The Role of Credible Carbon Removal in Corporate Climate Action
===============================================================

As the climate landscape evolves, credibility is the currency of net zero. This white paper offers a roadmap for leading organizations to pair deep decarbonization with high-integrity carbon dioxide removal (CDR), supporting results that withstand regulatory and public scrutiny. 

**Key takeaways**

*   The next climate challenge: moving from _whether_ to use CDR to _how_ to do so credibly
    
*   Tips on navigating fragmented accounting rules 
    
*   How to pair deep decarbonization with CDR to maintain climate leadership
