Course Content
--------------

Learn how to develop a financial model for a copper project with a typical mine plan including ore mined, waste mined, input grades, high- and low-grade stockpiling, and processing recoveries. Understand the key drivers of mining, processing, and transport costs. Understand how financiers and investors assess mining deals, including mining specific debt metrics such as the Reserve Tail Ratio (RTR) and the Reserve Life Coverage Ratio (RLCR).

This industry course builds on your project finance modeling skills developed in our project finance modeling courses. It focuses on the operational aspects and adjustments common in debt structures for mining projects. Buy this course as a bundle with our project finance modeling course for the ultimate mining project finance modeling experience.
