In the thick of the December 2023 holiday season, hydrogen industry stakeholders received long awaited guidance from the Biden administration on the proposed rules for 45V. The proposed requirements have generated controversy and debate in the hydrogen and energy sector. So what does the proposed guidance actually entail?

What if there was a surefire way to earn excess returns – two-to-three times the risk-free rate or more! – with almost no chance of loss? “Impossible!” you say? Well, according to a [recent report by the American Council on Renewable Energy (ACORE)](https://acore.org/resources/the-risk-profile-of-renewable-energy-tax-equity-investments/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8hPxcniiAkQX6D8VA2yXDokCIQhmzR5Gjf9O7R6-cuxUkmtosOSViGPGwkuaQiZ0srDvzI)
**,** such a mythical investment class does exist. Its name? Tax equity.
