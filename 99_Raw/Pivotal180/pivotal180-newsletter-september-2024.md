**

The Inflation Reduction Act has brought a number of changes to the structure and value of tax credits for renewable energy projects in the U.S. Most notably, projects must now comply with prevailing wage and apprenticeship rules in order to receive the full value of their tax credits. PIvotal180 has updated the tax credit lessons in our online courses to reflect the IRA’s changes, and we were fortunate to get Norton Rose Fulbright Partner and energy tax master David Burton to give us a hand.

**[Project Finance for Mining and Critical Minerals](https://pivotal180.com/project-finance-for-mining-and-critical-minerals/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zY5j6XAttD2GPpgPpq1p4IjHKIDIOAZZZd1q02PaTmiX_pShPK3r2PCpnnkRMrqarnP8k)

Mining is inextricably linked to renewable energy and infrastructure assets. This applies to the volumes of critical minerals feeding the build-out of projects, but there is also a clear connection from a project finance modeling perspective. Pivotal180 is developing content to highlight financing structures and risks with mining projects. Watch this space!
