#### Financial Modeling Consulting Services

Pivotal180 helps clients build best practice models to support their businesses in making critical decisions and to assist in the evaluation of transaction pricing, strategic decisions, setting tariffs, valuations, and new market opportunities.

#### Model Review

Pivotal180’s team is led by one of the most experience model auditors in the world. Having led transactions, made investments, and holding on board seats, our team truly understands risk. We understand financial models and risk better than anyone else in the market.

#### Model Audits

**Our Audit Methodology**

**Experienced team.** Your project manager will be one of the most experienced model auditors in the market. We don’t outsource all of the work to new analysts around the world. We have worked across renewables, infrastructure and mining. Our team are not just coders. We understand the big picture. Having led transactions and made investments, our team truly understands risk. We understand financial models and risk better than anyone else in the market.

**Global Resources.** With offices in New York and Sydney. We work overnight across time zones to deliver model audits quicker than our competitors.

**Methodology.** We utilize a combination of cell-by-cell, high-level review, and replication of core elements of the model to give you the most assurance possible. and provides a flexible and robust process.  As experienced financial modelers we are also able to identify outputs that do not match with commercial expectations. 

**Consultative.** We communicate proactively, helping you to work through issues and offer suggestions throughout the process. We talk to our clients regularly to ensure any issues are resolved efficiently and effectively. We believe in a collaborative approach so we truly understand and meet our clients' expectations.

**The Model Audit process**  Process is spearheaded by Haydn Palliser and takes roughly (2-4) weeks with four iterations. We utilize a combination of cell-by-cell model audit, high-level review, and replication of key elements of the model. We use experienced team members, rather than outsourcing the work to inexperienced analysts. The rates are highly competitive and calculated by the size of the model and the number of inputs.
