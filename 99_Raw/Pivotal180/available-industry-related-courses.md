### Battery Storage Financial Modeling

Comprehensive course for analysts, associates, and vice presidents seeking to understand battery storage economics, revenue streams, and investment analysis. Covers standalone and co-located battery projects with focus on energy market fundamentals and financial evaluation techniques. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Analyze battery storage revenue streams including arbitrage, ancillary services, and supply shifting
*   Model capital costs, operating expenses, degradation cycles, and augmentation strategies
*   Evaluate standalone versus AC-coupled and DC-coupled solar and storage configurations
*   Calculate battery project economics including capacity factors, depth of discharge, and efficiency losses
*   Structure Investment Tax Credit applications and financing considerations for merchant batteries
*   Assess energy market fundamentals, grid services, and curtailment risks for storage projects

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/battery-storage-financial-modeling/)

### Mining & Critical Minerals Project Finance Modeling

Specialized course for analysts, associates, and vice presidents seeking comprehensive mining project finance expertise. Covers operational modeling, mine-specific debt metrics, and financial evaluation techniques for copper and critical minerals projects with real-world case studies. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Build comprehensive mining financial models including ore reserves, waste ratios, and processing recoveries
*   Calculate mining-specific debt metrics including Reserve Tail Ratio (RTR) and Reserve Life Coverage Ratio (RLCR)
*   Model variable mining costs, processing expenses, transportation, and end-of-mine rehabilitation requirements
*   Structure hedging strategies, forward curves, and commodity price risk management for mining projects
*   Analyze unlevered returns, cash sweeps, and mandatory cash share provisions for mining debt structures
*   Evaluate construction funding, pre-operational cash flows, and multi-phase mining project development

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/mining-critical-minerals-project-finance-modeling/)
