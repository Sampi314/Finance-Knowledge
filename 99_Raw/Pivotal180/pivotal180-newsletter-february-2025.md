Thank you to all our Pivotal180 clients for helping to make 2024 our biggest year yet. In 2024 Pivotal180 trained over 2,000 participants. **Launch of Mining and Critical Minerals Course**

We are thrilled to announce that have launched, our newest online course Mining and Critical Minerals Project Finance Modeling  The course dives into mining operations as well as some common adjustments to debt structures in mining projects. Students who take this course will learn to understand the key drivers of mining, processing, and transport costs. Understand how financiers and investors assess mining deals, including mining specific debt metrics.
