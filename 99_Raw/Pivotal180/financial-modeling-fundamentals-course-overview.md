Financial Modeling Fundamentals Course Overview
===============================================

By Alison Leckie | April 5, 2025

Course Overview
---------------

This video is an overview of our Financial Modeling Fundamentals Course. This is being launched in April

This course bridges the gap between theoretical learning and practical application. Participants will build a best-practice financial model using a real-life case study. You will learn frameworks for evaluating your business and project, including future cash flows, income statement balance sheet and cash flow integration, and financial statement analysis. Key topics include understanding leverage, equity dilution from multiple capital raises, and CAP tables.

**Alison:** This Pivotal180 course was specifically crafted to teach you the fundamentals of financial modeling, an essential task that allows you or your organization to analyze data and make informed decisions.

**Haydn:** We’ll get you from having no financial modeling experience to understanding many of the essential concepts. The course is based on a practical case study, so you can apply your knowledge right away. After completing this course, you’ll be able to set up a financial model from a blank spreadsheet, forecast revenues and expenses for a startup battery recycling business, calculate unlevered and levered returns, run what if analysis known as scenarios and sensitivities. You can change assumptions and assess the impact on your business. Use a financial model to determine appropriate pricing for your products. Develop 3-way financial statements for your business and analyze what they mean. Produce outputs to communicate the model outputs to the decision makers.
