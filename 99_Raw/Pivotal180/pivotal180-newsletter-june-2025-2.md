Dylan Parkes, Director at Pivotal180, is our newest member responsible for our live stream and in-person course training, consulting, and audit services.

With a unique career that spans Shakespeare to spreadsheets.

A former actor, turned finance professional, Dylan has over a decade of experience across corporate and project finance. Dylan brings a dynamic, storytelling-driven approach to teaching. His mission is to make any subject, no matter how technical, clear, human, and even fun!

Most recently, as an Investment Officer with the U.S. government, Dylan managed global finance deals across sectors including renewable energy, natural gas, and surface infrastructure. He has collaborated with leading institutions such as Citibank, JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, Deutsche Bank, and Santander.

A true global citizen, Dylan has lived in Qatar, Mexico, Italy, and Ukraine. And his favorite Excel shortcut? **Ctrl + ~** — because _you can’t fix what you can’t see_.

This video provides an overview of our best-selling online course. Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling.

This course teaches participants how to develop a best-practice financial model and optimize it for both debt and equity investors for a renewable energy project finance deal. Using a wind project as a case study, participants learn the concepts required to develop a financial model related to wind and solar. This course will teach the participants concepts including how to size debt, how to structure the repayments, and how to model the output covenants in the deal, how to calculate generation curtailment and degradation, how to forecast wind, and the different probabilities.

Learn how project finance is the answer to the deceptively hard and almost impossible question, How do you convince lenders and equity investors to provide hundreds of millions of dollars to a company that has no credit history, has no employees, has never operated or generated any revenue and has no guarantees from anyone credit worthy, promising to repay the money and make good on the investment?

The U.S. renewable energy industry has been abuzz with speculation following the House of Representatives’ recent budget reconciliation bill. A key concern being the the potential reduction or even elimination of critical tax credits for solar and wind energy projects. But, just how much value would be lost if the credits were to be repealed, and how would project developers need to adjust to persevere?

That’s where a robust financial model comes into play. Graduates of Pivotal180’s Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling program know the power of a well-structured model. Learn more how our models help us find the answers!

### [Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zz1dOlRVTLwuBgoTV5S1efKgZdZdKqM3bTRLREZ-KPlgi1SPRPMaeuoDOmRQumuHIRPIC)

### [Introduction to Battery Storage](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/battery-storage-financial-modeling/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zz1dOlRVTLwuBgoTV5S1efKgZdZdKqM3bTRLREZ-KPlgi1SPRPMaeuoDOmRQumuHIRPIC)

### [Mining and Critical Minerals](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/mining-critical-minerals-project-finance-modeling/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zz1dOlRVTLwuBgoTV5S1efKgZdZdKqM3bTRLREZ-KPlgi1SPRPMaeuoDOmRQumuHIRPIC)

Learn how to develop a financial model for a copper project with a typical mine plan including ore mined, waste mined, input grades, high- and low-grade stockpiling, and processing recoveries. Understand the key drivers of mining, processing, and transport costs

### [Tax Equity and Hybrid Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/course-content/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zz1dOlRVTLwuBgoTV5S1efKgZdZdKqM3bTRLREZ-KPlgi1SPRPMaeuoDOmRQumuHIRPIC)

Gain an overview of the tax equity structures commonly used in U.S. renewable energy investments, with a focus on the development of a financial model. Dive deep into tax capital accounts, deficit restoration obligations, stop loss allocations, and HLBV accounting.

### [Financial Modeling Fundamentals](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/financial-modeling-fundamentals/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9zz1dOlRVTLwuBgoTV5S1efKgZdZdKqM3bTRLREZ-KPlgi1SPRPMaeuoDOmRQumuHIRPIC)

Building a model from a blank spreadsheet.this course bridges the gap between theoretical learning and practical application. Participants will build a best-practice financial model using a real-life case study. You will learn frameworks for evaluating your business and project, including future cash flows, income statement balance sheet and cash flow integration, and financial statement analysis.
