Associate/Senior Associate – Project Finance Financial Modeling and Training
============================================================================

**Associate/Senior Associate**
==============================

**Company**: Pivotal180

**Duration**: Permanent Full-time Position

**Remuneration: Competitive** + performance bonus

**Location:** Purchase, New York + remote working

Pivotal180 is the leading provider in tax equity and project finance financial modeling training and is preparing for rapid growth.

Pivotal180 provides world class advisory, financial modeling and training within climate change, sustainable finance, renewable energy, and infrastructure. The business started in October 2019 and has quickly redefined the standards of project finance modeling training.

Pivotal180 is seeking an associate/senior associate to teach our training courses and to assist in growing the financial modeling and advisory business. You must love sustainable infrastructure. You are deeply passionate about educating and mentoring others. You find financial modeling exciting. You want to be part of a small and engaging team with big growth plans.

**Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities:**

*   Tailoring and delivery of financial modelling training courses
*   Assist in business development and marketing to increase the online sales internationally
*   Assist in financial modeling advisory and other services
*   Help develop new training courses.

**Qualifications /Experience /Competencies**

*   At least three to four years’ experience in project finance, tax equity, infrastructure / renewables investments, corporate finance, or project development
*   Outstanding Excel skills and be able to operate and analyze project finance models
*   Knowledge of industry and company dynamics in the renewable energy, infrastructure, or project finance sectors
*   A passion for teaching
*   Ability to build and retain long-term client relationships with effective interpersonal skills
*   Spanish language skills a plus
*   You are reliable and inventive and have strong attention to detail
