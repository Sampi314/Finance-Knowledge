Holiday Readings to Close out 2024 
===================================

By Bastian Stroemsheim | December 13, 2024

**Pivotal180’s Holiday Readings to Close out 2024** 

With the holidays approaching fast, here’s a list of books covering the fundamentals, the award-conscious and hot topics in project finance.

**FT and Schroders Business Book of the Year 2024**

A mix of longlisted and shortlisted books covering mining, shareholder value and a former Citibank trader chronicling the trading floor drama. “The Trading Game” could be a good option for those seeking a new adventure after wrapping up “Industry” earlier this year.

*   The War Below (Ernest Scheyder)
*   The Corporation in the Twenty-First Century (John Kay)
*   The Trading Game (Gary Stevenson)

**McKinsey 2024 annual book recommendations**

*   How Infrastructure Works: Inside the Systems That Shape Our World (Deb Chachra)
*   Anatomy of a Breakthrough: How to Get Unstuck When It Matters Most (Adam Alter)
*   The Blue Machine: How the Ocean Works (Helen Czerski)

**ChatGPT**

It is clear ChatGPT has a preference for the textbook and academic approach to project finance. In its defense, we went light on the prompting.

*   Principles of Project Finance (E.R. Yescombe)
*   Project Finance in Theory and Practice (Stefano Gatti)
*   The Law and Business of International Project Finance (Scott L. Hoffman)

**For the holiday party conversations**

The second edition of “Renewables theory and practice” was released in October, with dedicated sections to financing energy storage and hydrogen projects. If this fails as a conversation starter, we recommend anything nuclear, AI or data center related.

*   Renewables theory and practice 2nd edition (Santosh Raikar & Seabron Adamson)
*   Power to Save the World: The Truth About Nuclear Energy (Gwyneth Cravens)
*   Data Centers of the Future (Jon Lin & Gary Aitkenhead)
*   The Psychology of Money: Timeless lessons on wealth, greed, and happiness (Morgan Housel)
