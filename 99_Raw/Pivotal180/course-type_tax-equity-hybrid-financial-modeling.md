Course Content
--------------

Financial modeling is a critical yet often underappreciated skill, essential for success in **corporate finance** and business. Whether you’re launching a new venture, managing operations, or evaluating **“what-if” scenarios** through **sensitivity analysis**, the ability to create a robust **financial model** is indispensable. Your boss, clients, or investors will demand professional and reliable forecasts that inspire confidence.

This course teaches participants how to Build a Tax Equity financial model using a **US renewable energy project as a case study**. This is an intensive course providing an overview of the tax equity structures commonly used in U.S. renewable energy investments, with a focus on the **development of a financial model**. In this course you will be exposed to many areas of tax equity such as: Tax equity and Back leverage concepts, IRA, Depreciation, tax credits and inside and outside basis.

In contrast to Renewable energy project finance modelling. The Tax Equity course goes deep into tax capital accounts, deficit restoration obligations, stop loss allocations, and HLBV accounting.

#### Course Syllabus

| **SECTION** | **OVERVIEW** |
| --- | --- |
| ****PRE-REQUISITES**** | *   This course presumes prior knowledge of advanced Excel functions, best practice financial modeling techniques and concepts such as debt sizing, debt sculpting, and optimizing a model to a required PPA price.<br>*   It is recommended to take the Pivotal180 Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling Course before enrolling in this course |
| ****PRE-COURSE**** | *   Excel functions, formulas and shortcuts<br>*   Best practice modeling concepts<br>*   Data validation and named ranges<br>*   Tax equity model familiarization |
| ****PROJECT BENEFITS FROM DEPRECIATION, TAX CREDITS, AND CASH**** | *   Model familiarization and SPV calculations<br>*   Book versus tax depreciation<br>*   Taxable income (Earnings Before Tax)<br>*   Efficient versus inefficient (self-sheltering) taxpayers and NOLs<br>*   US tax credits – PTC, ITC, 45Q<br>*   Project level benefits of depreciation and tax<br>*   Introduction to the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) |
| ****INTRODUCTION TO TAX EQUITY AND BACK LEVERAGE CONCEPTS**** | *   Review of US renewable energy incentives and project benefits<br>*   Introduction to tax equity<br>*   Tax equity rules, structures and terms<br>*   Partnership disproportionate allocations and flips<br>*   Tax equity risk and returns<br>*   Introduction to back leverage<br>*   Structure and terms of key tax equity contracts<br>*   Accounting considerations: GAAP and HLBV<br>*   Introduction to the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) |
| ****INITIAL TAX EQUITY MODEL**** | *   Partnership sharing of benefits<br>*   Modeling disproportionate partnership allocations<br>*   Sizing tax equity investments<br>*   Back leverage structure<br>*   Back leverage debt sizing and repayments<br>*   Construction funding and tax equity bridge loans<br>*   Sponsor equity benefits, investment and returns |
| ****INSIDE BASIS**** | *   Introduction to tax capital accounts<br>*   Inside basis / s704(b) capital accounts<br>*   Stop loss reallocations<br>*   Deficit restoration obligations (DRO)<br>*   Determining who gets what out of the partnership<br>*   Impact of reallocations on tax equity investment sizing |
| ****OUTSIDE BASIS**** | *   Outside (tax) basis capital accounts<br>*   704(d) suspended losses<br>*   Determining when losses can be utilized<br>*   Outside basis impact on taxable income and losses<br>*   s731(a) gains<br>*   s754 step-ups<br>*   s734(b) depreciation |
| ****CAPITAL ACCOUNTS, TAX EQUITY SIZING AND FMV**** | *   Review of tax capital account movements<br>*   DRO special income allocations<br>*   Impact of capital account reallocations and adjustments<br>*   Sizing tax equity investments after capital accounts<br>*   Circularity of tax equity sizing calculations<br>*   Fair Market Value (FMV) and step-ups |
| ****TAX EQUITY SIZING REVIEW**** | *   What matters most in tax equity transactions<br>*   Calendar dated flips (CDF)<br>*   Sponsor buyout options<br>*   Tax impact of buyouts |
| ****INFLATION REDUCTION ACT**** | *   Key changes under the IRA<br>*   Tax credit transferability<br>*   Direct credit transfers without tax equity<br>*   Hybrid tax equity structures (‘tax equity light’) |
