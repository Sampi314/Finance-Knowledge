### In-House Corporate Training

Tailored curriculum designed for your specific deal types and delivered at your office.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Custom curriculum** - Focused on your transaction types
*   **Team alignment** - Everyone learns the same methodology
*   **Confidential setting** - Discuss your actual deals privately
*   **Cost efficient** - Train multiple staff for less

**Ideal For:** Corporate teams, investment banks, consultancies

**What's Included:**

*   Pre-training needs assessment
*   Fully customized programs
*   Deal examples incorporated

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/customized-training/)

### In-Person Intensive Training

Master complex concepts in concentrated 2-4 day sessions with hands-on practice and immediate feedback.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Intensive focus** - No distractions, accelerated learning
*   **Hands-on practice** - Work through real deal structures
*   **Immediate feedback** - Real-time coaching from experts
*   **Professional networking** - Build lasting industry connections

**Ideal For:** Senior professionals, consultancies, finance teams

**What's Included:**

*   Face-to-face learning experience
*   Small class sizes
*   Catered meals included
*   Access to selected videos/material on our learning platform

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/public-sessions/)

### Live Streaming Classes

Join live sessions with experienced instructors and connect with finance professionals worldwide.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Live Q&A sessions** - Get immediate answers to your questions
*   **Peer networking** - Connect with global finance professionals
*   **Scheduled learning** - Structured progression with accountability
*   **Session recordings** - Review or catch up on missed classes

**Ideal For:** Professionals who value interaction and structured learning

**What's Included:**

*   Homework after each session
*   Access to selected videos/lessons on our learning platform
*   All session recordings
*   Live instructor training and feedback
*   Small class sizes

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/live-streaming/)

### Self-Paced Online Learning

Complete comprehensive training around your busy schedule with lifetime access to all materials.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Learn on your schedule** - Study anytime, anywhere
*   **12 months access** - to our online material
*   **Subscriptions** can be extended
*   **Mobile compatible** - Learn during commutes or travel
*   **Self-assessment tools** - Track your progress

**Ideal For:** Busy professionals, remote workers, international learners

**What's Included:**

*   Downloadable Excel models
*   Practice exercises with solutions
*   Certificate of completion
*   CPD/CPE Accredited

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/online-training/)
