**News**

Before moving to the exciting news for 2023, here is a quick recap of what Pivotal180 achieved in 2022. 43 courses,102 days of training, 650 online licenses, and over 1350 individual participants trained. With 1 new course launched, the portfolio of online courses is now 4. Thankyou to everyone for your support and trust in Pivotal180.

To make our content more accessible we have started translating the free resources into Spanish. Please click [this link](https://pivotal180.com/?p=1922)
 to see an example.

The Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) was signed into law by President Biden on August 16, 2022.The new tax credit structure of the IRA has led to a quest for clarification. Over a series of opinion pieces we aim to distill the key changes and highlight what to expect as the market adapts to this new environment. We have so far  written three articles and these are available in the [free resources page of our website](https://pivotal180.com/?page_id=161)
. In the coming weeks we will publish more articles about market expectations and tradeoffs between the different tax credit structures.

**Course Portfolio and Classes** 

The courses in our existing portfolio have been developed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills to tackle all the multi-faceted pieces of a transaction. and to understand the broader context..

We look forward to assist you in demystifying project finance and tax equity. Our upcoming courses are listed below.

[Project Finance and Infrastructure Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/?course_type=project-finance-modeling)

Learn the concepts to develop a financial model related to PPP/P3s for both social and economic infrastructure. A mining financial model will be added to this course soon. Watch this space!

[Tax Equity Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/?course_type=tax-equity-course-content)

Gain an overview of the tax equity structures commonly used in U.S. renewable energy investments, with a focus on the development of a financial model. Dive deep into tax capital accounts, deficit restoration obligations, stop loss allocations, and HLBV accounting.

[Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/?course_type=renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling)

Learn how to develop a best-practice financial model and optimize it for both debt and equity investors for a renewable energy project finance deal. Using a case study, participants learn the concepts required to develop a financial model related to wind and solar.

[Introduction to Battery Storage](https://pivotal180.com/?course_type=battery-storage-financial-modeling)

This course covers topics related to battery storage including energy markets fundamentals, types of battery storage, uses of storage capacity, battery terminology, economics behind batteries, risks associated with the storage investments.
