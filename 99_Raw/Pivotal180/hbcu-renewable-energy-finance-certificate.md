**Registration available until March 10, 2026**

e’re excited to announce that Pivotal180’s **Renewable Energy Finance Course** will be provided **on campus Saturday, March 14– Sunday, March 15**

*   Trusted, industry-leading training designed by experts
*   Build a rock-solid foundation in project finance concepts
*   Gain practical skills to effectively compete for internships and jobs in the renewable energy industry
*   Earn a nationally recognized certificate in only one weekend. Whether you’re a business major or psychology major, if you are interested in working in the renewable energy industry, this course is the perfect first step.

**[Reserve a place](https://share.hsforms.com/1Kntp75hkTpahvkws0n7byA3qnvt)**  and start building the skills that drive real-world transactions.

**What you’ll learn**:

*   Understand the many moving parts of a project finance deal and how they fit together
*   Build an annual transaction model from the ground up
*   Complete a case study to evaluate a renewable energy project finance transaction
*   Grasp the key modelling techniques that underpin project finance decision-making

**This course is designed for:**

*   **Student****s** preparing for careers in renewable energy finance and infrastructure

**What to expect**

*   Saturday 9.30 am - 5.30 pm 
*   Sunday 11.30 am – 3:30 pm
*   Plan to arrive at least 15 minutes early
*   Must be present during the entire course to receive certificate
*   No virtual option, class is held in-person
*   Mentor will be assigned upon course completion that will assist with job interviews, general career coaching, and introductions.

**What to bring**

*   Laptop with access to Microsoft Excel
*   Charging device for laptop
*   Business casual attire
*   Earphones

You’ll gain practical skills and insights into the concepts that drive project finance investments and debt structuring.

**Through the** **in-person classroom setting****, you’ll also benefit from:**

*   Specialized one-on-one instruction
*   The ability to ask questions to expert trainers

**Registration available until March 10, 2026**
