Financial model detective book is out!

*   **Step by step how to review and audit of financial models**
*   **for project finance transactions**
*   **Clear guide for professionals** 
*   **a Bible for young financial modelers** 

Within the pages of this financial modeling manual, you will find hints and tricks on how to conduct a preliminary review of a financial model and decide as early as possible whether you want to work with the inherited model or build your own model instead.
