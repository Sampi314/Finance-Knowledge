# Top 10 Real Estate Podcasts for Investors and REIT

In the ever-evolving world of real estate investing and REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts), staying informed and ahead of the curve is crucial. Podcasts have become an invaluable resource, offering expert insights, market analyses, and the latest trends. Here, we’ve curated a list of ten must-listen podcasts tailored specifically for professional real estate investors and decision-makers within the REIT industry.

### 1\. Real Estate Investing for Cash Flow

[Hosted by Kevin Bupp](https://kevinbupp.com/podcast/)
, this podcast delves into the nuances of commercial real estate and cash flow investments. Bupp interviews industry leaders, offering proven strategies for generating consistent cash flow and building wealth through commercial real estate and REITs. Gain insider knowledge on market opportunities and investment tactics that can help you scale your portfolio.

### 2\. BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcasts

A staple in the real estate investing community, [BiggerPockets](https://www.biggerpockets.com/podcasts/real-estate)
 used to feature Brandon Turner and David Greene discussing various facets of real estate investing. The podcast frequently includes guest appearances from successful investors and industry experts, providing actionable insights and advice tailored to both novice and seasoned investors. Learn from those who’ve been there and done it.

### 3\. The BetterLife Podcast: Wealth | Real Estate Investing

But why am I saying “used to”? Because [Brandon Turner now has his own podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-betterlife-podcast-wealth-real-estate-investing-life/id1679074529)
. He’s no longer with BiggerPockets. Whatever was the reason him leaving, I think you should keep an eye on both podcasts.

### 4\. The Real Estate Guys Radio Show

With Robert Helms and Russell Gray at the helm, [this podcast](https://realestateguysradio.com/)
 has been educating investors for over two decades. It covers a broad range of topics, including real estate markets, investing strategies, and economic trends, making it a valuable resource for REIT professionals and real estate investors alike. Tune in for deep dives into market dynamics and strategic investment principles.

### 5\. Commercial Real Estate Pro Network (CREPN) Podcast

Hosted by J. Darrin Gross, the [CREPN Podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/commercial-real-estate-pro-network/id989572322)
 focuses intently on commercial real estate investing. It features insightful interviews with seasoned professionals discussing various aspects of commercial real estate, including market trends, investment strategies, and effective property management. Equip yourself with the knowledge to navigate the complexities of commercial real estate.

### 6\. The Real Wealth Show

[Hosted by Kathy Fettke](https://realwealth.com/real-wealth-show-podcast/)
, this podcast is designed for both new and experienced investors looking to build and preserve wealth through real estate. It offers market insights, investment strategies, and expert interviews, making it a comprehensive resource for real estate investors and REIT professionals. Discover how to enhance your investment approach and safeguard your assets.

### 7\. Best Real Estate Investing Advice Ever Show

Hosted by Joe Fairless, this [podcast](https://open.spotify.com/show/28bzCmCZLWRHvvSHtqhkan)
 claims to provide the best real estate investing advice through interviews with high-profile guests. It covers a wide range of topics, from multifamily and commercial real estate to syndications and REIT investments. Get unparalleled insights that can help you navigate and succeed in a competitive market.

### 8\. America’s Commercial Real Estate Show

Hosted by Michael Bull, this [podcast provides professional analysis](https://www.youtube.com/c/commercialrealestateshow)
 on commercial real estate trends and investment strategies. It features industry experts discussing market conditions, investment opportunities, and strategies relevant to REIT professionals and commercial real estate investors. Stay informed with up-to-date, in-depth analyses that can influence your investment choices.

### 9\. RealCrowd Podcast

[RealCrowd’s podcast](https://www.listennotes.com/podcasts/real-crowd-podcast-realcrowdcom-JIzbNmctYKO/)
, hosted by Adam Hooper, focuses on institutional real estate investing. It features interviews with industry experts discussing various investment strategies, market trends, and insights into the real estate investment landscape, making it essential listening for REIT professionals. Learn from top-tier experts about the intricacies of institutional investing.

### 10\. Real Estate Investing Mastery Podcast

Hosted by Joe McCall, this [podcast is geared towards investors](https://www.realestateinvestingmastery.com/)
 looking to master various aspects of real estate investing. It covers a broad spectrum of topics, including wholesaling, rehabbing, and REIT investments, with expert guests providing practical advice and strategies. Enhance your mastery of real estate investing with tips directly from seasoned practitioners.

For professional real estate investors and REIT industry decision-makers, these real estate podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and insights. From market trends and investment strategies to expert interviews and actionable advice, these podcasts are essential tools for staying informed and making strategic investment decisions in the competitive real estate and REIT markets.

Stay ahead of the curve by tuning into these top podcasts and equip yourself with the knowledge needed to thrive in the dynamic world of real estate investing and REITs. As the market continues to evolve, having access to timely and relevant information can make all the difference in your investment success.

Start listening today and take your real estate investments to the next level. Don’t miss out on these valuable resources and stay ahead of the game in the REIT industry.
