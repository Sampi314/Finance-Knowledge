Where Does Model Audit 2.0 Rank? Inframation League Table
=========================================================

*   August 24, 2023

Once England’s lionesses get over the heartbreak of losing out to Spain in the World Cup final, I’m sure they will reflect on their journey and feel proud of how far they have come.

It is with similar mixed emotions that we take in the H1 [**2023 Inframation model audit league table**](https://infralogic.inframationnews.com/rankings?ranking=2Q2023&rankingBreakdown=modelAuditorAdvisors&rankingSortColumn=volume)
 result!

![Honor in second place](https://www.gridlines.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Table.png "Where Does Model Audit 2.0 Rank? Inframation League Table 1")

While we are still a long way off challenging for the top spot, we are hugely proud of how far we have come in such a short space of time.

I remember the frisson of excitement I felt when we got our first score on the board with our first financial close, and then again on entering the top 10.

To be second by deal volume after less than 3 years of operation, has far surpassed my expectations. We are firmly up there, ahead of the big 4 and among the established audit specialists.

This may be a momentary blip, and I’ll be shocked if we are ahead of BDO and Operis by the end of the year, but I wanted to take a moment to thank our amazing team for their super human efforts, and all of our fantastic clients who have put their faith in us.  

(**Note** – transactions shown are financial closes only – just 20% of our actual deals!)
