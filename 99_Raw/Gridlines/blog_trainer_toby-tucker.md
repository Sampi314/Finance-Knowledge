Toby Tucker
===========

Instructor & associate, Project Finance Institute

Toby is a KPMG-trained chartered accountant and an expert in project and corporate finance. He holds a PhD from the University of Oxford and has 25 years of commercial and transactional experience across various sectors and asset classes. As a lead instructor, Toby has taught courses for organisations such as MUFG Bank, Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners, EBRD and many others.
