**New York, NY (October 13, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced that Co-CEO PJ Patel; Managing Director Adam Smith; and Senior Vice President, Machinery & Equipment Kent Amarante will each speak at the American Society of Appraisers International Conference (ASAIC) in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

ASAIC, which will run from October 19-21, is designed to offer perspective and enhance appraiser expertise by delivering timely, relevant insights for appraisal professionals across all disciplines. Topics will include business valuation, personal property, machinery & equipment, real property, and appraisal review & management.

Smith will speak on two panels as part of the conference’s business valuation sessions. The first will cover consideration of secondary transactions in valuations and the upcoming updates to the AICPA’s Stock-Based Compensation Guide and will take place on Monday, October 20, from 2:25-3:25 pm AST. The second panel, titled “Hot Topics” will discuss trending issues and run from 11:00-12:00 pm AST on Tuesday, October 21.

Patel’s session, “A Panel Discussion on Practice Management,” is also part of the conference’s business valuations sessions. It will take place on Tuesday, October 21, from 8:00-8:45 am AST.

Amarante will lead “The Premise of Value and Installation Costs,” on Tuesday, October. 21, from 2:05-2:55 pm AST. His talk is part of the conference’s Machinery & Technical Specialties sessions.

As a member of the ASA’s International Conference Committee, VRC Managing Director Anthony Pumphrey, CFA, ASA will contribute to the event’s success by introducing selected sessions and moderating discussions with industry leaders.

More information about the conference can be found [here](https://www.appraisers.org/asa-international-conference)
.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
