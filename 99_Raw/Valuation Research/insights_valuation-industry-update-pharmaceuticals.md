#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/pharmaceuticals-IU.jpeg.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   On November 21, 2024, Novartis announced the $1.1 billion acquisition of Kate Therapeutics, a preclinical-stage biotechnology company that focuses on adeno-associated virus (AAV)-based gene therapies.
*   On January 13, 2025, Johnson & Johnson announced the acquisition of Intra-Cellular Therapies for $14 billion. Intra-Cellular Therapies Inc. makes Caplyta, a once-daily pill for treating adults with schizophrenia and depression tied to bipolar disorder.
