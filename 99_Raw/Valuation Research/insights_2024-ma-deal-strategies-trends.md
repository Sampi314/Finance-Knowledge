The M&A Advisor’s newest M&A Alerts column features a new article, written in partnership with VRC’s Co-CEO PJ Patel, [“Future-Proof Your Deals: Proven Strategies for Success in 2024 M&A.”](https://maadvisor.com/maalerts/future-proof-your-deals-proven-strategies-for-success-in-2024-ma/)
 The article highlights crucial trends and strategies in dealmaking, covering topics such as enhanced financial reporting, regulatory changes, and innovative deal structures. As the deal market normalizes in 2024 despite lingering economic challenges, the article offers valuable guidance for M&A professionals.

Highlights include:

*   Enhanced financial reporting and regulatory changes
*   Innovation in deal structuring
*   Normalization of deal market activity
*   Proven strategies to consider for the remainder of 2024
*   Exit challenges
*   Regulatory pressures
