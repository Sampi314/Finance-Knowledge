**New York, NY (July 26, 2024)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, has been named Valuation Firm of the Year by M&A Advisor’s 15th Annual International M&A Awards. This year marks 12 consecutive wins for the firm.

“Each year that VRC earns this award is an affirmation of our continued dedication to serving clients at the highest level,” said VRC Co-CEO PJ Patel.

The winners will be presented their awards at the [2024 Leadership in Dealmaking Summit,](https://events.maadvisor.com/2024LeadershipInDealmakingSummit#/)
  September 24 in New York. The complete list of winners can be found [here](https://www.maadvisor.com/IMA/2024-IMA/15th_Annual_International_MA_Award_Winners_List.pdf)
.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service, independent, global valuation firm. Since 1975, our network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as [VRG](http://www.vrg.net/)
 in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
