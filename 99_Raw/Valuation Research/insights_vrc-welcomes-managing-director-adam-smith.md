**New York, NY (October 15, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced the addition of longtime industry thought leader Adam Smith as a managing director. Smith, who was previously associated with PricewaterhouseCoopers, the International Valuation Standards Council (IVSC) and the Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB), will work across VRC’s offices and practices on complex transactions and reporting issues where his technical expertise can be most impactful to client outcomes.  

 “Adam brings a depth of knowledge that bolsters our already significant capabilities in valuation,” said Co-CEO PJ Patel. “We welcome his experience and leadership among industry organizations providing guidance on the most challenging valuation topics.” 

 Smith joins from PricewaterhouseCoopers, where he was a managing director. During his tenure at PwC, he was actively engaged with industry standard-setting bodies. Smith was technical director of Business Valuation Standards for the IVSC, where he worked with the IVSC Standards Board to develop an updated version of the International Valuation Standards. Before that, he served as a practice fellow at the FASB, specializing in fair value issues.  

Smith’s expertise includes financial reporting, litigation support, corporate planning and consulting, mergers and acquisitions, estate and gift tax reporting, and employee stock ownership plans. He is an active member of the valuations industry and played a key role in the development of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) Valuation Guide on Business Combinations. He currently serves on the working group developing an update to the AICPA’s guidance on Valuation of Privately-Held-Company Equity Securities Issued as Compensation. 

Smith is an Accredited Senior Appraiser, Business Valuation and Intangible Assets. He is also Accredited in Business Valuation (ABV) from the AICPA. He received a B.A. in Business and Economics from the University of California, Los Angeles. 

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
