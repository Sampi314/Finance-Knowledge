#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/CannabisiIU.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   On September 3, 2024, Tilray Brands, Inc., a global lifestyle and consumer  
    packaged goods company, successfully completed the previously  
    announced acquisition of craft breweries Hop Valley Brewing Company,  
    Terrapin Beer Co., and Revolver Brewing, from Molson Coors Beverage  
    Company.
*   On December 9, 2024, Canopy Growth Corporation and Acreage Holdings, Inc. confirmed that Canopy USA, LLC completed its acquisition of Acreage for approximately $258 million. Canopy USA now owns 100% of the issued and outstanding shares of Acreage, a leading cannabis cultivator and processor.
