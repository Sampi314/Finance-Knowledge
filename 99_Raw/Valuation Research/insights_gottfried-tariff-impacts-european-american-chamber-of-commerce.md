**New York, NY (August 11, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced that Managing Director-National Tax Valuation Tom Gottfried will present on a panel discussing tariffs and their effect on the EU and U.S. deal environment at the European American Chamber of Commerce.

The EACC program aims to provide an overview of the far-reaching consequences of the U.S. administration’s tariff actions and threats, as well as their implications on cross-border deals. Gottfried’s panel, “A Tale of Two Markets: Navigating Transatlantic Tariffs, Deals and Valuations in the Current Economic and Geopolitical Environment,” will cover:

*   Overview of the U.S. administration’s tariff actions – what is new, what is out, what is for debate
*   Impact of the tariffs on the U.S. and EU macroeconomic environment
*   Microeconomic and supply chain implications, including global business restructurings and reorganizations
*   Impact on the EU and U.S. deal environment including M&A transactions, divestitures, spin-offs
*   Technical implications on valuations

The event will run from 8:30am-10:30 am EDT on Thursday, September 4.

More information, including registration, can be found [here](https://eaccny.com/events/?event_id=1402)
.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service, independent, global valuation firm. Since 1975, our network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 250 U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
