#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/CRO-IU.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   In July 2024, Thermo Fisher Scientific participated in a private funding  
    round for CytoReason, which has developed a platform to map and  
    compare treatments, patient groups, and disease mechanisms. The  
    funding will aid in expanding CytoReason’s disease models into  
    additional indications and growing its molecular and clinical data with AI  
    capabilities.
*   On August 16, 2024, Audax Private Equity announced it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire the Clinical Services business of Avantor, Inc. for $650 million. The business operates 10 facilities across the US, UK, and Europe. It also maintains a global logistics and equipment services network.
