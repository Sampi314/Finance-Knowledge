#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Air-freight-IU.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   On July 30, 2024, Sennder Technologies GmbH, the leading digital road  
    freight forwarder in Europe, announced the acquisition of the European  
    Surface Transportation (EST) operations of global logistics provider C.H.  
    Robinson, one of the world’s largest logistics providers. The combined  
    business will become a leading Full Truck Load (FTL) player in Europe.
*   On November 22, 2024, Prospero Staff Capital, a private equity vehicle co-led by Ted Kellner and Chris Jamroz, announced the acquisition of a majority ownership interest in Roadrunner from affiliates controlled by Elliott Investment Management L.P. Elliott will retain a minority stake in the company, remaining a long-term partner in Roadrunner’s growth.
