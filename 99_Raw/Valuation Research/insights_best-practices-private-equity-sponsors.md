As the demand for transparency rises for private investment funds, the spotlight is on valuation practices, which is essential for any fund manager to understand. Your firm needs to be well-positioned to manage the scrutiny from enhanced regulations, standard-setter guidelines, stakeholder expectations, and audit pressures.

In our newest white paper, we give the knowledge and tools to guide fund managers and their boards down a path of best practices to avoid unnecessary, disruptive scrutiny around valuations and withstand an inquiry with confidence. Find out why private investment managers trust the accuracy and expertise of an independent, third-party valuation firm.
