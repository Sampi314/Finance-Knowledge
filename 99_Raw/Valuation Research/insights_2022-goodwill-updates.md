Even in today’s volatile economy, M&A continues to play an important role in corporate strategies. As a result, goodwill and other intangible assets continue to grow on the balance sheet — and factoring those assets into your financial analysis is as challenging as ever.  PJ Patel – VRC, Pranav Ghai – [Calbench](https://www.calcbench.com/blog/post/696662049201242112/Goodwill-Webinar-A-Recap)
, Sandy Peters – CFA Institute, and Scott Taub – Financial Reporting Advisors discuss why and how goodwill matters.

Topics addressed include:

*   How goodwill and other intangible assets keep rising as a portion of all a company’s assets;
*   How to examine those assets to better understand a company’s value;
*   The risks around impairment, and how sniff out warning signs;
*   What’s next on regulators’ wish list for better financial reporting and disclosure.
