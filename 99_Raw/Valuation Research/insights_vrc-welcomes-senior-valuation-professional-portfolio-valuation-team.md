**New York (March 12, 2025) –** Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, announced the addition of Stephanie Wittrock to its Boston office. She will join as senior vice president in the [Portfolio Valuation Group](https://www.valuationresearch.com/services/portfolio-valuations/)
.

Prior to joining VRC, Wittrock served in several leadership roles at Carta Inc., most recently as the product manager overseeing multiple teams focused on valuation and portfolio management. Previously, she served as a tax associate at Seattle-based firms Andersen Tax and Plum Creek Timber Company.

“Stephanie brings a unique combination of leadership and product management expertise to our firm,” said PJ Patel, co-CEO of VRC. ” Her expertise will significantly enhance our ability to deliver innovative, cutting-edge valuation services.”

Wittrock received her BSBA with an emphasis in Accounting and her MS in Accounting with an emphasis on Taxation from University of Colorado Boulder.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service, independent, global valuation firm. Since 1975, our network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300 U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
