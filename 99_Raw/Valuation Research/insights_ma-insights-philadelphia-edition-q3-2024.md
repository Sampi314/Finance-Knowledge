The Philadelphia region’s M&A market continues to evolve with a flurry of strategic transactions completed during the third quarter of 2024. Robust deal volumes, a focus on add-on acquisitions, and trends toward vertical integration and market expansion characterize the [Philadelphia region’s most recent quarter of M&A activity](https://527014.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/527014/M%2BA%20Insights%20Philly%20Edition/244291-VRC-MA-Insights-Philly-R1b.pdf)
.

In VRC’s newest M&A Insight update focusing on the region, commentary from [Edward Hamilton, CFA, Managing Director](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/edward-hamilton/)
 in our Yardley, Pennsylvania office, provides actionable insights for accounting and financial reporting professionals who are navigating their company’s growth plans for the remainder of 2024 and the start of 2025.

By analyzing recent transaction details and market trends, VRC offers a high-level summary highlighting key takeaways, forecasting deal activity, and providing valuation multiples and deal multiple estimates. These insights are designed to help you make informed decisions, optimize M&A strategies, and capitalize on the opportunities presented by a dynamic market.
