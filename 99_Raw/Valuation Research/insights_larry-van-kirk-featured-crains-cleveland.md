[Larry Van Kirk](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/lawrence-van-kirk/)
, managing director and leader of Valuation Research Corporation’s (VRC) Mid-Central Region, is a featured “Ask the Expert” in _Crain’s Cleveland_ discussing valuation’s changing role in financial and tax reporting.

Larry shares his perspective on advantages and other considerations associated with seeking valuations, as well as the important role valuation plays for financial reporting and tax professionals of both public and private companies when they are managing internal transactions, executive compensation and sale and investment decisions.

Considering his deep relationships with clients, partners and ACG chapters in the NE Ohio business community, he also offers his insight on the local business environment.
