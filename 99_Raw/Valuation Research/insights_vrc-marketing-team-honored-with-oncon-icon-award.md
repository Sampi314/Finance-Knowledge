**New York (Oct. 31, 2024)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, announced that the VRC marketing team has been named to the OnConferences OnCon Icon Top 50 Marketing Team list. Other OnCon Icon award winners included Hyatt Hotels, Walt Disney Company, and The Home Depot.

The OnCon Icon Top 100 Awards are peer-voted awards determined by community and organizational observation of work. Winners represent some of the top individuals and teams worldwide.

“We are incredibly proud of our marketing team for this well-deserved recognition,” said PJ Patel, Co-CEO of VRC. “It’s especially noteworthy because we operate in a complex industry, and our marketing approach emphasizes education and thought leadership. Not only does that elevate our brand in the outside world, but it also strengthens our connection with current clients.”

Jennifer Tischer Dries, senior VP, marketing director at VRC, added, “This recognition reflects our team’s collective commitment to innovation. We’re honored to be acknowledged among such esteemed companies and look forward to continuing to push boundaries in our industry.”

View the full list of companies included in the list [here](https://www.onconferences.com/24teamwinners-marketing)

### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service, independent, global valuation firm. Since 1975, our network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300 U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
