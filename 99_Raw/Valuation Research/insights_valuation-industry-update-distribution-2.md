#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Distribution-IU.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   On November 11, 2024, Cardinal Health announced two acquisitions  
    which consisted of a majority stake in gastroenterology practice  
    management group GI Alliance for $2.8 billion from Apollo Global, and the  
    purchase of Advanced Diabetes Supply Group for $1.1 billion from Court  
    Square Capital Management, LP.
*   On January 2, 2025, Cencora completed its $5.1 billion acquisition of  
    Retina Consultants of America. RCA is a network of retina practices,  
    specialists and surgeons. Webster Equity formed RCA as a platform  
    company in 2020.
