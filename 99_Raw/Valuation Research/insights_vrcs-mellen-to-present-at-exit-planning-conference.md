**New York, NY (October 21, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced that Senior Managing Director Chris Mellen will speak at the 16th annual International Exit Planning Association (IEPA) conference in Boston.

The yearly Exit Planning Conference provides professionals with an opportunity to advance their knowledge and expertise in the growing field of exit planning while networking with senior-level advisors and consultants committed to helping business owners prepare for and achieve successful exits from their privately held businesses.

Mellen’s session, “Valuation: Software and Professional Appraiser!,” will look at the difference between software valuations and valuations done by valuation professionals. It will provide an inside look at the new RISR tool that new CBEC candidates will get a chance to use on their case studies.

The session will run from 1:40-2:30 pm ET on Tuesday, October 28.

More information about the conference, which will take place from Tuesday, October 28-Wednesday, October 29, can be found [here](https://members.theiepa.com/page/2025annualconference)
.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
