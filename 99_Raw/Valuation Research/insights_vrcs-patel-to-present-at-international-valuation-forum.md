**New York, NY (October 24, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced that Co-CEO PJ Patel will speak on a panel at the International Valuation Standards Council (IVSC) Advisory Forum Day as part of the IVSC’s 2025 AGM and International Valuation Conference. VRC is a sponsor of the forum, which will take place on Tuesday, October 28 in New Delhi.

The Advisory Forum Day brings together representatives of IVSC’s member and sponsor organizations for dialogue and debate on the future of valuation. This year’s agenda focuses on the themes shaping standards and practice worldwide, including the future of learning and practice for next generation of valuation professionals, ESG and climate considerations, and AI, among other focuses.

On the panel, “The Future of Valuation,” Patel and other global valuation leaders will share their priorities for the next 18–24 months, reflecting on market developments, global trends, and the implications for the profession.

The panel will run from 4:20-5:20 pm IST.

More information about the Advisor Forum Day can be found [here](https://ivsc.org/ivsc-2025-agm-and-international-valuation-conference-to-be-hosted-in-new-delhi-india/)
