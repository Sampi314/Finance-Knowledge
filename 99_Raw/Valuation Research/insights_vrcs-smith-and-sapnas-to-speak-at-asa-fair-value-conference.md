**New York, NY (November 18, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced that Managing Director Adam Smith and Senior Managing Director Charles Sapnas will speak at the 2025 American Society of Appraisers (ASA) Winter Fair Value Conference on Thursday, December 11.

Smith, who is also the co-chair of the conference, will present the conference’s welcome remarks at 1:00pm EST and will be the moderator of the panel on AI in valuation.

Sapnas will speak on a panel, “Valuation Guide Updates (CSRP, Intangible Discount Rates and Cheap Stock).” The panel will be presented by leaders of several task forces working to advance best practices in valuation. Sapnas will provide an overview of key concepts and developments related to intangible asset discount rates. It will run from 4:25-5:25pm EST.

The conference brings together leading valuation professionals, industry experts, and regulators for a half-day program focused on the latest developments and practical applications in fair value. It will feature discussions on AI-related valuation practices and risk considerations, challenges and methodologies of tranched preferred stock valuation, valuation in the energy industry, among other topics.

More information about the conference can be found [here](https://learn.appraisers.org/products/2025-asa-winter-fair-value-conference-virtual#tab-product_tab_agenda)
.

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
