**Denver, CO (January 27, 2026)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced its new office in Denver as part of its expanding presence throughout the Rocky Mountain region. The expansion includes the addition of three senior valuation professionals and two associates.

[Luke Van Horn](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/luke-van-horn/)
, CFA, joins the Denver office as senior vice president of business development, bringing over a decade of experience in the valuation industry and business development. Prior to VRC, he was managing director at Intrinsic LLC. He began his career at Deloitte in a valuation consulting role and holds the designation of Chartered Financial Analyst.

[Evan Lewis](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/evan-lewis/)
, CFA, formerly a managing director at Intrinsic LLC, joins as senior vice president. He holds the designation of Chartered Financial Analyst and specializes in fairness and solvency opinions, complex security analysis, and valuations for 409A and financial reporting purposes.

[Rebecca Layman](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/rebecca-layman/)
 joins as a vice president. Before joining VRC, she was a manager at RSM where she focused on complex financial instruments, financial consulting, and valuation services. She began her career at EY on the assurance and transaction advisory teams.

[Jacob Malburg](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakemalburg/)
 joins the Denver team as an associate. Prior to VRC, he was a senior associate at Willamette Management Associates. [David Orenstein](https://www.linkedin.com/in/david-orenstein-cpa/)
, CPA, who was previously a senior consultant at Baker Tilly, also joins as an associate. He is a Certified Public Accountant.

“I’m extremely excited to formally launch VRC’s expansion into the Rocky Mountain region, with the opening of our Denver office,” says Justin Johnson, VRC co-CEO. “VRC recognizes the continued growth and demand in the region, and we look forward to providing our well-established industry expertise through our team based in Denver. Each member of our Denver team brings a wealth of experience; backed by the support of VRC’s national presence, they are well equipped to serve the needs of our current and prospective clients. I look forward to seeing this office grow and thrive.”

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Denver, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, Turkey, and the United Kingdom.
