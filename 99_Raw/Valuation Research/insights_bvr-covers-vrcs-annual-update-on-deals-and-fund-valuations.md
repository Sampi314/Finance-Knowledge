Business Valuation Resources features insights from Valuation Research Corporation (VRC)’s media briefing roundtable in its Business Valuation Updates publication.

The article details comments from [John Czapla](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/john-czapla/)
, [Parag Patel](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/parag-patel/)
, [Chad Rucker](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/chad-rucker/)
, [PJ Patel](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/pj-patel/)
, and [Charles Sapnas](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/charles-sapnas/)
 on market conditions, European private credit markets, the growth in secondary transactions and the growing need for third-party valuations in many contexts. The piece also touches on VRC’s strategic investments in AI and data management, while maintaining a focus on human expertise.

The VRC team said they anticipate robust transaction activity in 2025, and is preparing for evolving market challenges, particularly with the growth in retail-focused private credit funds that often require greater transparancy and require more frequent valuation in line with more generous investor liquidity terms.
