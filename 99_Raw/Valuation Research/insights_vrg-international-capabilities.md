### Valuation Research Group: A Global Entity

_\[Transcript\]_

Valuation Research Group, or VRG, was established a little over 20 years ago when we identified a strong need to have a global presence.

_Valuation Research Group provides independent valuation and appraisal services around the world._

We wanted to create an international group of valuation firms that we could depend on, and firms that shared our common goals and objectives when executing work for clients.

_We have a global reach with offices throughout Europe, the Americas, Asia and Australia._

VRG is not a membership group with a name in a book. Instead, they are valuation firms whose executives and senior management treat our clients as their own.

_With more than 750 professionals in over 40 offices around the world…VRG has an established reputation for providing expert and independent opinions for a variety of tax, financial reporting, and management planning requirements._

_All of our engagements are managed by a single point of contact…and are executed by local professionals._

We have executed international engagements for some of the largest corporations in the world.

_Our client list includes Global 100 companies…portfolio companies and private equity firms…large global banks, hedge funds…pension funds and other investors._

_Our primary services are the valuation of business and corporate entities…their intellectual property and intangible assets, and the appraisal of real property, machinery, and equipment.  
_  
_We serve multinational and local businesses and have an intimate knowledge of local economies, languages, and cultures…as well as legal requirements and accounting guidelines.  
_  
VRG is a group that has frequent communications amongst members. We discuss country-specific accounting, tax and valuation regulations. In addition, the local political and economic environments of our respective countries.

_This depth of experience allows us to better serve our clients.  
_  
We look forward to working with you.
