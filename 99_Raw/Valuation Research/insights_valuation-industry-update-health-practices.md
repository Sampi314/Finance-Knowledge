#### ![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Healthcare-IU.jpg)Industry Highlights

*   On September 3, 2024, U.S. Physical Therapy, Inc. announced the acquisition of a physical therapy and hand therapy practice with eight clinic locations located in Pennsylvania which currently generates approximately $5.5 million in annual revenues. The Company acquired a 70% interest in the Company with the current owners retaining a 30% ownership interest.
*   On December 2, 2024, U.S. Physical Therapy, Inc. announced the acquisition of a hand and physical therapy practice with eight clinic locations in Wyoming which currently generates approximately $6.5 million in annual revenues and 43,000 in annual visits. The Company acquired a 75% interest in the Company with the current owners retaining a 25% ownership interest.
