#### Industry Highlights**![](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Digital-Ads-Marketing_IU.jpg)**

*   On September 15, 2022, ZW Data Action Technologies, an integrated online advertising, precision marketing, data analytics, and other value-added services company, announced that it signed a letter of intent, and has formally proposed an acquisition offer to Henan Baodun Information Technology Co., Ltd. Baodun is a full-service internet marketing company integrating content marketing, advertising effect optimization and brand marketing.
*   On September 6, 2022, CommerceHub, an industry-leading commerce network connecting supply, demand and delivery for the world’s leading retailers and brands, and ChannelAdvisor Corporation, a leading provider of cloud-based e-commerce solutions, announced that they entered into a definitive agreement for CommerceHub to acquire the outstanding shares of ChannelAdvisor in a transaction led by global software investor, Insight Partners, for approximately $732 million.
