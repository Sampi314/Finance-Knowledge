**New York (March 17, 2025)**  – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of valuation support and advisory services, announced today its participation in the upcoming ASA Global Valuation Review Symposium World Tour. The virtual event, taking place on March 26th from 8 a.m. to 12 p.m. ET, will feature a presentation by VRC’s experts [Jacquelyn Marsac](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/jacquelyn-marsac/)
 and [Kent Amarante](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/kent-amarante/)
, in conjunction with [Rajeev Shah](https://www.vrg.net/professionals/rajeev-shah/)
 from RBSA Advisors, a member of Valuation Research Group in India.

The presentation is scheduled to begin at 11 a.m. ET and will focus on a case study titled “Tangible and Intangible Assets in Valuation Review Assignments.” This session will provide attendees with valuable insights and practical knowledge on navigating complex global valuation assignments.

“We are excited to share our expertise and experience with the global valuation community,” said Marsac, Managing Director at VRC.

The ASA Global Valuation Review Symposium World Tour is a three-part series designed to bring together valuation professionals from around the world to discuss the latest trends, challenges, and best practices in valuation review and management services. Registration for the event is now open, and interested parties can visit the ASA website to learn more and secure their spot [HERE](https://learn.appraisers.org/products/global-valuation-review-symposium-world-tour-part-2-of-3#tab-product_tab_agenda)
.

### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service global valuation firm. Since 1975, our network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, including VRC’s 300 U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable value conclusions to domestic and international clients. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.

### About VRG

[Valuation Research Group (VRG)](https://www.vrg.net/)
 is a global valuation and advisory services group. Formed in 1995, its global network has been providing quality valuations and value-related services for the U.S. and international business communities. Its member firms have nearly 1,500 professionals and an established reputation for providing expert and independent opinions of value for business enterprises, intangible assets, real estate, and personal property. Collectively, VRG professionals have served clients in more than 100 countries. VRG’s international offices span five continents, with over 50 locations in North, South, and Central America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
