**New York, NY (March 16, 2026)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, today announced Managing Directors Adam Smith and Ed Hamilton will present virtually with GAAP Dynamics for a fair value webinar on Tuesday, April 21.

The webinar, “Navigating Fair Value in M&A,” will bring together accounting, valuation, and audit perspectives to help participants navigate fair value measurements under ASC 820 in a business combination (ASC 805).

Smith and Hamilton, along with panelists Mike Walworth and Rachel Klein from GAAP Dynamics, will walk through a purchase price allocation report, allowing attendees to:

*   Revisit key accounting requirements
*   Explore common valuation methodologies and assumptions
*   Understand audit considerations related to accounting estimates and the use of specialists
*   Learn how to critically evaluate valuation conclusions

The webinar will run from 2:00-4:00 P.M. EDT. Attendees can earn two CPE credits.

More information about the event, including registration, can be found [here](https://hubs.li/Q044c7_n0)

**About VRC**

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, Turkey, and the United Kingdom.
