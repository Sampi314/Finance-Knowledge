Compared to more traditional business appraisal engagements, [valuing carried interests](https://www.valuationresearch.com/services/financial-reporting-valuations/ipo-readiness-equity-compensation/#_Carried_Interest)
 (also known as performance allocations) for [gift and estate tax planning](https://www.valuationresearch.com/services/tax-compliance-planning/exit-succession-planning/gift-estate/)
 purposes poses several unique challenges.

Each carried interest has unique characteristics, including high relative volatility, and to be defensible, valuations should be supported by documentation and meaningful discussions with the client.

We welcome you to read the full article featured in _Valuation Strategies_ magazine.
