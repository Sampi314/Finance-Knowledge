#### I![Biotech industry update](https://www.valuationresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/LifeScience-1024x683.jpg)ndustry Highlights

*   On September 28, 2024, Regeneron Pharmaceuticals purchased Quad/Graphic’s  
    nearly 1.1 million-square-foot plant, igniting hopes for the replacement of a major industrial operation that pulled up stakes earlier this year. Regeneron, based in Tarrytown, NY, develops and manufactures a variety of prescription medications.
*   On January 23, 2025, AbbVie completed the previously announced acquisition of Nimble Therapeutics for approximately $200 million. Nimble’s lead asset is an investigational oral peptide IL23R inhibitor in preclinical development for the treatment of psoriasis.
