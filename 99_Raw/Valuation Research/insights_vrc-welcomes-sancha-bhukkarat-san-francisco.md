**San Francisco, CA (December 9, 2025)** – Valuation Research Corporation (VRC), a leading global provider of independent valuation support and advisory services, announced today the addition of [Sancha Bhukkarat](https://www.valuationresearch.com/professionals/sancha-bhukkarat/)
 as Vice President, Business Development. Based in San Francisco, Mr. Bhukkarat will focus on building and strengthening relationships with private and public companies, private equity firms, and other partners across the M&A ecosystem, with an emphasis on Northern California and the Pacific Northwest.

“Sancha brings a strong combination of M&A and IPO experience, global relationship management, deep connectivity across the private equity and dealmaking communities,” said Kevin Meyers, Senior Managing Director. “His background further positions VRC to support clients and business partners with the independent expertise required throughout the transaction lifecycle.”

Before joining VRC, Bhukkarat held senior roles at Donnelley Financial (DFIN), Visa, and GoGlobal, where he advised founders, investment banks, private equity funds, M&A advisors, and corporate attorneys. His experience spans information security, due diligence, international workforce integration, deal execution and post-transaction integration and compliance across a range of industries.

Bhukkarat holds a bachelor’s degree in economics and international relations from the University of California, Davis, and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of California, Irvine.  He is an active member of the Association for Corporate Growth (ACG) San Francisco, the San Francisco Private Equity Network (SFPEN), and the International Business Advisors (IBA).

#### About VRC

Valuation Research Corporation is a full-service valuations partner and strategic consultant to private market investment funds, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies, and acquisitive global public and private companies. As one of the largest and best-resourced firms focused exclusively on valuation, VRC’s network of nearly 1,500 valuation professionals, which includes VRC’s 300+ U.S.-based colleagues, has provided objective, supportable conclusions of value to domestic and international clients since 1975. VRC has locations in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Cincinnati, Dallas, Los Angeles, Milwaukee, New York, Princeton, San Francisco, and Tampa; as well as international member firms operating as VRG in Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Colombia, Germany, India, Israel, Japan, Luxembourg, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, Turkey and the United Kingdom.
