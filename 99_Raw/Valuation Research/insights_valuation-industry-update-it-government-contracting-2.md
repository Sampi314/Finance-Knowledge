VRC is proud to release its latest Industry Report.

Our Government Contracting Industry Update highlights continued market bifurcation as defense and national security segments remain resilient while several civilian-facing businesses report notable revenue pressure. The report also outlines how major contractors are mitigating shutdown-related impacts, the ongoing strength of share repurchase programs, and expanded deployment of AI-driven solutions across the sector. Valuation multiples remained relatively steady, and public company performance trends reflect the broader divergence in end-markets. The update additionally captures robust M&A and capital markets activity through November, including significant investments across autonomy, space systems, cybersecurity, advanced sensors, modeling and simulation, and next-generation defense technologies.
