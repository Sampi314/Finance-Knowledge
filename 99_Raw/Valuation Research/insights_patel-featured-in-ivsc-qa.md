VRC Co-CEO PJ Patel shared his perspectives on how the valuation profession continues to evolve amid rapid market change in a new Q&A feature, [“Innovation, Integrity, and the Future of Valuation,”](https://ivsc.org/vrcs-pj-patel-on-innovation-integrity-and-the-future-of-valuation/)
 published by the International Valuation Standards Council (IVSC).

In the interview, Patel discusses how valuation professionals can adapt to the pace of innovation while maintaining the discipline and integrity central to the profession. He reflects on the importance of transparency, consistent application of standards, and the need for continuous professional development as valuation increasingly influences investment decisions and financial reporting worldwide.

Patel notes, “Our role as valuation professionals is not only to assess value but also to build confidence in the financial markets. That requires integrity, independence, and a shared commitment to high standards.”
