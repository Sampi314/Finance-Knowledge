Life Sciences Valuation, Forecasting and NPV Analysis
=====================================================

Life Sciences Valuation is the process of attributing value to assets and businesses which operate in sectors such as pharma, biotech, diagnostics, medical devices and so on. Valuation of such assets and companies involves the application of an array of valuation methods and leverages deep knowledge and idiosyncrasies of the sector. BiopharmaVantage provides valuation services to businesses, investors, lawyers and accounting firms.

Pharma, Biotech, Diagnostics and Medtech Valuation Services
-----------------------------------------------------------

Valuation is central to numerous decision-making processes; it is one of the most frequently applied tools in a wide range of business scenarios. It incorporates most business metrics and helps decision-makers choose optimally aligned value-creating options. Valuation is part art and part science. Our valuation experts use appropriate methods to derive the value of any asset including, _inter alia_, products, platforms, technologies, intellectual property and companies.

Our [deep knowledge of the life sciences sector](https://www.biopharmavantage.com/life-sciences-consulting)
 including pharma, biotech, diagnostics, medical devices, therapy and disease areas, and of [key drivers of valuations](https://www.pharmiweb.com/article/pharma-valuation-critical-factors-for-capturing-value)
 such as competitive landscapes, brewing pipeline, clinical trials, asset differentiation, value propositions, market access, attrition rates etc., along with our superior knowledge of economics, accounting, and finance, enable us to better estimate the value of life science assets – learn the [best practices for conducting valuations of pharma and biotech assets](https://www.biopharmavantage.com/pharma-biotech-valuation-best-practices)
.
