**Pharma Competitive Intelligence Services**
============================================

[Pharma competitive intelligence](https://www.biopharmavantage.com/competitive-intelligence)
 (CI) is the process by which pharmaceutical companies gain insights from other pharma companies in order to make better decisions. It is a type of market or external analysis that specifically focuses on the competition. Pharma CI involves four activities: 1) systematic collection of intelligence, 2) rigorous analysis, 3) dissemination to relevant stakeholders, and 4) action based on the findings – collectively, the objective is [to gain and sustain competitive advantage](https://www.biopharmavantage.com/role-competitive-intelligence-pharma-strategic-assessment)
.

Competitive intelligence enables pharma companies to leverage the learnings from their competitors and peers and provides them with opportunities to refine their own strategies. Over the years, [the usage of competitive intelligence has matured](https://www.biopharmavantage.com/competitive-intelligence#The_4_Levels_of_Competitive_Intelligence_Usage_in_Pharma_Companies)
, and it has become a routinely procured service in the pharmaceutical industry. BiopharmaVantage provides premium quality competitive intelligence services to pharma and biotech companies.
