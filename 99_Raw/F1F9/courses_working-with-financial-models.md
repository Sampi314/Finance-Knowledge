Course Content
--------------

What will I learn?
------------------

We will learn how to navigate and read models more effectively, including understanding the best practices that models should follow. We will perform technical reviews of models, including reviewing them for inconsistent formulas, embedded constants, unused inputs and workings.

We will build a best practice input structure including scenario and sensitivity functionality, use standard Excel structures to maintain version control and an audit trail and undertake high level analytical reviews of a model’s outputs and a more focussed review on targeted elements of the model.

Is this course right for me?
----------------------------

This course is designed for professionals who need to work with and rely on complex financial models, either on an ongoing basis or to support a particular transaction.

The case study for the course is based upon a going concern but the principles and techniques we teach are transferable to all business sectors and all types of financial models.
