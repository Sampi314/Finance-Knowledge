Why F1F9?

Build Models & Learn Core Skills With F1F9
------------------------------------------

We have been at the forefront of financial modelling training for 20 years. Our focus on rigorously structured and easy-to-read, easy-to-navigate financial models is highly valued by our clients and is critical to managing the power and flexibility of financial modelling in Excel and its environment. We work for leading banks, funds, equity investors, accountants and major corporations, with several embedding our courses in their wider training programmes.

Our model build team practises what we teach on our courses, where structure and rigour is extended to the project management process. Collaboration is critical, you should expect us to spend as much time as it takes to ensure you understand the model we’re developing and we would ask you to reciprocate with the project or business specific details. Once built you should expect your financial model to be easy to operate and to modify for changes in the project or business.
