Course Content
--------------

What will I learn?
------------------

We will build together, step-by-step, the calculations required to build a fully dynamic forecast model ready for sensitivity analysis and the running of scenarios. More advanced topics will include inflation and equity valuation.

Working with a case study based on a business acquisition and a template start model, your detailed modelling will follow best practice principles and the FAST Standard. As you build your model from scratch, you can expect intensive, immersive hands-on learning supported by detailed instruction from an expert instructor.

Following one of our live courses, you will receive full and ongoing access to the video-based version of the course, online support from our team of modelling experts and the opportunity to submit additional case studies for review and feedback.

Is this course right for me?
----------------------------

Best Practice Financial Modelling is designed for professionals who need to build, modify or run financial models.

For those with limited experience, the course is an excellent introduction to best practice financial modelling. Experienced modellers find the quality and productivity of their modelling improves through the introduction of a standardised approach to model design and construction.

Best Practice Financial Modelling Course from F1F9
==================================================

F1F9’s Best Practice Financial Modelling course teaches you how to build clear, robust and best-practice financial models using Microsoft Excel and the FAST Standard. You’ll learn how to construct fully integrated income statements, balance sheets and cash flow forecasts, while improving model structure, accuracy and transparency for real-world finance applications.

Designed for finance professionals at all levels, the course combines practical instruction with hands-on exercises to strengthen your financial forecasting and modelling skills. With flexible delivery options including live online and classroom training, you’ll gain the confidence to build, review and adapt financial models used in corporate finance, banking and advisory roles.
