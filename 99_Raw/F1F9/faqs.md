Training FAQs

Why don't F1F9's keyboard shortcut macros open when I double click on the file?

###### Why don't F1F9's keyboard shortcut macros open when I double click on the file?

The file is opening but it is designed to open in a hidden state. That’s so that the file doesn’t get in the way while you are working on a model for real.  
To unhide the open file, try Alt, W, U and you’ll see the documentation (as well as the coding for the shortcuts in the Visual Basic environment). To hide the open file, its Alt, W, H.

Why don't the keyboard shortcuts work even once I've confirmed the file is open?

###### Why don't the keyboard shortcuts work even once I've confirmed the file is open?

The most likely reason is that something is blocking the macros from running. Unhide the file and confirm that all “Enable macros” requests have been clicked.  
Follow our blog: [https://www.f1f9.com/blog/keyboard-shortcuts/](https://www.f1f9.com/blog/keyboard-shortcuts/)
 to have the macros open automatically when you open Excel. This approach works well with particularly sensitive corporate firewalls.

Why do some of the keyboard shortcuts work and others don't?

###### Why do some of the keyboard shortcuts work and others don't?

This is normally to do with other add-ins that you may have running in Excel e.g. Bloomberg or Arixcel.  
Either disable the add-ins while you are modelling or change our coding so that you use a different shortcut key. Our coding is open to all so you make whatever amendments you deem appropriate.

Model Build FAQs
----------------

How long will it take to build my model?

###### How long will it take to build my model?

Turnaround times can range from 4 to 12 weeks from start to finish.   Most assignments are delivered on a 4 week / 6 week  (first draft / full draft) or 6 week / 8 week timescale.

Can you continue to support me after the model has been built?

###### Can you continue to support me after the model has been built?

Yes, we won’t disappear at the end of the model build assignment.  We’ll always be available to deal with any questions you may have. 

If you need further support to run the model, or to make structural changes, we can do this on an hours incurred basis.  If there are discreet packages of work, we can also agree a fixed number of hours in advance.

Ongoing maintenance contracts are also available with pricing to reflect the amount of regular work required.

Do you provide model review services?

###### Do you provide model review services?

We do provide model review services, but we don’t provide model audit services.  Our reviews are based on fulfilling an agreed scope of work, and are suitable if you need increased confidence in your model but not contractual reliance.

Our reviews cost in the range £4,000 to £8,000 and depends on model size and the required scope of review.  Please contact us for further information.

What does an F1F9 model look like?

###### What does an F1F9 model look like?

Our models follow the FAST Standard, which stipulates adherence with modelling best practice and a standard approach to structuring models.  The FAST Standard is independently governed by a not for profit organisation ([The FAST Standard Organisation](https://www.fast-standard.org/)
.) 

We build models that are transparent and easy for a user to operate and understand.  You will find examples of our models on the ‘Download Resources’ page.
