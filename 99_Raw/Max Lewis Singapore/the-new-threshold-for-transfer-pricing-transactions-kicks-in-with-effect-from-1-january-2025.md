The new threshold for Transfer pricing transactions kicks in with effect from 1 January 2025
============================================================================================

by [MaxLewis](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/author/maxlewis/ "Posts by MaxLewis")
 | Mar 25, 2025 | [Transfer Pricing](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/category/transfer-pricing/)

The new threshold for [Transfer pricing](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/our-services/transfer-pricing/)
 transactions kicks in with effect from 1 January 2025. Companies need to be aware of that the new threshold applies from Year of Assessment 2026 for the basis period 1 January 2025 till 31 December 2025 which means that they should be prepared to make the relevant changes in their transfer pricing documentation reports for their financial year ending in 2025. The deadline will be 30 November 2026 to ensure that companies have prepared their reports and is ready to provide to [IRAS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inland_Revenue_Authority_of_Singapore)
 if and when requested.

The key changes are mainly the provision of service to and from the taxpayer to or by a related party increase from S$ 1 million to S$ 2 million. This includes the grant of a right to use movable property to the taxpayer by a related party, lease of any property to the taxpayer by a related party and also, the grant of a guarantee to the taxpayer by a related party.
