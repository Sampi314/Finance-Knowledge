Voluntary or Compulsory GST registration?
=========================================

by [Albert Tan](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/voluntary-or-compulsory-gst-registration "Posts by Albert Tan")
 | Oct 15, 2020 | [Taxation & GST](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/category/taxation/)

If a company or limited liability partnership or sole proprietorship reasonably expect its turnover or revenue to be more than S$ 1 million in the next 12 months, then it is liable to apply for [GST registration](https://www.iras.gov.sg/IRASHome/GST/Non-GST-registered-businesses/Registering-for-GST/Applying-for-GST-Registration/)
. IRAS do allow for voluntary GST registration if entities do not expect its revenue to be more than S$1 million in the next 12 months, but there are conditions to be fulfill.

You can visit IRAS “Do I need to register for GST” at [https://bit.ly/2FuWVqt](https://bit.ly/2FuWVqt)
