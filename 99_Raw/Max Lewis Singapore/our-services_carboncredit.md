**Carbon Credit Valuation & Advisory Services (Singapore)**

As carbon markets mature and regulatory frameworks tighten, the need for robust, defensible valuation of carbon credits has become critical for financial reporting, transactions, and tax purposes.

We provide independent, technically rigorous carbon credit valuation services tailored for corporates, funds, and carbon market participants.

**Typical Clients**

We advise corporates, asset managers, carbon market participants and project developers on the valuation of carbon credits and related environmental assets. Our clients typically seek support for IFRS-compliant financial reporting, transaction and investment analysis, audit defensibility and cross-border tax considerations. Engagements often involve complex portfolios or structured arrangements where robust valuation methodologies and market-based pricing frameworks are essential.

**Our Services**

**1\. Fair Value Measurement (IFRS / Financial Reporting)**

*   Valuation of carbon credits under IFRS (IAS 2 / IAS 38 / IFRS 9 considerations)
*   Support for audit and financial statement disclosures
*   Day 1 valuation and subsequent measurement

**2\. Transaction & Deal Support**

*   Valuation of carbon credit portfolios
*   Pricing support for forward purchase agreements (ERPAs)
*   Independent valuation for investment decision-making

**3\. Tax & Transfer Pricing Advisory**

*   Valuation support for cross-border carbon credit transactions
*   Intercompany pricing and documentation
*   Alignment with OECD and IRAS expectations

**4\. Dispute, Audit & Assurance Support**

*   Independent valuation reports for audit review
*   Support in discussions with auditors and regulators
*   Defensibility analysis of pricing assumptions

**Why Us**

*   Independent valuation specialist — not a broker or trader
*   IFRS-aligned methodology consistent with audit expectations
*   Deep understanding of carbon market pricing dynamics

**Typical Engagement Scope**

*   Carbon credit identification (type, registry, vintage, geography)
*   Market benchmarking (comparable trades and indices)
*   Pricing adjustments (quality, liquidity, delivery risk)
*   Discounted cash flow modelling (where applicable)
*   Comprehensive valuation report with supporting evidence

**Our achievements include:-**
------------------------------



*   Led the valuation of a large-scale carbon credit portfolio (500,000+ tCO₂e) for financial reporting, applying market-based benchmarking and asset-specific adjustments to derive an audit-defensible fair value.
*   Advised on the valuation of premium nature-based carbon credits, incorporating pricing differentials for co-benefits, project integrity, and market demand dynamics.
*   Designed and implemented a structured valuation framework for carbon credits, integrating observable market data with quantitative adjustment methodologies.
*   Performed valuation of forward carbon credit contracts (ERPAs) using discounted cash flow techniques, including risk-adjusted pricing and sensitivity analysis.
*   Supported audit processes by preparing robust valuation documentation aligned with IFRS 13, addressing key areas of judgment and uncertainty.
