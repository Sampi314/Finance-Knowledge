### **Singapore Sustainability Reporting Services**

We help Singapore-listed and private companies navigate the fast-evolving landscape of **sustainability reporting and ESG disclosure requirements**. With mandatory reporting under the **SGX Listing Rules** and increasing alignment with global standards such as **ISSB (IFRS S1/S2), GRI, and TCFD**, businesses must ensure their sustainability reports are not only compliant but also meaningful to investors, regulators, and stakeholders. Our services provide end-to-end support, from **materiality assessments** and **stakeholder engagement** to **ESG data collection, KPI design, and climate-related risk disclosures**, enabling companies to communicate their sustainability journey with clarity and confidence.

Beyond regulatory compliance, our approach emphasises how sustainability reporting can be a **strategic tool**. We help clients identify ESG risks and opportunities within their operations, supply chains, and markets, translating sustainability performance into actionable insights that support long-term value creation. By embedding sustainability considerations into corporate strategy, companies can strengthen brand reputation, improve investor trust, and future-proof their business models against emerging environmental and social challenges.

Our tailored solutions are designed to meet the needs of diverse industries — from manufacturing and logistics to real estate, financial services, and technology — helping organisations balance compliance with innovation. By integrating sustainability reporting with broader corporate objectives, we enable companies to achieve **greater transparency, resilience, and sustainable growth** in line with Singapore’s Green Plan 2030 and global sustainability benchmarks.

### **Enterprise Risk Management services**

Risk is something that all business face in their daily dealings and coupled with increased regulation such as data protection, compliance risk has emerged as a key subject that directors need to manage well given this litigious landscape which see hefty penalties in the event of non-compliance. We are able to help review your processes and systems to ensure that the right controls are in place and operating effectively as they should be.

Additionally, as opposed to managing risk haphazardly, having a proper Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) framework in place allows the company to have a good read into the overall risk exposure and whether any of the potential issues falls out of the tolerable threshold so that appropriate actions can be taken. We are able to assist you with the establishment and maintenance of an ERM framework tailored to your organization with the right rigour to ensure that it is always kept up to date with the emerging risks that is pertinent to your industry.

### **Corporate Advisory – M&A and Governance**

We provide strategic **corporate advisory services** to listed and private companies, with a focus on **mergers and acquisitions, board governance, and regulatory compliance**. Our work includes advising on deal structuring, preparing board and shareholder documentation, and ensuring compliance with SGX and regulatory requirements. We also support boards in strengthening governance practices through **director independence reviews, risk management frameworks, and corporate governance code compliance**, enabling companies to achieve sustainable growth while upholding transparency and accountability.

### **Due Diligence Services**

We provide independent and comprehensive **due diligence services** to support mergers and acquisitions, investments, partnerships, and regulatory compliance. Our work covers **financial, tax, legal, operational, and commercial due diligence**, helping clients identify risks, uncover hidden liabilities, validate value drivers, and make informed decisions. We also conduct **integrity and background checks** on counterparties to mitigate reputational and compliance risks. By combining rigorous analysis with practical business insights, we ensure our clients have the clarity and confidence needed to negotiate, structure, and execute transactions successfully.

### **Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Advisory Services**

We help businesses in Singapore navigate their obligations under the **Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)** by providing practical, compliant, and business-friendly advisory solutions. Our services include **PDPA compliance reviews, drafting of data protection policies, employee training, gap assessments, and advisory on data retention, cross-border transfers, and consent management**. We also assist companies in responding to **PDPC investigations, data breach incidents, and customer complaints**, ensuring that personal data is handled in line with regulatory requirements. By balancing compliance with operational needs, we enable organisations to protect customer trust while minimising regulatory risk.
