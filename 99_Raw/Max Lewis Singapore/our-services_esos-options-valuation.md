##### What is ESOP ?

*   ESOP is an plan devised to award options to the employees of the company if they fulfil certain vesting criteria.
*   Once the employees are awarded the options, they can choose to exercise the options into shares of the company, effectively becoming a shareholder of the company just like the founders who started the company, except that they are the minority shareholders.

##### Pros and Cons of having a ESOP

**Pros**:

*   Attract or retain talent or competent employees instead of giving cash compensation.
*   Reward long serving employees for their past loyalty.
*   Allow the employees to exit with a windfall gain when the company goes into listing or sold off.

**Cons:**

*   The shares obtained from the exercise of the share options is illiquid and lacks marketability because the company is a private limited whose shares are not listed for buy or sell on the stock exchange.
*   As minority shareholders, they can make trouble if they are vocal.
*   Run into legal issues with bad leavers if the ESOP is not structured to protect the company’s interest.
