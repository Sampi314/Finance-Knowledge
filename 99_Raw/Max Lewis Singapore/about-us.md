**Established in 2007, Max Lewis Consultants Pte Ltd** is a professional firm specialising in fair valuation of businesses, assets, equity, debt, complex financial instruments, [employee share options](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/our-services/derivatives-valuation/ "Singapore Employee Shares Option Plan, Singapore Employee Shares Option Services")
 in addition to valuing [intangible assets](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/our-services/intangible-assets-valuation/ "Intangible Asset Valuation Services, Intangible Asset Valuation Singapore")
 and intellectual property. We also specialise in [transfer pricing advisory](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/our-services/transfer-pricing/ "Transfer Pricing Advisory Singapore")
, enterprise risk, fraud risk management services, incorporation, company secretarial, tax advisory work like local, international tax planning, [GST ASK](https://www.maxlewis.com.sg/our-services/gst-ask/ "GST ASK Review, GST ASK")
 review.

We are an ACCA approved employer and an Accredited Training Office (ATO) of the ICAEW (UK).

Our focus and belief are on partnering clients for future sustainable success, besides continually improving and providing the best professional service because what matters to our clients matter to us. We are committed to a long-term business relationship as your trusted professional adviser.

### Mr Albert Tan Tiong Heng 

_Managing Director and CEO_

Mr. Albert Tan has near 30 years of finance, M&A, tax, secretarial, legal experience, where he was CFO for both private and public companies across diverse industries. He started from Price Waterhouse, one of the Big Four International Firms and last left Deloitte as Audit Senior.

He has led in pre-IPO capital raising, debt restructuring, and transactions relating to business valuation, tax restructuring, cross border tax planning and statutory compliance, mergers and acquisitions, due diligence and financial reporting under US GAAP, IFRS and SFRS. His clients include SMEs, MNCs, government agencies, public listed companies across diverse industries both locally in Singapore, ASEAN as well as China, Hong Kong, Australia, Europe and USA.

Mr Albert Tan is a Fellow of the Institute of Singapore Chartered Accountants, Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (UK), Fellow of the Certified Practising Accountant of CPA Australia, Fellow of Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (UK), an Associate Chartered Accountant (ACA) of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales (ICAEW) and Chartered Accountants Australia and New Zealand (CA ANZ), ASEAN Chartered Professional Accountant, Chartered Tax Adviser (Australia), Accredited Tax Advisor (Income Tax & GST), Senior Accredited Director of the Singapore Institute of Directors, International Certified Valuation Specialist (ICVS) of the International Association of Certified Valuation Specialists (IACVS), Certified Valuation Analyst (CVA) of the [National Association of Certified Valuators and Analysts](https://www.nacva.com/directory.asp?version=1)
 (NACVA), Certified Business Valuer of the Australian Valuers Institute (AVI), Certified Patent Valuation Analyst, CA Business Valuation Specialist, CA ANZ, Fellow of the Society of Share and Business Valuers (UK), Accredited Valuation Institute Analyst, Business Valuation Institute UK (BVIUK), Accredited Member (AM) of the American Society of Appraisers (ASA), member of the Business Valuers Association Malaysia (BVAM) and [Chartered Valuer and Appraiser (CVA) charter holder of the Institute of Valuers and Appraisers](https://www.acra.gov.sg/accountancy/professional-development/chartered-valuer-and-appraiser-programme/list-of-charter-holders)
, Singapore (IVAS). He is an Individual member of the [Asia Pacific Institute of Experts](https://apiex.org/2015-07-17-09-48-11/userslist/1-member-listing?name=tan+tiong+heng)
. Besides this, he also obtained the International M&A Expert and Certified Post Merger Integration Expert credentials from the Institute for Mergers, Acquisitions and Alliances (IMAA) GmbH. He holds a Masters of International Taxation, New South Wales University, MBA (Finance), Leicester University and Bachelor of Laws (Honours) from the University of London.

Currently, Albert sits on the [Board of Advisors, NEBB Institute](https://lnkd.in/gKbvcTGQ)
, USA and was previously the Director, ASA International Virtual Chapter Board from 2021 to 2024. He is a member of the Asia Transfer Pricing subcommittee, Singapore Chapter, TEI. He obtained his Diploma in Transfer Pricing from the Association of Taxation Technicians (UK) and IBFD Advanced Professional Certificate in Transfer Pricing I.
