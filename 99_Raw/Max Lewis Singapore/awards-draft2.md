Featured in

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20width='300'%20height='61'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%2061'%3E%3C/svg%3E "The-Business-TimesLogo")

Friday, 30 June 2023

Max Lewis’ Managing Director, Mr. Albert Tan, was approached by The Business Times to share his insights on the new guidelines for intangible asset valuation in Singapore. In the article titled “Intangible asset valuation guidelines will bring clarity: analysts,” Mr. Tan emphasised the importance of clear guidance in approaching IA valuations in Singapore. He highlighted the differences between the US and Singapore contexts and stressed the need for guidelines suitable for the local market. Mr. Tan also emphasised the significance of professional standards in regulating valuation practices and providing proper advice to local entrepreneurs. His expertise contributed valuable perspectives on the subject and shed light on the benefits these guidelines will bring to businesses and investors in Singapore.
