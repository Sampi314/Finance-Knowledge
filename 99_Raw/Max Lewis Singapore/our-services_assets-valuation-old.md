### What are the types of assets required valuation

Assets range from metalworking, plastics, construction, chemical, woodworking, textile, food processing, printing, rubber, steel mill and foundry machinery and equipment. Some included shipping vessels, furnitures, overhead cranes, forklifts and specialised mechanical equipment.

### Purpose of asset valuation

It can be for auction, court assessment in writ of seizure, or required by statutory auditors for financial reporting purposes, or in a case where the assets required valuation to pledge to banks as collateral loans or disposal purposes. Typically, assets are valued for purposes of auction them after obtaining a Court order of seizure. Or it could be valuing for insurance claim’s purpose. Sometimes, there are F&B players looking to sell or purchase such items for starting a business or disposing of such business.

### Why do you need a qualified appraiser for assets valuation ?

A CMEA’s Certified Machinery and Equipment Appraisal is defensible, substantiated, and will withstand scrutiny! That’s why a CMEA is called upon by business owners, lenders, CPAs, attorneys, courts, adjusters and others for a qualified and substantiated CERTIFIED appraisal of their machinery and equipment.

Here, we can assist you with the best method of valuation to fit your appraisal needs. You can trust us with our professional evaluation of your requirements whether it is a single equipment/machine or a complex structure covering multiple plant locations.
