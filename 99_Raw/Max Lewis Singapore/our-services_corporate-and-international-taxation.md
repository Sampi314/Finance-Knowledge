**Pillar Two (Global Minimum Tax) Advisory & Compliance**
---------------------------------------------------------

We support multinational groups in addressing their obligations under the OECD Pillar Two (BEPS 2.0) framework, including Singapore-specific requirements. Our services cover the review of group-level GloBE computations, assessment of Undertaxed Profits Rule (UTPR) implications, and preparation of local filings. We help clients translate complex global tax rules into practical, defensible outcomes—ensuring compliance while managing potential top-up tax exposure.

Why It Matters
==============

Pillar Two introduces a **minimum 15% global tax regime** that can significantly impact cross-border structures, incentives, and effective tax rates. Early preparation and robust analysis are critical to avoid unexpected exposures and ensure compliance.
