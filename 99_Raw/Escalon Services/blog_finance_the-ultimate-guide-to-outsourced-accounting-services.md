Benefits of Outsourcing Accounting Bookkeeping Services
-------------------------------------------------------

  
Outsourcing your [accounting and bookkeeping services](https://escalon.services/blog/a-beginners-guide-how-to-choose-the-best-outsourced-accounting-service-provider/)
 provides you access to a team of highly qualified and trained professionals who use the latest technology and tools to ensure your data is accurate and secure. Outsourcing accounting can be a critical asset for forecasting and budgeting through every stage of your business. It can make your business more profitable in the long run by reducing overhead costs and improving your cash flow.

Accounting services can help small businesses find new benefits and strengths while ensuring they complete the revenue-generating responsibilities like sales, marketing, and partnerships necessary to keep your company growing. In addition, consider outsourcing your accounts receivable services to ensure that companies pay you on time. The following are the four significant benefits that you can discover in the long run after you start outsourcing your accounting services.

###      1. Solve Business Financial issues

  
Your outsourced accounting team can provide timely advice to your organization to solve cash flow problems and avoid future issues. They can identify areas damaging your income and suggest strategic plans for your business growth that will keep money coming in. They can keep your financial records up-to-date and accurate while helping you eliminate errors, mistakes, omissions, and oversights in your financial documents. They’ll also be able to address responsibilities you may have put on the back burner but need to bring forward, such as following up on invoices you’ve sent and collecting money owed.

###       2. Track and Improve Cash Flow

  
Outsourcing accounting, bookkeeping, and tax services can provide your company with solutions to improve overall cash flow and deliver effective cost-control measures. The outsourced team can even issue a precise and thorough cash flow analysis and oversee your business. This analysis will help you and your investors review the financial health of your business.

### Cost of Accounting Services

  
The cost of outsourced accounting services can vary depending on the size of the company, the number of transactions flowing through your accounts monthly, the number of employees, and overall engagement level. The types of services needed and the background and experience of the professionals will also impact the monthly pricing. The price of accounting services can differ from a one-time consultation fee to short-term problem-solving to a full-time service. According to a recent survey by the National Society of Accountants (NSA) for the years 2020-2021, fees charged on average for business needs can be broken down into several categories:

55% of gross income goes to tax return preparation

15% goes to write-ups

6.8% goes to payroll

3.2% goes to Bookkeeping and QuickBooks

1.9% of financial statement presentations

5.2% from other services

You should evaluate its pricing structure before working with an outsourced accounting firm. Do they bill you based on hourly charges, or is there a flat monthly fee? Can you expand your utilization of their services as your business grows, or will they only allocate a particular number of resources to your company?

### How to Outsource Accounting Services

  
You can outsource to a company that fits your business’ accounting needs by following the steps below.

**Step 1:** Check the credentials of the team members to ensure that:

*   They have appropriate certifications and licenses.
*   They are up to date on all laws and compliance issues.
*   They have handled the accounting for similar businesses in the past.

**Step 2:** Confirm that you’ll have a fixed point of contact (POC) for every need and 24/7 access to your records.

**Step 3:** Ensure the accounting firm has strategies to protect and keep all of your data safe. They should provide a secure way to upload and download documents and send encrypted or protected emails to one another.

**Step 4:** Check if they will allow you to review your documents whenever you want. They should also provide accurate analytics and insights to make informed decisions for your business.

**Step 5:** Ask the company for references and contact their previous clients for feedback.

**Step 6:** Ask the most important questions to you as the business owner, such as their usual turnaround time and whether they ever failed to reach their goal on time.

**Step 7:** Get price estimates from several different organizations and compare the services they offer with the prices they quote you. Use this data to make an informed decision about which option will work best for you.

**Step 8:** You can ask the firm to sign a non-disclosure agreement for peace of mind.

**Why Use Outsourced Accounting Services**
------------------------------------------

  
Check the following ten reasons why you should consider outsourcing your accounting functions.

###      1. Significant Cost Savings

  
By outsourcing, you can save on paying employees’ salaries and benefits and the cost of hiring and training employees. You can also eliminate expenses for hardware, software, and office supplies while keeping up with technological advances and upgrades.

When you outsource, you’ll have access to expert accountants, finance professionals, and bookkeepers at a much lower cost. You can also save on infrastructure fees, employment taxes, and other overhead expenses since experts will be employed by your outsourcing firm rather than directly by your company.

###      2. Access to the Latest Technology

  
An outsourced accounting firm invests in cutting-edge technology and software to be competitive. With an outsourcing partner, you can benefit from having access to this latest technology at no additional cost.

###      3. Manage Operations in Down Economic Times

  
As economic situations change, companies shift their workforces, which can be challenging and resource-intensive. Since the outsourcing company is responsible for managing the talent assigned to a project (and paying those team members), you can quickly scale up or down in terms of staffing.

###      4. Flexibility and Scalability

  
As your business grows and expands, having the right software and expertise becomes critical to meeting your accounting needs. Therefore, it’s best to outsource your accounting services to a firm that can scale with your business.

Outsourcing accounting services enables you to scale up or down the provided resources according to your growing business needs. With outsourcing, you only pay for the time and services you need, and you can shift that when your business grows.

###      5. Boost Compliance with the Latest Rules and Regulations

  
A specialized accounting outsourcing company can help you ensure compliance with changing financial and tax regulations regarding city, state, and federal laws. It can help you avoid tax penalties and maximize tax benefits by complying with present federal, state, and local laws. Thus, you’ll always be on top of the new tax or accounting requirements and potential changes.

###      6. Avoid Mistakes

  
Your outsourced accounting team can help you avoid accounting mistakes and government penalties. With the help of these experts, it’s easy to solve even the most challenging issues of cash flow management. Your team of accounting experts will accurately check your books and transaction reports. Thus, they lower the risk of having your books manipulated.

###      7. Enhance Data Security and Confidentiality

  
The team of experts handling your accounting responsibilities can protect your information and keep it safe and confidential. They can implement measures to prevent data security threats with impenetrable security systems. Thus, outsourcing helps you mitigate the risks of a data breach, compromised security program, or internal fraud.

###      8. Provide 24/7 Access to Your Records

  
When you outsource your accounting services to a third party, you can operate 24/7 with a time-zone advantage and access your data anytime. You can be sure that essential tasks will be taken care of promptly.

###      9. Save Time

  
Outsourcing accounting services can free up valuable time for managing and growing your business. As the outsourced company handles your accounting functions, you can focus on value-added, revenue-generating tasks, such as building business strategies, making financial projections, bringing in new customers, and developing new products.

###      10. Accurate Financial Reporting

  
When a company goes public, the requirement for accurate financial reporting fuels the need for quality accounting services. Your outsourced team can provide powerful analytics to help you understand budgeting so you can always make informed decisions. They can also provide you with audit support and appropriate records and assist you with added financial reporting as you grow through the years.
