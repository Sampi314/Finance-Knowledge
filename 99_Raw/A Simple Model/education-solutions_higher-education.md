Professor Sign Up Form

Free Access for University Professors
=====================================

The ASM+ platform is available free of charge for professors that would like to introduce students to financial modeling.

With a “professor” account the entire class can be uploaded at no expense to the student or university. It also permits tracking the student’s progress as they work through the associated quizzes.

THE “LINK TO UNIVERSITY BIO” MUST LINK DIRECTLY TO THE PROFESSOR FOR APPROVAL. THIS SERVICE IS NOT AVAILABLE TO STUDENTS.

If you are a student seeking to gain access for a student-led organization, please reach out through the contact page.
