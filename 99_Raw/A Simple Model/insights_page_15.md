### [Representations and Warranties](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/representations-and-warranties)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Reps-and-Warranties-Image-TinyPNG-1.png)

Think of the representations in this section as promises that certain statements about the company are true, and think of the warranties as a promise to protect the buyer in the event that these statements are not true. In the event that a representation made is inaccurate, the buyer knows that they can be made whole for the associated loss (we will cover this in detail under Indemnification).

 

[Continue Reading](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/representations-and-warranties)

### [The Working Capital Adjustment](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/the-working-capital-adjustment)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Working-Capital-Adjustment_Purchase-Price_Ink_TinyPNG.png)

The working capital adjustment can have a direct impact on the price paid for the business. Given that price is arguably the most important variable in a transaction, and that the working capital adjustment can impact price, it follows that the working capital adjustment deserves special attention.

 

[Continue Reading](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/the-working-capital-adjustment)

### [The Stock Purchase Agreement](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/the-stock-purchase-agreement)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/SPA-Intro-SINGLE-DROP_Image_SPA-Series_Youtube_1920_TinyPNG.png)

A stock purchase agreement is the primary transaction document for a stock acquisition. The purpose of the stock purchase agreement is to confirm the price paid for the securities sought, to control risk to the degree possible and to provide a roadmap for the hold period.

 

[Continue Reading](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/the-stock-purchase-agreement)
