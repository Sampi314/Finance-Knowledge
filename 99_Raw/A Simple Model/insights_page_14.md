### [Indemnification](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/indemnification)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Indemnification-Image-TinyPNG.png)

An indemnity is simply protection against loss. Indemnification protects a party from losses due to broken “promises” (i.e., breaches of representations and warranties or covenants), and requires that the breaching party make the other party whole for losses realized.
