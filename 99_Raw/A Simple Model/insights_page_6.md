*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/mary-reed)

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Private-Credit-Fund-Day-to-Day-Life.png)

If you are thinking about a career in private credit, you have good timing. The field is booming right now, with average entry-level compensation exceeding that of private equity. According to this [report](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/page/6#mailSentPopup)
 from human resources firm Selby Jennings, associates at private credit funds are earning $250,000-$350,000 annually in total compensation, compared to $180,000-$280,000 for similar positions at middle-market PE funds.
