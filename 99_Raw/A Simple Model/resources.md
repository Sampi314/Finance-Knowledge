[Stock Purchase Agreement](https://www.asimplemodel.com/resources/stock-purchase-agreement)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If there is anything I wish I had been exposed to earlier in my career it is this document: the stock purchase agreement. A stock purchase agreement is the primary transaction document for a stock acquisition. But the negotiation that surrounds this document is typically a guarded process, and few resources exist to get someone up to speed quickly.

For this reason we are excited to start introducing this topic at ASimpleModel.com as part of our content creation initiative with Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP. Please click on the links that follow for more information.
