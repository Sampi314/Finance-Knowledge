### [Introduction to Independent Sponsors (aka Fundless Sponsors)](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/introduction-to-independent-sponsors-aka-fundless-sponsors)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Indpendent-Sponsor-Structure_YT-VIDEO_TinyPNG.png)

**What is an independent sponsor?** In contrast to a private equity fund, an independent sponsor sets out to source an acquisition target (business for sale), and then raises the funds to complete the acquisition. This can be achieved by an individual or a team of people working to raise capital on a deal-by-deal basis.

 

[Continue Reading](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/introduction-to-independent-sponsors-aka-fundless-sponsors)
