### [How Independent Sponsors Make Money](https://www.asimplemodel.com/insights/how-independent-sponsors-make-money)

*   [](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    [Peter Lynch](https://www.asimplemodel.com/contributors/peter-lynch)
    

![](https://www.asimplemodel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/Independent-Sponsor-Economics.png)

How and when do independent sponsors get compensated for their work? There are three broad forms of compensation: (1) a closing fee, (2) the management fee, (3) and carried interest. The latter is the one that gets private equity professionals—independent or not—the most excited. But since it only comes at the end of the process, sponsors need to be compensated somehow in the interim. That’s why all three fees are so critical.
