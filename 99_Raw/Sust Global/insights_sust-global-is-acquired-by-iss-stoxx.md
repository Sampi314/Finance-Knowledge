Sust Global is acquired by ISS Stoxx
------------------------------------

![](https://www.sustglobal.com/img//assets/jg_ge-headshot-(1).jpg?w=100&fit=max&blur=50)

#### A note from the founders:

  
We’re excited to announce that Sust Global has been acquired by ISS Sustainability Solutions, the sustainable investment business of ISS STOXX and the leading provider of investment research and technology solutions. 

This marks an exciting new chapter in our mission to deliver transformative geospatial intelligence across capital markets. 

Through this acquisition, we will rapidly scale our solutions, enabling more global investors to understand and respond to tangible asset risks, by harnessing the latest in earth system understanding, data science and AI.

Our cloud-native APIs and analytics dashboards have revolutionized how investors can rapidly assess, understand and act on geospatial, financial, climate, and nature-related risk data across every major asset class. 

In 2020, we identified a clear gap between climate risk expertise and the workflows used for sustainability reporting and quantitative finance. Integrating climate-risk metrics into day-to-day operations across varied functions in capital markets was inconsistent and often complex.

Sust Global closes this gap. Our products deliver high-resolution, globally consistent analytics on climate hazards and financial materiality across multiple scenarios. Through our APIs, our partners embed climate-risk factors directly into their products and services. Through our dashboard, our customers can monitor exposure hotspots, analyze portfolio-level risk, and export detailed visuals and reports, all in one place.

Over the last five years, we’ve moved the climate risk modeling industry forwards, through our industry-leading geospatial AI platform. Our product and research innovations have been presented at conferences including ICLR, AAAI, NeurIPS and Google Cloud Next.

We’ve built a global customer base across blue chip investors and financial institutions, and developed partnerships with leading quantitative research teams and advisory businesses. 

As we look towards the future, together with ISS STOXX we will accelerate our product roadmap and expand our global footprint, bringing our leading geospatial risk products to institutional investors worldwide. We remain committed to our mission of empowering the financial sector to build more resilient portfolios in the face of accelerating climate change.

We express our deepest gratitude to our customers, partners, and team members who have been instrumental in our journey. 

For our existing customers, you will continue to receive the same exceptional service and support, now backed by ISS STOXX's extensive resources and global reach.

We’re now positioned to make an even greater impact on how the world's leading investors understand, quantify and manage emerging real asset risks.

We couldn't be more excited about what lies ahead. 

**About Sust Global**

Sust Global is a climate risk intelligence company that leverages geospatial AI to help investors and businesses understand physical climate risks across their portfolios. Founded in 2020, the company has pioneered the use of advanced machine learning and satellite data to deliver actionable insights on climate-related risks to real assets.

**About ISS STOXX**

ISS STOXX is a leading provider of investment research, data, and technology solutions, serving institutional investors worldwide. ISS Sustainability Solutions, the sustainable investment business of ISS STOXX, helps investors integrate ESG considerations into their investment processes.
