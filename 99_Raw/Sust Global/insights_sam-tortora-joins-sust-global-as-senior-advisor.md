Sam Tortora Joins Sust Global as Senior Advisor
-----------------------------------------------

![](https://www.sustglobal.com/img//assets/screenshot-2024-11-20-at-12.05.06-1732110414.png?w=100&fit=max&blur=50)

Check out [our new video](https://youtu.be/2DfmrOfluhg)
 where Sust Global’s new Senior Advisor Sam Tortora talks about her vision and why she joined the team.

[**_Play video_**](https://youtu.be/2DfmrOfluhg)

Sam joins Sust Global as Senior Advisor after 13 years at BlackRock leading Corporate Investor Relations, Corporate Sustainability and the buildout of the Aladdin Sustainability Platform.

Her energy, expertise in financial markets and sustainability have been significant contributors to Sust Global's recent success in delivering transformational geospatial analytics to institutional investors.

Please join us in welcoming her to the team!
