Sust Global Joins NVIDIA Inception
----------------------------------

![](https://www.sustglobal.com/img//assets/nvidia-inception-program-badge-cmyk-for-print.jpg?w=100&fit=max&blur=50)

Sust Global has joined [NVIDIA Inception](https://nvda.ws/2BvtUc9)
, a program that nurtures new ventures revolutionizing industries with AI-led technological advancements.

[Sust Global](https://www.sustglobal.com/)
 is a Geospatial AI company building models, middleware and data ontologies that enable analytical teams to draw  spatial inference that links factors across financial, environmental and population dynamics. Today, institutional investors and global asset managers leverage our geospatial AI platform to make decisions on equities, global real estate and energy infrastructure investments by modeling the financial materiality of climate hazards such as wildfire, floods, cyclones and heat stress and insurability risk utilizing AI models fine tuned with satellite observations and population dynamics.

Joining NVIDIA Inception will help Sust Global to scale our offerings to institutional investors and the financial services industry. The program will also offer Sust Global the opportunity to collaborate with industry-leading experts and other AI-driven organizations.

“Nvidia’s pioneering AI modeling and geospatial processing libraries such as Modulus and RAPIDS will provide foundational capabilities for us to design, scale and deploy our next generation geospatial AI powered innovations. Inference on multimodal datasets using complex geospatial foundation models optimized for scale on Nvidia hardware has the potential to amplify the impact of our solutions across the financial services ecosystem,” says Gopal Erinjippurath, cofounder and CTO at Sust Global. 

Sust Global will be featured at NVIDIA Inception sessions at GTC2025, the world’s leading AI event bringing together developers, researchers, and business leaders, taking place from March 17-21. NVIDIA Inception helps startups during critical stages of product development, prototyping and deployment. Every Inception member gets a custom set of ongoing benefits, such as [NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/deep-learning-ai/education/?ncid=em-ded-n1-96486)
 credits, preferred pricing on NVIDIA hardware and software, and technological assistance, which provides startups with the fundamental tools to help them grow.
