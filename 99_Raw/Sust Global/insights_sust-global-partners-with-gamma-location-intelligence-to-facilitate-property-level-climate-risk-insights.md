Sust Global partners with Gamma Location Intelligence to facilitate property level climate risk insights
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sust Global will provide [Gamma Location Intelligence (LI)](https://gamma.ie/)
, a leading provider of location intelligence and risk assessment technologies, with actionable, global climate insights through our latest data partnership.

The partnership marks the first time Sust Global's data has been available on an underwriting platform, with Gamma LI's clients including financial organizations such as banks, lenders, insurance providers and assessment management companies, along with businesses with multiple property assets, such as real estate and private equity firms.

As well as significantly expanding Gamma LI's international coverage, the partnership will mean they can offer their clients new datasets, consisting of billions of data points. This will include visualizations of forward-looking global climate risk exposures, including wildfire, floods and cyclones, and will support property-level assessments for current and future climate risk.
