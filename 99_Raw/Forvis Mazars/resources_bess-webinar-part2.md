Welcome to our follow-up webinar, building on the success of our first session “**BESS financial models – modelling approaches and common errors**.” In this continuation, we aim to address the questions raised during our initial webinar and delve deeper into the complexities of financial modelling for BESS including debt notional life vs BESS asset life, technological obsolesce risk and BESS projects bankability.

  
Our panel of market experts will represent both the debt and equity sides, sharing their invaluable experiences and insights. Speakers include Ben Kwan, Hitansh Doda and Shifali Aggarwal from Forvis Mazars and featuring Jean-Marie Verrier from Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation SMBC Group. They will provide detailed answers to your queries, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter

**The learning objectives in this webinar:**

*   In-depth Understanding of Debt and Equity in BESS Financial Modelling: Gain insights from market experts on how debt and equity are represented and managed in BESS financial models.
*   Answers to Key Questions from the First Webinar: Receive detailed explanations and solutions to the questions and challenges raised during the initial webinar around battery cost in recent years, augmentation capex and breakeven debt sizing.
*   Real-world Experiences and Case Studies: Hear from experts as they share their experiences and case studies, providing practical examples and lessons learned in the field of BESS financial modelling.
