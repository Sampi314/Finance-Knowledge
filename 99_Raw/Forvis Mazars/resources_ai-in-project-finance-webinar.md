Over recent months, the market has seen a rapid acceleration in the development of AI-driven tools for Excel and financial modelling. The underlying technology is advancing quickly, yet adoption across organisations remains uneven.

This webinar will outline how AI is reshaping workflows in project finance and infrastructure finance modelling, drawing on insights from industry discussions, pilots and capability‑building sessions.

**Learning points:**

*   Classify AI use cases across core modelling activities, including model build, document processing, project comparison, integrity review, version control, credit/investment paper preparation, etc
*   Gain insights in how AI tools benefit everyone working with financial models, not just the person developing them from scratch
*   Identify the tools, platforms and solutions gaining traction as preferred options within the sector.
*   Understand the key barriers preventing individuals and organisations from scaling AI adoption in financial modelling.
*   Review practical examples highlighting efficiency gains and constraints across both simple and complex modelling workflows.

While many applications are now available or in development, this session will focus primarily on Copilot as the platform with the broadest accessibility for the project finance community, driven largely by enterprise IT and data‑security requirements.
