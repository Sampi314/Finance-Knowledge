[Forvis Mazars Financial Modelling](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/)

Financial Model Audit

Forvis Mazars’ market-leading **financial model audit team** is the world’s largest dedicated team of model audit professionals, with over 70 staff located in London, New York, Sydney, Toronto, Delhi and Johannesburg. With a particular focus on the energy and infrastructure sector, we have been trusted by clients to provide opinions on more than 2000 projects.

We have been ranked as the world’s #1 model auditor for seven consecutive years (_Inframation, deal count tables)_ and have been recognised as model auditor of the year in each of the Europe and Africa, Middle East, LatAm and North American regions by IJ Global.

Our specialist financial model review skills combined with the local expertise of the wider Forvis Mazars team (44,000 staff across over 90 countries) allow us to provide the tailored expertise and informed opinions needed to support critical investment decisions wherever your project is located.
