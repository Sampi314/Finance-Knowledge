Mazars expands its Canadian presence with new Toronto office and launch of the Infrastructure Finance business unit
===================================================================================================================

By [Rickard Wärnelid](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/author/rickard-warnelid/ "Posts by Rickard Wärnelid")

Monday 26th February 2018

We are pleased to announce the opening of our new office in Toronto and the launch of our Infrastructure Finance business unit.

The new Infrastructure Finance team is part of Mazars’ Global Infrastructure Finance group, providing financial advisory, modelling and training services to the infrastructure market.

### Our expansion across Canada and North America

The Toronto office, our second business location in Canada based outside Montréal, will allow us to be in closer proximity to our clients located in the region, expand the national scope of our services in assurance, accounting, tax and advisory as well as reinforce the Global Infrastructure Finance team’s position as a major advisor to clients in North America. Mr. John Casola is appointed Head of the Infrastructure Finance business unit for the Americas and will be joining the Toronto office.

Serge Principe, Managing Partner of Mazars in Canada, said: “We are confident that Mr. Casola’s experience and expertise as well as our presence in the region will greatly contribute to the continuity of our growth and will allow us to further develop the marketplace. Subsequently, other experts and professionals with complementary expertise will join our Toronto office.”

Learn more about Mazars in Canada and Mazars Global Infrastructure Finance [https://eng.mazars.ca/Home/News/Latest-news/Opening-of-our-new-office-in-Toronto](https://eng.mazars.ca/Home/News/Latest-news/Opening-of-our-new-office-in-Toronto)
