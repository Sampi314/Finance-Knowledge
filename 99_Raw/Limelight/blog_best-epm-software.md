Key Takeaways
-------------

*   Choosing the right EPM software ensures smooth integration, real-time insights, and streamlined financial planning.
*   Limelight stands out as a **no-code, user-friendly platform** with strong ERP/CRM integrations and flexible modeling.
*   Tools like **Anaplan and Oracle** suit large enterprises but require more technical expertise and longer implementation times.
*   **Excel-based tools** like Vena and Datarails are easier to adopt but have limited scalability.
*   Pricing varies significantly — Limelight starts at **$1,400/month**, while others like Oracle and OneStream require custom quotes.
*   Prioritize scalability, ease of use, and real-time reporting when selecting an EPM solution.  
      
    

Choosing the wrong [enterprise performance management](https://www.golimelight.com/blog/what-is-enterprise-performance-management)
 (EPM) software means your team will spend months implementing a system, only to discover it doesn’t fully integrate with your current tools.

Data will not sync properly, workflows will be disrupted, and employees will have a complex, unintuitive interface. Instead of improving performance, you will be stuck in delays and troubleshooting.

Choosing the right EPM software from the start can easily avoid this costly mistake. The right solution will not only integrate smoothly into your existing systems but also deliver real-time insights, streamline operations, and support long-term growth.

To ensure you make the right choice, look for EPM software with these key features:

*   Syncs effortlessly with your current ERP, CRM, and other business systems
*   Offers an intuitive design that makes adoption easy for all team members
*   Provides up-to-date reporting for quicker, data-driven decision-making
*   Grows with your business and adapts to evolving needs
*   Includes features for budgeting, forecasting, and scenario analysis

In this article, we will look at the best enterprise performance management software to help you choose the right solution for your business.

3\. Jedox
---------

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXci6GlYzDJBD8cH7R9y9TmkHxgRk4qAeOaLvPYvX0eWiZXgOkl7VVJj-btH89xTs3pa_EsJFxMVVCX5JnK-CcKJmXL0St04ESBR5_AR6zxMVz8KPsgBon-5rXW3uRgdf8wa38QzldyR7-v9yUHIMuCQpeD6?key=Yl2gz-h5ToSP069dBPhriw)

[Jedox](https://www.jedox.com/en/)
 offers a hybrid solution, combining the familiarity of an Excel-like interface with advanced EPM capabilities for budgeting, planning, and forecasting. 

Jedox’s ability to integrate with existing data structures appeals to companies that prefer working in Excel but need more powerful tools for automation and analytics. It is highly customizable, allowing them to tailor it to their specific needs. 

However, this customization is complex and requires technical expertise to fully optimize the solution. For users comfortable with Excel but seeking an EPM upgrade, Jedox offers a smooth transition to more advanced functionalities.  

#### Jedox Key Features

*   Automates financial planning and analysis processes with pre-configured templates and seamless data integration
*   Leverages pre-built and customizable financial models for P&L, cost center, cash flow, and balance sheet management
*   Integrates existing financial models from Excel using Jedox's extensive connectors and ETL tool
*   Facilitates user adoption with a familiar Excel-like interface and an intuitive, drag-and-drop tool for customized dashboards
*   Guides implementation with step-by-step configuration and support from Jedox consultants to ensure a smooth rollout

#### Jedox Pros

*   Flexible with large datasets and easy to modify; enables you to connect Jedox with SQL databases and also make input files in Excel and upload it in Jedox 
*   Quick and effective customer service 

#### Jedox Cons

*   Frequent bugs and a complicated rule editor. Features like moving dimensions in reports are challenging and sometimes fail.  
*   Significant training is required for administrators and users to fully utilize the platform, which is time-consuming and resource-intensive 

#### Jedox Pricing

Jedox offers four main subscription plans:

*   **Essential**: Basic features for small businesses
*   **Business**: Includes Jedox Basic Models and allows for additional user types (Full Users, Planners, Viewers). Optional add-ons include premium models, ERP/CRM connectors, and performance upgrades.
*   **Professional**: Adds premium features such as advanced security, test systems, and Jedox AIssisted Planning and Forecasting
*   **Performance**: Designed for large-scale deployments with high performance for complex planning models and many concurrent users
