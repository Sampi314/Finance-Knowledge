 that enables [SaaS](https://www.golimelight.com/saas)

This article explores the 16 finest Anaplan alternatives and highlights their features, pricing, and user reviews to inform your choice.

### 1\. Steep Learning Curve

The complex user experience of Anaplan hinders the implementation of company-wide financial solutions. Navigating this tool requires new users to undergo training and steady support, which is time-consuming and sometimes incurs extra expenses for the organization. 

### 2\. Limited reporting capabilities 

Most users sometimes need help formatting or customizing their reports. Aside from downloading as PDFs, other formatting options are not available.

### 3\. Negative feedback from users; low NPS score

Most users have expressed dissatisfaction with the platform's usability, performance, and customer support. As per [Comparably data](https://www.comparably.com/brands/anaplan)
, Anaplan’s NPS is 11, with 46% promoters, 22% passives, and 32% detractors.
