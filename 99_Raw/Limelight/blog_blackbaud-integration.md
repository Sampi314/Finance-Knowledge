Limelight joins a network of companies providing application and service solutions that complement Blackbaud’s cloud offerings.  
  
  
**Toronto, ON**  – Limelight, a modern FP&A platform designed to save time, enhance data accuracy, and provide actionable insights for nonprofits, higher education, and mission-driven organizations, today announced that it has joined the Blackbaud Partner Network as a Blackbaud Partner. With this partnership, Limelight joins a network of companies that provide application and service solutions that extend Blackbaud’s cloud offerings in new ways.  
  
  
This partnership allows Limelight’s FP&A platform to leverage Blackbaud’s Financial Edge NXT, enabling Blackbaud customers to streamline financial planning, reporting, and analysis for grant, program, and project funding. With prebuilt templates tailored specifically for nonprofits, Limelight simplifies onboarding, connects data seamlessly, and provides enhanced visibility and control.  
  
  
Limelight has helped hundreds of nonprofits modernize their FP&A processes—replacing error-prone spreadsheets with automated, real-time planning tools. By working closely with mission-driven teams, Limelight has built a deep understanding of nonprofit financial needs and challenges. This experience is now being brought to the Blackbaud community, offering customers proven best practices, faster time to value, and a powerful toolset designed to improve collaboration, accuracy, and financial insight.  
  
  
“**Joining the Blackbaud Partner Network reinforces our commitment to empowering nonprofits and educational institutions with powerful, accessible FP&A solutions**,” said Inder Seth, CEO of Limelight. “Our integrated platform, combined with Financial Edge NXT will provide users the ability to quickly model, plan, and make informed strategic decisions, enhancing their overall impact and efficiency.”  
  
  
To learn more about Limelight’s FP&A Platform for Blackbaud Financial Edge NXT, visit: [https://www.golimelight.com/erp/blackbaud](https://www.golimelight.com/integrations/blackbaud)
  
  
  
The Blackbaud Partner Network is a group of leading technology and services companies providing social impact organizations with the applications, services, and strategies they need to propel their mission and make a difference in their local communities and worldwide. To learn more about Blackbaud’s Partner Network, visit: [https://www.blackbaud.com/partners](https://www.blackbaud.com/partners)
