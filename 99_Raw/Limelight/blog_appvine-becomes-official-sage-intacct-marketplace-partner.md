**March 13th, 2018 Toronto, Ontario** \- Limelight is proud to announce our new partnership with Sage Intacct to help companies improve their financial reporting, planning, and forecasting. As a Cloud-based ERP, Sage Intacct provides its clients with the ability to capture, oversee and manage its accounting data. With this new partnership, Sage Intacct clients can now easily use Limelight to expand the tools and capabilities of their Office of Finance and empower them to make stronger, data-backed decisions through Limelight’s integrated, automated, collaborative, and secure platform.

“We’re pleased to offer Limelight as a premium financial reporting, planning, and forecasting platform that seamlessly syncs with Sage Intacct,” said Inder Seth, CEO of Limelight. “For Sage Intacct clients looking to truly maximize the efficiency and effectiveness of their FP&A workflow, Limelight is an excellent solution that will allow their Finance teams to spend more time analyzing and drawing vital insights from their data.”

Sage Intacct is a leading ERP with over three million customers and the Limelight team looks forward to working with Sage clients to meet all their financial reporting, planning, and forecasting needs. “Cutting edge technologies like Limelight and Sage Intacct work well together because they are both top-of-the-line, “ said Jade Cole, CTO. “Together, Limelight and Sage Intacct will lead to many executive wins for our joint clients.”

Limelight is featured as a best-in-class finance application in the [Sage Intacct Marketplace](https://ca-marketplace.sage.com/en-ca/apps/84380/limelight)
. Find out more about [**Sage Intacct**](https://www.sageintacct.com/)
 and [Limelight](https://info.golimelight.com/schedule-a-demo)
 and how taking your FP&A to the next level can expand and grow your business.

For more information about this press release, please contact our marketing department at [marketing@golimelight.com](mailto:marketing@golimelight.com)
.
