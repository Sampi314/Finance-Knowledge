**Update: Please visit the following link for information regarding [IP Week 2013](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/ip-week-2013 "IP Week 2013")
.**

We're currently in London for IP Week 2012.  On Wednesday afternoon Mike Corley will be speaking about energy hedging [](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/energy-hedging-seminars/ "energy hedging  ")
and risk management as part of the IP Week's Investing in Energy program. His presentation will address how oil and gas producers can benefit from a dynamic hedging program.

If you're in London and interested in attending IP Week, you can obtain the full program, the agenda for the Investing in Energy session, as well as the registration form via the following links:

[IP Week 2012 Program](http://www.energyinst.org/events/ip-week/programme-of-events "IP Week Program")

[IP Week - Investing in Energy](http://www.energyinst.org/filegrab/?ref=1165&f=IP+Week+2012%3A+Wednesday+programme "IP Week - Investing in Energy Agenda")

[IP Week Registration](http://www.energyinst.org/events/ip-week/to-register "IP Week Registration")

If you're not attending IP Week but would like meet to with us while we're in London, please [contact us](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/contact/ "contact us")
 and we will do our best to arrange a mutually convenient meeting.
