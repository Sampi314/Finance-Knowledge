We're thrilled to announce that Energy Risk has awarded us [Hedging Advisory Firm of the Year](http://www.risk.net/energy-risk/analysis/2458051/hedging-advisory-firm-of-the-year-mercatus-energy-advisors)
.

Regarded as the benchmark awards for the global energy trading and risk management industry, the Energy Risk Awards were announced at an awards ceremony on May 17th in Houston.  

We sincerely appreciate the support of all of our clients - several of which have been with us since our inception - and partners who have helped ensure our success as well as the Energy Risk editorial team for recognizing us as the best energy hedging advisory firm across the globe.

As an aside, we’re all too aware that we haven’t been posting new blog posts on a regular basis over the past few months; however that will be changing for the better in the coming weeks. In addition, in the very near future we will also be introducing some new products and services which have been requested by numerous clients and industry colleagues. Stay tuned…
