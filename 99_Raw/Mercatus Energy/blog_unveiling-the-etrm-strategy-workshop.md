Unveiling The ETRM Strategy Workshop
====================================

[Energy](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/blog/topic/energy)

In this week's edition of [The Week in Energy Markets](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/newsletter)
, we publicly unveiled a service that we have been quietly providing to numerous clients over the past couple of years, the [ETRM strategy workshop](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/etrm-strategy-workshop)
.  
  
Our flagship workshop is designed to align your executive team around your most important opportunity or risk in the energy commodity markets. This is a facilitated two-day workshop where we work with you to properly define a specific opportunity or risk and translate it into a clear, compelling strategic plan that will provide you with the results that you need to meet your goals.  
  
The workshop has earned rave reviews from companies ranging from energy consumers to commodity traders to institutional investors

> ###### "The workshop provided us with a solid strategy and roadmap to manage our fuel price risk, a task that has been a major challenge for the company over the past several years. It's the best investment we've ever made when it comes to risk management." - CFO of commercial airline  
>   
> "The workshop was exactly what we needed to help us develop strategies to pursue challenging opportunities in several products and geographies that are new to us." - CEO of an energy marketing company  
>   
> "Our team has long struggled to help our portfolio companies better manage their commodity market exposures. The workshop gave us a clear path forward that is already providing excellent results  for all of our stakeholders." - Partner of a private equity firm

Do you have a smart, opinionated team that can't agree on how to manage a risk or pursue an opportunity? Does your strategy tell a different story than your results?  
  
Have you spent weeks or months trying to develop a clear, compelling answer to the question – “What’s the best way to address this?” This workshop is the answer.

If you want to learn how your team can benefit from an ETRM strategy workshop, please [get in touch](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/etrm-strategy-workshop)
 with us.
