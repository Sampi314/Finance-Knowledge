As our blog approaches its third anniversary, we want to know what aspects of energy hedging and risk management you want us to cover, and from what perspective, in the coming year. After all, if our blog isn't valuable to our subscribers, we're wasting your time.

Given that we have hundreds of loyal subscibers across the globe, we aim to address numerous aspects of hedging and risk management across the entire energy complex (bunker fuel, crude oil, diesel fuel, fuel oil, gasoil, gasoline, heating oil, jet fuel, natural gas, natural gas liquids, etc.) from the perspective of not only consumers, producers, marketers, processors and refiners but professional service providers (e.g. accountants, bankers, lawyers), investors, journalists and regulators as well.

That being said, if you have any opinions or suggestions regarding how we can make our blog more valuable, please let us know in the comment section below or via this [anonymous form](https://www.mercatusenergy.com/blog-feedback/ "anonymous form")
