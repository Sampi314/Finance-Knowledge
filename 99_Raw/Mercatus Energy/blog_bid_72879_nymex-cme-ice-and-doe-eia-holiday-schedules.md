For those of you who have hedging needs to take care of over holidays, the trading calendars for NYMEX-CME and ICE energy products as well as the DOE-EIA statistics for the next two weeks are as follows:

**NYMEX-CME**

Dec 23: Trading floor closes early at 13:30 ET. Globex (electronic trading) will close at regular times for all products except crude, heating oil, gasoline and natural gas TAS (trade at settlement) products which close at 13:30 ET.

Dec 25: Globex closed.

Dec 26: Trading floor closed. Globex opens at 18:00 ET. 

Dec 30: Trading floor and Globex both close at regular times. 

Jan 2: Traidng floor closed. Globex open at 18:00 ET.

**ICE**

Dec 23: Crude & refined products close early at 14:00 ET.  Physical next day US natural gas trades for Dec 24-27.

Dec 26: All trading closed.

Dec 27: Crude & refined products open regular hours.

Dec 29: Physical next day US natural gas trades for Dec 30-31.

Dec 30: Crude & refined products close at 18:00 ET. Physical next day US natural gas trades for Jan 1-3.

Jan 2: All trading closed.

**EIA**

EIA's report of crude oil, distillate and gasoline stocks will be delayed one day to Thursday, December 29, at 11 AM ET. The report is normally released on Wednesday at 10:30 AM ET.   

EIA's report of crude oil, distillate and gasoline stocks will be delayed one day to Thursday, January 5, at 11 AM. The report is normally released on Wednesday at 10:30 AM ET.   

EIA's natural gas stocks report will be released as usual on Thursday, December 29 at 10:30 AM ET.  

EIA's natural gas stocks report will be released as usual on Thursday, January 5 at 10:30 AM ET.
