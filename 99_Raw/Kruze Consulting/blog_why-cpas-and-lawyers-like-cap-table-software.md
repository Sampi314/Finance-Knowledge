Cap table software is quickly becoming a necessity for managing startup equity, not just for founders, but for accountants and attorneys as well. Here’s why the team at Kruze Consulting (and your legal team) strongly recommends upgrading from spreadsheets.

Why Lawyers Like Cap Table Software
-----------------------------------

*   **Automates Complex Equity Tracking**  
    Cap table software automates equity calculations and tracking, which significantly reduces errors and saves attorneys time when overseeing transactions and [share issuances](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/outstanding-shares-vs-authorized-shares/)
    .​
*   **Boosts Transparency**  
    Transparency is vital, especially in deals and [acquisitions](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/startup-acquisition-process/)
    . Lawyers appreciate how software generates clear, shareable reports so stakeholders instantly understand the company’s ownership breakdown.​
*   **Facilitates Compliance**  
    Managing equity isn’t just about keeping investors happy, it’s also about meeting [regulatory requirements](https://kruzeconsulting.com/should-my-w2-include-income-from-an-83b-election/)
    . Modern cap table platforms help ensure compliance and provide the audit trail lawyers need, which is especially important for future funding or M&A events.​
*   **Reduces Legal Complexity**  
    As startups grow, [equity structures](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/startup-accounting/eso/)
     get complicated. Cap table software tames this complexity, making legal review and due diligence much easier for everyone involved.​

Why CPAs Like Cap Table Software
--------------------------------

*   **Catches Costly Errors Early**  
    Easy [reconciliation](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/reconcile-cap-table/)
     helps CPAs ensure every share matches dollars received, catching mistakes like unfulfilled wire transfers or misplaced records before they become big problems during audits or exits.​
*   **Simplifies Tax Preparation**  
    CPAs depend on accurate, up-to-date ownership information for [tax filings](https://kruzeconsulting.com/startup-tax-returns/)
    . With [cap table access](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/startup-cap-table-cpa/)
    , accountants can quickly gather data required by tax authorities and streamline annual filings, including state franchise reports.​
*   **Enables Precise Share Count Calculations**  
    Many [tax forms](https://kruzeconsulting.com/tax_docs/Startup%20Tax%20Forms.pdf?_cchid=4674109ac69bf2b1a17be85e67e78990)
     – and compliance processes like [Delaware franchise tax](https://kruzeconsulting.com/blog/delaware-franchise-tax/)
     – require exact information on share counts and ownership percentages. Cap table software automates these calculations, reducing manual errors and compliance risk.​

Using cap table software benefits everyone: it saves time, ensures accurate records, meets compliance needs, and gives your team and advisors the clarity required for confident decision-making, both now and as your company scales.
