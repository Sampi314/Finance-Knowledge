Working Capital Metrics Cheat Sheet (DSO, DPO, DIO) + 3‑Statement Forecasts
===========================================================================

Share

Master working capital management using key turnover ratios and day conversions. Includes all formulas and 3-statement model examples you need.

1\. Accounts Receivable Turnover
--------------------------------

**What it measures:** Efficiency of collecting receivables.

**Formula:** Net Credit Sales / Average Accounts Receivable

**Learn more / Practice:**  
[📄 Accounts Receivable Turnover Formula & 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/accounts-receivable-turnover-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Accounts Receivable Turnover Concept & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/accounts-receivable-turnover)

2\. Accounts Receivable Days
----------------------------

**What it measures:** Average days to collect receivables.

**Formula:** (Accounts Receivable / Net Credit Sales) \* 365

**Learn more / Practice:**  
[📄 Accounts Receivable Days Formula & 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/accounts-receivable-days-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Accounts-Receivable Days Concept & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/accounts-receivable-days)

3\. Accounts Payable Turnover
-----------------------------

**What it measures:** How quickly a company pays suppliers.

**Formula:** Cost of Goods Sold / Average Accounts Payable

**Learn more / Practice:**  

[📄 Accounts Payable Turnover Formula & How to Use It in a 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/accounts-payable-turnover-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Accounts Payable Turnover & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/accounts-payable-turnover)

4\. Accounts Payable Days
-------------------------

**What it measures:** Average days to pay suppliers.

**Formula:** (Accounts Payable / Total Cost of Goods Sold) \* 365

**Learn more / Practice:**  

[📄 Accounts Payable Days Formula & How to Use It in a 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/accounts-payable-days-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Accounts Payable Days Concept & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/accounts-payable-days)

5\. Inventory Turnover
----------------------

**What it measures:** Efficiency of inventory management.

**Formula:** Cost of Goods Sold / Average Inventory

**Learn more / Practice:**  
[📄 Inventory Turnover Formula & 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/inventory-turnover-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Inventory Turnover Concept & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/inventory-turnover)

6\. Inventory Days
------------------

**What it measures:** Average days inventory is held.

**Formula:** (Inventory / Total Cost of Goods Sold) \* 365

**Learn more / Practice:**  
[📄 Inventory Days Formula & 3-Statement Forecast](https://quantus.finance/cheatsheets/inventory-days-formula-3-statement-forecast)
  
[🔍 Inventory Days Concept & Practices](https://quantus.finance/learn/concept/inventory-days)
