Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Who is Quantus best for?+

Quantus is for people who want to build real finance skills by doing—whether you’re preparing for a finance role, strengthening on-the-job skills, or learning financial modeling.

Do I need prior finance experience?+

No. You can start from the fundamentals and build up gradually. If you’re new, the free Financial Statement course is a great place to begin.

Is this content too basic if I already know finance?+

Not at all. Quantus offers hands-on practice with adjustable difficulty. Choose easy, medium, or hard levels, follow different learning paths, skip guidance, and build real modeling intuition and speed.

Can I learn financial modeling for free?+

Yes. The Free Starter plan lets you practice real finance skills with hands-on exercises, limited ‘Check Answer’ uses. No credit card required.
