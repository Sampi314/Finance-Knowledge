Every week [Pointy Haired Dilbert](http://chandoo.org/wp)
 features some of the excel links from around the web here. Get the archives [here](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/excel/excel-links/)
.

[**Teaching physics using excel**](http://peltiertech.com/WordPress/2008/09/08/graphical-approach-to-a-simple-physics-problem/)
 My friend Jon @ [PTS Blog](http://peltiertech.com/WordPress/)
 tries a different approach to teach physics to his daughter. Who says excel is only for corporates?

Daily dose of Excel compiles the **[ultimate list of excel blogs](http://spreadsheetpage.com/index.php/excelfeeds)
**. It is very useful if you want to keep track of what is happening on the spreadsheet front.

**[Linking excel to C](http://newtonexcelbach.wordpress.com/2008/09/02/linking-excel-to-c/)
** – Often it is important to access other programming languages from excel VBA. For example you may have already developed a complex scientific calculation function in C and would like to use it in VBA. [Newton Excel Bach’s blog](http://newtonexcelbach.wordpress.com/)
 offers a way to do this.

[10 things to know before you make your next chart](http://infographicsnews.blogspot.com/2008/09/ten-advices-for-editors-and-writers.html)
, Infographic news compiles a list of things you should be aware before you set out to make your next dashboard / info-graphic. My favorite tip is no.1 : Infographics are information, not decoration.
