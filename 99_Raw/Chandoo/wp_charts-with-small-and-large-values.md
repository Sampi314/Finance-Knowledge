![Data with large and small values - Excel Charting](https://chandoo.org/img/c/data-with-small-and-large-values.png)_**Here is an interesting charting problem we come across once in a while**_. We have a lot of small numbers and a few very large numbers. How do we effectively plot all of them in a chart?

That is, you have data like the numbers shown to right, with a surprising bump during Christmas time.

### Now, how do you go about making a chart?

**I can think of 4 options:**

![Charting options when you large and small numbers in your data - Charting](https://chandoo.org/img/c/charting-options-large-small-data.png)

1\. You make a chart as – is  
2\. Use log scale for vertical axis (axis option > check logarithmic scale)  
3\. Split the axis in to two: This is the most trickiest technique. I have used 2 charts and aligned them neatly. Thankfully Peltier Man comes to rescue. Read [broken y-axis](http://peltiertech.com/Excel/Charts/BrokenYAxis.html)
 to know how to do this.  
4\. Let the bars reach sky: You can of course, set axis max to an arbitrary value, just so that all the small values are clearly displayed and let the higher values literally cut thru plot area.
