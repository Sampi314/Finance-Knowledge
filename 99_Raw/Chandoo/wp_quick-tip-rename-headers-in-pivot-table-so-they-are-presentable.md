_**[Pivot tables](https://chandoo.org/wp/excel-pivot-tables/)
**_ are fun, easy and super useful. Except, they can be ugly when it comes to presentation. Here is a quick way to make a pivot look more like a report.

*   Just type over the headers / total fields to make them user friendly.

See this quick demo to understand what I mean:

![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/rename-in-pivot.gif)

So simple and effective.

**Keep in mind:** You can not rename to an existing column data in your data. So if you want to rename to “Amount” which is a field in the data table, simply type “Amount ” _with an extra space at end._
