Today FIFA World-cup 2010 is beginning. For the next 30 days or so, no matter where you go, you are bound to hear about soccer, world cup, teams, points, goals and what not.

**As a tribute to this beautiful and lovely sport, I want to showcase some really awesome excel spreadsheets that are designed to track FIFA Worldcup 2010.** Follow the links and Enjoy.

**[Most Comprehensive and Awesomest FIFA Worldcup Tracker – by Graham](http://sites.google.com/site/paramorefifawc/home)
**

![Most Comprehensive and Awesomest FIFA Worldcup Tracker - by Graham](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-6.png)  
This is by far one of the most comprehensive and slickest Excel files on footbal that I have seen. The workbook can play national anthems of teams, show scores from past finals, has team ranks, automated scoring and much more. Just download and play with it. You are going to love it even if you are not a sucker for soccer.

**[FIFA Worldcup tracker from Excely – 2018](http://www.excely.com/football/2018-fifa-world-cup-schedule.shtml)
**

![FIFA Worldcup tracker from Excely](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-1.png)  
This one page spreadsheet can easily provide team points and help you track everything you want in one place.

**[Comprehensive World cup Tracker with Live Scores](http://www.inflexionary.com/sports/world-cup-2010-excel)
**

![Comprehensive World cup Tracker with Live Scores](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-7.png)  
This tracker can update scores from web. Very useful if you want to know where teams stand at any point. Also, this is very comprehensive and provides extensive analysis of matches, goals, shots etc.

**[A FIFA Worldcup Tracker from Microsoft](http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/templates/TC103911551033.aspx?pid=CT101436151033&AxInstalled=1&c=0)
**

![A FIFA Worldcup Tracker from Microsoft](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-2.png)  
Another football world cup tracker / planner sheet with an added twist of ability to filter the team you want. Interesting stuff.

**[Worldcup Planner with team points](http://satwentyten.wordpress.com/2009/12/28/2010-world-cup-wallchart-in-excel/)
**

![Worldcup Planner with team points](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-3.png)  
This is a straight forward tracker with option to track team points and calculate group standings. It uses macros, so enable them to get it work.

**[An Excel Tracker from Our Reader – Alan](https://img.chandoo.org/d/World_Cup_2010-1.xlsx)
**

![An Excel Tracker from Our Reader - Alan](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-5.png)  
Alan from [Computer Gaga](http://computergaga.com/)
 shares this excel tracker using which you can find group level standings, track scores and do so much more.

**[Well designed Excel Tracker and Planner](http://www.en.excel-soccer-2010.de/downloads)
**

![Well designed Excel Tracker and Planner](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-8.png)  
Another very well designed Excel based football tracker / planner. It doesn’t seem like an excel file, but more like a web page. Check it out.

**[One Page FIFA 2010 World cup Tracker from ExcelTemplate.net](http://exceltemplate.net/sports/world-cup-2010-schedule-and-scoresheet/)
**

![One Page FIFA 2010 World cup Tracker from ExcelTemplate.net](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-9.png)  
One page tracker to keep track of scores and more.

**[Beautiful Flash based FIFA 2010 Calendar \[non-excel\]](http://www.marca.com/deporte/futbol/mundial/sudafrica-2010/calendario-english.html)
**

![Beautiful Flash based FIFA 2010 Calendar [non-excel]](https://chandoo.org/img/2010/fifa-2010-planner-tracker-sheet-10.png)  
Ok, this is not an excel based thing, but it is very beautiful and easy to use. It is a flash based calendar with clear views of matches and scores by date, group, country.
