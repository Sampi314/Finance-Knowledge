Strategic Repositioning of Blog
===============================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

Join me in bidding ‘bye bye’ to modus indoramus. The blog is no more.

Welcome to world of hopeless engineers.  
Welcome to the world full of maniac decision makers.  
Welcome to Pointy Haired Dilbert.  
Welcome to the Ultimate Management Reality.

Aaah, That sure is a grandoise way of putting things. Well, to make it simple, i have changed my blog title and focus. I am slowly coming away from the institution i am associated with. The new focus is – “Deriving optimum utility from leptokurtic distribution of leadtimes in jobshop”. Ok, ok, i am kidding. As it is the focus of blog hasnt changed much. But what is changed is its packaging. (Any annoyment caused by reading the previous as well as following content should be attribute to the marketing course i am attending this term)

With this back ground, lets proceed and see what this Pointy Haired Dilbert (PHD) is all about.

**Mission of the PHD**  
_To Make more and more readers especially b school aspirants, current students and corporate karmics happy.  
_

**Vision of the PHD**  
_PHD becomes one of the most popular and most read B School blogs in India and World and Mars if possible.  
_

**Values of PHD**  
_  
– Treat all engineers equally  
– Spare no moments of truth in B school life when it comes to blogging  
– Thinking about new and possible values so that the **value addition** takes place.  
_

Apart from the change of title i have removed some of the annoying formatting on the page and tried to increase the width of the page to 1000 pixels. 70 percent of the visits have 1024\*768 resolution and no point in assuming that the other 30 has no scollbar on screen. As it is the rightmost portion is consumed by the sidebar selling you links and products.

With that let me present you the all new PHD.

Goodnight.  
Chandoo
