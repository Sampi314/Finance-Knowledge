### Bonus Excel Tip – How to get quick zebra lines in Excel?

Just because 100 of you have decided to purchase excel school doesn’t mean rest of you should endure my sales pitches. So here is a [quick tip](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/quick-tip/)
.

If you want to format a table of data with zebra lines (banded rows):

1.  First format the second row of your table in a different color
2.  Now select first 2 rows and click on format painter icon
3.  Select rest of the rows
4.  Done!

![Quick Zebra Lines in Excel - Tip](https://chandoo.org/img/q/quick-banded-rows-in-excel.gif)
