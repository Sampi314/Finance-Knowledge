**[Dutch Architecture on a 5 Euro Coin](http://pythonide.blogspot.com/2008/10/how-to-make-money-with-free-software.html)
**

![Dutch Architecture on a 5 Euro Coin](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/dutch-coin-design.jpg)

The Dutch govt. is releasing these 5 Euro coins to commemorate Nether land’s architecture. The selected coin (shown above) is creative combination of typography, design, programming and visualization skills. The portrait of Dutch queen is constructed with the names of important Dutch architects. The backside of the coin (shown on the left in image) is designed as bookshelf. Read more on this [here](http://pythonide.blogspot.com/2008/10/how-to-make-money-with-free-software.html)
. \[via [Information Aesthetics](http://infosthetics.com/archives/2008/11/dutch_architecture_5_euro_coin_design.html)\
\]

**[Google Flu Trends – Bringing Search & Visualizations together for a good cause](http://www.google.org/flutrends/)
**

![Google Flu Trends - Bringing Search &  Visualizations together for a good cause](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/google-org-flu-trends-visualization.gif)

Google.org has analyzed the search terms and flu occurrence trends over the past few years and using that data to alert people. They say this system is able to predict flu 2 weeks faster than other systems. \[via [The Official Google Blog](http://googleblog.blogspot.com/2008/11/how-we-help-track-flu-trends.html)\
\]

**[First 100 days of a US President](http://awesome.goodmagazine.com/goodsheet/goodsheet009First100Days.html)
**

![First 100 days of a US President](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/first-100-days.jpg)

Ever wondered what can a president do in the first 100 days in his office? The above visualization tries to answer this question by looking at past. \[via [Flowing Data](http://flowingdata.com/2008/11/11/exploration-of-our-presidents-first-100-days-in-office/)\
\]

**[Retro Graph – Relative Sizes and Populations of Cities of the World from 1983](http://strangemaps.wordpress.com/2008/11/09/327-city-maps-as-a-rorschach-test/)
**

![Retro Graph - Relative Sizes and Populations of Cities of the World from 1983](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/size-of-cities-us.jpg)

This graph shows the sizes and populations of world cities in 1983. You can see how each city is shaped in a funny way. I think Washington DC looks like a winter shoe Chicago looks like roaring lion standing on its hind legs. What do you think?

**[Future Predictions on a Time Line](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_yGpz3Q5FBj8/SRxaoDOvVaI/AAAAAAAAAMM/TkvBBKpcxcY/s1600-h/extinction+timeline.jpg)
**

![Future Predictions on a Time Line](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/future-predictions-timeline.jpg)
