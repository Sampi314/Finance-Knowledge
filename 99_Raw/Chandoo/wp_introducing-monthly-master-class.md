Hi folks,

I have an exciting announcement for you. Starting this month, I will be running a **monthly master class** on advanced Excel. This is a video class to help advanced users learn creative, powerful ways to use Excel in their day to day work.

![mmc-logo-m](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/mmc-logo-m.png)

### What is this Master Class?

The idea is simple. Every month, I will publish a lengthy video discussing one of the below topics, specifically targeted at advanced Excel users. As our blog grew, we have a lot of experienced, advanced users and I want to help you achieve more. You will learn,

*   Dashboards
*   Data Analytics
*   Visualizations
*   VBA / Macros
*   Business Modeling
*   Business Intelligence

Each class will be on an different topic with in-depth coverage, creative outputs and awesome techniques. You can sign up for the class that suits your needs.

### I am not an advanced user. Can I still join?

If you are a beginner or intermediate level Excel user, I recommend our [Excel School program](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-school/)
 for you. Once you finish that class, you will have necessary skills and experience to enjoy the Master Class.

### What do I get in this Master Class?

You get,

*   A video lesson (that can be watched anytime) on the topic of the class
*   An example workbook
*   Links to additional material

### Can I watch the Master Class anytime / anywhere?

Of course yes. Our Monthly Master Class is _**video on demand.**_ Its like tuning in to Netflix to watch your favorite flick. Login when you have sometime to learn, watch a class or two and improve your skills. You can enjoy the class on your PC, Mac, Tablet or Phone.

### How much is this class?

**Each Master Class is US $10**. You can buy the lessons for the topics you want. For the $10, you can watch and download the video + workbook.

### When is it starting?

The first episode of our Monthly Master Class will be live this Thursday – 14th of April – 2016. We will be discussing advanced charting techniques in that.

I will share instructions on how to purchase the class on 14th (Thursday).

### Any questions?

Do you have any questions about this Monthly Master Class? If so, please post them in the comments or email me at chandoo.d@gmail.com.

**Thank you:**

I am super excited to offer this program. Thank you so much for your continued support to Chandoo.org. Your passion to learn and excel makes me work harder. Thanks for inspiring me and letting me help you. You are awesome.
