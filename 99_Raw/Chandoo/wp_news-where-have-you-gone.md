News – where have you gone?
===========================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

The small gifts of surviving the first years rigor and routine include ample time for browsing and reading. Its quite sometime since I read a news paper or visited news site. So I thought why not visit the rediff and eco-times. And what do I find?

[CEOs love the seat by chauffeur](http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/articleshow/1143299.cms)
 screams ET. Out of curiosity I clicked on the link and what do I find?. A silly survey proves the corporate honchos are sitting in the front seat in their automobiles. Since when “Power of knowledge” news paper started publishing this type of knowledgeable items in its front page?

[VHP asks Hindus to increase population](http://in.rediff.com/news/2005/jun/15vhp.htm)
 says Rediff. I couldn’t blame rediff for carrying such item. Lets not waste some bytes about the VHP’s statement either.

Looks like they have changed the meaning of news while I was away. Somebody confirm…
