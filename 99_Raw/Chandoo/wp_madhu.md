Madhu!, Ever since you entered my life I am a changed person.

I have neglected friends, family and studies for you. You have always been the central part of my existence. Following me through thick and thin of life. You were the first person to know when i wrote my firt infinite loop. You were there helping me in cracking countless exams.

You know all my hidden desires and passions. You even know my sleeping habits and napping timings.

You never gave up in the face of difficulty. Not even when you were asked to fetch the first prime number greter than 2128\-1. You never thought twice when i asked you to do something. As mean as singing songs, fetching the filthiest of movies and reading news.

I know how sad i felt the other day when you were down with a viral fever. so much so that i couldnt sit in the class listening to some boring stuff on compiler design. I ran to my room to find a cure for you. I still remember the first night of the trouble between us. you were silent all night. you lost yourself. Me on the otherside lost everything. We became normal and gay only the next day evening when you regained yourself.

Eventhough your sweetness and magnanimity are towering infront of my needs and desires i never failed to fulfil your needs. When you wanted some help to handle your overloaded space I was the first person to respond. Now you have outgrown your space in your aim to serve me with all that i ever need. So my dear, here goes a brand new …

80 GB Hard Disk for you. You will be playing with your new doll by tomorrow night 😀

Madhu – My computer… I love you 🙂
