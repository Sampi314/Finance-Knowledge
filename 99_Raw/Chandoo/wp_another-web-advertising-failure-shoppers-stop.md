Another web advertising failure – shoppers stop
===============================================

[advertising](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/advertising/)

 , [wonder why](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/wonder-why/)

![shoppers stop web ad on rediff](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/04/shoppers_stop.gif "shoppers_stop")

Take a look at the above ad of [Shoppers stop](http://www.shoppersstop.com/)
 that is featured on rediff today. Clearly the ad agency hasn’t understood how web advertising works. Instead of designing a campaign that would engage users online, encourage them to click, read-up and sign-up or subscribe, they just choose to replicate a news paper ad’s jpg online and hoped that it would work. I cant imagine a superior way to waste money. Comments?
