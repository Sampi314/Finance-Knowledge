My First Podcast!!! – Listen to know some cool charting and dashboard stuff
===========================================================================

[interviews](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/interviews/)
 - 0 comments

  

![My First Podcast!!! - Listen to know some cool charting and dashboard stuff](https://chandoo.org/img/i/podcast-logo.png)Just weeks before Christmas, Ross McLean, who runs [Methods in Excel](http://www.blog.methodsinexcel.co.uk/)
 (a very cool and useful Excel blog) asked me if I can join him for a small podcast interview on things we both love – excel, charts and talking. I couldn’t be more happier to accept his offer. We did the podcast and life kept busy both of us for a while. That is until yesterday, when Ross made the podcast available online for all of us.

[Please go here to listen to my first podcast interview](http://www.blog.methodsinexcel.co.uk/2010/03/23/the-mie-podcasts-%E2%80%93-chandoo/)
.

We talked 40 minutes about,

*   How chandoo.org and various products that I sell are working out for me.
*   How to make dashboards, what are the ideas, frameworks and principles I follow.
*   How to format charts, what is the secret.

Go ahead and enjoy the podcast. Let me know what you think by commenting here or on Ross’ blog.

Btw,

*   We talk about DTP framework in the interview. You can find more details about that here: [Juice Analytics on DTP Framework](http://www.juiceanalytics.com/writing/delivering-data-excel-dtp-framework/)
    
*   We talk about a book called “Non-Designer’s Design Book”. You can find more details about that here: [Non-Designer’s Design Book](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0321534042?ie=UTF8&tag=poinhairdilb-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0321534042)
    ![](https://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=poinhairdilb-20&l=as2&o=1&a=0321534042).
*   The Excel Formula Sandbox, new version of formula e-book will be released in the next 45 days. 🙂
