Nothing like a weekend making fun of something worthless. So we will pick on some of the plump, overloaded and visually disgusting [pie charts](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/pie-charts/)
 featured on various extremely popular websites.

### Top 100 Twitter Users in a Pie Chart

The first pie chart comes from [readwriteweb](http://www.readwriteweb.com/archives/10_ways_to_archive_your_tweets.php)
. Although they did not make it, they shared it with the world. The real crime is committed by Archivist, a desktop application for archiving your tweets. See this yourself.

![Top 100 Twitter users - Bad charts](https://chandoo.org/img/cb/top100-twitter-users-bad-pie-chart.jpg)

### How average American spends their paycheck?

The next pie chart comes from [visualeconomics](http://www.visualeconomics.com/how-the-average-us-consumer-spends-their-paycheck/)
. It is a giant complex oval donut that will tell you how you are spending your money. If time is money, then interpreting this chart will cost you a lot.

![How are americans spending their money - bad charts](https://chandoo.org/img/cb/where-does-money-go-bad-pie-chart.jpg)

### Market share of twitter publishing tools

The third and final pie chart is featured on [techcrunch](http://www.techcrunch.com/2009/06/10/the-more-followers-you-have-the-more-you-tweet-or-is-it-the-other-way-around/)
. It shows the market share of twitter publishing tools. Obviously the people behind this chart must think James Bond is reading this chart. How else can we know what is what percentage? And most importantly who uses the “destroy twitter” application to tweet?

![Market share of twitter publishing tools - bad charts](https://chandoo.org/img/cb/market-share-of-twitter-publishing-tools-bad-pie-chart.png)

### A pie chart on the use of pie charts

After seeing all these really [bad charts](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/bad-charts/)
 I had to make a pie chart that represents the pie chart menace while the chart itself being bad. My excel nearly crashed when I attempted it. So I hand-drew the below pie chart on state of pie-charts.

![a pie chart on usage of pie charts](https://chandoo.org/img/cb/pie-chart-on-piecharts.png)
