Sample Power BI Training Outline
--------------------------------

Most Power BI remote training programs I run are custom designed. Here is a sample outline if you are not sure where to start. The below outline is for a standard 2 day (2x4hrs) training program.

**Session 1: Getting started**

*   Quick tour of Power BI
*   Various components of Power BI software
*   Case study – crowd-sourced data to Power BI visuals

**Session 3: DAX & Power Pivot**

*   Simple DAX measures
*   Implicit vs. Explicit measures
*   Filter & Evaluation context
*   CALCULATE
*   Conditions, variables and more
*   Column level features
*   Advanced DAX concepts

**Session 2: Power Query & ETL**

*   Simple transformations with Power Query
*   Add columns
*   Dates & Calendars
*   Merging & Appending files

**Session 4: Business Reporting with Power BI**

*   Report design
*   Interaction & filters
*   Report tooltips
*   Drillthrough
*   Bookmarks
*   Conditional formatting
*   Working with various visual types
