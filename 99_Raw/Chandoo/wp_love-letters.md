Unsolicited Testimonial

by _David Dimon_

I stumbled across your site as I was looking for information on arrays after my employer asked me to build a spreadsheet that required formulas I haven’t used more than once. After quickly looking over the information on this site I immediately registered and signed up for the newsletter.

In just a few minutes of looking I found more information gathered in this one location than I had in a dozen Google searches. And to top it all off the information is presented in a clear and concise manner. I don’t have to go looking for another explanation to help me understand the first one. This is an excellent resource for the Excel DIY’ers out there.

**Chandoo.org has become my go to source for all things Excel.** Thank you and keep up the most awesome work. 😀
