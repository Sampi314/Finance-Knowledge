### What does Excel Wedding Planner do?

Excel wedding planner may not throw you a surprise bridal shower or arrange a bachelor party like the friends did in [hangover](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1119646/)
. But it is pretty robust and can manage all aspects of a wedding with ease. Some of its key features are,

*   Set up and manage guest lists (along with menu preferences, seating arrangements etc.)
*   Manage budgets (by vendor, by activity etc.)
*   Create and maintain todo lists / tasks related to wedding, show alerts for pending items
*   Manage vendors for flowers, music, photo / video etc.
*   Show the entire wedding progress in a dashboard so that you know where you need to spend the time
