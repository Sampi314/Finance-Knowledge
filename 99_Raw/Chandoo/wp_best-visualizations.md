**[Anatomy of a Great Speech – Obama’s acceptance speech at DNC](http://www.presentationzen.com/presentationzen/2008/09/obama-delivers-his-speech-like-a-symphony.html)
**

![Anatomy of Great speech - Obama's acceptance speech at DNC mile high](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/anatomy-of-a-great-speech-obama-acceptance-dnc.jpg)

[Presentation Zen](http://www.presentationzen.com/)
 captures Obama’s symphony like acceptance speech in a graph shown above. Do read Garr Reynold’s remarks.

**[Tracking Hurricane Gustav a la infographic style](http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/26295161?preferredName=Gustav)
**

![Tracking Hurricane Gustav - Infographics](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/tracking-hurricane-infographic-gustav.jpg)

These days telling a complex story like how a hurricane strikes is made easy, thanks to tons of flash based visualization tools available for news reporters. \[via [Flowing Data](http://flowingdata.com/2008/08/31/tracking-hurricane-gustav-how-hard-is-it-going-to-hit/)\
\]

**[How would tell your story if you are aiming to change a habit? – Google Chrome Comic Book](http://www.google.com/googlebooks/chrome/)
**

![Google Chrome Launch - Comic book infographic](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/google-chrome-story-comic-book.jpg)

Google has launched their open source browser Chrome. Now this is a very bold step, when millions of people have developed a hobby of using IE or Firefox and pretty much satisfied with them. How did Google tell their story of new browser and created a need for it ? By creating a comic book and telling every one how browsers work, about memory leaks, garbage collection, UI design. Very effective.  
**  
[More Olympics visualizations – News paper comes to life and tells impressive stories](http://infographicsnews.blogspot.com/2008/09/infographics-departments-around-world.html)
**

![News paper infographics - olympics atheletics](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/olympics-news-paper-infographic.jpg)

What would you do when you are revamping infographics department of a leading news paper? Folks at [El Heraldo](http://www.elheraldo.com.co/elheraldo/home.asp)
 have done a fantastic job.

**[Sony Walkman Subway map advertisement](http://www.frederiksamuel.com/blog/SINGLE_AD_PAGE.php?ad=sony_walkman_1.jpg)
**

![Sony walkman sub way map advertisement](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/sony-walkman-subway-map.jpg)

Okay, this is not infographic, but it is a very creative way to tell that Sony Walkman is a constant companion no matter which route you take on the metro. Very creative, very effective. I can’t imagine taking metro to anywhere without company or music. \[via [cool infographics](http://coolinfographics.blogspot.com/2008/08/sony-walkman-advertising.html)\
\]
