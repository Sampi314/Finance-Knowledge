What is in this Masterclass & Who should attend?
------------------------------------------------

This masterclass is conducted **only in Houston, Texas**. It has 2 programs.

*   Advanced Excel & Dashboards – 15 & 16 September (Monday & Tuesday), 2014
*   Power Pivot, Power Query & Data Analytics – 17 & 18 September (Wednesday & Thursday), 2014

This 4 day masterclass is aimed at managers, analysts, reporting professionals, executives in sales, marketing, customer service or anyone who wants to learn about Data Analytics, Advanced Excel, Dashboard reporting

**Advanced Excel & Dashboards Masterclass:**

In this masterclass we will be learning,

*   **Day1: Our first dashboard in Excel**
    *   Excel formulas for dashboards
    *   Applying advanced conditional formatting & sparklines for dashboards
    *   Building our first dashboard – hands-on exercise
*   **Day 2: Advanced dashboard skills**
    *   Selecting right charts for any situation
    *   Enhancing dashboards with Excel 2010 & 2013 – sparklines, slicers, timelines
    *   Customizing Excel charts – top 5 problems
    *   Building our second dashboard – hands-on exercise
    *   Q&A

**Power Pivot, Power Query & Data Analytics Masterclass:**

*   **Day1: Data analysis with Pivot & PowerPivot features  
    **
    
    *   Introduction to PowerPivot & Case study
    *   Using Slicers & Pivot Tables
    *   Using DAX to analyze data
    *   Understanding Important DAX functions
        *   Simple measures
        *   CALCULATE()
        *   ALL()
        *   Disconnected Tables & FILTER()
        *   DATE Intelligence formulas
*   **Day 2: Advanced Analysis thru PowerPivot  
    **
    
    *   Power Query, let your data pains be gone
    *   Selecting right charts for any situation
    *   Building PowerPivot Dashboard
    *   Dashboard Best Practices & Ideas
    *   Q&A
