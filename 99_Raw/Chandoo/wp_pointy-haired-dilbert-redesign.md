Introducing new design of Pointy Haired Dilbert
===============================================

[blogging](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/blogging/)
 - 11 comments

  

![phd-screenshot-new-theme](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/phd-screenshot-new-theme.jpg "phd-screenshot-new-theme")

It has been a while since the blog’s look and feel has changed. The number of visitors to blog has been doubling for the last 2 months. I felt the need for a simpler but more elegant blog look and feel. So during the labor day weekend I have spent a few hours and changed the blog theme and added better navigation etc. Take a look at the brand new [Pointy Haired Dilbert](http://chandoo.org/wp)
 and tell me what you think.

### What is new?

*   **Home page no longer shows full posts:** Since most of the posts here are reasonably long, I figured it would be better if the user is given a choice to take a quick look at what is available and choose which one(s) to read.
*   **Single Posts have more screen space:** While home page, category pages have 3 column view, single posts have only 2 columns – 1 wide content area and another small column for further navigation. This means, people landing individual articles through search, social media should find what they are looking for without distractions.
*   **Restructured Ads:** Even though I am very passionate about excel, technology and business ideas and love to share them with you all, I have to use ads (mainly google ads) to support this website. As part of the restructure I tried to optimized the ad locations and layouts.
*   ![hand-drawn-share-social-icons](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/hand-drawn-share-social-icons.jpg "hand-drawn-share-social-icons")**Hand drawn social sharing icons:** Ok, this is not totally new, some of you must have seen these last week. I have a set of social sharing icons beneath each post that I have drawn myself.
    
    So next time you see one of them act as if I have personally asked you to share a post with your friend 😀
    
*   **Best posts of PHD:** You will also see last 5 most popular posts on PHD, most commented posts on the home page. If you are keen to browse the best posts, bookmark the “[featured posts of pointy haired dilbert](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/best-of-phd/)
    ” link

So let me know how you like this new time, any bugs, broken links you see, any garbled renderings in your browser. I will try to fix them.
