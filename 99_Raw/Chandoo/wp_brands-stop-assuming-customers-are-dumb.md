Brands, Stop assuming customers are dumb!
=========================================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

![MTR Upma](https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v388/purnachadraraod/blog/upma.jpg) There are brands, and then there are brands, which make us look like the dumbest asses possible. The case in point â€“ [MTR foods](http://www.mtrfoods.com/)
 Their latest television ads about [MTR Upma](http://www.mtrfoods.com/snackmixes/upma.htm)
 make you feel like you have the IQ of a paper napkin.

One of the ads go like this. There will be a bunch of guys sitting. One of them is cooking something. Others ask him “what is there to eat?”. He shouts Up**ma**.

![ MTR Upma 1](https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v388/purnachadraraod/blog/mtr1.jpg)![ MTR Upma 2](https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v388/purnachadraraod/blog/mtr2.jpg)

The **ma** in the end reaches up to a flight that is traveling what is like 10 km straight up and a old women suddenly hears it. She takes a parachute and lands up in the terrace to finish the upma. The guys say happily, “MTR Upma fetched us ma”

![MTR Upma 3](https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v388/purnachadraraod/blog/mtr3.jpg)![ MTR Upma 4](https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v388/purnachadraraod/blog/mtr4.jpg)

**Now, what is it they want to say?**

*   It is so tough to make upma with MTR instant upma mix that your mom has to jump from where ever she is to help you out.

*   You are dumb and incapable of making upma out of this ready mix, thatâ€™s why MTR Upma mix can fetch you a maa to cook for you

*   Or is it something else?

The other ad is even worse. Just because somebody has stuffed his mouth with upma he gets a portion in a play as _Hanuman._ Oh, come on now; please realize that the **customers are probably not interested in getting para-gliding moms or instant _hanumans_.** They want a story that they can relate to.

**What is a successful brand?** A brand that can tell you stories and makes you feel proud when you associate with it.

With that I end my argument.
