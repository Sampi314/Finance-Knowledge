Add more superstars to your gmail
=================================

[technology](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/computer/)
 - 0 comments

  

![gmail-superstars-add-more-just-drag](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/gmail-superstars-add-more-just-drag.png "gmail-superstars-add-more-just-drag")

I use new superstars [gmail labs](http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en&ctx=mail&answer=29418)
 feature a lot, to mark reader mails that I need to followup, comments I should respond to, ideas that are interesting, bill mailers that should be paid. The default setting allows any of the 4 superstars to be added. while trying to change my mail signature, I have realized that now you can add more super stars to your account with simple drag & drop. Pretty cool feature. [Read more](http://groups.google.com/group/gmail-labs-help-superstars/browse_thread/thread/3745c0567ba3f12a#)
.

Thanks gmail team 🙂
