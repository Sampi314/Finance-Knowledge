As usual in the morning I opened CNN IBN homepage to check what is happening in India. To my surprise the IBNLIVE site insists that I must read these happening news items.

![CNN IBN LIVE Must read news items fun](https://phd.images.googlepages.com/cnn_ibn_must_read_news_items.jpg)

Mind you, they are not about state of union or lost test series or Australian open or dropping mercury in Delhi or impending recession that must be read by news-seekers, but the stories on mankinis (wtf?), saif and vidhya, art of lovemaking that are of import to everyone. May be they meant Hindi _**must**_ \[fun\] when they say must read. Talk about editorial control.
