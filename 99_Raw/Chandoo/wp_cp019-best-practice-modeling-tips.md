### What is in this session?

I am very happy to interview my good friend, blogger, author, excel trainer & business-women – [Danielle Stein Fairhurst](http://plumsolutions.com.au/danielle-stein-fairhurst)
 for this session. I first met Danielle when I went to Sydney, Australia in April 2012. Our friendship & collaboration grew a lot in the last 2.5 years. She is a great speaker & trainer. This episode is loaded with her trademark style commentary, explanation & tips for better modeling. I hope you will enjoy it.

In this podcast, you will learn,

*   Introduction to Danielle & her work
*   6 Tips for Best Practice Modeling
    *   Write consistent formulas
    *   Avoid hard-coding
    *   Smart referencing
    *   Ditch the bad habits
    *   Document assumptions
    *   Format & label things
*   Resources for learning more
