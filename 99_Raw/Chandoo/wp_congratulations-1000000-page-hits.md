_Congratulations **Chandoo.org** on reaching **1,000,000** page hits in November 2011._

_Chandoo.org achieved 1,002,226 Hits in November, passing 1,000,000 hits for the first time. An increase over October of 25,595 hits.  
_

_This doesn’t include the other 585,504 hits at Chandoo.org/Forums or the Excel Classes Chandoo runs !_

_Especially great as November was a short month and had a major US Holiday !
