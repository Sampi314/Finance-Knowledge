[WTC site – ground zero](http://www.flickr.com/photos/chandoo/2532699062/)

This is the first of a new series of posts I am planning, “Photographic Fridays”, Every last weekday I will post a photo I have taken for your review / comments. I want to be a better photographer and all your comments are going to help me in becoming one.

To begin with, this is the photo of [World Trade Center site – Ground Zero](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Trade_Center_site)
, I took while I was in NY during the memorial weekend. We got down at wall st. metro station and walked to this place, it was getting dark by the time we reached here and since some construction work was going on, we were not to be allowed to the memorial site.
