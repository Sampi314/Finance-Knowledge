5 Beautiful Visualizations \[Oct 24\]
=====================================

[Cool Infographics & Data Visualizations](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/cool-infographics/)
 - 5 comments

  

Every week, [Pointy Haired Dilbert](http://chandoo.org/wp)
 celebrates the art of chart making by sharing 4-5 of the most beautiful visualizations floated around the web in the last few days. Take a look at them and have some inspiration and fun 😀

**[Map of most popular social networks in each country](http://www.oxyweb.co.uk/blog/socialnetworkmapoftheworld.php)
**

![Map of most popular social networks in each country](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/world-map-of-social-networks.jpg)

This is a simple world map, but each country colored based on the most popular social networking site there. As you can see, US is [MySpace](http://myspace.com/)
, Canada is [Facebook](http://facebook.com/)
 and India is [Orkut](http://orkut.com/)
. Findout what is most popular in your country. \[via [Digital Inspiration](http://www.labnol.org/internet/world-map-of-top-social-networks/5049/)\
\]

**[The world is red, deep red](http://finviz.com/map.ashx?t=geo)
**

![The world is red, deep red](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/world-is-red-companies-market-cap-share-movement.jpg)

If you want to get a pulse of global finances (and feel scared) then this is the place to go. They have a world map with each country having square bubbles of the leading company stocks traded there. The bubble size represents the market capitalization and the color for the price movement. As you can immediately see, the world is red, deep red. \[via [Information Aesthetics](http://infosthetics.com/archives/2008/10/3d_and_geographical_financial_treemap.html)\
\]

**[Can a president tame the business cycle?](http://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2008/10/18/business/20081019-metrics-graphic.html)
**

![Can a president tame the business cycle?](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/us-statistics-by-president.jpg)

NY Times tries to answer the important question: “Can a president tame the business cycle?” by looking in to the past and plotting how each of the past US presidents have managed various key parameters. As usual, awesome work by the NYT Team. \[via [Infographic News](http://infographicsnews.blogspot.com/2008/10/nytimescom-again.html)\
\]

**[Obesity system influence diagram](http://www.shiftn.com/obesity/Full-Map.html)
**

![Obesity system influence diagram](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/plotting-obesity.jpg)

What causes Obesity? This huge system influence diagram tries to connect all parameters related to obesity and how they effect each other. Very informative and interactive. \[via [Information Aesthetics](http://infosthetics.com/archives/2008/10/obesity_system_influence_diagram.html)\
\]

**[Hookup Maps](http://hookupmaps.com/)
**

![Hookup Maps](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/hookup-maps-craigslist.jpg)

If you can combine personal ads posted in sites like [craigslist](http://craigslist.org/)
 with the location of the hookup, the mash up would be a fun way to explore relationships. What you are seeing is a part of New York [hookupmap](http://hookupmaps.com/)
 as on today. \[via [CNET](http://news.cnet.com/8301-17939_109-10072768-2.html?part=rss)\
\]
