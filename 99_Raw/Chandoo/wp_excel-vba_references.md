Recommended Sites for Excel VBA
-------------------------------

*   [Contextures.com](http://contextures.com/)
    : Debra shares some insightful tips, examples & ideas on VBA constantly.
*   [Daily Dose of Excel](http://dailydoseofexcel.com/)
    : DDoE maintained by a slew of Excel MVPs & professionals has a lot of advice on VBA & Advanced Excel.
*   [Excel-VBA-Easy](http://www.excel-vba-easy.com/)
    : Excel VBA Easy has good number of articles on VBA & various examples too.
*   [Ozgrid VBA](http://www.ozgrid.com/VBA/)
    : A huge collection of tips, code samples & examples
*   [Getting Started with Excel 2010 VBA – Microsoft help](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ee814737.aspx)
    
*   [Excel-VBA.com](http://www.excel-vba.com/)
