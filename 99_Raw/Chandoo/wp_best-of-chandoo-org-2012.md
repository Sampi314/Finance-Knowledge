This year has been the busiest year since the inception of Chandoo.org. **We had 161 posts, 8,900+ comments, 33,500+ forum posts**. We have trained more than 2,500 students thru training programs like [Excel School](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-school/)
 & [VBA Classes](http://chandoo.org/wp/vba-classes/)
 this year alone.

**More than 6.5 million people visited our site this year** (up 82%),  consuming a whopping total of 17.8 million pages (up 69%). Each of these visitors spent an average of 2min 15 seconds on our site becoming awesome in Excel. There are 1.6 mn people who spend at least 15 minutes or more on our site.

We have also added 20,000 members to our newsletter / RSS readership this year, closing at 55,000 mark by end of 2012. _It has been a hectic year._
