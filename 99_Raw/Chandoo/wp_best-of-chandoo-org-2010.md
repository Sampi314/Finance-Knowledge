Best of Chandoo.org – 2010
==========================

[blogging](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/blogging/)

**This year has been hectic for Chandoo.org. We have posted 229 excel tips & articles this year.** In addition, we had 6500+ comments & a lot of new pages on the website. We have also trained more than 700 students in Excel & Financial Modeling thru [Excel School](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-school/)
 & [Financial Modeling School](http://chandoo.org/wp/financial-modeling/)

Key Trends this year
--------------------

In year 2010, the key themes for Chandoo.org were,

*   Making more people awesome in Excel thru blog content, news letters & [Excel School](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-school/)
    
*   Having diverse content thru [Guest posts](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/guest-posts/)
     (thanks to Hui, Paramdeep, Itay, Yogesh, Larry, Faheem, Daniel, Myles and many others who shared content / ideas with us)
*   Sharing more ideas, implementations about [Dashboards](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-dashboards/)
    
*   Engaging our readers more with [homework](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/homework/)
    , [awesomeness week](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/reader-awesomeness-week/)
     & [polls](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/polls/)
    
*   [Showcasing Excel 2010’s new features & uses](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/excel-2010/)
    
*   Having fun all the while.
