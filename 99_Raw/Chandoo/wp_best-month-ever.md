Best month ever \[blogging updates\]
====================================

[blogging](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/blogging/)
 - 15 comments

  

![Pointy Haired Dilbert - Chandoo.org - July 2009 - Report](https://chandoo.org/img/n/google-analytics-jul-2009.png)I am very happy to share this exciting news with you. June, 2009 has been the best month ever since I started this blog…

*   **We have received 239,000+ page views from 95,000+ visitors**
*   **4,300+ of you have signed up for our [RSS feeds](http://feeds2.feedburner.com/PointyHairedDilbert)
     and [e-mail news letters](http://feedburner.google.com/fb/a/mailverify?uri=PointyHairedDilbert&loc=en_US)
    .** That is 800 new members in our community this month.
*   **There are 345 comments** in this month. This is a true testimonial of how buzzing our community is.
*   The blog has been featured on [Lifehacker](http://lifehacker.com/5288578/save-time-with-excels-double%20click-tricks)
    , [delicious home page](http://delicious.com/url/9792dea800699d8926afa5243d2fdfbc)
    , [MSN Japan](http://digitallife.jp.msn.com/article/article.aspx/genreid=121/articleid=431169/)
     and [BNet](http://blogs.bnet.com/businesstips/?p=4170)
     and many more sites in this month.
*   We have revised the [excel formulas e-book](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-formula-helper-e-book)
     this month and it has been selling like hot cakes ever since.
*   We have started 2 new series of posts – [Project Management using Excel](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/06/16/gantt-charts-project-management/)
     and slightly controversial [Chart Busters](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/06/12/introducing-chart-doctor/)
    

I am very thankful to you. I really enjoy sharing whatever little I am learning with you all. **Your support is truly amazing. Thank You.**
