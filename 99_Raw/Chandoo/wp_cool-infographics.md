[**Average gas prices in US regions from 1993 plotted in dynamic bar**](http://projects.flowingdata.com/gas-prices/)
  
  
  
![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/gas-prices-dynamic-bar1.jpg "gas-prices-dynamic-bar1")

Flowing data takes a look at the historical gasoline price data and provides us this eye candy.

My friend Jon takes a look at this and recreates it in excel. [Read more](http://peltiertech.com/WordPress/2008/08/09/gas-prices-animated-bar-chart-for-excel-2/)
.\[via flowingdata\]

[**Cost of Food around the World**](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/in_depth/7284196.stm)
  
  
![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/world-food-prices-bbc1.jpg "world-food-prices-bbc1")  
  
  
This BBC page provides insights in to changing food price, consumption trends around the world. Scary picture for future generations. \[via [cool infographics](http://coolinfographics.blogspot.com/2008/08/bbc-world-food-prices.html)\
\]

[**Infographic on the Tees, the shirt project**](http://www.theshirtproject.org/)
  
  
![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/the-shirt-project-infographic-on-tee.jpg "the-shirt-project-infographic-on-tee")  
  
  
With so many beautiful visualizations, the shirt project is the next natural thing to happen. They spread the story from the charts in a fun way. Go buy one if you like it 🙂

**[Google Search Insights for search term “excel”](http://www.google.com/insights/search/#cat=&q=excel%2Ccharts%2C&geo=&date=today%2012-m&clp=&cmpt=q)
**  
  
![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/india-excel-search-trend-google.jpg "india-excel-search-trend-google")  
  
  
Google has launched [search insights](http://www.google.com/insights/search/)
 which provides excellent detail about various search trends. I have looked up search term excel, and surprisingly India leads the pack with index of 100. Now I know where majority of my audience are 🙂

[**How the marginal taxes are changing with Obama’s new tax policy**](http://gregmankiw.blogspot.com/2008/08/obama-tax-plan.html)
  
  
![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/obama-vs-mc-cain-how-they-tax.jpg "obama-vs-mc-cain-how-maringal-tax")  
  

This chart explains how the marginal taxes change with proposed tax structure by Barack Obama. Remember, the key word is **marginal** (loosely put, since Obama’s tax policies put more money in your pocket, your other taxes will go up) \[via [marginal revolution](http://www.marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2008/08/the-median-vote.html)\
\]
