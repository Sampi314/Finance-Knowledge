A quick update on Excel School before you start your weekend. So far Excel School – online excel training program has been very well received by our members at PHD.

[![Excel School Registration closes in 5 more days](https://chandoo.org/img/es/excel-school-logo-3.png)](http://chandoo.org/wp/excel-school/)

_**How are the registrations so far?**_

*   **We have 44 students in the classroom.** Every day I am hearing from people who are willing to join.

_**We have guest faculty!!!**_

*   [**Jon Peltier**](http://peltiertech.com/)
    , Excel MVP, blogger, friend and excel charting guru will deliver a short lecture on “how to choose right chart type for your data”  during week 4 of program.
*   [**Debra Dalgleish**](http://blog.contextures.com/)
    , long time MVP, author of several Pivot Table books and blogger at Contextures will tell us about “top pivot table tricks” during week 6.
*   [**Mike Alexander**](http://datapigtechnologies.com/blog/)
    , Excel MVP, Author and Blogger of Bacon Bits will talk about “Working with large data sets in Excel” during week 8 of Excel School.

Each of these lessons will be 20 minute long audio with additional commentary & video by me.

_**Only  5 days left!**_

My Excel School Program will close for new registrations on 17th Feb. Only 5 more days to go.
