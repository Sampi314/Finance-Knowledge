I is Wet…
=========

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 4 comments

  

yeah.. _**I**_ is wet.  
  
this is what happened today ( or should i say yesterday and today 😛 )  
  
since there is no work ( well, not much 😀 ) I slept at 11:45 types with full hopes of catching a 6-7 hour sleep. at about 1:20 am…

Bang Bang….  
  
Bang Bang….  
  
Saale bahar aaaja….

It took quite sometime to recognize the fact that there is something going out. finally came out half conscious. And as soon as i locked my door, thud…. came the poring of chill water. Instatntly i became aware of myself and others. Every single soul around me is wet and drenching. Whoa…. The rest as they say is history. We as a team worked together to ensure that, no door is unopened, no body is warm and no single soul is trying to dry his body after his first drenching. All the advanced management tools used to tame the wild human behavior were used to accomplish this task of great import. Tools like, toothpaste, shampoo, surf and breaking the doors. Fianlly it worked. Once the PGP1 is collectively wet and roaring, we went to PGP2 rooms. and the procession then moved to those outliers who tried to disobay the norms of iimi. more banging, more hot water ( added to the cold water ) and more energy.. finally most of the batch members had the opportunity of getting bathed by one of the future managers with whatever detergent the manager felt appropriate. phew… now i am feeling like changing…  
  
catch you tomorrow.
