Chandoo.org produces it’s Second MVP.
=====================================

[Huis](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/huis/)
 , [Posts by Hui](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/huis-posts/)
 - 97 comments

  

[![Hui_MVP_Award](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hui_MVP_Award.jpg)](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Hui_MVP_Award.jpg)
It is with great pride and humility that I announce that I (Hui) was recently awarded an MVP (Excel) by Microsoft.

This award is given in recognition for my contribution to the Excel community which is mostly based here at Chandoo.org and also at [ExcelHero.com/blog](http://www.excelhero.com/blog/ "Excel Hero Blog")
 and the [Excel Hero Academy](http://academy.excelhero.com/index.php?page_id=135 "Excel Hero Academy")
.

You can see a full lists of my Excel work at [About Hui](http://chandoo.org/wp/about-hui/ "About Hui")
.

So in part this award also belongs to Chandoo, all the Chandoo.org readers and my fellow [Excel Ninja’s](http://chandoo.org/forum/threads/chandoo-org-ninjas.10830/#post-62823 "The Excel Ninja's")
 here at the Chandoo.org Forums as without the Chandoo.org platform, without the readers and without the ongoing support from my fellow Excel Ninjas,  I am certain this wouldn’t have been possible.

I need to especially thank Chandoo and Daniel Ferry for their support over the past 5 years.

To the Chandoo.org community please accept my sincere thanx for your support and I look forward to our ongoing interaction in the years ahead.
