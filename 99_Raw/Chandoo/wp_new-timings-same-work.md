New Timings, Same Work
======================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 1 comment

  

Our class timings have changed. infact they were interchanged. Now we have to go to classes at 9 am and section A can enjoy 30 more minutes of valuable deep slumber. This means, we have to sleep 30 mins earlier than what we used to do. But there is no such concept as absolute sleeping time, so, almost all of us are sleeping in the afternoon for a couple of hours. That is barring whatever sleep we catch under prof’s nose.

That is all about it. Now, i am off to take a bath and rush to classes. 🙂
