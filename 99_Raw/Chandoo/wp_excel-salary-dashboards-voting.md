Hi there,

On Monday, we published [66 dashboards visualizing Excel salaries around the world](http://chandoo.org/wp/2012/07/30/excel-salary-survey-contest-results/ "Visualize Excel salaries around world with these 66 Dashboards")

How does the voting work?
-------------------------

The voting has 2 components

1.  Readers (that is you) pick a winner and runner-up using online voting.
2.  Judges (2 of them) will also pick winners. Judges vote carries 20% weight.

Go ahead and pick a winner
