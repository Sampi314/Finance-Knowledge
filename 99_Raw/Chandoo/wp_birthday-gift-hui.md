### Some Key Findings

*   [Our forums](http://chandoo.org/forums/)
     have a total of 5,227 posts, **out of which [Hui contributed](http://chandoo.org/forums/profile/hui)
     24% (roughly 1 in 4)**
*   On average Hui takes 13 and half hours to get back to the questioner with an answer. The fastest he has been was 31 seconds. (only last year data)
*   **Hui has been very busy on November 30th – 2010, answering 19 times on that day.**
*   The person who got benefited most from Hui is [_indi visual_](http://chandoo.org/forums/profile/indi-visual)
    , with 44 replies from Hui.
*   People used the words _**thank**_ and _**Hui**_ in same post 395 times.
*   Hui wakes up early and answers questions on our forums. Then, before sleep, he spends some time helping people.
*   **If Hui had $2.5 per each letter he typed in our forums, he would be a millionaire.**
*   All data as of Jan 11, 2011. Between that and now, Hui has already answered 37 more times (and still helping…)

### How is this dashboard constructed?

With lots of love of course. But quite a few excel techniques were used too.

![How this dashboard is constructed](https://chandoo.org/img/vp/hui-poster-technique.png)

*   Structurally, this dashboard is similar to my [10,000 comment dashboard](http://chandoo.org/wp/2010/11/24/10k-comments-excel-dashboard/)
    .
*   Word cloud of Hui’s replies is from [wordle.net](http://wordle.net/create)
    
*   Scrollable chart at the bottom is a grid of 20×42 cells [conditionally formatted](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/conditional-formatting/)
    . Scrolling effect thru [OFFSET formula](http://chandoo.org/wp/2008/11/19/vlookup-match-and-offset-explained-in-plain-english-spreadcheats/)
    .
*   [Pivot tables](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/08/19/excel-pivot-tables-tutorial/)
    , a whole lot of them were used to find the summaries I want
*   The header content is [picture links](http://chandoo.org/wp/2010/10/19/how-to-use-picture-links/)
     & [shapes](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/12/03/use-shapes-in-dashboards/)
    .
*   Rest of them are left to your imagination.
