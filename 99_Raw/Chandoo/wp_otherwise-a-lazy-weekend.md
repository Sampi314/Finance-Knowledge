Otherwise a lazy weekend
========================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

Just when i finished publishing the last post [vishy](http://supervish.blogspot.com/)
 buzzed me on Y! and invited me to [NASEOH](http://www.naseoh.org/)
 (an NGO based in mumbai) to participate in an afternoon session with differently-abled people teaching them computers and communication skills. I was in two minds whether to go there or not. But after a hefty lunch at crystal i thought why not.

So there i was boarding an empty sunday local heading towards bandra. But soon the sunday part of the local gone for a toss and my box is full with people people and more people. After performing my best ever getting out act i was on the bandra platform looking out Wayne (my junior in I) who promised me to take to chembur where NASEOH is located.

Soon 3 of us (Wayne, [Puneet](http://sdfn.blogspot.com/)
 and me) were in a BEST bus that is cutitng through most of the mumbai’s poor people and pathetic roads. By the time we changed another transportation mode and reached NASEOH [PG](http://pagalguy.com/)
, [Simba](http://simba.pagalguy.com/)
 and Muneera (yeah, the Muneera Lokhandwala, IMS, CL fame) were already there teaching them how internet works.

Even though I didnt teach them anything, I had good fun watching others do it. Somewhere here [geetu](http://geets.blogspot.com/)
 joined us. Poorthing, she got stuck in a local.

After the classes are over all of us headed to Barista, thanks largely to Vishy’s treat for he got into [WIMWI](http://iimahd.ernet.in/)
.

When i am back in room its already 9. All of a sudden we (hairy, doctor and me) decided to have good food, thanks largely to our weeklong _kanjoos_ eating outs. This time we managed to find the right restaurant. I enjoyed the food verywell.

Back on monday its another hectic day at work. Me will be going to field to do some data collection in a couple of days. God bless the locals.

Tada!

PS: Excuse the pelling mittakes if any.
