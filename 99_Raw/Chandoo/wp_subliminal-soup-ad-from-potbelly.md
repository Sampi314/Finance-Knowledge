 in Washington for lunch and the restaurant instantly became my favorite. Its the kind of place with a soul of its own, you can feel the passion the shop people have shown in every aspect. Of course the food was excellent, I had a chicken salad sandwich, veg garden soup and tons of pleasure.

I couldnt help but awe at this extremely well done subliminal advertising near the soup counter, see it for yourself 🙂
