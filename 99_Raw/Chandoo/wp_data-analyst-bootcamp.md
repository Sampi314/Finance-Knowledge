Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

**Who is this training for?**

This training is perfect for Excel & Power BI beginners & intermediate users. If you work in a data profession (data analyst, reporting analyst or similar) and need to use Excel, Power BI & SQL on a regular basis, you will find this training _extremely_ valuable.

**How this is training is run?**

This training will be thru a Zoom video session. You will be able to see my presentation & explanations as well as share your audio (and screen if you want) when asking questions.

**What software do I need?**

You need:

*   Microsoft Excel (365 or 2016+)
*   Power BI Desktop software (latest version)
*   SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS)
*   Zoom

**What topics are covered?**

This training covers Advanced Excel, SQL & Power BI based data analysis. You will learn a ton of useful, practical concepts developed from my 15+ years of experience using these technologies.

**Do I need any prior skills?**

It is recommended that you have prior basic skills in Excel & Power BI. No prior knowledge of SQL is necessary although a familiarity with databases / SQL is appreciated.

**Can I ask questions?**

The session will be highly interactive. You will have lots of opportunities to share your experiences or ask questions.

**What if I can’t attend part of the training?**

It is _highly_ recommended that you plan to attend all 5 days. While you can watch the recording, you will miss out on the interaction and break-out sessions.
