### Moving on to Excel links..,

**[Show sparklines for hidden data too](http://blog.contextures.com/archives/2012/11/20/show-excel-sparklines-for-hidden-data/)
**  

Debra tells how to setup sparklines for data that is in hidden cells. If you have not tried sparklines, you are missing out on one of the most fun, easy to use charting feature in Excel 2010 / 2013. [Check out this tutorial to get started](http://chandoo.org/wp/2010/05/18/excel-sparklines-tutorial/)
.

**[Interactive tooltips in Excel](http://www.clearlyandsimply.com/clearly_and_simply/2012/11/interactive-tooltips-on-excel-worksheets.html)
**  

Robert shares an elegant mechanism to display rich, interactive tool tips in Excel. Very useful for data heavy dashboards & reports.

**[Book Recommendation: Power Pivot for Analysts](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0789743159/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0789743159&linkCode=as2&tag=poinhairdilb-20)
**  

I just finished reading Bill Jelen’s Power Pivot for Data Analyst, an excellent book for those of us getting started with PowerPivot. It is easy to read with insightful, explanative & humouros commentary of Bill. Make sure you download the example workbooks and practice more.

**[Format Charts Quickly](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/11/30/quick-chart-formatting/)
**  

From our archives, this one shows you how to quickly format charts. Works in Excel 2007 and above.
