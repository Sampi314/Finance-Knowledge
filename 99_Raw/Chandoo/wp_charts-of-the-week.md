**[Democratic or Republic – Which books are Popular?](http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html/?docId=1000261611)
**

![amazon-books-sold-democrat-republican](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/amazon-books-sold-democrat-republican.jpg "amazon-books-sold-democrat-republican")

Amazon has put together the above graph to show last 60 day sales / favorites of red vs. blue books across various states in US. Provides good insights in to what people are reading. Apparently most states are reading RED books. \[via [DSA Insights](http://supportanalytics.com/blog/2008/09/amazons-top-books-by-political-party-mapped-by-bluered-states/)\
\]

[**Mess on Wall Street – Market Capitalization Erosion from Oct 2007 to Sept 2008**](http://www.techcrunch.com/2008/09/16/the-mess-on-wall-street-four-trillion-dollars-down-the-drain/)

![mess-on-wall-street-oct-2007-market-capitalization](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/mess-on-wall-street-oct-2007-market-capitalization.jpg "mess-on-wall-street-oct-2007-market-capitalization")![mess-on-wall-street-sep-2008-market-capitalization](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/mess-on-wall-street-sep-2008-market-capitalization.jpg "mess-on-wall-street-sep-2008-market-capitalization")

This is a square pie of 29 financial companies’ market capitalization. The left side is as of October 2007. You can see how much value is eroded in mere 11 months.

**[Per-capita Spending on Recreation for Each Country](http://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2008/09/04/business/20080907-metrics-graphic.html)
**

![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/money-spent-on-recreation-by-each-country.jpg "money-spent-on-recreation-by-each-country")

Another awesome graphic by NYT, this one shows how much each country spends on various purchase categories. The one you see above is for recreation. I am not sure if the figures are adjusted based on [purchasing power parity](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Purchasing_power_parity)
. \[via [Flowing Data](http://flowingdata.com/2008/09/17/how-consumers-around-the-world-spend-their-money/)\
\]

**[More on US Elections – Article References by Obama and McCain](http://everymomentnow.com/)
**

![us-political-election-references-mccain-obama](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/us-political-election-references-mccain-obama.jpg "us-political-election-references-mccain-obama")

Another election visualization. [EveryMomentNow](http://everymomentnow.com/)
 is a project to capture number of news article references by presidential candidates – McCain & Obama. This pyramid like chart is updated everyday. \[via [Information Aesthetics](http://infosthetics.com/archives/2008/09/every_moment_now_reference_frequency_pyramid.html)\
\]

**[Your life in bubbles – All important moments in life](http://abstrusegoose.com/51)
**

![your-life-in-bubbles](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/your-life-in-bubbles.jpg "your-life-in-bubbles")

What would you get when you create one bubble for every month of your life and then color them based on important events in your life ? The one you are seeing above is cartoon like visualization of 931 months of life and colors mark achievements made in that month by famous people. So in a way it is a visualization of life data of several famous people like Ayn Rand, Einstein etc. \[via [Information Aesthetics](http://infosthetics.com/archives/2008/09/a_blob_for_each_month_in_your_life.html)\
\]

That is all. [Browse archives for more eye candy](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/cool-infographics/)
.
