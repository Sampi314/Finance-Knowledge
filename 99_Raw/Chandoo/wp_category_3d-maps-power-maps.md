Analyzing Regional Trends
-------------------------

As introduced in part 1, our complaints dataset has geographical information too. We know the state & _zip code_ for each complaint. Please note that _zip codes_ are partial or missing for a 10% of the data.

In this article, let’s explore three ways to analyze regional trends.

1.  Regional trends by state, product & issue
2.  Complaints per million by state
3.  Complaints by zip code
