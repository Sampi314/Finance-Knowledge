[Jimmy Carter, erstwhile US President](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_carter "Jimmy Carter, erstwhile US President")
 was in Lonavla this week to build houses as part of his 23rd JC Work Project. [Hindu ran a story](http://www.hindu.com/2006/10/31/stories/2006103101671100.htm "Hindu ran a story")
 on it yesterday. Today [Rediff ran it](http://specials.rediff.com/movies/2006/oct/31sld1.htm "Rediff ran it")
 too. And look at how differently they presented the same item. Huge disappointment comes from knowing that noble intentions are hit insearch of hits.
