**How does Excel School Work?**

Once you pay the course fees and sign up for excel school,

*   You will be given a userid and password to access excel school classroom
*   Once you login to excel school, you will find links to all the material
*   You can view lessons in any order or follow the order recommended by me
*   You can download example excel files, home work and videos (if you sign up for download option) for further learning
*   You can ask questions or discuss topics with other classmates thru comments
*   Once every week, I will send you a news-letter with information, links and discuss course progress

**How much time does it take to finish the lessons?**

Since you can access all the lessons on Day 1, the answer to this question depends on your free time and potential. If you are a fast learner, you can finsih all the lessons in a week. Otherwise you can take up to 4 weeks to finish the lessons. The school remains open for 8 weeks from June 28 (till August 29th) so that you get ample to go thru all the material.

Most lessons in excel school are for 20 minutes. However, there are some lessons that go on for 60 minutes. This is because some topics take more time to explain. At this point, we have material for over 900 minutes in Excel school. So, even if you spend an hour a day, you can take up to 15 days to complete the material.

**What should I do if I have a question?**

Simple, you ask. Each lesson comes with a comments feature that you are so used to on Chandoo.org. You can post your questions and discuss lesson topics in the comments area. I will reply to your questions or point you in right direction so that your question can be answered.

Please note that I am not in a position to solve any specific problems you have related to your work. For that, you can hire me as a consultant.

Yes, for Indian Students the pricing will be in INR. For online+ download option, pricing is INR 2000 and for online option it is INR 1400. Please sign up for my notification list (form above) and I will send you details once I have the registrations open.

**Can I drop off / cancel my course and get a refund?**

Sure you can. **Each excel school membership comes with 15 day money back guarantee**. So if you do not like what you see in the classroom, you can leave and I will refund the full money. No questions asked. It is that simple.

**What if I get too busy after signing up and cannot spend time on this?**

The classroom will be open for 2 months. So you might find sometime in all the days between June 28 and August 29 to finish the lessons.

In the off chance that you could not spend any time in this period on excel school, you have 2 options:

1.  Use the downloadable videos to back up entire school and then view them whenever you get time.
2.  Opt in to stay back in school for some more time (by paying nominal monthly fee)
