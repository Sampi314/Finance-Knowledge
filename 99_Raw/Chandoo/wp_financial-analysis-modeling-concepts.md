### What is in this session?

In this podcast,

*   Quick announcement about [Awesome August](http://chandoo.org/wp/resources/awesome-august/)
    
*   5 key finance concepts
    *   Time value of money
    *   Compound interest
    *   Risk free rate of return
    *   Net Present Value – NPV

*   Case study – Uber vs. Your car
*   Conclusions
