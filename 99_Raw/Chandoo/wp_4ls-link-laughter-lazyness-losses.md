**Laughter**

seen [Hera Pheri](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0242519/)
 last night. Awesome movie. Was lughing like hell throught out the movie. The mere thought of Baburao (Paresh Rawal) saying “Yeh _Kabeera Speaking_ bahut achha naam hai” is making me laugh.

**Lazyness**

I am again at one of my crazy spells of lazyness. everynow and then i feel so lazy that even taking bath (given the fact that i have time) or attending classes or forthat matter having snakcs doesnot motivate me to move out of my room. If any of you have a feeling that life in IIMs or any b-school is tough, let me take the opportunity to break that illusion.

After watching Hera Pheri last night, at about 12 i have crashed for no particular reason. well, there is lot of fin and costing to read. but i crashed. I woke up at 7:30. without even brushing my teeth, i went on to solve the costing problems. we need to submit them in class today. once the problem is solved i thought i will take a small break by sleeping again. That is it. 9:25. potti woke me up. I told him that i want to bunk Fin class. Then i woke up again at noon. Then i went to sleep again. atlast i woke up at 1:20 pm. Aftertaking lunch, again at 4 pm i have crashed. Woke up at 6:30. after cleaning the wardrobe by dumping the clothes in washing machine and taking a long bath and dinner now i am updating my blog as if i have no care in my life. :D. well, to put things mildly we have a couple of submisssions lined up in the next few days. also the mids. also the classes. also the quizzes. High time i woke from this sleep.

**Losses**

To the discerning user it might occur that we have made losses in the stock market. well, not to a big tune. as of now our losses stood at 0.75%. well, did some research and bought more shares of a particular bank. lets hope that we make our first profits.

That is all for now. Me going back to books.
