Mute conversations in Gmail

**Do you know that you can mute conversations in gmail** if you are do not want to see that lengthy thread on single subjected that people wont give up.

*   Just select the mail / conversation you want to mute
*   Click on “More actions” and select “mute”
*   Enjoy the bliss

**If you want search for muted conversations use “is:muted” search operator.** Read up more on muting conversations at [Gmail Help](http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=47787)
