### Who is this boot-camp for?

**This boot-camp is aimed at business analysts, financial analysts & managers, who use Excel all the time**. This boot-camp provides content on intermediate & advanced level Excel. So if you are an absolute newbie, you might find it difficult to follow the boot-camp.
