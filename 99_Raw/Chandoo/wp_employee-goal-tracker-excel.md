_**Marko**_, who is a long time PHD reader and an excel ninja sent this via email,

> I work at an insurance company in Slovenia. At the beginning of each year we have a conversation with our superiors to review our work in the past year and to set new goals (main activities) that we’re gonna work on throughout the year. To keep track of these activities I took the liberty of modifying Jennie’s [A New Year Resolutions Template that Kicks Ass](http://chandoo.org/wp/2010/01/07/new-year-resolutions-template/)
>  and created a more “dynamic” template.

_**He sent me this:**_

![Annual Employee Goal Sheet Tracker - Excel](https://chandoo.org/img/c/annual-goalsheet-tracker.gif)

I immediately liked this implementation, for various reasons. This example shows how we can combine various powerful features in MS Excel to create something truly unique and outstanding. It uses,

![Goal sheet Tracker - Excel Features Used](https://chandoo.org/img/c/annual-goals-tracker-detail.png)

*   Conditional formatting to highlight rows when the activity is 100% complete. \[[learn how to](http://chandoo.org/wp/2008/03/13/want-to-be-an-excel-conditional-formatting-rock-star-read-this/ "conditional formatting highlight rows")\
    \]
*   Formulas to show check boxes when the activity is done. \[[learn how to](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/09/15/get-tick-marks-in-excel/ "excel checkboxes")\
    \]
*   A thermometer chart to show the progress against the target. \[[learn how to](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/12/17/quick-thermometer-chart/ "thermometer charts")\
    \]
*   An elegant date formula to show how many days are remaining to finish the goals. \[[learn how to](http://chandoo.org/wp/tag/date-and-time/ "Date and Time in Excel")\
    \]
*   And scrollbar form controls to adjust the % completion values. \[[learn how to](http://chandoo.org/wp/2010/01/20/mortgage-payment-calculator/ "Scrollbar Form Control in Excel")\
    \]
