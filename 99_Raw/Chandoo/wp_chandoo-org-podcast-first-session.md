2.  Subscribe thru Stictcher, Blackberry or Zune Podcasting directories: The process of adding our podcast to these listings is underway. I will share links once we are ready.
3.  **[Subscribe to our Podcast RSS](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/podcast/feed/)
    ** This is ready. Just click on the [Podcast RSS link](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/podcast/feed/)
     and add it to your feed reader. You get new podcast episodes right to your computer / tablet.

     This is better. You get not just podcast sessions, but regular Excel tips, templates and tutorials too.
5.  **Just visit Chandoo.org regularly**. Whenever we have a new podcast, you can just listen to it right here. **Book mark our [podcast page](http://chandoo.org/wp/category/podcast/)
     too**.
