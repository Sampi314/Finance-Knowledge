All you need to do is to tell me what the above photo is about. Last night I got crazy and managed to get this shot. Answers tomorrow :D.

**Answer:** It is my monitor running “starfield simulation” screen saver. The other day I was about to sleep and the idea came to my mind. What would a screen saver look like if i expose it for a long enough duration. I switched off all the lights in the room. Made a temporary tri-pod with the help of all the books I got here so far. Then I ran the starfield screensaver with decent number of stars and took a photo with exposure of 10 secs. The above photo is when I tried to move the camera here and there while its exposed.
