Innovative New year calendar from Kotak
=======================================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

Check out this pocket calendar released by Kotak Life Insurance. Its simple and effective. Remember those trick watches we used to play with when we were kids that have only 2 times and when you tilt the watch you get to see the other time. The calendar is based on that funda shows “bulls you win” in one angle and “bears you win” in another angle. Must say very good application.
