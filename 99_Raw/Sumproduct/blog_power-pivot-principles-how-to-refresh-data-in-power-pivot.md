Power Pivot Principles: How to Refresh Data in Power Pivot
==========================================================

25 September 2018

_Welcome back to our Power Pivot blog. Today, we go over how to refresh data in Power Pivot._

To refresh data in your model, navigate to the ‘Power Pivot’ tab on the Ribbon.

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/e774d10cbbb9450fc45efbe51abdf434-562.jpg)

Click on the ‘Manage’ option in the ‘Data Model’ group.

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/f32e5a15e2cf9c3e4d2d058458ce054d-584.jpg)

In the ‘Power Pivot for Excel’ dialog box, click on the drop-down icon on the ‘Refresh’ option on the Ribbon.

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/f1140ff857fc3b6f5f97a6a24f4a6fc7-540.jpg)

Then select the ‘Refresh All’ option:

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/72aa864d2854c6fefb1083fba0ab5792-492.jpg)

The ‘Data Refresh’ dialog box will appear displaying the refresh stats of each connection source.

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/36776d1da4d05b45bb5a5d09375f407c-410.jpg)

With that our data has been refreshed. Was that a refreshing change?
