Financial Modelling Book Out Soon
=================================

5 July 2018

It’s taken a while (due to error removals), but SumProduct is proud to announce that _Introduction to Financial Modelling by Liam Bastick (edited by Tim Heng)_ is coming out this month. Indeed, we have received a limited number of copies already and we will be fulfilling existing orders shortly.

It’s been a labour of something (love they say, but we are not convinced…) and contains 325 pages explaining the financial modelling basics so that you can get started building models in a professional manner without falling for the usual traps, trials and tribulations.

If you would like your very own copy, email [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
 with the subject “FINANCIAL MODELLING BOOK” and we will be in contact to coordinate payment and delivery. Prices are from US$50, A$65, €40 and UK£35, plus postage and packaging. Payment accepted via PayPal only.

Happy reading!!
