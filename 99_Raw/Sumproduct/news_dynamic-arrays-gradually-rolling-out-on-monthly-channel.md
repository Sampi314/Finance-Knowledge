Dynamic Arrays Gradually Rolling Out on Monthly Channel
=======================================================

28 November 2019

Microsoft has begun the “gradual rollout” of [\>dynamic arrays](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought/getting-arrays-spilling-the-beans-on-seven-new-functions)
 on the monthly channel of Office 365. This is great news, although Microsoft will not be drawn regarding how long this rollout will take to complete as they are “…\[monitoring\] the feature health and user feedback…”.

Hopefully, it will be too long. It’s also noted that dynamic arrays are still offline on Excel Online – there has been no further update as to when these coll features will be back. Keep watching this space and we will keep you informed!
