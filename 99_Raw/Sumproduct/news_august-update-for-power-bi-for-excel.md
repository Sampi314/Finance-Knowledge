August Update for Power BI for Excel
====================================

26 August 2016

Microsoft has continued its regular updates of [Power BI Publisher for Microsoft Excel](http://www.powerbi.com/)
 with its [late August update](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/august-update-of-power-bi-publisher-for-excel/)
. With this latest release, you can now:

*   connect to data in read-only groups
*   connect to reports and datasets that were shared with you
*   identify shared content easily
*   filter out shared content by clicking on the **Hide content that is shared with me** option.

Additionally, this update addresses several known issues and glitches which Microsoft addresses in its documentation [here](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/en-us/documentation/powerbi-desktop-troubleshooting-analyze-in-excel/?#cant-drag-anything-to-the-pivottable-values-area-no-measures)
.

As always, you can find out more about Power BI by visiting the dedicated website [www.powerbi.com](http://www.powerbi.com/)
 or by dropping us a line at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproducct.com)
.
