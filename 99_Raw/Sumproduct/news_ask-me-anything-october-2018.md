Ask Me Anything October 2018
============================

18 October 2017

It’s that time of the year again. Microsoft are having another of their “Ask Me Anything” (AMA) sessions. From the team that designs, implements and tests Excel across each of their platforms (Windows desktop, Windows mobile, Mac, iOS, Android and the Web), they are reddy (deliberately spelt that way!) and willing to answer your questions.

They have an army of people from the Excel team with deep product experience ready to answer your questions. You may recall they did this last year and they have just about got over the trauma! They state that they wish to focus on answering questions that pertain to Excel usage, its various platforms and the Excel team. So no questions on fastest land animals or whether you should fake your organisms.

There may be questions that come up around bugs, feature requests, what’s new, training and resources. So it may well be worth your while tuning in. You can link to the session [here](https://www.reddit.com/r/excel/comments/74ytcd/microsoft_excel_product_team_ama_confirmed_for/)
 as it runs from 9:30am PST / 12:30 EDT (US times) on Wednesday 18 October. Go try and stump them! Let us know how you get on…
