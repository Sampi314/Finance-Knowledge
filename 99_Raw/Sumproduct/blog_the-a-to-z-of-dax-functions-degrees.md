**_The_** _**DEGREES**_ **_function_**

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

The _**DEGREES**_ function is one of the mathematics and trigonometry functions. It converts radians into degrees. It has the following syntax:

**DEGREE(angle)**

It has one \[1\] argument in the syntax:

*   **angle**: this is required. It is an angle in radians that you want to convert.
