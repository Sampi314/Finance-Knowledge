Xmas Quiz 2014 Now Live!
========================

3 December 2014

Further to our last post, SumProduct teams up with the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (**CIMA**) to deliver a Christmas Quiz based on an Excel problem for its **Insight** magazine.

We gave advanced notice back in late November and now we’re giving our Newsreaders the link. We hope you find it an enjoyable pursuit – there’s treasure to be had. The first prize will be a GBP100 Amazon voucher, the second a GBP75 Amazon voucher and the third prize one for GBP50. Please read the challenge carefully though as it’s not quite as simple as you might first think!

The Quiz may be found at [Spreadsheet Skills Christmas Quiz](http://www.cimaglobal.com/Thought-leadership/Newsletters/Insight-e-magazine/Insight-2014/Insight-December-2014/Christmas-quiz-spreadsheet-skills/)
. Be quick though – entries close **Friday 19 December 2014**.

Good luck! Feel free to also check out the rest of the CIMA website at [www.cimaglobal.com](http://www.cimaglobal.com/)
.
