New York Office
===============

13 June 2016

SumProduct is excited to announce the opening of our New York office at long last! With various training work and past clients such as Tiger Capital and Microsoft our new New York office allows us to better connect and assist our current and future American clients.

Our office is located at 48 Wall Street, in between Federal Hall and the New York Stock Exchange. Also local, is Deutsche Bank and the Museum of American Finance. We are delighted to be sharing this news with you all and hope to talk with some of you very soon!

For all enquiries please see our contact details below:

Address: 48 Wall Street, New York, NY, USA 10005
