Course Overview
---------------

#### **Overview:**

This course demonstrates tools and features available in Excel to help create dashboards and charts in a fraction of the time you might previously have taken.  Furthermore, it highlights the importance of conveying the key message and not losing sight of the proverbial trees hidden in the corporate forest of data.  

#### **Delivered:**

Online, enrolment for 12 months.  

#### **Course prerequisites:**

While there are no entry requirements a basic understanding of Microsoft Excel is recommended.

A completion certificate is issued by SumProduct upon successful completion of the course.
