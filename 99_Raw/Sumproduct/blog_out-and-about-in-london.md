Out and About in London
=======================

30 November 2016

Early December sees us in London for client meetings, presentations on Power BI and Excel Tricks & Tips (5 and 6 December) for Microsoft at the Global Training Camp and possibly taking in the finals of ModelOff as well (we’re still waiting on our invite there…). Congratulations to last year’s winner and fellow Aussie **Joseph Lau** who has made it through to the Final 20 yet again!

If you are in the area (see [here](http://www.gtc.modeloff.com/london/)
 for further details), drop in and say hello. If you’d like to meet up, let us know beforehand by emailing us at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
