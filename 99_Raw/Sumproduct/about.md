CRaFT, Our Proprietary System
-----------------------------

We believe a good financial model should exhibit four key qualities: consistency, robustness, flexibility, and transparency.

**C**onsistency is achieved through structured modelling, which reduces errors and enhances reliability.

**R**obustness is ensured by keeping models simple, strong, and auditable, eliminating hidden complexities and incorporating error checks.

**F**lexibility is provided by interactive insights, allowing key assumptions to be adjusted within defined boundaries for deeper exploration.

**T**ransparency is maintained through clear structures and consistent formulas, fostering trust in the model’s results.
