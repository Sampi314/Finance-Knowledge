New Features for Excel May 2025
===============================

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-15-1024x187.png)

This month introduces another raft of improvements, the majority of which revolve around Copilot (perhaps Microsoft is not going to make it so intrusive?). 

Here is this month’s list:

**_Excel for Windows_**

*   Clean Data with Copilot in Excel

**_Excel for Windows, Mac and web_**

*   Think Deeper in Copilot in Excel with Python
*   Text analysis with Copilot in Excel

**_Excel for Windows and web_**

*   Hear Copilot responses out loud

**_Excel for Mac_**

*   Forms integration for Mac

**_Excel for web_**

*   Show changes – extended lineage.
