Microsoft Press: Discount on Books
==================================

24 November 2016

Need to Excel at Excel? Why not get it from the horse’s mouth (so to speak)?

Microsoft Press is offering a discount on its books and eBooks as part of the Black Friday sales initiative. If you buy one book, you save 35% on the Recommended Retail Price (RRP). Two or more gives you 55% off. All you have to do is visit the [store](https://www.microsoftpressstore.com/promotions/black-friday-2016-141287?MC=MachLearn&MC=MSAzure&MC=Open&MC=SQL&MC=Windows)
 and use the discount code **SHOPNOW** during checkout to apply the appropriate discount.
