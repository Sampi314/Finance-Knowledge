Copilot in Power BI Mobile: Now Available for iPads and Android Tablets
=======================================================================

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Copilot support is now available in the Power BI Mobile applications on iPads and Android tablets, extending the functionality of AI to more mobile devices.  Introduced for phones several months ago, Copilot in the Power BI Mobile apps empowered users to quickly analyse data, gain insights and make informed decisions on the go.  Now, the same powerful capabilities have come to iPads and Android tablets, making it easier to explore your data anytime, anywhere.  Roll out the martinis.

Getting started with Copilot on your mobile app is easy: simply tap the Copilot button located in the report header (for reports that meet Copilot requirements in Power BI).  From there, you can choose whether to receive a summary or uncover insights.  Copilot will deliver a response based on your request, which you can then copy, share or continue interacting with.  Suggestions at the bottom of the screen help you refine your request or create new ones, making it simple to explore your data further.
