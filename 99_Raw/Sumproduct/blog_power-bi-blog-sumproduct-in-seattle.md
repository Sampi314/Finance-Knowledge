Power BI Blog: SumProduct in Seattle!
=====================================

19 July 2018

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

We’re taking a break from our regular Power BI blog series to let you know that our Sydney Director, Tim Heng, is heading this weekend to Seattle to attend the Microsoft Business Applications Summit! In this two day conference, he’ll be hearing about the latest advancements to Power BI that are coming up in the next few months, along with other new and improving technologies in the Business Applications sphere such as Flow and PowerApps.

While he’s there, he’ll also be catching up with members of the Excel and Power BI teams, so if you have any questions that you’d love to ask them, send them to [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
, and we’ll see if we can’t find the answers for you!
