AI Blog: Claude for Excel– Basics, Setup and Licence Requirements
=================================================================

_Welcome back to our AI blog series!  Today we explore Claude for Excel, a new add-in from Anthropic that brings natural-language intelligence directly into your workbook.  In this article, we introduce what it is, how to install it and what licence requirements we need to be aware of._

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

**_What Is Claude for Excel?_**

Claude for Excel is an add-in that integrates Anthropic’s Claude directly into Microsoft Excel, enabling us to converse with our entire workbook using plain English.  As the official help article explains, _“Claude for Excel is an add-in that integrates Claude into your Excel workflow… designed for professionals who work extensively with spreadsheets, particularly in financial analysis and modelling.”_

In short, Claude becomes a collaborator inside the spreadsheet rather than a separate chat window.

**_Key Features_**

Claude for Excel can:

*   answer questions about specific cells, formulae and whole worksheets, returning cell-level citations so we can verify the logic
*   update key assumptions without breaking downstream dependencies
*   diagnose errors such as _#REF!_, _#VALUE!_or circular references and trace them back to their source
*   help build new models or complete templates while retaining structure and formatting.

For users working across multi-tab financial models, these features offer clarity and significant time savings.

Just image that we are asked to a analyse a financial report with many cross-references, we might ask Claude, “Which assumptions drive the Q3 revenue forecast?”.

**_Setup and Installation_  
**

For individual users to get started, open Excel, activate the add-in via **Insert -> Add-ins** or **Home -> Add-ins**, then sign in using your Claude account.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

For Organisations (Administrators), IT administrators can deploy the add-in centrally:

*   In the Microsoft 365 Admin Centre, navigate to **Settings -> Integrated apps -> Add-ins**
*   Search for Claude by Anthropic for Excel in AppSource
*   Deploy the add-in organisation-wide or to selected groups
*   Share sign-in instructions with the team.

**Technical Specifications**

*   Supported formats: **.xlsx** and **.xlsm**
*   After installation, a Claude sidebar appears in Excel for conversational analysis
*   Works within standard Microsoft 365 security and governance controls.

**_Licence and Access Requirements_**

Claude for Excel is currently in **beta**, offered as a research preview with limited access.  Anthropic notes: “Claude for Excel is available in beta as a research preview to Max, Team and Enterprise plan customers.  We’ll gradually expand access as we build confidence through this limited preview”.

**_Word to the Wise_**

Claude for Excel opens the door to faster model reviews, clearer formula tracing and quicker assumption updates.  It is an intelligent assistant inside the workbook, not a replacement for professional judgment.  Complex or specialised models may still require full manual oversight, especially those reliant on macros, PivotTables and VBA (Visual Basic for Applications).

If your team is thinking about using Claude for Excel, it’s a good idea to start with a small group first.  Set up clear ways to check and review work and make some basic rules for how you’ll use it.  If used properly, Claude for Excel can help your team work faster and make modelling easier.
