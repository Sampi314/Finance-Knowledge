Office 365 Adoption Content Pack in Power BI Preview
====================================================

Office 365 Adoption Content Pack in Power BI Preview
====================================================

24 October 2016

Now that’s a mouthful.

Understanding how your users adopt and use Office 365 may be mission critical for your business. The new Office 365 admin center _(sic)_ provides usage reports which can help you understand business usage. However, the ways you can slice and dice the data are restrictive but this is where Power BI comes in…

To provide you with richer and more personalised usage insights, Microsoft has provided a preview combining the intelligence of the usage reports with the interactive reporting capabilities of Power BI. The new Office 365 adoption content pack enables you to visualise and analyse Office 365 usage data, create custom reports and share the insights within your organisation and pivot by attributes such as location and department.

Last week saw a limited preview release of the adoption content pack is now available for Office 365 customers, with an envisaged general access roll-out in December.
