Out and About in Seattle, San Francisco, and Kuala Lumpur
=========================================================

4 November 2016

The next week our director Liam Bastick from the Melbourne office will be over in Seattle attending the MVP summit, after making a quick stop at San Francisco (just for another ride on the cable cars of course). He will then be off to Kuala Lumpur the week after, running a Project Finance training course.

Our Sydney director Tim Heng will also be on the move, and will be in Darwin on the 9th to the 10th of November, presenting at the CPA Congress!

In the meantime, if you’d like to catch up with Liam or Tim while they are out and about, drop us a line at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
