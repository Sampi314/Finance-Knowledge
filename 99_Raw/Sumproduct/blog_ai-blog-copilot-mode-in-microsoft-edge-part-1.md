AI Blog: Copilot Mode in Microsoft Edge – Part 1
================================================

_Welcome back to our AI blog series!  After transforming how we work inside Excel and Word, Microsoft has now placed Copilot directly inside the browser.  Copilot Mode in Microsoft Edge turns a traditional web experience into an interactive workspace where we can learn, plan, compare and act all in one view._

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

**_What Is Copilot Mode in Microsoft Edge?_**

Copilot Mode lets us use plain English to interact with the web.  Instead of searching, clicking and switching between multiple tabs, we can simply ask:

_“Summarise this page”, “Compare what is in my tabs” or “Draft a short report”._

Copilot reads the context from the page we are viewing and delivers structured results without breaking our flow.  Microsoft describes it as “AI at the point of thought”.

**_Contextual Search and Learning Assistance_**

Copilot understands what we are reading and can turn curiosity into an organised plan.  For example, suppose we want to improve our Excel skills by reading blogs published on the SumProduct website.   First, open the site and then ask:

_“Find Excel-function courses and blogs from SumProduct, then create a four-week study plan”._

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Within seconds, Copilot lists recommended articles and courses, arranges them week by week and links directly to each source.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Furthermore, it offers suggestions for future actions, including transforming this plan into a downloadable PDF and incorporating quizzes or exercises for each week.

**_Smart Shopping and Voice Interaction_**

Instead of typing queries, you can now speak to Copilot using the microphone icon.  It listens, interprets and acts. 

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Let’s say we’re planning to bake a cheesecake.  We open the tabs for cheesecake recipe tips, Coles and Woolworths, click the voice icon and say:

_“Find a cheesecake recipe, check ingredient prices at Coles and Woolworths, compare the cost and create a shopping list.  Choose healthy options if possible and add them to my shopping bag”._

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Copilot will search for suitable recipes, compare ingredient prices across the two \[2\] retailers, highlight the cheapest and healthiest alternatives and produce a neatly itemised list ready for checkout.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

According to Microsoft’s introductory video for this new browsing method, Copilot can even add products directly to a shopping cart.  However, Coles and Woolworths currently restrict external tools from accessing their baskets due to privacy and security considerations.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

**_Why It Matters_  
**Copilot Mode in Microsoft Edge changes how we browse from searching and reading to asking and doing.  Whether studying, shopping or researching, it saves time by turning multiple actions into one intelligent conversation.

**_Word to the Wise_**

Copilot Mode in Microsoft Edge represents another step in Microsoft’s journey to embed AI across everyday tools.  By understanding page context, summarising content and even comparing live data, it helps us move seamlessly from exploration to action without leaving the browser.
