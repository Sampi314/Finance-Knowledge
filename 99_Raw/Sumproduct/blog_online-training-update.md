Online Training Update
======================

6 July 2016

Aside from keeping up with the Australian Election, we’re sure you’d like to know that our online training program is well underway!

With a trial release to a few clients several days ago, we are in the process of receiving feedback from those who are having a go at our first release. We are continuing to build on that initial release, and we are adding on more content, improving the courses and developing more creative ways to make online learning engaging for users.

While we’re developing new content to go along with our Excel Tips and Tricks launch course, we’ve been receiving requests to set up online training on PivotTables and Power BI, as well as our popular Financial Modelling course. If there’s anything that you’d like to see though, let us know, and we can make sure we have something ready for you soon!

Keep checking back here for updates, and you’ll be the first to know when it’s officially launched.
