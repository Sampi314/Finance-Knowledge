==================

London Trip Update
==================

12 December 2016

Penultimate trip of the year over! SumProduct’s Liam Bastick has been on the road for the past four months and may be looking forward to the Christmas break, but he still had work to do in London setting up training courses and conferences for 2017 (more on that next year) as well as presenting at ModelOff’s Global Training Camo down in Docklands.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Liam presented in his usual indomitable style **_An Introduction to Power BI_** and **_Excel Tricks and Tips_**, in its usual “free flow” style. He also got to see Australia’s **Joseph Lau** become the first ever “back to back” financial modelling world champion and he looks forward to congratulating him in Sydney next week – if he can get through the autograph hunters and Hollywood producers. Congratulations Joseph!

Don’t forget we are in London the whole time – SumProduct has an office on site already with staff happy to take your queries / calls. And Liam’s even threatening to go back soon too…
