Bringing Free (Down)Loaders to Book
===================================

13 July 2017

Microsoft’s Eric Ligman, Director – Sales Excellence, has today announced their annual free Microsoft eBook giveaway. This year’s event has more freebies than in any previous year. These books are on a range of Microsoft-related topics – and to our readers, the Excel ones may seem quite small and basic – but they’re free!!

There are large number of informational resources here, including simple eBooks, reference guides, step-by-step guides, etc. Each year this seems to get bigger and better and you can pick and choose what you want [here](https://sumproduct.com/assets/news-graphics/free-books-july-2017.pdf)
 – if you want you can simply get everything…
