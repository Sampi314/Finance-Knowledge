Final Friday Fix: June 2025 Challenge
=====================================

_On the final Friday of each month, we set an Excel / Power Pivot / Power Query / Power BI problem for you to puzzle over for the weekend.  On the Monday, we publish a solution.  If you think there is an alternative answer, feel free to email us.  We’ll feel free to ignore you._

**_The Challenge_**

This month’s challenge is to design a set of grade icons, formatting and assign corresponding letter grades based upon a list of student marks supplied.  A list of marks and the corresponding grade ranges to guide your design are provided as an example below:

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-51.png)

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-52.png)

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-53.png)

The single-column dataset **Mark** contained a list of marks.  Your challenge was to assign the correct background colour, icon and grade according to the **tbl\_Grade** table, as shown in the dataset at the bottom.

You can download the original question file [here](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/SP-Format-Grades-Challenge.xlsx)
.

The requirements for this month’s challenge are as follows:

*   the formatting must be dynamic, meaning it should update automatically should the marks change
*   you cannot use VBA, Office Scripts or any other programming code.

_Sounds easy?  Then why not have a go?  We’ll publish one solution in Monday’s blog.  Have a great weekend in the meantime!_
