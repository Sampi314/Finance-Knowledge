Microsoft Excel World Championship on ESPN
==========================================

2 November 2022

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Tomorrow, SumProduct’s very own **Tim Heng** and **Liam Bastick** are both taking part in the Microsoft Excel World Championship 2022, albeit in two _very_ different roles.

Tim will be a commentator for ESPN Sports whilst Liam shall be a competitor who has successfully progressed to Round 2 where he’ll be competing as one of the last 32 challengers.

This is the first time Liam has entered and to put it mildly, he’s not exactly confident! Watch him and have a laugh with him anyway; at least he’s tried!

So tune in This Thursday at 7:00pm, Sydney (AEDT).. and watch Tim laugh as Liam endeavours!

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0G8umOnbwFE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0G8umOnbwFE)

Good Luck Liam – SumProduct is cheering for you!
