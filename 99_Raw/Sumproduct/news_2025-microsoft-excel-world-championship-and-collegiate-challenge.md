2025 Microsoft Excel World Championship and Collegiate Challenge
================================================================

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-75.png)

_Diarmuid Early finally wins the MEWC!_

The 2025 Microsoft Excel World Championship (MEWC) and Collegiate Challenge (MECC) wrapped up their season in early December at the Las Vegas’ HyperX Arena, drawing competitors from all over the globe and crowning new champions in the fast-growing world of Excel esports. 

SumProduct’s **Tim Heng** and **Liam Bastick** were both in attendance along with many Excel MVPs, competitors, students, sponsors and Microsoft Excel representatives.

Congratulations to winners INSCAE in the MECC – Teams final, Pieter Pienaar in the MECC – Individuals final and fellow MVP Diarmuid Early in the MEWC final.  The standard only ever seems to go up and up: even those who missed out were winners in our eyes.

You can read more about the event in our January newsletter.  Subscribe now if you haven’t already.
