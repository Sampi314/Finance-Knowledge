Welcome to Our New Blog

6 June 2016

SumProduct welcomes you to our new SumProduct Blog page! Here we aim to inform our clients, colleagues and followers about Excel features and functions, updating you on what’s happening at SumProduct and announcing any exciting news we might have.

We will also be presenting informal technical items such as our ongoing A to Z of Excel Functions series, so make sure to keep an eye out for that starting very soon…

Want to keep up to date? Make sure to check back on our regular blogs, news and Thought articles and subscribe to our monthly Newsletter, where we keep you up to date with all things SumProduct.
