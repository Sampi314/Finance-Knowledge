Course Overview
---------------

#### **Overview:**

This practical course provides you with the analysis that can be built into forecasting and budgeting.  Using simple statistical tools and techniques, learn a pragmatic approach to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of your forecasting to develop a greater understanding of key business drivers.  

#### **Delivered:**

Online, enrolment for 12 months.  

#### **Course prerequisites:**

While there are no entry requirements a basic understanding of Microsoft Excel and accounting is recommended.  

#### **Outcome:**

A completion certificate is issued by SumProduct upon successful completion of the course.
