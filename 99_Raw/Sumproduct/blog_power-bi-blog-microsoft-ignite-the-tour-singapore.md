Power BI Blog: Microsoft Ignite | The Tour Singapore
====================================================

17 January 2019

_This week, there are no new tips and tricks, but that’s because we’re presenting at Microsoft Ignite – The Tour over in Singapore!_

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

This week is a quieter one in the SumProduct office, as our Sydney Director, Tim Heng, is over in Singapore presenting at Microsoft Ignite | The Tour! He’s running a session on Thursday morning on Power BI, Excel and Office 365: Unlocking the Value of your Enterprise Data, and catching up with clients and contacts while he’s there. If you’ve got a question to ask or if you happen to be in town, why not reach out at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
 and get in touch?

Tim is presenting in one of the morning sessions, but he’ll be around throughout the day. Look for him at the Meet The Experts booth if you’re attending the conference!

In case you’re not aware, the Microsoft Ignite | The Tour events are scheduled around the world, with a focus on growing skills and exploring new technologies in the world of intelligent data processing and collaboration. They’re also free, so if The Tour is coming around to your city, make sure to go check it out!
