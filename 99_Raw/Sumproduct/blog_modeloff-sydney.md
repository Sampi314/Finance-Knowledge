ModelOff Sydney
===============

11 September 2017

This week in Sydney, ModelOff hosted their Global Training Camp, featuring speakers from Microsoft and several other companies, including our very own Tim Heng. Alongside Carlos Otero and Mark Traverso from Microsoft, Tim ran two presentations on how to make the most of DAX and Power Pivot, as well as highlighting a range of tips and tricks in Excel generally.

Thank you to Microsoft and ModelOff for getting us involved, and looking forward to the next one!

If you have any questions about Tim’s presentations or if you generally have a question on anything Excel related, please feel free to get in touch!
