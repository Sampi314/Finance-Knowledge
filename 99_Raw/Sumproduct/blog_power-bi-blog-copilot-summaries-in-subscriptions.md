_Welcome back to this week’s edition of the Power BI blog series. This week, we look at Copilot’s presence in subscriptions._

If you need to extract insights from Power BI report images in your email or quickly digest a summary of your Power BI report, you might wish to consider subscribing to Copilot summaries for Power BI reports. This feature is available with Standard subscriptions and for reports in a copilot-eligible capacity.

You may set up the Copilot summaries for Power BI reports that you wish to subscribe to as follows:

*   Select the ‘Subscribe’ option from the Ribbon for the Power BI report that is of interest

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

*   Select ‘Standard Subscription’

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

*   Subscribe to the report

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

*   Add a Copilot summary to the email delivered by the subscription. If you are eligible, your subscription will receive the Copilot summary by default

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

*   You can ‘Preview summary’ to view a sample of the suggested summary for review

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

*   Test your subscription by selecting ‘Send Now’ after you Save the subscription.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)
