Happy Spreadsheet Day 2025!
===========================

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Not long after Excel’s 40th birthday, we are already celebrating again!  Fellow Excel MVP **Debra Dalgleish** instigated “Spreadsheet Day” back in 2010 as October 17, celebrating 46 years to the day since VisiCalc (the world’s first electronic spreadsheet) started production.  It put spreadsheets, er, on the grid! _\[Stop now – Ed.\]_

Before spreadsheets, recalculating a budget meant everything had to be written and / or performed manually.  Suddenly, the grid’s calculation engine, formulae and charts were available to the masses.  A forerunner to Excel, VisiCalc (sadly, I remember it!) allowed the start of the spreadsheet revolution, leading to the empowerment of finance, engineering, economics, mathematics – even art:
