Continuing Financial Modelling – Out Now on Kindle
==================================================

1 September 2020

We’re back for Round 2… Following on from [An Introduction to Financial Modelling](https://sumproduct.com/news/article/news/financial-modelling-book-out-now)
, our second book has been released.

SumProduct is proud to announce that **_Continuing Financial Modelling by Liam Bastick (edited by Tim Heng)_** is out now (September 1).

Continuing from where the last book ended, this weighty tome contains _c_.400 pages detailing some of the things not mentioned in the first book. Topics include what-if? analysis, discussions on inventory modelling, depreciation modelling, debt sculpting, valuations modelling, rolling budgets and charts, and some of the very newest features coming out of Excel, amongst other things.

If you would like your very own copy, due to current pandemic circumstances, the physical release has been understandably delayed, but the Kindle version will still be available on [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Continuing-Financial-Modelling-Working-Industry/dp/1615470689)
 websites for the time being (the link is to the US website, but it is available [elsewhere](https://www.amazon.com.au/Continuing-Financial-Modelling-Working-Industry-ebook/dp/B08GGB8VNJ)
 too).

Happy reading – and watch out for Volume 3!!
