Excel Mastery: Practical Training Workshops
-------------------------------------------

All of our courses are presented from a practical first-hand perspective, using the most experienced trainers in the business.  Rather than delivering formal lectures, we have developed discursive workshops and provide many practical examples to work through.

Have you ever wondered why some people are better than others at using Excel? Better, of course, is subjective – if we narrow it down, maybe you want to:

*   Navigate your way around spreadsheets faster
*   Learn to use new and existing key Excel features
*   Understand how particular functions work
*   Develop user friendly and intuitive models
*   Apply your modelling skills to solve a wide range of business problems

If any of these appeal to you or your team, then you probably want to check out some of the training courses that we offer. We run hands-on training course that focus on these sorts of practical issues faced by business professionals on a day-to-day basis. Rather than delivering formal lectures, we engage classes with interactive workshops based on real-life problems that we have solved for clients in the past.

Our trainers are all experienced professionals, with over 10 years’ of experience. Each one brings the depth of their knowledge to a course, gathered from across many industries and sectors. Not just trainers, they also build and audit models as part of our consulting services and as such, stay up to speed with the current issues and ideas in Excel and modelling generally.
