Final Friday Fix: August 2025 Challenge
=======================================

_On the final Friday of each month, we set an Excel / Power Pivot / Power Query / Power BI problem for you to puzzle over for the weekend.  On the Monday, we publish a solution.  If you think there is an alternative answer, feel free to email us.  We’ll feel free to ignore you._

**_The Challenge_**

This month’s Final Friday Fix invites you to return the final weekdays of each month of the year in 2025.  Conscious “weekday” means different things around the globe, we are assuming a weekday is Monday through Friday (feel free to modify the challenge if you wish though).

Guidelines to get the most out of this challenge:

*   you may use a reference cell that contains an input for the year (here, 2025)
*   the formula should be calculated in just one cell (no “helper” cells)
*   no Power Query / Get & Transform or VBA.

_Sounds easy?  Then why not have a go?  We’ll publish one solution in Monday’s blog.  Have a great weekend in the meantime!_
