Melbourne follow-up
===================

30 June 2016

You may have missed it amongst all the Brexit news, but Sydney Director Tim Heng spent most of last week in Melbourne, catching up with clients and friends about Power BI and how it can be applied to a range of business reports and challenges.

“Most clients have the data available, but don’t have the time or know how to get started with Power BI,” Tim points out. “However, the time that you invest in setting up Power Query, Power Pivot and getting it all bedded into Power BI is soon repaid in spades, freeing up analysts to actually analyse their data, rather than simply report on it.”

With training in Melbourne coming up over the next few months, it’s likely that Tim will be back down here soon. However, for those who can’t wait that long, Melbourne Director Liam Bastick will still be in town if you wanted to catch up and learn more about Power BI or any of SumProduct’s other products and services.
