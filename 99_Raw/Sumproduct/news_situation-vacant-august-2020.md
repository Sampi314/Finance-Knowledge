Situation Vacant – August 2020
==============================

28 August 2020

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/22b0b5bb4a8fa30c716239b4b3f60113.jpg)

Economic downturns, bushfires, storm damage, pandemics, furloughed staff, Jobkeeper, Jobseeker _et al._ I think we can all agree 2020 has been a rough ride for everyone. There does appear to be a light at the end of the tunnel though, and SumProduct is pleased that it is currently seeking fresh blood to add to its experienced team.

We are seeking new recruits. If you have a right to work in Australia and you have modelling skills, online / in-person training experience and / or business development acumen, we’d love to hear from you. We are keeping the details vague deliberately, as we are seeking fellow like-minded individuals who are interested in the challenges set by an entrepreneurial team with work coming in from around the globe, forever looking for new and improved ways to help our clients – old and new.

If you are interested or would like to know more, please drop us a line at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
 with an outline of your experience and remuneration expectations. Please note we expect successful applicants to be most likely based in or around Sydney, but that is not the be all and end all.

We’d love to hear from you as we look to continue to grow the business through these difficult times.
