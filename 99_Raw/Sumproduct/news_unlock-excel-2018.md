Unlock Excel 2018
=================

17 February 2018

It’s back!! Unlock Excel is to return. In conjunction with CPA Australia and Microsoft, SumProduct is proud to announce our annual Unlock Excel Conference this April.

Across four locations – [Melbourne](http://click.e.cpaaustralia.com.au/?qs=4673a87cf07d9345c8d96625dcf004a8c5397a5452501d9366b85af907fac57223e560b4690f6d355376446e7bd31503d847fa1368e1209c)
, [Brisbane](http://click.e.cpaaustralia.com.au/?qs=4673a87cf07d9345c7b3e3b60f8478c198152776bd99c65b41282a4d8dc571352f8b167e397753fb59810f8ee22cc29153db8d2f733ca845)
, [Sydney](http://click.e.cpaaustralia.com.au/?qs=4673a87cf07d934559726ed8f6fd0ea3b4f8d8d783d449d19f2fa6ad6a6d2667c19248c32112de0aa5965ab71c8ef9e606b2fe3506b3d84e)
 and [Wellington](http://click.e.cpaaustralia.com.au/?qs=4673a87cf07d93456ec2d79b9bb479b87e51907320fe50c259ac6233055eadf7e9f54f639db0b262ceac932e1bf7857dde2564c1d33e3bfa)
 – up to six of the world’s leading Excel experts, Microsoft’s Most Valued Professionals (MVPs), will be sharing their knowledge, tips and tricks on all things Excel over two captivating days! We’v e had a blast for the past two years and we’re hoping to make it bigger and better this year.

If you’re coming, **book soon**. There’s an Early Bird discount until Tuesday 13 March, which can save _c_.15% off the standard price. Check out all the information and book [here](https://www.cpaaustralia.com.au/training-and-events/conferences/unlock-excel)
.
