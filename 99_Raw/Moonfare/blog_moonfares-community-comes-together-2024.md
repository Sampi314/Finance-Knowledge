Moonfare’s community comes together: highlights from Milan and St. Moritz

Our teams are back on the road in 2024. In the first months of the year, we've held a number of exciting events — here's a quick report from two of them.

![Moonfare’s community comes together: highlights from Milan and St. Moritz](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

March 26, 2024

It's always a pleasure to see Moonfare's community of likeminded investors in action. There's been plenty of opportunities for networking this year, with more events to follow.

We kicked off 2024 with two events in one night — reception in Hauser & Wirth Gallery, followed by dinner in the picturesque Swiss town of St. Moritz.

Amidst the exhibition by artist Gerhard Richter, our guests enjoyed drinks and meeting investors from around the world. A special thanks to Margareta (Molly) McConnell, Gilad Karmon, Maximilian De Martini, Ilana Walters and the rest of the Moonfare team.

More recently, in March, we co-hosted another enthralling evening, this time in Milan with AssetClassic — a specialised platform enabling investors to access the classic car market.

The event, held at the historic Palazzina Appiani, managed to bridge the worlds of classic car and private equity investors.

Our guests heard enriching insights from speakers including Moonfare’s Sanjay Gupta, Marine Eugene, and Marwa Khan. Pavel Ermoline, our investment director, also had a captivating fireside discussion with Camillo Mekacher-Vogel, CEO of AssetClassic.
