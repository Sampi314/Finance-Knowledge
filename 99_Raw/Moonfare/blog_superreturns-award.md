SuperReturn Awards – SaaS Provider of the Year

![SuperReturn Awards – SaaS Provider of the Year](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

November 20, 2019

##### **Moonfare has been recognized as the software service provider of the year at the SuperInvestor private equity conference this week in Amsterdam. The award focused on advancements in digital innovation in the private equity industry over the past year.**

We were cited in particular for our investment platform, which provides a safe, seamless, and secure investment experience for our clients. By combining individual investments, our feeder fund structure invests directly in premium private equity funds that are beyond the reach of most private investors.

The SuperInvestor event is a SuperReturn conference, which is the world’s largest private equity and venture capital event series. The independent judges included nearly a dozen private equity experts with diverse backgrounds in the field.

Winning this prestigious award is truly an honour for our firm. And while our main focus is on delivering top-notch service for our clients, participating in this fantastic event and winning the award was certainly a lot of fun. Many thanks to the judges and committee at SuperReturn!
