Moonfare CEO named in top 50 most influential people in private equity

Steffen Pauls one of only nine new names on globally recognised Private Equity News list.

![Moonfare CEO named in top 50 most influential people in private equity](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

December 16, 2022

A leading European private equity magazine has named Steffen Pauls as one of the most influential people in the industry.

_Private Equity News_ unveiled the Moonfare CEO as a new entrant on its annual ‘Most Influential in Private Equity’ list, one of nine new names making their debut in 2022. In its report, the magazine describes Pauls as “one of the people at the forefront of the democratisation trend in private equity”.

Pauls takes his place on the list among partners and CEOs from established European firms including Permira, KKR and CVC Capital Partners.

_Private Equity News_ is a European-based publication owned by Dow Jones, and is a sister title of _The Wall Street Journal_.
