Company

Moonfare co-invests in a leading global provider of private education

Moonfare co-invests alongside a consortium led by EQT into Nord Anglia Education, a leader in international private education.

![Moonfare co-invests in a leading global provider of private education](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

September 17, 2025

Moonfare has offered investors access to a direct investment opportunity in Nord Anglia Education, a world-leading provider of private education backed by global investment organisation EQT and a consortium of premier institutional investors and financial asset managers.

Nord Anglia Education has been a long-standing success story under EQT’s stewardship, achieving growth through both strategic acquisitions and investments in educational innovation.¹

Since 2017, the company has completed over 30 acquisitions and forged partnerships with leading institutions such as UNICEF, Juilliard, MIT, IMG Academy, King’s College London, and Project Zero at Harvard Graduate School of Education.

With the support of EQT and consortium partners including Neuberger Berman Private Markets and the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board, Nord Anglia is well positioned to build on its strong track record and global impact in education.²

About Nord Anglia Education
---------------------------

As a leading international schools organisation, Nord Anglia is shaping a generation of creative and resilient global citizens who graduate from its schools with everything they need for success, whatever they choose to be or do in life.

Nord Anglia’s strong academic foundations combine world-class teaching and curricula with cutting-edge technology and facilities, creating learning experiences like no other. Inside and outside the classroom, the organisation inspires its students to achieve more than they ever thought possible.

No two children learn the same way, which is why Nord Anglia schools around the world personalise learning to what works best for each student. Inspired by high-quality teachers, Nord Anglia students achieve outstanding academic results and go on to study at the world's top universities.

The Nord Anglia global family includes more than 80 day and boarding schools in 37 countries, teaching over 90,000 students aged 3 to 18.
