Meet our Chief Economist: Mike O’Sullivan on politics, the economy and what it all means for your private market investments

In a video interview with Moonfare, Mike O’Sullivan discusses the role of European private capital in the new economic order, explores opportunities in artificial intelligence and shares his view on interest rate cuts.

![Meet our Chief Economist: Mike O’Sullivan on politics, the economy and what it all means for your private market investments](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

June 6, 2024

“The investor portfolio of the future will contain much more private equity and venture capital.¹² These are the ways to capture the fascinating new technologies emerging in the post-globalization era,” says Moonfare’s new Chief Economist and Senior Advisor.

Mike O’Sullivan is a seasoned investor, commentator and author. He has spent 20 years in investment management, most recently as Chief Investment Officer at Credit Suisse International Wealth Management division.

He has  served on the boards of various organisations, including the Harvest Innovation Advisory and the World Economic Forum's Council on the New Economy. Additionally, he has taught finance and economics at Oxford and Princeton, while regularly contributing to numerous journals and media outlets, such as CNBC and Forbes.

Mike is also the author of ‘The Levelling,’ which outlines what’s next in politics, economics, finance and geopolitics in the post-globalization era. “Markets are the best place to watch and test the way the world evolves,” he believes.
