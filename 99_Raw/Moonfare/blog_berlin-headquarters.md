More space to grow: Welcome to our new Berlin HQ

Moonfare recently relocated to a new Berlin office to support our growing team and to gear up for ambitious plans ahead.

![More space to grow: Welcome to our new Berlin HQ](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

May 27, 2025

We celebrated the move with a lively housewarming party and talks from Moonfare’s leadership who shared their vision for this exciting new chapter.

A little bit about Moonfare’s new home: it's a modern workspace located in the recently built House A of the Eiswerk complex on Köpenicker Straße in Berlin Mitte. It’s built around how we operate: with a client-first mindset, a shared drive for excellence, a belief in collective success and a culture that rewards growth and merit.

“The office blends contemporary design with historic industrial charm which makes it a perfect fit for our brand and provides our team with an inspiring and dynamic environment,” said Steffen Pauls, co-CEO and founder of Moonfare.

The building itself was designed by architecture firm Graftlab, while the interior was designed by Studio Karhard together with the Moonfare Internal Design Team. The premises will offer space for 120 desks, 22 conference rooms and 75 meeting spaces spread across almost 2,100 square meters.
