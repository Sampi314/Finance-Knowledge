Moonfare Co-CEO joins CNBC urging plain-English education on semi-liquids and private credit

Steffen Pauls, Moonfare Co-CEO and Founder, appeared on CNBC’s Squawk Box for a wide-ranging interview.

![Moonfare Co-CEO joins CNBC urging plain-English education on semi-liquids and private credit](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)
