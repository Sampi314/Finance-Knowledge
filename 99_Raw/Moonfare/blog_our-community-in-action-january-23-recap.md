The month in Moonfare: our community in action

Our community of investors came together toward the end of January with a trio of events in Munich and St Moritz.

![The month in Moonfare: our community in action](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

February 2, 2023

It’s been a busy start to 2023 at Moonfare as we continue working hard on several exciting projects — one of which is to bring our community of like-minded investors together much more often this year.

We kicked it off with three exciting events in January: a philanthropy dinner in Munich and two investor gatherings in St. Moritz.

**Celebrating our commitment to children’s education**
------------------------------------------------------

Last week, we brought the Moonfare community together in Munich as we kicked-off [**our long-term partnership with The Dariu Foundation**](https://assets-global.website-files.com/5ffb7d86352880856dbd363e/63d26395ba70d6ca13b8265f_Press%20release_Moonfare%20and%20Dariu%20Foundation_260123_EN%20(2).pdf)
, a charity focused on fostering digital education for children.

The partnership will give Dariu access to more than 50,000 people connected to Moonfare, with the aim of bringing more visibility to its work and access to an engaged and digitally driven group of people.

![Image](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

Moonfare CEO Dr Steffen Pauls with Thomas Trüb, head of The Dariu Foundation.

To celebrate, we hosted an exclusive dinner for Moonfare’s community and Dariu. The evening highlighted Dariu’s ‘The Impossible Project’ initiative, which focuses on supporting disadvantaged children in rural South East Asia through school and digital education.

You can find out more about Dariu and its work [here](https://dariu.org/)
.

‍**Enjoying the glitz in St Moritz**
------------------------------------

The following day, we opened the door for our investors to one of Switzerland’s most exclusive art galleries, Hauser and Wirth. Our community enjoyed drinks and canapes, as well as a private tour of the gallery space.

![Image](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

Members of our Moonfare community enjoyed a private gallery tour in St Moritz.

That weekend, we stayed in Switzerland to host our community members at the St Moritz Winter Cup, a polo event played on the city’s eponymous lake. We were delighted to see so many of our investors enjoy the thrilling action at one of the world’s most unique sporting events.

![Image](https://www.moonfare.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1256)

The St Moritz Winter Cup, where we hosted several of our investor community.
