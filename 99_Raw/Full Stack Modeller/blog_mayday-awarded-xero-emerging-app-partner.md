Industry News | Mayday Wins Xero Award
======================================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Apr 26, 2023 1:37:27 PM

Mayday, a software solution for businesses with multiple related entities, was awarded the prestigious Xero Emerging App Partner of the Year Award.

Xero Awards - 19th April 2023
-----------------------------

[Mayday](https://www.getmayday.com/)
, a software solution for businesses with multiple related entities, was awarded the prestigious Xero Emerging App Partner of the Year Award. This award recognises Mayday as a new app partner that has made great progress with Xero in a short space of time, providing a great user experience and making an excellent start with engaging Xero’s customer base.

“We're absolutely thrilled to take home the Emerging App Partner of the Year award. A huge thank you to all of the Xero team for being such an incredible partner” said David Tuck, Co-Founder and CEO at Mayday. “A huge thank you to our users. It is a leap of faith to be an early adopter of a software product. Your support means so much to us”.

Xero’s online accounting software connects small business owners with their numbers, and partners with more than 1000 third-party apps for an integrated, streamlined business solution. Other apps nominated in the Emerging App Partner of the Year category included Capitalise and NextMinute. The winning app was determined by Xero’s judging panel, made up of Xero leadership and guest judges from the Xero community. Judges’ comments included: 

“Mayday has identified a gap in the market and built an app that solves those problems, providing a new and interesting function within the app ecosystem that automates multi-entity businesses’ accounting processes, bringing real-time intercompany accounting to the finance team. Customers are raving about the solution and are thrilled to have an app that caters to this specific challenge.”
