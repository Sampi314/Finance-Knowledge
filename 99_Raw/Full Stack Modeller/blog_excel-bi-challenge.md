What is the Excel BI Challenge series?
--------------------------------------

The Excel BI Challenges series has been created by [Vijay A. Verma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/excelbi/)
. It is an (ever-growing) set of Excel-based challenges. Each challenge is designed to test your mastery of Excel-based modelling.

Challenge walkthrough with Giles Male
-------------------------------------

Giles has recorded his attempts at these challenges live - no prep, no fluff, just opening the file and getting going. This means that you get to watch Giles' thought process and his journey, "warts and all" through each of the challenges.

You can see all of Giles' walkthroughs on our [Excel On The Road channel](https://www.youtube.com/@ExcelOnTheRoad)
.

What can I expect to get from this series?  

---------------------------------------------

This is an incredible opportunity to join Giles' journey as he works through each of these challenges. You will be able to attempt each challenge yourself with our support.

  

In each episode I will be sharing:

*   The Excel-based challenge file

*   Giles' walkthrough video
*   Giles' solution file
*   Explanations for each core function / concept used
*   My thoughts on the challenge and Giles' solution  
    
*   Key feedback from other Full Stack Modeller members and viewers
