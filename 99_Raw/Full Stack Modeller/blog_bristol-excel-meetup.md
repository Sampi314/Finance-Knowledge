The Bristol Excel Meetup Group
==============================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Jan 31, 2025 11:45:00 AM

 Today we launched the Bristol Excel Meetup Group.

Who is the Bristol Excel Meetup Group for?
------------------------------------------

This group is for anyone based in the South West of England who is interested in learning about Excel, reporting, and financial modelling.

Everyone is welcome, whatever your role and level of experience using Excel.

How often will you meet?
------------------------

We will meet once a quarter in Bristol for a live event and chance to catch up over a drink, or two.

We are also planning to host remote sessions over Zoom monthly.

How can I find out more?
------------------------

To find out more about the Bristol Excel Meetup Group and see the events schedule visit the site  [here](https://www.meetup.com/bristol-excel-meetup-group/)
