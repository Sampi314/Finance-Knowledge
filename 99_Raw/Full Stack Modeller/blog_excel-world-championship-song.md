The Excel World Championship Song

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

_Excel is an e-sport - and it has it's own theme tune!_

The Excel World Championship Song: A Cult Hit
---------------------------------------------

  
The Microsoft Excel World Championship (MEWC), an annual competition that brings together the world's top spreadsheet experts, has found an unlikely, yet fitting, addition to its competitive flair: a dedicated rock-style theme song.

Released in late 2024, "The Excel World Championship Song" was created by our very own Giles Male with the help of AI. The song quickly became a talking point, adding a layer of humor and unique identity to the Excel e-sport".  It's available on major streaming platforms like Spotify.

  
Lyrics that Resonate (and Rhyme with "Bin")
----------------------------------------------

The song, a soft rock ballad style, leans into the self-aware humor of a highly competitive event focused on solving challenges in Excel. Its lyrics capture the tension and quirky spirit of the competition, with a memorable chorus that has become a fan favourite:  
  
"It's the Excel World Championship / Who's going to win?  
  It's the Excel World Championship / Who's going in the spreadsheet bin?"

More Than Just a Meme
---------------------

While a fun and unexpected element, the song is part of the MEWC organizers' broader effort to elevate competitive Excel into a more mainstream spectator event, complete with "are-you-ready-to-rumble" style announcers and a championship belt.

The electric crowd reaction and tens of thousands of YouTube livestream viewers suggest the approach is working, and the theme song has successfully cemented its place as a catchy and essential ingredient of the championship experience.
