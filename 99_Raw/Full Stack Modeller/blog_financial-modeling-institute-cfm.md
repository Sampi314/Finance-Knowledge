The Financial Modeling Institute's CFM qualification
====================================================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Jan 20, 2020 11:05:00 AM

Find out more about the Chartered Financial Modeler (CFM) qualification from the Financial Modeling Institute (FMI).

**What is the Financial Modeling Institute?**
---------------------------------------------

![FMI-1](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/FMI-1.png?width=774&height=585&name=FMI-1.png)

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) was founded in 2017 with the goal of “Promoting awareness, excellence and discipline in Financial Modeling through world-class accreditation programs”.

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) focuses on assessment, leaving the delivery of training to independent and proven partners like us at Full Stack Modeller.

**What is the CFM qualification?  
**
-------------------------------------

![Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg?width=981&height=633&name=Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg) The Financial Modeling Institute Chartered Financial Modeler (CFM) qualification is a financial modelling accreditation designed to showcase the modeller's problem-solving and creativity in relation to real-world modelling scenarios.  

To be successful in the CFM qualification the financial modeller must demonstrate their ability to apply their financial modelling skills to various complex, real-world scenarios.  

**What is the format of the Chartered Financial Modeler (CFM) exam?**
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The CFM exam is a tough, proctored, four-hour online exam. You are provided with a selection of three financial modelling problems, two of which you must solve within the four-hour time limit.  

  

To be successful in the CFM exam you must have a strong Excel modelling toolkit and be able to apply those skills in real-world financial modelling scenarios.

*   **Previous exam scenarios include:**
*   Assist a growing fashion boutique with their international expansion sales projections.
*   Audit a junior banker’s model to help their client reapply for refinancing.
*   Help an Investment Fund choose their best financing option for a major asset purchase.
*   Support a manufacturer’s strategic planning by forecasting future operating profits.

The topic areas covered in the CFM exams include:

*   Revenue
*   Operating and non-operating costs
*   Working Capital
*   CAPEX & Depreciation
*   Income Tax
*   Debt
*   Equity
*   M&A / Subsidiaries
*   Sensitivity Analysis & Summary Automation
*   Timing Flexibility
*   Model Checking
*   Data Management
*     
    
    **When are the FMI CFM exams?**
    -------------------------------
    
*   The CFM exams take place once a year in October.
    

**How much does the FMI CFM exam cost?**
----------------------------------------

The Chartered Financial Modeler (CFM) exams cost $695.
