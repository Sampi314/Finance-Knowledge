Giles Male awarded MVP by Microsoft
===================================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Sep 9, 2024 9:30:00 AM

  ![IMG_2504](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/IMG_2504.jpg?width=555&height=741&name=IMG_2504.jpg)

We are delighted to announce that Giles Male, our co-founder and MD, has been recognised as a Microsoft MVP.

You can see Giles' announcement on LinkedIn [here](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7244663706968555520/)
.

But what is a Microsoft MVP?  
➡ Most Valuable Player?  
➡ Most Versatile Poet?  
➡ Minimal Viable Product?  
  
It stands for "Most Valuable Professional" - where Microsoft recognises "exceptional community leaders for their technical expertise, leadership, speaking experience, online influence, and commitment to solving real-world problems."  
  
I suggested that Giles announce it discreetly and humbly.
