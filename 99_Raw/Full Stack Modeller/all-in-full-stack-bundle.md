Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

### Everything you need to know.

Why have you put this bundle together?

We have so much respect for the amazing work each of the participating organisations carries out for finance and analytics professionals. Each organisation involved in the bundle offers something truly unique. By bringing our services together, and helping students navigate between each of the subscriptions, we offer something not seen on offer anywhere else.

Who is this bundle for?

Any finance or analytics professionals who are ready to transform their modelling and analytics skill sets. You don't have to be an expert to join us. We'll guide you through the fundamentals of Excel, financial modelling, data analytics and data visualisation, and then support you as you move into more advanced territory. The bundle is also available for teams who are looking to collectively improve their modelling and analytics skill sets. Just reach out to us using the Contact Us form on this page if you'd like to speak to us about team bundle packages.

You mentioned flexibility around membership start dates. Can you tell me more about that?

You start off just with your Full Stack Modeller membership activated on day one of your bundle. We'll welcome you into the Full Stack community and make sure you know what your options are for all of the additional subscriptions. At any point within your initial 12-month membership with Full Stack, you can tell us to activate your memberships with the FMI, Maven, or your season passes with the FMWC or the Excel Battle Series. That gives you up to two years in total to work through everything we've made available to you as part of this bundle.

Can I pay monthly?

Not for the bundle. You can choose to join Full Stack on an individual membership and pay for that monthly, but the bundle must be paid for upfront.

Can I cancel an individual subscription within the bundle if I sign up and end up not liking one of the subscriptions?

No, unfortunately not. The bundle gives you access to all of the participating organisations' platforms and events. We cannot make adjustments to this mid-way through the bundle membership period.

What happens at the end of the initial 12-month membership period?

Each membership or season pass is activated individually, and each membership or season pass can then be renewed or repurchased individually. Essentially the bundle comes to an end after each initial subscription finishes. You will hear from each participating organisation individually as you approach the end of each subscription with information about your renewal options. It's completely up to you whether you renew with each organisation.

What if I already have a subscription with one of the participating organisations? Can I still take advantage of some form of bundle offer?

Contact us using the form on this page if you already have an FMI, Maven, or season pass with the FMWC or Excel Battle series. We can look at an adjusted bundle offer in these circumstances.

Is there a free trial period?

Contact us if you'd like a 7-day free trial with Full Stack before you commit to the bundle. We can't offer a full trial of all memberships and season passes, but your trial experience with us should give you an indication of the experience you can expect from the bundle package.
