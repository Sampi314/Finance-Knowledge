Welcome to my teaching page. This page contains links to much of the teaching that I do, in the classroom, online and in external venues.

1.  [Philosophy](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/teachingphil.html)
    : Contrary to the widely-used insult, I don't teach because I cannot do. I teach because I love teaching.
2.  [Regular Classes](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/regularclass.htm)
    : Semester-long (15 weeks, 26 sessions of 80 minutes each) classes in Corporate Finance and Valuation.
3.  [Executive Classes](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/execclass.htm)
    : Shorter executive programs (2-3 days) in corporate finance and valuation.
4.  [Online Classes](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/onlineclass.htm)
    : Classes specifically designed for an online audience, with shorter sessions and self-tests.
5.  [Seminars/sessions](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/seminars.htm)
    : Classes that I teach globally that range from 30 minutes to 3 days on a variety of topics and to diverse audiences.
6.  [Other:](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/teachingother.html)
     Anything that I cannot fit into the above slots.
