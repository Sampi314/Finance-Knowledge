Welcome to my data page. This page contains links to almost everything you ever wanted to know about the data that is available on my site (and more). I have broken the page down into five constituent parts to make it more naviagable.

1.  [About Data](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datahistory.html)
    : I lay out the [history/philosophy](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datahistory.html#history)
     of my datasets, the [timing](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datahistory.html#timing)
     of the data, the sources I use and some caveats/rules for data usage.
2.  [Data Breakdown](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/databreakdown.html)
    : I explain how I break the data down by [variable](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html#variables)
    , by [industry](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html#industry)
    , by [region](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html#region)
    , by [time](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html#time)
     and by [company](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html#company)
    .
3.  [Current Data](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datacurrent.html)
    : This is where the data resides, broken down into corporate finance, valuation and portfolio management sections.
4.  [Archived Data](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataarchived.html)
    : If you need the data from prior years, you will find it here, broken down into corporate finance, valuation and portfolio management sections.
5.  [Data Add ons/Changes](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/dataaddon.html)
    : Since some of use the data over time, I have a section on changes (if any) to my data or calcuations, and add ons in this section.
6.  [Webcasts/Tools](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datatools.html)
    : These are [webcasts](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datatools.html#webcasts)
    , [tools (spreadsheets)](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datatools.html#tools)
    , [blog posts](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datatools.html#posts)
     and [writings](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/datatools.html#writings)
     about data analysis.

The data is updated in the first two weeks of every year and the most recent update was on January 9, 2026. The next major update will be in early January 2027, God willing, though a few of the data sets will get updated more frequently. The data is broken down by an industry categorization that is my own, but largely derived from industry grouping by my raw data providers. While I would love to share the company-level data (like I used to), I am afraid that I am no longer allowed to do that by the data services.
