_**Webcasts  
**_This page will include the webcasts from the current class. The webcasts can be accessed in one of four ways:

1.  _Streaming video_ (The file will be streamed to your computer. This will work only if you have a good internet connection, but should be of the best quality and the most information)  
    
2.  _Video podcast_ (The file will be downloaded to your computer and you can watch the video using Quicktime or video software or on your iPod)  
    
3.  _Audio podcast_ (The .mp3 file will be downloaded as an audio file and can be played on any audio player). The AV system used to record the lectures has changed and I think it is much improved.  
    
4.  _YouTube videos_ (if you have slower internet connection and/or a small device).
