* * *

This is a class about valuation, pure and simple. It is about valuing and pricing small businesses and big ones, simple businesses and complex ones, young firms and those in distress. It is about valuation in all its many forms and I hope that by the end of this class, you can value just about anything that has a value and price just about everything else.
