In my day job, I teach the Corporate Finance and Valuation classes in the MBA program at the Stern School of Business. Once in a while, I get lucky (and I not being sarcastic) and get to even teach an undergraduate class. These are semester-long classes that last 14 weeks and 26 sessions (with the undergrad classes lasting 28 sessions). The classes are webcast and you can download the lecture notes and other material for the class.
