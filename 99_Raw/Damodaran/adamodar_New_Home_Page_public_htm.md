_Websites:_ Each of these books has a dedicated web page, where you can find material relevant to the book, including excel spreadsheets, datasets and pdf versions of presentations. Click on the book in question for the website.

_For readers:_ If you are interested in getting any of these books, you can try [Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/)
 or at your friendly neighbourhood bookstore - I am afraid it will not be in the bestseller section and you may have to look hard to find them... If you are wondering which book to buy (since I have a few on each topic), you may want to [read this very quick summary of what these books contain](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/valbookcomp.html)
, how they differ from each other and the readership that they are directed at.

_For instructors_: If you are an instructor using one of these books in your classes, you will also find instructor's sections on the book websites. It is the only part of this website that requires a password and a username, which you can get by contacting me.
