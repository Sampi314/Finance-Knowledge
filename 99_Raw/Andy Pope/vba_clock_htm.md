### Userform Clock

 

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
|     | ![](https://www.andypope.info/vba/clock.gif)<br>--------------------------------------------<br><br>##### You can adjust the hands by moving the mouse over the clock face.<br><br>##### The left button controls the hour hand while the right button controls the minute hand.  <br>In both cases the other hand will adjust to reflect the current portion of the hour.<br><br>##### The trick to animating the hands is to use a collection of label controls for each hand. The close proximity of the labels give the illusion of a solid line.<br><br>##### The construction, positioning and drawing of the hands are all done within a class module. |
