Beef Margin Management Seminar – Aberdeen, SD
=============================================

The CIH Beef Margin seminar is designed to help beef producers identify and protect forward profit margins. This comprehensive seminar includes a thorough review of cash market alternatives and a fundamental background of futures and options.

Topics Covered
--------------

**

*   Fundamentals of Beef Margin Management**

An introduction to modeling forward profitability and using various contracting alternatives to protect profit margins.

***   Contracting Alternatives**

Comparing available contracting choices in the cash and futures markets.

***   Introduction to LRP and Options**

An overview of LRP and options, including how they work and components of option premium/value.

***   Core Options/Futures Strategies**

How to use futures and options positions to establish protection against the risk of higher costs and lower sale prices.

***   Core Strategy Adjustments**

Factors – costs and benefits – to consider when weighing strategic position adjustments as prices fluctuate over time.

***   Applying Concepts Learned**

An interactive price management simulation to reinforce and test new skills. Participants will make several price management decisions over a marketing period and evaluate the resulting effect on net margins.
