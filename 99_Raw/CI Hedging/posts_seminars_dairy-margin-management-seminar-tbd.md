Dairy Margin Management Seminar – TBD
=====================================

Topics Covered
--------------

**

*   Introduction to the Futures Market and Profit Margin Management:**

Identifying forward profit margins using the futures market as a price discovery tool. Cash market versus futures market, price risk versus basis risk, contracting choices.

***   Introduction to Options:**

Overview of options, including definitions/terms, contract specifications, components of premium, expiration, exercise or assignment, and offset.

***   Introduction to DRP/LRP:**

Comprehensive overview of both DRP and LRP, including program basics and best use cases.

***   Core Strategies:**

Review of basic futures and options positions typically used by dairy producers to protect the risk of both lower and higher prices, as well as discussion of option margining.

***   Group Exercises:**

Using futures and options strategies, teams will attempt to manage a dairy producer’s historical forward profit margin by initiating coverage and managing those positions.
