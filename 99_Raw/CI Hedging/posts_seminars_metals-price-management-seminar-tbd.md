Metals Price Management Seminar – Steel and Copper
==================================================

You’ll Learn How To:
--------------------

*   Manage the risk between inventory and forward sales
    
*   Leverage market volatility while maintaining protection
    
*   Protect yourself and your customer from adverse price  
    movements in the sales process
    

Topics Covered
--------------

**

*   Introduction to Price Risk Management**

Cash market versus futures market, basis definition, price risk versus basis risk, contracting choices.

***   Introduction to Options**

Types of options, definitions, contract specifications, components of premium and expiration versus offset or exercise/assignment.

***   Core strategies and Adjustments**

Review of basic futures and options positions typically used by commodity buyers to protect the risk of both higher and lower prices. Discussion of position management and making tactical adjustments to strategies over time to manage both delta and volatility to take advantage of opportunities that may arise.

***   Price Management Simulation:**

Integrated online simulation incorporating both futures and options strategies to manage the risk of a metal buyer’s price risk through a historical market.
