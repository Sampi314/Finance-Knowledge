Key Takeaways
-------------

Wondering how top CFOs are tackling today’s challenges? We surveyed over 200 finance leaders from high-growth venture-backed companies to get insights into their operations, tools and growth trends helping them navigate the complexities of their jobs. This inaugural report reveals how CFOs did more with less in 2024, as profitability grew despite a drop in median cash runway. Looking ahead, CFOs expect hiring and AI spending to increase, while inflation and interest rates are top concerns.

### 88% Of VC-backed companies allow some level of remote work

The split between remote and hybrid work is about 50/50.

### 2 in 3 Companies surveyed encourage employees to use AI at work

Only 3% oppose its use.

### 42% Growth in subscription-based revenue year over year

Revenue from one-time sales grew by only 28%.
