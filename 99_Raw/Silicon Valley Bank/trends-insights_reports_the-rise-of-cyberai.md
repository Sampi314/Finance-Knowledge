Key Takeaways
-------------

Generative AI has begun to transform everything from how companies develop software to how they engage with customers. It has also shifted the cybersecurity landscape to the point where practically every Chief Information Officer (CISO) has plans to change their cybersecurity stack within the next three years, if they haven’t done so already. Our latest report explores the security challenges brought by GenAI and opportunities for startups at the intersection of GenAI and cybersecurity to develop meaningful solutions.

### 163% Growth in US VC investment in AI between 2018 and 2023

While companies remain eager to adopt solutions produced by new GenAI startups, cybersecurity concerns could slow their pace.

### 29% Increase in cash runway for AI companies between 2021 and 2023

In contrast to other sectors, a favorable investment environment has increased runway among AI companies.

### 74% Of CISOs have already changed their cybersecurity stack

Most CISOs have adjusted their approach to cybersecurity in the 18 months since ChatGPT’s release.
