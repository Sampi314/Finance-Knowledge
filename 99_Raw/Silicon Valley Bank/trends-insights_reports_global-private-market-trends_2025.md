Key Takeaways
-------------

The Global Private Market Trends report explores the forces shaping VC ecosystems around the world. The report focuses on three key sectors at the center of VC’s evolving role globally: artificial intelligence (AI), cybersecurity and fintech.

### 50% AI VC and growth deals

Half of AI VC and growth deals are in companies based outside North America.

### 13 Non-US cybersecurity unicorns

This is the number of non-US active cybersecurity unicorns.

### 127% Growth of Pix transaction value

This is the growth in transaction value of Brazil's Pix, a payment system quickly being adopted internationally.
