Key Takeaways
-------------

Private funds manage what’s under their control in adapting to an uncertain landscape.

### 01 Both private equity and venture capital firms

expect the challenging fundraising market to persist through 2025.

### 02 As fund families mature,

they tend to attract an increasing number of institutional LPs.

### 03 Of the private markets firms surveyed,

nearly 90% outsource at least half of their tax work.
