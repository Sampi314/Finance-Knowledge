Key Takeaways
-------------

Changing trade policy and continued technological innovation are top of mind for private funds CFOs.

### 01 Publicly traded PE firms

have been focused on managing tariffs, according to earnings call transcripts.

### 02 The AI rush has produced

a 65% increase in the amount of capital called by tech funds between Q4 2023 and Q2 2025.¹

### 03 85% of PE firms are using

or plan to use AI in the next twelve months, up from just 49% a year ago.²
