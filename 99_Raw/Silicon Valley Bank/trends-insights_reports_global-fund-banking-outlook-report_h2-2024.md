Key Takeaways
-------------

Fund CFOs remain optimistic despite difficult exit markets and fundraising headwinds.

### 01 A prolonged weak exit market,

has funds seeking additional liquidity.

### 02 A difficult fundraising environment

is reducing check size and increasing the number of LPs per fund.

### 03 55% of PE and VC fund managers

are using or plan to use AI in their firm processes.
