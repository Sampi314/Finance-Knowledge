Key Takeaways
-------------

As a new generation emerges as the dominant wine consumer, what milestones will shape the industry?

### 01 Increased visitation returning to wine country.

The modest increase in visitation seen in late 2023 will likely continue throughout the summer.

### 02 Club member acquisition sources continue to diversify.

Email acquisition remains effective while digital strategies beyond social media are gaining momentum.

### 03 Variable tasting fees can attract new consumers during slow periods.

Fees, products and entertainment tailored to entry-level consumers may help revive quieter tasting room times.
