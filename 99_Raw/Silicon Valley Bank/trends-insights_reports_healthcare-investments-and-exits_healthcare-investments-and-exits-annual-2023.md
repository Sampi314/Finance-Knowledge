Key Takeaways
-------------

Our 13th Healthcare Investments & Exits report examines how fundraising, investment and exit trends are impacting the VC-backed healthcare ecosystem. This edition unlocks new insights through our first-ever analyses around generative AI, spoon-fed deals and the occurrences of down, flat and up rounds.

### $19B Raised by U.S. VC firms for health-focused funds

2023 was the third best year for healthcare fundraising in the past decade.

### 42% Of healthcare deals were spoon-fed

The proportion of deals involving later-stage companies valued at more than $20M raising $15M or less is on the rise.

### 19 Total number of biopharma IPOs

Biopharma exits are historically low, but the IPO market is showing signs of life.
