Key Takeaways
-------------

Investment in women’s health reached an all-time high in 2024, driven by growing recognition of the ways various health conditions affect women differently and disproportionately, and the causes and biological drivers behind this imbalance. While women’s health startups will face headwinds in 2025 such as investor reluctance and scarce exits, the long-term potential of the sector remains undeniable.

### 55% Growth in VC investment in women’s health in 2024

Total dollars invested exceeded 2023 numbers by almost $1B.

### $2.6B VC investment in women’s health in 2024

When the category is expanded to include all health issues known to disproportionately affect women, the level of investment grows to $10.7B.

### 38% Of women’s health VC investment that went to healthtech

Healthtech still leads the women’s health sector, but its dominance is waning.
