# The Financial Modelling Handbook

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/start-here

---

I spent more than a year writing the Financial Modelling Handbook. I published each chapter on this site as I went.

During the writing, more than 2,500 people subscribed and gave me valuable feedback that helped me to shape the book.

The first version of the book was published on September 22nd 2023. You can [purchase the book here](https://buyfinancialmodellinghandbook.org/?add-to-cart=380&quantity=1&ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
.

While you can still read the draft chapters online, all of the accompanying worked example files are only available when you purchase the book.

I have now started to write the next volume of the Handbook focusing on Project Finance Modelling. I'll be following the same process and publishing online as I go.

You can [read more about why I’m doing that here](https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/p/why-im-writing-in-public)
. Please remember that all the material I publish here is a draft. Expect frequent typos and mistakes.

You can receive email updates as I publish. Enter your email address here, and all new content will come straight to your inbox.
