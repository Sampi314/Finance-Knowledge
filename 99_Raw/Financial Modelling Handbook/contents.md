_As I wrote the Financial Modelling Handbook, I published the draft of each chapter online. This helped me to get feedback and input before publishing. More than 2,500 people read the book online before it was published, and helped me to create a much better-finished product. I'm leaving all the drafts online. Please note that these may differ slightly from the finished chapters in the book._
