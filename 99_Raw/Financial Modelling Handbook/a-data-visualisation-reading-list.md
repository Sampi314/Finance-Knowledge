# A data visualisation reading list

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/a-data-visualisation-reading-list

---

I've long believed that the best way to learn something is to teach it. To increase my own knowledge and skill around data visualisation, I created [this free introduction to data visualisation course](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/free-data-visualisation-course?ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
 as part of my teaching work with [Full Stack Modeller](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/?ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
. The course has proven to be pretty popular.

The following are the websites and books I drew upon to teach myself more about the topic and create the course. Please comment below with your own personal recommendations, and I'll add them to the list.

### Data visualisation books

*   **Now you see it. Simple Visualization Techniques for Quantitative Analysis.** _Stephen Few_
*   **Show me the numbers. Designing tables and graphs to enlighten.** _Stephen Few_
*   **Data Visualisation. A Handbook for Data Driven Design.** _Andy Kirk_
*   **Envisioning Information.** _Edward R. Tufte_
*   **The Visual Display of Quantitative Information.** _Edward R. Tufte_
*   **The Art of Statistics: How to Learn from Data.** _David Spiegelhalter_
*   **Calling Bullshit: The Art of Scepticism in a Data-Driven World.** _Carl Bergstrom and Jevin West_
*   **How Charts Lie.** _Alberto Cairo_
