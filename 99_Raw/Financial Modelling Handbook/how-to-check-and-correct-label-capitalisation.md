# How to check and correct label capitalisation

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/how-to-check-and-correct-label-capitalisation

---

I'd forgive you for thinking that this is just me getting completely carried away about consistency and standards. After all, how we capitalise a label has no bearing on how well a model does its job of helping people make important decisions.

It is, however,  an area where a considerable amount of "scrappiness" can creep into our models, however. And, like it or not, many people will judge your models based on how they look.

In this guide, we'll look at the capitalisation convention my team uses and the tool we have created to check capitalisation.
