===================================================

December 11, 2024 / 00:04:54 / E67

In today’s episode of _What’s New at CFI_, join us as we talk all about the G in ESG. Our latest ESG offering is a mini module of three courses with a module dedicated to each of environmental, social, and governance issues and financial materiality.

Today, we focus on governance issues, which are arguably the most important, because without proper governance, how could anything be actioned?
