Corporate Finance Institute Awards 50 Scholarships, Empowering Aspiring Finance Leaders Globally
================================================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published January 29, 2025

Read Time 1 minutes

**January 29, 2025** – Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, is proud to announce that it has awarded 50 scholarships to aspiring finance professionals worldwide. This initiative is part of CFI’s ongoing commitment to expanding opportunities for individuals seeking to advance their careers in the finance and banking industry.

Scholarship recipients selected from a diverse pool of applicants will gain access to CFI’s comprehensive training programs, equipping them with the skills and knowledge required to excel in corporate finance, investment banking, financial analysis, and more. Through this initiative, CFI aims to break down financial and geographical barriers to high-quality education and skill development.

“We believe that access to financial education should not be limited by location or financial constraints,” said Angie Baliem, COO at CFI. “By awarding these scholarships, we are investing in the next generation of finance professionals, helping them build the expertise and confidence they need to succeed.”

Since its inception, CFI has been dedicated to bridging the skills gap in finance by offering world-class, practical learning experiences. The scholarships are part of a broader mission to create a more inclusive and skilled financial workforce.

Corporate Finance Institute (CFI) is the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools. Established in 2016, CFI is the go-to resource for over 2 million individuals and thousands of organizations worldwide.
