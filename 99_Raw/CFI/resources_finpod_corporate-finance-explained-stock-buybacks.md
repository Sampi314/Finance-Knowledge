What’s Really Behind the Rise of Corporate Stock Buybacks?
----------------------------------------------------------

In this episode of _Corporate Finance Explained_, we break down how buybacks work, when they create value, and when they can backfire. You’ll learn the financial strategy behind share repurchases, how they compare to dividends, and what finance professionals need to know about timing, capital structure, and investor perception.

Featuring real-world case studies from Apple, Boeing, and Bed Bath & Beyond, this is a must-listen for mid-career finance professionals navigating boardroom decisions and capital allocation models.
