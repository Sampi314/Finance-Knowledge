CFI Recognized as a Top Global Software Company in 2023
=======================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published February 8, 2023

Updated September 6, 2024

Read Time 2 minutes

February 8, 2023 – We’re thrilled to share that [**Corporate Finance Institute**](https://www.g2.com/products/corporate-finance-institute/reviews)
 **has been ranked #55 on** [**G2’s Best Global Software Companies of 2023**](https://www.g2.com/best-software-companies/top-companies#list)
 and [**Macabacus by CFI**](https://www.g2.com/products/corporate-finance-institute-macabacus/reviews#reviews)
 **has been ranked #42 in best Office Products**! 

More than 1700 reviews from aspiring and seasoned finance professionals around the world—each dedicated to learning, building and deepening their skills, and creating the future they want—led to this exciting recognition.

CFI is the practical learning and development resource for finance and banking professionals, offering accredited online courses, certifications, and tools to enhance skills, knowledge, and productivity. [Macabacus by CFI](https://macabacus.com/)
 is the leading professional workflow tool for Excel, Word, and PowerPoint, used as an add-in for productivity and brand compliance.

G2 is the largest software marketplace, visited by 80 million customers each year. Their [2023 Best Software Awards](https://www.g2.com/best-software-companies/top-companies#list)
 are based on a combination of Satisfaction and Market Presence scores per company, with the top 100 ranked across all categories they belong to.

![CFI review from G2 2023 Best Software Awards](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/g2-review-rahul.png)

![CFI review from G2 2023 Best Software Awards](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/g2-review-anna-n.png)

![CFI review from G2 2023 Best Software Awards](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/g2-review-jacob.png)

“While G2 publishes the Best Software Awards each year, they’re really awards from customers,” said Sara Rossio, Chief Product Officer at G2. “Representing a vote of confidence from real software users, these awards also spotlight those that have risen to the top among thousands of companies.”

**We’re deeply thankful to our customers for taking the time to show their appreciation, a gratitude that goes beyond reviews. Each learning journey taken, lesson accomplished, question asked, piece of feedback given, certification earned, LinkedIn comment shared, resource or productivity tool used, shows our team how to provide a meaningful and valuable learning platform.**

Thank you! Here’s to reaching higher, aiming further, and building success for years to come.

 or choose from all-access [individual](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/pricing/)
 and [CFI for Teams](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/cfi-for-teams/)
 plans—to see what everyone’s talking about. You can also try [Macabacus](https://macabacus.com/)
