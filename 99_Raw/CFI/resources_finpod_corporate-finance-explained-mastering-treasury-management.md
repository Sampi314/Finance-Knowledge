**How Do Major Corporations Manage Billions in Cash While Minimizing Financial Risk?**
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In this episode, we explore treasury management strategies used by Apple, Microsoft, and Coca-Cola to handle liquidity, working capital, and funding decisions.

### **Key Topics Covered:**

1.  How Apple invests in short-term securities to optimize liquidity
2.  How Microsoft moves global cash reserves to lower tax burdens
3.  How Coca-Cola hedges against currency risk to stabilize revenue
4.  The difference between short-term vs. long-term funding strategies

Treasury teams don’t just manage cash—they drive financial strategy. If you’re an FP&A analyst, treasury manager, or finance leader, this episode is packed with real-world insights to help you level up.
