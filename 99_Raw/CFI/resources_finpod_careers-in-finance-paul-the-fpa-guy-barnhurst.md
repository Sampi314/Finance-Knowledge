Careers in Finance | Paul “The FP&A Guy” Barnhurst
==================================================

April 24, 2025 / 00:26:47 / 106

In this episode of _Careers in Finance_, we sit down with Paul Barnhurst—also known as The FP&A Guy—to explore what it means to build a career in Financial Planning & Analysis. From his early days in procurement to leading FP&A functions across multiple companies, Paul shares how the role has evolved into one of the most strategic areas of corporate finance today.

If you’re exploring a future in FP&A—or wondering how to turn finance experience into something entrepreneurial—this is the episode to tune into.
