Corporate Finance Institute Announces the Launch of Ten New Online Courses
==========================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published May 15, 2024

Read Time 2 minutes

April 30, 2024 – Corporate Finance Institute® (CFI), the leader in online finance and banking training, certifications, and productivity tools, is excited to unveil the addition of ten new courses to its comprehensive learning platform. This significant expansion in April underscores CFI’s commitment to providing the most relevant and up-to-date educational resources to finance professionals around the globe.

The newly introduced courses are designed to cater to a variety of finance sectors and proficiency levels, featuring in-demand topics including financial planning and analysis (FP&A), risk management, and data visualization. The new courses include:

*   FP&A Professional Contractor Forecasting & Analysis
*   FP&A Professional Debt and Capex Forecasting & Analysis
*   FP&A Professional Financial Statement Aggregation & Analysis
*   FP&A Professional Dashboard Visualization & Analysis
*   Accounting – Reading and Analyzing Bank Financial Statements
*   Accounting for Business Combinations
*   Market Risk Fundamentals
*   ESG Risk Management
*   BIDA – Data Visualization
*   FMVA – AI-Enhanced Financial Analysis

These courses are structured to enhance knowledge and skills and equip professionals with the tools needed to succeed in a rapidly evolving financial landscape. Each course is developed by industry experts and includes practical, real-world applications to ensure that learning is both comprehensive and applicable.

“Our mission at CFI is to empower finance professionals to advance their careers through education that bridges the gap between theory and practical hands-on skill application,” said Scott Powell, Chief Content Officer at CFI. “With these new offerings, we are thrilled to continue expanding our curriculum to meet the growing needs of the global finance community.”

For additional information about the new courses and to enroll, visit [www.corporatefinanceinstitute.com](http://www.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/)
