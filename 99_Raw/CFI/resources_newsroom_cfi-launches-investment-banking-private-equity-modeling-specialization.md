CFI Launches Investment Banking & Private Equity Modeling Specialization
========================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published January 14, 2025

Read Time 1 minutes

**January 14, 2025**  — Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), the #1-rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, launches a new Investment Banking & Private Equity Modeling Specialization today. 

This specialization is designed to help professionals master advanced financial analysis and modeling techniques used by leading investment banks and private equity firms. The specialization focuses on developing expertise in key areas, including financial modeling (3-statement, DCF, LBO, and M&A), valuation, forecasting, and transactional decision-making—essential skills for excelling in high-stakes finance roles.

CFI’s Investment Banking and Private Equity Modeling Specialization delivers in-depth knowledge and skills to demonstrate on the job expertise for associates and analysts at any level.

“The demand for highly skilled investment banking and private equity professionals continues to grow,” says Anna Talerico, CEO of CFI. “Our Investment Banking & Private Equity Modeling Specialization meets this demand by providing professionals with the modelong skills that align with industry standards.”

This flexible, self-paced online program combines hands-on learning with real-world application to help develop desk-ready skills. Participants will gain practical experience applying best-in-class financial modeling standards, ensuring they are ready to excel in investment banking, private equity, or related fields.
