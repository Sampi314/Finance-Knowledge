Mastering FP&A 3-Statement Modeling
-----------------------------------

Building a 3-statement financial model is a critical skill for finance professionals, but many struggle to get their models to balance. In this episode of _What’s New at CFI_ on FinPod, we break down CFI’s latest course: **FP&A 3-Statement Modeling**—designed to help finance professionals build structured, scalable models with confidence.

### What You’ll Learn in This Episode:

*   Key differences between FP&A-style 3-statement modeling and investment banking models
*   How monthly forecasting adds complexity and how to manage it effectively
*   The relationship between cash flow and balance sheet that ensures model accuracy
*   A step-by-step approach to troubleshooting and balancing financial models
*   How this course helps finance professionals apply best practices in real-world scenarios

### Why This Matters:

Whether you’re an FP&A analyst, finance manager, or corporate finance professional, mastering 3-statement modeling is a must-have skill. This episode explores real-world applications, shares common pitfalls, and introduces a systematic approach to ensure model accuracy.
