### Piotroski F Score Factors

CFI’s Piotroski F score calculator can be used to assess a company’s financial strength by looking at nine factors. A score of either 0 or 1 is rewarded for each of these factors, depending on whether it has been fulfilled or not. The higher the score, the more reliable a stock is to invest in. These factors are categorized into three different sources of financial strength that analysts like to look at.

#### Profitability

**ROA:** [Return on assets](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/return-on-assets-roa-formula/)
. [Net Income](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/what-is-net-income/)
 divided by year beginning total assets. F score is 1 if ROA is positive, 0 otherwise.

**CFO:** [Operating cash flow](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/operating-cash-flow/)
 divided by year beginning total assets. F score is 1 if CFO is positive, 0 otherwise.

**∆ROA:** Change in ROA from the prior year. If ∆ROA > 0, F score is 1. Otherwise, F score is 0.

**ACCRUAL:** CFO compared to ROA. If CFO > ROA, F score is 1. Otherwise, F score is 0.

#### Leverage, Liquidity, and Source of Funds

**∆LEVER:** Change in [long-term debt](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/long-term-debt-ltd/)
/average total assets ratio. If the ratio compared to the prior year is lower, F score is 1, 0 otherwise.

**∆LIQUID:** Change in [current ratio](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/current-ratio-formula/)
. If the current ratio increases from the prior year, F score is 1, 0 otherwise.

**EQ\_OFFER:** Total [common equity](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/equities/common-stock/)
 between years. If common equity increases compared to prior year, F score is 1, 0 otherwise.

#### Operating Efficiency

**∆MARGIN:** Change in [gross margin ratio](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/gross-margin-ratio/)
. If the current year’s ratio minus prior year’s ratio > 0, F Score is 1, 0 otherwise.

**∆TURN:** Change in [asset turnover ratio](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/resources/accounting/asset-turnover-ratio/)
 (revenue/beginning year total assets). If current year’s ratio minus prior years > 0, F score is 1, 0 otherwise.
