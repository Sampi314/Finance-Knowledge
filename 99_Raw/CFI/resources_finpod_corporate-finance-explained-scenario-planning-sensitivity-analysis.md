In a world of constant change, rising interest rates, inflation, and global disruptions, how do top companies prepare for the unexpected? In this episode of _Corporate Finance Explained_, we break down scenario planning and sensitivity analysis, the essential tools finance teams use to stay agile, resilient, and ready for anything.

Learn how Airbnb, Starbucks, Delta Airlines, and Unilever use these strategies to thrive despite uncertainty, from pandemic disruptions to geopolitical shifts.
