CFI launches the FinTech Industry Professional (FTIP®) Certification
====================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published December 18, 2023

Read Time 2 minutes

December 18, 2023 – Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), a #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, announces the launch of its FinTech Industry Professional (FTIP®) Certification, addressing the growing skill gap in FinTech. The segment is set to reach $1.5 trillion in revenue by 2030, according to the Boston Consulting Group, growing four times faster than traditional finance and offering significant opportunities for professionals and organizations alike. There are already over 30,000 global FinTech companies and this number is rapidly growing.

The FTIP® program, led by industry experts Andrew Loo, Sebastian Taylor, and Matt Christopher, is a comprehensive certification addressing the dynamic landscape of FinTech. The course material covers key sectors, including Digital Banking, Cryptocurrencies, WealthTech, InsurTech, Cybersecurity, and Data Science. Uniquely integrating finance fundamentals with tech innovations, this certification program ensures professionals stay ahead in the rapidly evolving FinTech landscape. The program’s thorough coverage across multiple domains makes it a valuable asset for those seeking a deep understanding of FinTech.

Anna Talerico, CFI’s CEO, says, “CFI’s FTIP® designation empowers professionals to confidently navigate the FinTech landscape, providing a competitive edge and unlocking opportunities for career growth.”

Upon completion, professionals will gain a thorough understanding of FinTech trends and practical skills in this emerging technology. The FTIP® designation suits various FinTech careers, including Quantitative Analysts, Data Analysts, Business Analysts, and Software Developers. As the industry evolves, FTIP®-equipped professionals will be strongly positioned to lead the way in finance, banking, insurance, and tech.
