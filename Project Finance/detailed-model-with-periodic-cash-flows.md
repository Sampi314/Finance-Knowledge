# Detailed Model with Periodic Cash Flows

**Source:** https://edbodmer.com/detailed-model-with-periodic-cash-flows

---

This webpage works through the time based flip with periodic modelling where the construction period has a month by month analysis and the subsequent periods are modeled with quarterly flows to reflect the tax payments.  This model also has some more detail on the yield including the P90 etc.
