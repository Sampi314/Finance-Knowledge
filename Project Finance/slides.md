# Slides Used in Project Finance and Corporate Finance Analysis

**Source:** https://edbodmer.com/slides

This page includes some of my project finance, corporate and energy analysis slides that I use in courses. I generally spend more time on project finance, corporate finance and energy finance models  to demonstrate concepts along with flip charts than with the power point slides. The power point slides for project finance, corporate finance and energy finance however may be a good reference and provide structure to the analysis.

 Project Finance Lecture:
-------------------------

The power point slides below are used for an overview of project finance. I start with risk analysis and suggest that a few famous and old case studies can give a good background on different types of risks in projects. Then I move to debt structuring and discuss various issues in the sizing, funding, repayment, interest rates and credit protections. For both the risk case studies and the structuring, you can find much more detail in other places on the website.
