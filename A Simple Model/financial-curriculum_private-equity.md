What You Will Learn
-------------------

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_ugTsN.png)

### [Introduction to Private Equity](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/overview)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/overview)

*   Lessons:**4**
*   Videos:**4**
*   Quizzes:**3**

This series will introduce private equity by first exploring the industry, and then explaining how both an independent sponsor and private equity fund operate. 

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_uj6gE.png)

### [Sourcing a Transaction](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/sourcing-transaction)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/sourcing-transaction)

*   Lessons:**3**
*   Videos:**3**
*   Quizzes:**2**

This series is focused on how private equity firms source attractive investment opportunities.

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_tB0jZ.png)

### [Private Equity Due Diligence](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/due-dilligence)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/due-dilligence)

*   Lessons:**3**
*   Videos:**3**

This course will discuss preliminary due diligence for a target company. More advanced due diligence will be addressed in the course titled "Closing the Transaction."

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_V0msU.png)

### [Securing Exclusivity](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/securing-exclusivity)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/securing-exclusivity)

*   Lessons:**5**
*   Videos:**4**
*   Quizzes:**1**

In this course you will learn how a private equity firm secures an "exclusivity period" to evaluate an investment opportunity. This is done by executing a letter of intent, but before the letter of intent is signed the private equity firm must compete with all potential bidders. 

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_NgDpN.png)

### [Letter of Intent](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/letter-of-intent)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/letter-of-intent)

*   Lessons:**2**
*   Videos:**2**
*   Quizzes:**2**

This series is focused on the letter of intent (LOI) in a private equity transaction. This document helps serve as a guide for the documentation required to consummate the transaction (the “definitive agreements”).

![](https://portal.asimplemodel.com/Assets/Files/Cat_xmSgQ.png)

### [Closing the Transaction](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/closing-transaction)

[Overview](https://www.asimplemodel.com/financial-curriculum/private-equity/closing-transaction)

*   Lessons:**8**
*   Videos:**6**
*   Quizzes:**3**

Once a private equity investment has been secured under letter of intent, the private equity firm is granted an exclusivity period to close the transaction. This starts the clock and provides a deadline for the work required to complete due diligence and negotiate the definitive agreements. In this course you will learn what is required to close.
