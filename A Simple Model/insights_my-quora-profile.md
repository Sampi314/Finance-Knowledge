I started answering questions on Quora a couple years ago to help create awareness for ASimpleModel. I have since found that I rather enjoy answering questions of a wider variety, and thought it would be relevant to share them here. Below you will find a selection of answers that relate to finance and / or investing, but not necessarily to financial models.
