### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

      

[a\
\
#### How do I upgrade my ASM+ account to ASM+ PRO? How do I upgrade from a monthly plan to a quarterly plan?](https://www.asimplemodel.com/about-us/contact-us#)

You can upgrade at any time with the link under Account -> Subscription History -> Upgrade. The new payment plan will apply any sums already paid so that you are not penalized for upgrading.

[a\
\
#### The quizzing platform is asking me to enter an ID. How do I work around this?](https://www.asimplemodel.com/about-us/contact-us#)

Please be sure that cookies are enabled in your browser of choice before proceeding with the quizzing platform. With cookies enabled you will not need to enter any additional information to access the quizzes or exams.

[a\
\
#### Do you offer consulting work or one-on-one instruction?](https://www.asimplemodel.com/about-us/contact-us#)

I offer consulting services to a very small collection of entities. I unfortunately do not offer one-on-one instruction.

[a\
\
#### Can I contact you about my job search process? Can you introduce me to people in private equity?](https://www.asimplemodel.com/about-us/contact-us#)

Unfortunately I do not make introductions if we do not have a history of working together beyond this website.

[a\
\
#### How do I cancel my subscription?](https://www.asimplemodel.com/about-us/contact-us#)

You can cancel at any time with the link under Account -> Subscription History -> Cancel Subscription. Once you cancel you will receive an email confirming cancellation, but your account will remain active until the pay period has expired.
