Financial Modelling Foundations course
======================================

The _Financial Modelling Foundations_ is the perfect starting point for anyone looking to build robust and practical financial models. Using a solar project case study, this programme introduces key concepts in model design and structure while teaching participants how to build an integrated three-statement model from scratch.

What you’ll learn:
------------------

Learn the core skills that every financial modeller needs

Learn how to build an integrated three-statement model from scratch

Create models that are flexible, easy to update and easy to use

Create consistent and robust model structures

01 Revenue and operating costs

— Understanding solar power production metrics  
— Solar revenue  
— O&M costs  
— Sign convention

02 Non-current assets and depreciation

— Non-current assets  
— Depreciation

03 Equity financing

— 100% equity financing  
— Profit restricted dividends  
— Equity IRR

04 Senior debt financing

— Understanding debt sizing  
— Debt repayment profiles  
— Senior debt interest payment  
— Debt service cover ratio

05 Tax

— Understanding the fundamentals of modelling tax  
— Tax depreciation and thin capitalisation  
— Tax loss carry forwards

06 Other advanced / follow-on topics

— Debt sizing  
— Sculpting debt repayments to meet minimum DSCR  
— Working capital assignment and solution  
— Amortising / level debt service debt repayment profile

Frequently asked questions
--------------------------

What prior knowledge is required to attend?

This course is designed for beginners and those looking to strengthen their financial modelling foundations. You will need a basic understanding of Excel and finance concepts to help you get the most out of the training. If you’re unsure about your suitability, feel free to contact us for guidance.

What is included in the course fee?

The course fee covers online or in-person training led by Kenny Whitelaw-Jones and Toby Tucker, both recognised experts in financial modelling.

You’ll receive access to all the working files used during the course, along with a free digital copy of the _Financial Modelling Handbook_. Lunch and refreshments will be provided on both days (applies to in-person sessions only).

Upon completion, you will be awarded a certificate of attendance displaying 14 CPD hours. Additionally, the course fee includes a 100% money-back guarantee, giving you complete confidence in the value of your investment.

What do I need to bring?

Participants need to bring a laptop with Excel 2016 or higher installed (Office 365 is recommended), plus a power lead and also an adaptor if travelling from overseas.

How is the live online training different from the in-person course?

The content, learning outcomes and exercises are the same. The difference is the delivery format.  
Live online training is delivered **across four half-day sessions** rather than two full-day workshops, making it easier to balance learning with work commitments.
