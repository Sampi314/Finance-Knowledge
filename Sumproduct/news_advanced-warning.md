Advanced Warning
================

29 November 2014

Each year SumProduct teams up with the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (**CIMA**) to deliver a Christmas Quiz based on an Excel problem for its **Insight** magazine. You don’t have to be a member of CIMA to read the magazine or enter the competition. And you might want to think about entering.

The first prize will be a GBP100 Amazon voucher, the second a GBP75 Amazon voucher and the third prize one for GBP50. You don’t need to be an Excel guru either – the problems are usually real-life issues faced every day by finance professionals in the world of spreadsheeting.

The competition will go live very soon. We’ll bring you more details at [www.sumproduct.com/news](https://www.sumproduct.com/news)
 shortly or you can check out the CIMA website at [www.cimaglobal.com](http://www.cimaglobal.com/)
 in the meantime.
