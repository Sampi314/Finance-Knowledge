Out and About in Jakarta
========================

18 July 2016

Melbourne director Liam Bastick is off gallivanting in Indonesia in the week commencing **Monday 25 July 2016**. The main purpose of his travels is to run an in-house training course in strategic forecasting and budgeting, but he will be meeting up with one or two clients as well whilst over there.

If you would like to meet up with Liam in person to enquire about the many financial modelling services we offer (_e.g._ modelling, auditing, valuations, data management, training), he would be delighted to catch up with you and your colleagues and make your acquaintance.

SumProduct provides many modelling services across Asia and the Pacific region. We may not have an office in Indonesia presently, but please do not let this deter you if you have an enquiry. We are often in the locale and understand local business requirements.

If you’d like to catch up in Jakarta drop us a line in the first instance to [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
, preferably by close of business on **Thursday 21 July**. We’d be delighted to hear from you and try to arrange a mutually convenient no-obligation catch-up.

By the way, we’ve _never_ seen the traffic move as quickly as it’s shown in our photo above!
