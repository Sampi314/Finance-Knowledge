M&A Training in Sydney
======================

M&A Training in Sydney
======================

16 August 2016

On special request, we are hosting our Financial Modelling for Mergers and Acquisitions at our office in Sydney! This course is running from Thursday 25 August to Saturday 27 August (yes, that’s not a typo, and part of the request too!).

There’s room for a couple more attendees, so if you’re interested in learning more about financial modelling or M&A (or both, as the case is), email [training@sumproduct.com](mailto:training@sumproduct.com)
and we’ll send you more details.

On a related note, Liam is going to be in town to meet with clients and colleagues before the course from Tuesday 23rd onwards, so if you were interested in what we do and want to catch up for a coffee, let us know at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
. Looking forward to hearing from you!
