Power BI Blog: Last Day of Month Subscriptions
==============================================

17 October 2024

_Welcome back to this week’s edition of the Power BI blog series. This week, we look at subscribing to reports on the last day of the month._

It is now possible to subscribe to standard and dynamic per recipient reports on the last day of the month. You no longer need to manage leap years or figure out how to make things work on a 30-day and / or 31-day basis. When you set up the monthly subscription, select the ‘last day of month’ option.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

For the dynamic per recipient subscription, choose the ‘last day of month’ option as part of the Schedule setup.
