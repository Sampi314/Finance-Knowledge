SumProduct in Toronto
=====================

21 September 2016

As promised, SumProduct is coming to Toronto on Thursday and presenting at the ModelOff GTC on Friday morning. If you happen to be attending the event, please come by and introduce yourself during one of the breaks! Otherwise, if you’d like us to come by your office and show how we can help your business with our services, we’d be happy to come and see you.

To arrange a mutually convenient time, drop us a line at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
