Annual Quiz Update
==================

Annual Quiz Update
==================

15 December 2016

Formerly known as the Christmas Quiz, the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants’ (CIMA’s) Annual Quiz has just been published on [CIMA’s Insight website](http://www.cimaglobal.com/Members/member-benefits/insight-magazine/Insight-December-2016/Spreadsheet-skills-multiple-criteria-with-errors/)
. Due to the fact that CIMA is about to merge with another accounting institute, unfortunately – and contrary to what was written in December’s newsletter – **this year’s quiz will no longer be able to offer cash prizes and is for fun only**. Sorry – this is beyond our control. Hopefully, the prizes will return next year!

It’s still the same essentially though: challenge your work colleagues, Excel gurus, family and friends as to who can come up with the best solution quickest. Enjoy and have fun!

**_Update:_** Presently, the link to download the associated Excel file does not appear to work. We have raised this with CIMA and the matter should be fixed shortly.
