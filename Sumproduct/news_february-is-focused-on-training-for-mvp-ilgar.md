February is focused on training for MVP Ilgar
=============================================

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/image-110.png)

SumProduct Senior Manager Ilgar Zarbaliyev has a busy month coming up!

On 4th February 2026**,** he will be delivering _Advanced Microsoft Word_ training in Abu Dhabi, covering lots of advanced topics, from creating a Table of Contents to tracking changes and everything in between.

On the following 5th February, he will be conducting more training in Abu Dhabi, this time taking the attendees through the delights of _Copilot in Microsoft Office_.  

On 11th February, Ilgar will be a speaker at the Get Certified: Exam DP-600 event, part of the Become a Fabric Analytics Engineer series hosted by Microsoft Reactor.  In his session, he will focus on _Exploring the Data Warehouse in Microsoft Fabric_, covering essential topics such as loading and querying data, monitoring performance, and securing a Data Warehouse.  This session is designed for professionals preparing for the DP-600 certification as well as anyone working with enterprise analytics solutions in Fabric.

Find out more and register at:

[https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/reactor/events/26714/?wt.mc\_id=facebook\_26714\_social\_reactor](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/reactor/events/26714/?wt.mc_id=facebook_26714_social_reactor)
