Summit To Talk About… Again
===========================

26 October 2016

SumProduct is going back to Redmond this November. Microsoft has announced its annual Most Valuable Professional (MVP) Summit this year for us all to get a chance to pick the brains of the various product groups – in our case the Excel team.

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/2a8331eacf96cb84f85214b737de896d.jpg)

Excel MVPs at a past summit. Picture courtesy of Tom Urtis.

Whilst much of the content may have to remain confidential for the time being, SumProduct would like to invite our readers to submit any queries or issues they may have for the Excel team (please note that we’ve found we get more traction with general rather than technically specific questions!). If you have questions, drop us a line at [contact@sumproduct.com](mailto:contact@sumproduct.com)
.

We can’t promise we can get answers to any questions raised, but we’ll report back what we can in the December newsletter.
