Final Friday Fix: June 2018 Challenge
=====================================

29 June 2018

_On the final Friday of each month, we’re going to set an Excel challenge for you to puzzle over so that you can get your “Excel fix”. Challenge your office colleagues to see who can solve the puzzle quickest. There’s no prizes at this stage, you’re playing for bragging rights only!_

**_Where’d my row headers go?_**

Every now and then we release a question which almost breaks the internet (_Ed. Note: not true)_: something so ingrained in the psyche of Excel modeller and / or financial analyst that it will change their way of thinking forever more. Today is not one of those days.

**_The Challenge_**

Simple question this month:

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/cce66982e5b92636ae313f6ee36af02e.jpg)

Take a look at the row headers – the even ones have gone missing! Where did the row numbers go (_i.e._ take a close look at the event rows)? You can download the [attached Excel file](https://sumproduct.com/assets/blog-pictures/2018/challenges/06-june/sp-where-did-my-row-headers-go.xlsx)
 here and have a play…

_Sound easy? Have a go. We’ll publish one solution in Monday’s blog. Have a great weekend!_
