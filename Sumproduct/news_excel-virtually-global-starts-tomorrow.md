Excel Virtually Global Starts Tomorrow!
=======================================

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Excel Virtually Global 2025 is starting tomorrow (Tuesday 14 October)!

Microsoft’s Most Valuable Professionals, or MVPs, are technology experts who passionately share their knowledge with the community.  They are always on the “bleeding edge” and have an unstoppable urge to get their hands on new, exciting technologies. This virtual conference, now in its tenth year, presents Excel, Data Platform and PowerPoint MVPs, together with other acknowledged experts from Microsoft and / or around the globe to present, answer questions and demonstrate the future of Excel and how it will make your life easier, personally and professionally.

The subjects covered include Excel, Power BI, Fabric, ChatGPT, Copilot, Regex and Python amongst other topics.  And note well – it’s not all in English, with sessions in Spanish and Portuguese too.

Most sessions will be recorded so you may watch them later with downloads aplenty – there are no medals for staying up to watch the entire event live, especially given we have a record-breaking 60 presenters this year!

From your own favourite chair, bring a laptop, an inquisitive mind and your sense of humour.  Be prepared to learn heaps.  And remember, it’s for charity – please be generous and donate to a charity of your choice!

The event is free and you don’t need to pre-register.  We’d love it if you donate to a favourite charity of your choice and tell us about it in the Contact form at the foot of each page of the website ([www.excelvirtuallyglobal.com](http://www.excelvirtuallyglobal.com/)
).
