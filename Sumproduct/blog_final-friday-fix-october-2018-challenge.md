**_A Prime Example_**

A positive integer (whole number) is said to be **prime** if and only if two different integers divide it: one (1) and itself. Therefore, 1 is not prime and 2 is the only even prime _etc._

Prime numbers are useful for scenario analysis, public key encryption, reconciliation tricks and Final Friday Fixes.

**_The Challenge_**
