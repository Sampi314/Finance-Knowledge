New Features for Excel June 2025
================================

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

The updates for June introduce further features that enhance Copilot’s capabilities in Excel and deliver an improved iOS experience for the Insiders program.

In full, here is this month’s list:

**_Excel for Windows, Mac and web_**

*   Smarter context awareness for Copilot in Excel

**_Excel for Windows and Mac_**

*   Value tokens to visually indicate a data type or other rich format

**_Excel for web_**

*   Formula generation from the grid upon “-“

**_Excel for mobile_**

*   Open shared files on Android devices (Insiders)
*   Seach for templates for iOS (Insiders).
