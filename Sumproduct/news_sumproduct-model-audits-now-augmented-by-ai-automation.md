SumProduct Model Audits: Now Augmented by AI Automation
=======================================================

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

_Dummy example of SumProduct audit findings raised by AI and automated tools in parallel, augmenting the full model audit process._

In today’s fast-paced, data-driven world, financial models are the backbone of decision-making in business, large-scale projects and corporate finance.  SumProduct sees these models all the time.  They serve as sophisticated tools for forecasting, valuation, risk assessment and strategic planning.  Their accuracy, reliability, and integrity directly influence key business outcomes, ranging from investments and mergers to project finance and restructuring.

Given their pivotal role, the need for rigorous scrutiny through a financial model audit has never been greater – as we’ve discussed many times before.  But the world of financial modelling is auditing – and so is SumProduct.

Now more than ever, the effect of due diligence, effective processes and material significance, accountants and analysts need to blend of commercial judgment, industry knowledge, structured scepticism and automation via coding scripts and artificial intelligence to stay ahead of the competition.

We can provide a financial model audit, which is a systematic, independent review of a financial model to assess its accuracy, completeness, consistency and suitability for your intended purpose. 

Typically, we know they are used for feasibility, refinance and project / investment appraisal in a wide variety of arenas (_e.g._ project finance, public private partnerships, contingency plans, optimisation, strategic forecasting).  Our aim is to ensure that your model is free from material errors, aligns with commercial objectives, complies with industry standards and can withstand scrutiny from stakeholders, regulators, financiers and investors.

Together with automation tools and AI, our auditors _still_ lead every engagement, _still_ challenge assumptions, _still_ trace the logic that connects a model’s inputs to its outputs and _still_ apply the commercial context that only comes from years of experience.  AI helps analyse and raise interesting questions faster, but humans still need to pull the findings together — the ones about whether the model will hold up under stress, whether the control structure does what the user expects and whether the outputs can be trusted for the decisions they’re being used to support.

Automated tools using AI trained by seasoned professionals raise findings quicker and more efficiently and can review _everything_, rather than just samples.  High level summaries – not final reports – may be produced in minutes, if not _seconds_.  Audits will become faster, more streamlined, more efficient and cheaper.  Of course, issues may still be missed.  That’s where experience comes in.  And SumProduct is one of the most experienced model audit companies out there.
