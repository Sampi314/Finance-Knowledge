Power BI Modern Filter Experience Upgrade Delayed
=================================================

7 May 2020

Microsoft has announced that in light of the current COVID-19 pandemic, they have decided to give more flexibility by providing an additional month for users to move over to the modern filter experience before all reports in Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service are automatically migrated. Therefore, users have a little more time to update your reports so you can tweak / customise any of the Filter pane formatting before your reports are scheduled for an auto-upgrade in May.
