Resurfacing Surface
===================

12 September 2014

Launched in the United States June, the past two months have seen the latest hybrid laptop / tablet become available in a further 25 markets. However, you might have had difficulty in procuring one with some retailers (e.g. Australian) reporting scorched surfaces (well a sell out at least but we couldn’t resist the pun) by lunchtime on launch date.Therefore, you might be interested to learn that Microsoft has announced stock replenishment is its “top priority” and that they should be available in existing markets within “…the next week or two…”.Decisions, decisions: Surface 3 v iPhone 6? It sounds like a football score…
