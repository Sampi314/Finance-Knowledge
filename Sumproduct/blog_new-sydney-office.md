New Sydney Office
=================

1 July 2016

SumProduct decided that Sydney needed more room for a growing team and have re-located to a new Sydney office! As of the 1st of July you can now find us at Suite 52, 88 Pitt Street Sydney. For those of you who may know our previous office location, you’d know that we are quite literally moving down and around the corner.

This exciting move didn’t come without a few ups and downs. Running across Sydney CBD like a mad chicken and seeing some (way too many) downright wrong office locations on the way, until we finally found the ‘the one’.

For anyone who isn’t familiar with Sydney CBD, please allow me to fill you in! In between George Street and Castlereagh Street, we are local to the Reserve Bank of Australia, Martin Place Station, Channel 7 and the NSW Treasury.

In Sydney alone, we’ve had the pleasure of working with some great companies that include NAB, the NSW Treasury, and Helmsman. We endeavour to continue providing high quality work for current and future clients.

For all enquiries please see our contact details below. We do hope to speak to you soon!
