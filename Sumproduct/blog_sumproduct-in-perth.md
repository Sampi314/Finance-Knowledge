SumProduct in Perth
===================

7 June 2016

Conscious we presently do not have an office in Perth but we do have plenty of clients, we thought we’d better mention we’ll be presenting at no less than two accounting conferences in Perth in mid-June. We’ll also have some time to spare, if you’d like to schedule a catch-up with Melbourne director Liam Bastick. Liam will be in Perth from Tuesday 14th to Friday 17th June should you wish to discuss Excel, modelling work and / or training. Please contact our mistress of ceremonies, Stefenie Long, if you’d like to arrange a catch-up.
