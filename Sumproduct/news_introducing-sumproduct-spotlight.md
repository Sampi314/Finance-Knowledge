Introducing SumProduct Spotlight
================================

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/image-160.png)

_Artwork not finalised!_

We’re excited to introduce **SumProduct Spotlight** — a new, streamlined way to stay on top of the latest developments across Excel, AI, Power BI, Fabric and the wider Microsoft analytics ecosystem.

For those of you who know and value the long‑running **SumProduct Newsletter**, rest assured: the monthly deep dive isn’t going anywhere.  Spotlight’s role is to complement it by offering a more concise, focused update for those readers who want the highlights without the wait.

Every issue brings together a mix of event highlights, Microsoft product updates and practical insights.  Think of it as a collection of short reads with depth, designed to respect your time while still delivering value.  Each article includes clear links to explore further if a topic catches your interest.

In the first edition, we share highlights from international events, explore recent advances in Excel and Power BI and revisit core modelling principles that underpin reliable, trusted analysis — the ideas we use every day when helping organisations build better financial and analytical models.

Whether you’re an analyst, modeller, finance professional or decision-maker, Spotlight is designed to give you clarity, context and direction through a quick read.  And this is just the beginning, as Spotlight evolves, we’ll continue refining its focus based on what you find most valuable.  As always, we’d love to hear your thoughts so please [get in touch](https://sumproduct.com/contact/)
!

Spotlight launches mid-March and will be circulated to all our usual subscribers.  If you aren’t subscribed already, simply join at the foot of any SumProduct page.
