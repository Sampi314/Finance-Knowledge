New Features for Excel March 2024
=================================

26 March 2024

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/b53d4df14ca3db330e6e711e41cc50cc.jpg)

This month sees Export to CSV and Show Details in PivotTables are now available in Excel for the web. Also, creating Power BI-connected tables is available in Excel for Windows users, together Office Scripts for Office 365 E1 and F3 licenses.

In summary:

**_Excel for the web_**

*   Export to CSV
*   Create Power BI Connected Tables in Excel
*   Show Details in PivotTables

**_Excel for Windows_**

*   Office Scripts for Office 365 E1 and F3 Licences.

As always, we’ll be detailing these updates in our May newsletter (sadly, they came out too late for the April newsletter). Please remember we have virtual / online training in Excel which you can find out more about [here](https://www.sumproduct.com/courses/excel-tips-tricks)
