The Excel Function Bible
========================

20 June 2014

SumProduct would like to pass on its congratulations to Norman Harker and Ron de Bruin who have recently created the Excel Function Bible.

The Bible contains 468 example files and a Function Index workbook to aid navigation. And it’s free – although donations are especially welcomed. Check out the Bible at Ron de Bruin’s website [here](http://www.rondebruin.nl/efb/efb.htm)
. Very useful for Excel devotees everywhere.
