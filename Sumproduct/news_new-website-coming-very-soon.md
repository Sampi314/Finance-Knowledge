New Website Coming Very Soon
============================

7 September 2015

We know you will probably find it hard to contain yourselves, but SumProduct will have a re-vamped website coming to a theatre near you very soon. Hold on to your (construction) hats!Aside from a new look, the new website will allow access to archived articles, online training, new improved information areas and other such niceties. Not all of these new features will be available on Day 1, it’s a work in progress, but it will allow for greater flexibility reflecting the content building up on this website. We hope you like it.Watch this space: the site is coming **very** soon…
