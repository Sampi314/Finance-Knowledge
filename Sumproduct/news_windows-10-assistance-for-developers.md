Windows 10 Assistance for Developers
====================================

5 September 2015

After recently having to explain some of the issues between Windows 10 and Excel 2010, it’s good to provide a more positive bulletin this time around. Microsoft has announced a whole range of informative courses and blogs on Windows 10, chiefly aimed at the IT developer seeking to write applications rather than the Windows 10 newbie or Excel dabbler.Feel free to peruse:

*   Click [here](https://www.microsoftvirtualacademy.com/en-US/training-courses/getting-started-with-windows-10-for-it-professionals-10629/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for the Microsoft Virtual Academy for Windows 10
*   Click [here](https://dev.windows.com/en-us/getstarted/whats-new-windows-10/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for what’s new in development
*   Click [here](https://dev.windows.com/en-us/getstarted/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for getting started
*   Click [here](https://dev.windows.com/en-us/design/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for designing
*   Click [here](https://dev.windows.com/en-us/develop/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for developing
*   Click [here](https://dev.windows.com/en-us/publish/?Wt.mc_id=dx_MVP4038226)
     for publishing.
