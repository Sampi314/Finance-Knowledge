Unlock Excel Back InTheBlack
============================

13 April 2017

Our upcoming _Unlock Excel_ event has made the news again. Fellow presenters and Excel MVPs Inge Hawighorst and Ken Puls have passed on their thoughts with “deep diving” into Excel’s functionalities and features, notably some of the visualisation tools and Power Query in the latest [InTheBlack article](https://www.intheblack.com/articles/2017/04/06/go-beyond-basic-excel)
. This follows on from our own Liam Bastick’s interview back in February – click [here](https://www.intheblack.com/articles/2017/02/27/influencing-with-excel)
 if you missed it.

They will both be presenting at the Unlock Excel events in Melbourne, Brisbane, Sydney and Auckland in early May. Tickets are selling fast but you can still secure yours from the official website [here](https://www.cpaaustralia.com.au/training-and-events/conferences/unlock-excel)
. Hope to see you there!
