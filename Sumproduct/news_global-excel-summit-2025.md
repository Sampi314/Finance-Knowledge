Global Excel Summit 2025
========================

15 January 2025

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/2ef15ec20c2090ca4ba881a1943646fe.jpg)

Well, with our own **Liam Bastick** one of the hosts, we thought we should point out that the London-based Microsoft Excel conference for professionals and enthusiasts returns 4 to 6 February at the Soho Place Theatre in London. Apparently, the wifi is even reliable!

Whilst Liam will finally achieve the lifetime ambition of performing on stage to a paying audience in Soho _(we presume they’re paying for him to stay clothed – Ed.)_, the speaker list sounds like a who’s who in Excel, with fellow Excel MVP **Leila Gharani** headlining amongst many other notables.

The first two days are in person as well as online with Day 3 online only.

For full details, please see [https://globalexcelsummit.com/](https://globalexcelsummit.com/)
.
