**Final Friday Fix**

This month, our Final Friday Fix comes from a query that came in to us. So without further ado, let’s take a look!

If you weren’t aware, it’s possible to bold specific characters in a cell, without bolding others. The same applies to italics, underline, and so on. So, you can **create**_lines of text_ that look like this, quite _e_**a**_s_**i**_l_**y**.

**The Challenge**

This week’s challenge is likely to need a VBA solution, so feel free to use it! Can you find a way to extract and output just the **bold** characters from a cell?

As always, our solution to the problem will go up next week. Have a good weekend!
