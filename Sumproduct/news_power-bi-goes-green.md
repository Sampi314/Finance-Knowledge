Power BI Goes Green
===================

30 November 2022

This is going to stuff up a few textbooks! Power BI is updating its accent colour to teal (look it up, it’s a colour apparently). This change was made to ensure Power BI is more accessible for users with disabilities. The new colour supposedly improves contrast and increases visibility of the user interface in Power BI (we really aren’t so sure), making the experience easier to use and more inclusive.

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

The Power BI brand colour and icon logo will remain yellow.
