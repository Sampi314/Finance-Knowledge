Power BI Development
====================

10 June 2016

Back in March of this year, Sydney director Tim Heng travelled to Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle, Washington where he was lucky enough to attend the Data Insights Summit. Heavily discussed (aside from the weather), was Power BI – the next big thing in report development and distribution.

Since the Summit, Tim has had the pleasure of introducing the product to a large amount of our clients and colleagues all around Australia. Power BI has been so well received that several Melbourne clients have asked for encore demonstrations.

Tim will be visiting our Melbourne office from Tuesday the 22nd to Friday the 24th of June. Should you want your own Power BI demonstration from Tim or wish to set up a catch up at some point on training or consulting work, please let us know.
