A Comprehensive Strategic Process
---------------------------------

Our method focuses on understanding both the current and ideal states of your organisation, evaluating all possible options, and taking decisive action. Here is an outline of the key stages of our strategic process, showing how each phase builds on the previous one to deliver a cohesive plan and business roadmap.

[Interested? Contact us](https://sumproduct.com/contact/)

1\. Parameters
--------------

Company View
------------

We begin by establishing a clear understanding of your organisation’s current position. This involves an in-depth review of your vision, mission, strategic objectives, and overall culture. By grasping the present state and aspirations of your business, we lay the groundwork for a strategy that is both relevant and forward-thinking.

Financial Analysis
------------------

A detailed financial review is undertaken to assess performance across key areas. We examine revenue streams, profitability, budgets, and forecasting data to gauge financial health. This analysis ensures that any recommended strategies are not only innovative but also economically viable, supporting sustainable growth.

Other Stakeholders
------------------

Identifying key stakeholders is essential to forming a holistic view of the business environment. This includes recognising internal influencers such as employees and management, alongside external parties like customers, suppliers, investors, and community representatives. Their insights are vital in aligning the strategic direction with broader interests and mitigating potential risks.

2\. Development
---------------

External Analysis
-----------------

This stage focuses on the market environment in which your business operates.

*   **Supply Chain Analysis:** We assess your supply chain to identify any inefficiencies or opportunities for optimisation.
*   **Competitive Forces:** By analysing the competitive landscape, we determine your market positioning relative to peers.
*   **Opportunities and Threats:** A thorough evaluation of market trends helps in recognising external opportunities and potential challenges.
*   **Market Perspective:** We study industry dynamics and customer behaviours to anticipate shifts and innovate accordingly.

Internal Analysis
-----------------

A comprehensive review of internal capabilities is crucial.

*   **Brand and Resources:** We evaluate the strength of your brand and the resources available, ensuring they support your strategic ambitions.
*   **Core Competence:** Identifying your unique strengths enables us to focus on what sets you apart in the market.
*   **Strengths and Weaknesses:** An honest assessment of internal performance helps in pinpointing areas for improvement.
*   **Management Team’s Skill Set:** We review the expertise of your leadership team, ensuring they are equipped to drive strategic initiatives.
*   **Promotion and Distribution:** An analysis of your current marketing and distribution methods informs adjustments needed to maximise market reach.

Ideal State vs Current State
----------------------------

By comparing where your organisation currently stands with where you aspire to be, we identify the gaps that need to be bridged. This gap analysis is instrumental in shaping the strategic interventions required to move from the present state to your ideal future state.

3\. Evaluation
--------------

Strategic Options
-----------------

Once the analysis is complete, we explore a range of strategic options. Each pathway is carefully reviewed to ensure it aligns with your goals and addresses identified gaps, taking into account both the opportunities and risks present in the market.

Modelling
---------

Our approach includes both quantitative and qualitative modelling techniques to simulate different scenarios. This process helps to test the viability of each strategic option, allowing us to forecast potential outcomes and fine-tune strategies before implementation.

Valuation Insights
------------------

Drawing on the modelling outcomes, we provide robust financial and operational insights. This stage is critical for assessing the return on investment and the feasibility of each strategy, ensuring that decisions are underpinned by data and aligned with long-term growth objectives.

4\. Decision
------------

Board Evaluate & Decide
-----------------------

In the decision-making phase, the strategic recommendations are presented to the leadership or board. Through a collaborative review process, the board evaluates all analyses, models, and insights to approve a plan that best meets the organisation’s needs. This stage ensures that the strategy is fully endorsed at the highest level, paving the way for successful execution.

5\. Action
----------

Strategic Plan
--------------

Following approval, we formalise a long-term strategic plan. This document encapsulates your organisation’s vision, mission, and core strategic initiatives. It serves as a blueprint for guiding sustained growth, innovation, and competitive advantage over time.

Business Plan
-------------

In parallel, a detailed business plan is developed to translate the strategic vision into operational terms. This plan outlines the specific steps, financial targets, and tactical initiatives required to implement the strategy effectively. It includes clear timelines, resource allocations, and measurable milestones to ensure accountability and track progress.
