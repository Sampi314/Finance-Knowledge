Are You Getting it Five Times a Week?
=====================================

8 January 2018

We’ve made a New Year’s Resolution for 2018 we are hoping we can stick with… Our [Blog](https://www.sumproduct.com/blog)
 page is going to have a new blog each day Monday to Friday starting from this week! If you’re not already a regular reader, maybe you should be from now on.

Our standard schedule will be as follows:

*   **_Mondays:_** [A to Z of Excel Functions](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought/a-to-z-of-excel-functions)
     – start your week with a function. Do you know your **COUNTA** from your **COUNTBLANK**?
*   **_Tuesdays:_** Power Pivot Principles – we’re starting a new series dedicated to the Power Pivot COM add-in
*   **_Wednesdays:_**[Power Query Pointers](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought/power-query-blog-series)
     – our regular series of ideas for the Extract, Transform and Load (ETL) tool will continue. Can you clean up your act and keep **TRIM**?
*   **_Thursdays:_** Power BI Tips – another new series, coming soon to a theatre near you. If you haven’t used Power BI yet, you really need to start
*   **_Fridays:_**[VBA Blog](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought/vba-blog-series)
     – our regular articles on VBA tips and tricks continues unabated.

With the [Final Friday Fix](https://www.sumproduct.com/blog/article/final-friday-fix-december-challenge)
, [Monday Morning Mulling](https://www.sumproduct.com/blog/article/monday-morning-mulling-december-challenge)
 and other _ad hoc_ blogs appearing from time to time, hopefully there’s something for everyone. This is all in addition to our [Excel Tip of the Day](https://www.sumproduct.com/)
 and our [News](https://www.sumproduct.com/news)
 and [Thought](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought)
 series which will continue to be updated on an (ir)regular basis too. And if you really do want _more_, do remember there’s the monthly newsletter you can subscribe to at the foot of any [SumProduct](https://www.sumproduct.com/)
 webpage too.
