Sing Along with SumProduct
==========================

It is amazing what AI can do – and even more amazing what members of our team can do with it. Our very own Lennon & McCartney (all rolled into one), Ilgar Zarbaliyev, has “composed” the SumProduct Groove. If you need a laugh (with tongue firmly positioned in cheek), feel free to singalong a SumProduct. No doubt Ed Sheeran will be covering it on his next album, ∑∏!!

![](https://sumproduct.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Screenshot-2025-07-31-104439.png)

You can read about how Ilgar put the whole thing together and how “toned down” versions of these ideas may be useful for your business and / or training programs in September’s newsletter (we have to get a plug in somewhere!).  Now, “…multiply and sum for me…” 😊

  Watch this space – but in the meantime – listen to it for now!
