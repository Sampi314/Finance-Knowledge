SumProduct on Financial Modeler’s Corner
========================================

20 November 2024

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

SumProduct’s very own **Liam Bastick** was recently invited onto the most recent podcast episode of Financial Modeler’s Corner by “The FP&A Guy” AKA Paul Barnhurst. Liam and Paul waxed lyrical on a variety of topics including:

*   the importance of developing problem-solving skills
*   how Power BI opens up new dimensions – quite literally! – for financial modelling
*   the future of Excel
*   why cash is as important as ever it was
*   the need to embrace AI.
