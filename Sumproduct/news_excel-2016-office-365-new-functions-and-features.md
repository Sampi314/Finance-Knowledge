Excel 2016 / Office 365: New Functions and Features
===================================================

1 March 2016

If you are an Office 365 subscriber and have access to Excel 2016, you will notice some brand new features and functions coming your way _very_ soon – if not already – in Excel 2016.

Microsoft has added six new functions, **IFS**, **SWITCH**, **CONCAT**, **TEXTJOIN**, **MAXIFS** and **MINIFS**, as well as a cool new funnel chart _(pictured)_, a more forgiving AutoComplete and a new Black theme that’s still out with the jury.

You can read more about them in both March’s and April’s newsletters and [here](https://www.sumproduct.com/thought/excel-2016-functions-and-features)
. If you’re not already a subscriber, join now at the bottom of any **SumProduct** webpage.
