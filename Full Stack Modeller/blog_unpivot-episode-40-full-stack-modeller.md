Question of the Week:   What Best Practices do we ignore
--------------------------------------------------------

This week, they confess the best practices that they don't follow... be prepared for some shocks 😲.

Some sound pretty out there, but I agree with all of them (well, the Excel ones – no idea about the PowerBI ones!)  – as always, it’s all about context - which you’ll need to listen to get.

**Mark**

*   Uses Merge & Center
*   Doesn’t always use the simplest formula that he could

**Sue**

*   Sometimes uses calculated columns in PowerBI
*   Could be better at documentation

 **Wyn**

*   Separation of Inputs, workings and outputs
*   Doesn’t always hide unused columns and dimension keys in PowerBI
*   Needs to be better with accessibility
*   Doesn’t mark he calendar as a data table – which based on Sue’s response is very naughty!

**Giles**

*   You should always be prepared to bend the rules if it delivers a better outcome
