Excel Meetup Groups
===================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Nov 18, 2025 11:40:41 AM

 Our directory of Excel Meetup Groups across the world

Looking for an Excel Meetup Group?

Globally there are 77 Excel related meetup groups, across 59 cities in 19 countries with almost 100,000 members.

Here is our directory of some of the main Excel Meetup Groups globally. A great way to connect with your local Excel Tribe and join a community of like-minded modellers. I've also included some PowerBI groups. We're all friends after all!! 😁
