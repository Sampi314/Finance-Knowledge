Giles Male is one of only two experts globally with both MVP and MFM awards
===========================================================================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Sep 23, 2024 9:45:00 AM

     ![IMG_2504](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/IMG_2504.jpg?width=774&height=1032&name=IMG_2504.jpg)   ![IMG_2503](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/IMG_2503.jpg?width=777&height=1035&name=IMG_2503.jpg)

Giles Male, co-founder and MD of Full Stack Modeller, is one of only two people globally to hold both the Microsoft MVP and the Financial Modelling Institute's MFM awards.

Microsoft MVP
-------------

Giles was awarded the Microsoft MVP award in September 2024.

MVP stands for "Most Valuable Professional" - where Microsoft recognises "exceptional community leaders for their technical expertise, leadership, speaking experience, online influence, and commitment to solving real-world problems."

Financial Modelling Institute's MFM
-----------------------------------

Giles achieved the prestigious Master Financial Modeler award from the Financial Modeling Institute in June 2024.

In their announcement statement, the Financial Modeling Institute said:

"Giles joins an esteemed group of world-class financial modelers, and is recognised for his exceptional contributions to the field."  

"... the addition of Giles Male as an MFM strengthens our commitment to supporting and mentoring financial modelers at every stage of their journey. "  

"The Financial Modeling Institute is grateful for Giles's leadership and contributions"
