The Financial Modeling Institute's AFM qualification
====================================================

Published [by Myles Arnott](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/myles-arnott)

Jan 13, 2020 10:05:00 AM

Find out more about the Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) qualification from the Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) .

**What is the Financial Modeling Institute?**
---------------------------------------------

![FMI-1](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/FMI-1.png?width=774&height=585&name=FMI-1.png)

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) was founded in 2017 with the goal of “Promoting awareness, excellence and discipline in Financial Modeling through world-class accreditation programs”.

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) focuses on assessment, leaving the delivery of training to independent and proven partners like us at Full Stack Modeller.

**What is the AFM qualification?  
**
-------------------------------------

![Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg?width=759&height=489&name=Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg) The Financial Modeling Institute Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) qualification is a financial modelling accreditation designed to prove a modeller’s knowledge and skills.

In order to be successful in the AFM qualification, the financial modeller must demonstrate that they are able to build a fully-integrated three-statement model in line with best practice concepts. As such the AFM exam demonstrates your detailed knowledge of finance, accounting, and Excel.

**What is the Format of the Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) exam?**
--------------------------------------------------------------------

*   The AFM exam is a tough, proctored, four-hour online exam. You are provided with a case study and you must build a three-statement model of a company from scratch.
    
*     
    
    **When are the FMI AFM exams?**
    -------------------------------
    
*   The AFM exams take place four times a year, typically in February, May, July and October.
    

**How much does the FMI AFM exam cost?**
----------------------------------------

The Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) exams cost $695.

**Does the Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) offer scholarships?**
-------------------------------------------------------------------

Yes. FMI offers scholarships based on financial need for first-time AFM candidates. Applications for FMI scholarships can be made for any open AFM exam up to three months before the exam date.

The scholarship entitles the awardee up to a 50% discount on the standard AFM exam fee.

Find out more about the FMI scholarship scheme on the [FMI website](https://fminstitute.com/afm-scholarships/)
