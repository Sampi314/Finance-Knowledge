Interview with Victor Momoh
===========================

Published [by Giles Male](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/resources/author/giles-male)

Oct 25, 2022 9:36:53 AM

 In October 2022 I caught up with Victor Momoh - a new addition to the Microsoft MVP list and an incredibly gifted Excel user!

Victor talks about his journey to becoming an MVP, the pressure that brings with it, and then gives me a pretty incredible training session on LET.

Victor has presented to our Full Stack community on a couple of occasions already, and we are always blown away by what he has to share with us.

I have no doubt he'll be racking up the MVP awards in years to come.
