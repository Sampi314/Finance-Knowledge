**What is the Financial Modeling Institute?**
---------------------------------------------

![FMI-1](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/FMI-1.png?width=774&height=585&name=FMI-1.png)

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) was founded in 2017 with the goal of “Promoting awareness, excellence and discipline in Financial Modeling through world-class accreditation programs”.

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) focuses on assessment, leaving the delivery of training to independent and proven partners like us at Full Stack Modeller.

**What are the FMI certification levels?**
------------------------------------------

The Financial Modeling Institute (FMI) has three certification levels. These are: Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM); Chartered Financial Modeler (CFM); and Master Financial Modeler (MFM).

![Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg?width=759&height=489&name=Advanced-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg)  ![Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg?width=759&height=489&name=Chartered-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg)  ![Master-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Master-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg?width=753&height=486&name=Master-Financial-Modeler-Podium-500x323.jpg)

**When do the AFM FMI exams take place?**
-----------------------------------------

*   The Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) exams are typically in February, May, July, and October.
*   For 2026 the dates are: 21st February, 9th May, 11th July and 24th October.

**What is the cost of the AFM exam**
------------------------------------

The Advanced Financial Modeler (AFM) exams cost $695. 

**Is AFM worth it?**
--------------------

The Advanced Financial Modeler certificate is fast becoming recognised as THE financial modelling certification program. The exam is a rigorous test of the modeller's ability to build a three statement model under pressure. It is tough, and as such those who pass the exam have proven their ability.

If you are looking for a certification process to demonstrate your financial modelling ability then we highly recommend the AFM certicate from FMI.

**How hard is the AFM exam?**
-----------------------------

The AFM exam is tough. You have four hours to build a three-statement model from scratch. You are not allowed to use a starting template, and automations are restricted.

Passing the exam requires strong technical knowledge and excellent exam technique, including time management. 

The pass rate is typically around 60%.

How long is the AFM exam?
-------------------------

You have four hours to complete the AFM exam.If you have prepared well then this is certainly achievable.

**How to prepare for the AFM exam**
-----------------------------------

There are three core elements to being in the best possible position to pass the exam:

  1. Ensure you have the technical modelling knowledge

  2. Refine your exam technique

  3. Practice, practice, practice
