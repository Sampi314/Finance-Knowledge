Full Stack Modeller becomes a FAST Standard accredited training centre
======================================================================

Published [by Giles Male](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/blog/author/giles-male)

Jan 1, 2021 4:21:00 PM

We are pleased to announce that Full Stack Modeller have become a FAST Standard accredited training centre; one of the first training centres to achieve this accreditation in the UK.

What does it mean to be FAST accredited?  

-------------------------------------------

As a FAST Standard accredited training centre we have gone through a formal accreditation process with the FAST Standard Organisation (“FSO”) and have demonstrated a significant commitment to the FAST Standard in the development of our people, products and services.  
  
We are also a sponsoring body for the FAST Level 1 certification exam.

For more information about the FAST Standard visit their website [here](https://www.fast-standard.org/)
