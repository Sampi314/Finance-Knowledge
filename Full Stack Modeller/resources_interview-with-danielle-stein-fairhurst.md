Interview with Danielle Stein Fairhurst: Reflections & Future Plans
===================================================================

Published [by Giles Male](https://www.fullstackmodeller.com/resources/author/giles-male)

Apr 14, 2022 5:41:00 PM

 In April 2022 I got the chance to interview [Danielle Stein Fairhurst](https://www.linkedin.com/in/daniellesteinfairhurst/?originalSubdomain=au)
; to essentially reverse the typical role she plays and put her on the spot!

Danielle works extremely hard to support others in the modelling industry, and it was fantastic to learn more about her career, her reflections on the steps she has taken, and her outlook for the future.

Danielle is the founder of [Plum Solutions](https://plumsolutions.com.au/)
.  She is also doing an enormous amount of work to support women in the financial modelling industry.  You can join her Women in Modelling group from her website link above.

Enjoy!
