### A Dot matrix display

  

![](https://www.andypope.info/fun/pixilate.gif)
-----------------------------------------------

 

##### After reading a entry on my friend Dick Kusleika's [blog](http://www.dicks-blog.com/)
 about [pixilation](http://www.dicks-blog.com/excel/2004/07/pixilation.html)
 I was compelled to construct this example. 

##### The responses he received to his follow-up [blog entry](http://www.dicks-blog.com/excel/2004/08/pixilation_part.html#comments)
 were a pleasant surprise

##### The display is constructed from a standard xy chart. All of the chart data is calculated via worksheet formula. The displays movement is manually controlled through a scrollbar, although I have included a vba routine that can be copied to a code module and used to automate the scrolling.

##### You can also construct you own typeface via the Alphabet worksheet. The example below shows how the letter A was defined.
