### Su Doku Puzzle

![](https://www.andypope.info/fun/sudoku1.gif)  
The object of the puzzle is to complete the grid using the following rules:  
Every digit from 1 to 9 must appear in each of the 9 columns and 9 rows AND within each of the 9 boxes.  
  
Here is the solution to the puzzle above.  
 ![](https://www.andypope.info/fun/sudoku2.gif)  
  
The [workbook](https://www.andypope.info/fun/SuDoku.zip)
 will generate unique puzzles as well as attempt to solve your own.
