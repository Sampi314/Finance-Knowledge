# Accounts Receivable Turnover

$$ \text{Accounts Receivable Turnover} = \frac{\text{Net Credit Sales}}{\text{Average Accounts Receivable}} $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Net Credit Sales}$ — Net Credit Sales.
- $\text{Average Accounts Receivable}$ — Average Accounts Receivable.

**Notes**

Measures efficiency of collecting receivables.
