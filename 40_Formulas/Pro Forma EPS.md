# Pro Forma EPS

$$ \text{Pro Forma EPS} = \frac{\text{Combined Net Income} \pm \text{Transaction Adjustments}}{\text{Pro-Forma Shares}} $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Combined Net Income}$ — Acquirer Net Income + Target Net Income
- $\text{Transaction Adjustments}$ — Incremental interest expense (after tax), lost interest income on cash deployed, amortization of acquired intangibles, and recurring synergies net of costs.
- $\text{Pro-Forma Shares}$ — Existing diluted shares + new shares issued as consideration + incremental dilution from equity-linked instruments.

**Notes**

Extracted directly from "Accretion & Dilution Modeling: The Complete M&A Analyst Framework".
