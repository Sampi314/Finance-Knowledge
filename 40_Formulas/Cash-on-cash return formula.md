# Cash-on-cash return formula

$$Cash\ on\ Cash\ Return\ (\%) = \frac{Annual\ Pre\text{-}Tax\ Cash\ Flow}{Invested\ Equity}$$

**Variables**

- $Annual\ Pre\text{-}Tax\ Cash\ Flow$ — Net Operating Income (NOI) minus debt service (mortgage payments).
- $Invested\ Equity$ — The initial cash outlay or equity contribution at purchase.

**Notes**

The annual cash flow is a "levered" metric calculated post-financing but before taxes.
