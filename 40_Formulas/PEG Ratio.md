# PEG Ratio

$$PEG = \frac{PE}{Expected Growth Rate in Earnings}$$

**Variables**

- $PEG$ — Price/Earnings-to-Growth ratio
- $PE$ — Price-to-Earnings ratio, the ratio of market price to earnings per share
- $Expected Growth Rate in Earnings$ — The expected growth rate in earnings per share over a specified period

**Notes**

When estimating the growth rate, verify it's from the same base year, over the same period, and from the same source as the earnings used to compute the PE ratio.
