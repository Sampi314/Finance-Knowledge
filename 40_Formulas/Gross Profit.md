# Gross Profit

$$ \text{Gross Profit} = \text{Revenue} - \text{COGS} $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Revenue}$ — The total amount of money brought in by a company's operations, measured over a specific period.
- $\text{COGS}$ — [[COGS and gross margin|Cost of Goods Sold]], the direct costs attributable to the production of the goods sold in a company.

**Notes**

Gross Profit represents the absolute dollar amount a company earns after subtracting the direct costs of producing its goods or services. It does not account for operating expenses, interest, or taxes.
