# Net debt

$$Net\ Debt = Short\text{-}Term\ Debt + Long\text{-}Term\ Debt - Cash\ and\ Equivalents$$

**Variables**

- $Short\text{-}Term\ Debt$ — Obligations due within 12 months.
- $Long\text{-}Term\ Debt$ — Obligations due beyond a 12-month period.
- $Cash\ and\ Equivalents$ — The most liquid assets of a company.

**Notes**

If a company has little to no debt, the calculation may yield a negative number, which implies a net cash position.
