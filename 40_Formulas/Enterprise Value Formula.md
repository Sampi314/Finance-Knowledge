# Enterprise Value Formula

$$EV = \text{Market Capitalization} + \text{Total Debt} + \text{Preferred Stock} + \text{Minority Interest} - \text{Cash and Cash Equivalents}$$

**Variables**

- $EV$ — Enterprise Value
- $\text{Market Capitalization}$ — Value of outstanding shares
- $\text{Total Debt}$ — Short-term and long-term debt
- $\text{Preferred Stock}$ — Preferred Stock
- $\text{Minority Interest}$ — Minority Interest
- $\text{Cash and Cash Equivalents}$ — Cash and Cash Equivalents

**Notes**

(none)
