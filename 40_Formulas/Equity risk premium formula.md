---
type: formula
title: Equity risk premium formula
used_in: ["[[Equity risk premium]]"]
updated: 2026-04-11
---

$$
\text{Equity Risk Premium (ERP)} = E(R_m) - R_f
$$

Where:
- **$E(R_m)$** = Expected return on the market (e.g., S&P 500)
- **$R_f$** = Risk-free rate (e.g., yield on a 10-year Treasury note)
