# Accounting Equation

$$Assets = Liabilities + Shareholders Equity$$

**Variables**

- $Assets$ — Resources owned by a company with positive economic value.
- $Liabilities$ — A company's future financial obligations or debts.
- $Shareholders Equity$ — The net assets of a company representing internal funding.

**Notes**

The expanded version of the equation breaks out Shareholders Equity:
$$Assets - Liabilities = Contributed Capital + Beginning Retained Earnings + Revenue - Expenses - Dividends$$
