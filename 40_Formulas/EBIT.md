# EBIT

$$EBIT = Revenue - COGS - Operating Expenses$$

**Variables**

- $Revenue$ — Total sales generated by the business.
- $COGS$ — Cost of Goods Sold.
- $Operating Expenses$ — Expenses required to run the day-to-day operations (e.g., SG&A, R&D).

**Notes**

Alternatively, EBIT can be calculated bottom-up by starting with Net Income and adding back Interest and Taxes:
$$EBIT = Net Income + Interest Expense + Taxes$$
