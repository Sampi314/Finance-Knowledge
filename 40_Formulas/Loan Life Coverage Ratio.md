# Loan Life Coverage Ratio

$$LLCR = \frac{NPV\ of\ CFADS\ over\ remaining\ debt\ tenor}{Current\ Debt\ Balance}$$

**Variables**

- $LLCR$ — Loan Life Coverage Ratio
- $CFADS$ — Cash Flow Available for Debt Service
- $NPV$ — Net Present Value

**Notes**

Calculated using the debt's interest rate as the discount rate for the NPV calculation.
