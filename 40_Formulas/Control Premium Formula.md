# Control Premium Formula

$$ \text{Control Premium} = \frac{\text{Offer Price} - \text{Unaffected Price}}{\text{Unaffected Price}} \times 100\% $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Offer Price}$ — The per-share price an acquirer is offering to pay for the target company.
- $\text{Unaffected Price}$ — The target company's share price before any acquisition speculation, rumors, or news affected its trading value.

**Notes**

Alternatively, if the minority discount is known, the control premium can be calculated as the mathematical inverse:
$$ \text{Control Premium} = \frac{1}{1 - \text{Minority Discount}} - 1 $$
