# Accounts Receivable Days

$$ \text{Accounts Receivable Days} = \left( \frac{\text{Accounts Receivable}}{\text{Net Credit Sales}} \right) \times 365 $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Accounts Receivable}$ — Accounts Receivable.
- $\text{Net Credit Sales}$ — Net Credit Sales.

**Notes**

Measures average days to collect receivables.
