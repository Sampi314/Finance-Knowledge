# Project life coverage ratio formula

$$PLCR = \frac{PV \text{ of } CFADS \text{ over Project Life}}{Debt}$$

Or, if there is a debt service reserve account (DSRA):

$$PLCR = \frac{PV \text{ of } CFADS \text{ over Project Life}}{Debt - DSRA}$$

**Variables**
- $CFADS$ — Cash Flow Available for Debt Service
- $Debt$ — Outstanding debt balance at COD (or after COD)
- $DSRA$ — Debt service reserve account

**Notes**
The PLCR puts PV factors around the CFADS and the Debt Service, and since the PV of debt service is the same as debt at COD (or after COD), it can be defined as the PV of CFADS over Project Life / Debt.
