# Cash-on-Cash Return

$$\text{Cash-on-Cash Return} = \frac{\text{Annual Pre-Tax Cash Flow}}{\text{Invested Equity}}$$

**Variables**

- $\text{Annual Pre-Tax Cash Flow}$ — The annual pre-tax cash income generated after deducting financing costs (e.g., mortgage payments).
- $\text{Invested Equity}$ — The initial equity contribution or down payment made by the investor.

**Notes**

Calculated on a post-financing (levered) basis, so it accounts for current debt service, making it distinct from ROI or Cap Rate.
