# Implied marginal cost in availability penalty

$$Marginal Cost/MWH = Capacity Charge * Penalty Factor * 1000/730 + Energy Charge/MWh$$

**Variables**

- $Capacity Charge$ — The cost per kW-month.
- $Penalty Factor$ — The specific factor for the penalty.
- $Energy Charge/MWh$ — The charge per MWh for energy.

**Notes**

Used to gauge the implied avoided cost from the availability penalty in a power purchase agreement.
