# Debt Service Coverage Ratio Formula

$$
\text{Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)} = \frac{\text{Net Operating Income (NOI)}}{\text{Annual Debt Service}}
$$

**Variables**

- NOI — Total income generated by a property (rental and ancillary income) minus direct operating expenses (management fees, maintenance, taxes, insurance).
- Annual Debt Service — Total financing obligations a property must fulfill, including principal amortization and periodic interest payments.

**Notes**

A DSCR of 1.25x means the NOI is 125% of the annual debt payments, providing 25% of excess NOI as a buffer for unexpected underperformance.
