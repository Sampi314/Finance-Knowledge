# EV/Revenue

$$ \text{EV/Revenue} = \frac{\text{Enterprise Value}}{\text{Total Revenue}} $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Enterprise Value}$ — The total market value of the business (Equity Value + Net Debt). See [[Enterprise value and equity value]].
- $\text{Total Revenue}$ — The top-line sales generated by the business, usually measured over a Last Twelve Months (LTM) or Next Twelve Months (NTM) period.

**Notes**

When calculating this multiple, it is critical that the numerator and denominator are matched. Because revenue is available to all capital providers (both debt and equity holders), the numerator must be Enterprise Value, not Equity Value. Using Market Capitalization / Revenue is a flawed metric for companies with debt.
