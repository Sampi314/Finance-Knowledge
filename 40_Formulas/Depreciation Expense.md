# Depreciation Expense

$$Depreciation\_Expense = \frac{Purchase\_Cost - Residual\_Value}{Useful\_Life}$$

**Variables**

- $Purchase\_Cost$ — The total cost incurred by the company to acquire a fixed asset (PP&E) on the original date of purchase
- $Residual\_Value$ — The estimated value of the asset as of the end of its useful life (or "scrap value")
- $Useful\_Life$ — The estimated number of years the fixed asset (PP&E) is expected to continue providing positive economic utility

**Notes**

This represents the straight-line depreciation method, which gradually and evenly reduces the carrying value of a fixed asset across its useful life assumption.
