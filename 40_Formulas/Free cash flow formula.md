**Unlevered Free Cash Flow (UFCF):**
$\text{UFCF} = \text{EBIT} \times (1 - \text{Tax Rate}) + \text{Depreciation \& Amortization} \pm \text{Change in Working Capital} - \text{Capital Expenditures}$

Alternatively, starting from EBITDA:
$\text{UFCF} = \text{EBITDA} - \text{Taxes} \pm \text{Change in Working Capital} - \text{Capital Expenditures}$
