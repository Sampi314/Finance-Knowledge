# WACC Formula

$$WACC = \frac{E}{V} \times R_e + \frac{D}{V} \times R_d \times (1 - T)$$

**Variables**

- $E$ — Market value of equity
- $D$ — Market value of debt
- $V$ — Total capital ($E + D$)
- $R_e$ — Cost of equity
- $R_d$ — Cost of debt
- $T$ — Corporate tax rate

**Notes**

WACC is simply the weighted average of two costs: the cost of equity (weighted by equity's share of total capital) and the after-tax cost of debt (weighted by debt's share of total capital).
