# Future value formula

$$FV = PV \times \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{n \times t}$$

**Variables**

- $FV$ — Future value of the investment or asset
- $PV$ — Present value, or initial principal amount
- $r$ — Annual interest rate (expressed as a decimal)
- $n$ — Number of compounding periods per year
- $t$ — Term in years

**Notes**

For simple annual compounding ($n = 1$), the formula simplifies to $FV = PV \times (1 + r)^t$.
