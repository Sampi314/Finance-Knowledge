# Amortization Expense

$$Amortization\_Expense = \frac{Historical\_Cost - Residual\_Value}{Useful\_Life}$$

**Variables**

- $Historical\_Cost$ — The total amount paid on the initial date of purchase for the intangible asset
- $Residual\_Value$ — The estimated value of the asset at the end of its useful life span ("salvage value"). This is usually assumed to be zero for intangible assets.
- $Useful\_Life$ — The estimated number of years by which the intangible asset is expected to contribute positive economic value to the company

**Notes**

The standard method for the amortization schedule for intangible assets is on a straight-line basis, so the recognition of amortization abides by a uniform distribution.
