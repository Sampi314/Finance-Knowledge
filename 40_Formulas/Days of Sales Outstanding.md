# Days of Sales Outstanding

$$ \text{DSO} = \left( \frac{\text{Average Accounts Receivable}}{\text{Net Credit Sales}} \right) \times 365 $$

**Variables**

- $\text{Average Accounts Receivable}$ — Calculated as (Beginning Receivables + Ending Receivables) / 2.
- $\text{Net Credit Sales}$ — Net Credit Sales.

**Notes**

Also referred to as DSO.
