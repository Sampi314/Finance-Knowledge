# Debt to Equity Ratio

$$Debt\ to\ Equity\ Ratio\ (D/E) = Total\ Debt \div Total\ Equity$$

**Variables**

- $Total\ Debt$ — Total debt outstanding (short term + long term)
- $Total\ Equity$ — Total shareholder's equity

**Notes**

A common leverage ratio.
