# Cash Conversion Cycle

$$CCC = DSO + DIO - DPO$$

**Variables**

- $DSO$ — Days Sales Outstanding, the average number of days it takes to collect payment after a sale.
- $DIO$ — Days Inventory Outstanding, the average number of days a company holds inventory before selling it.
- $DPO$ — Days Payables Outstanding, the average number of days a company takes to pay its trade creditors.

**Notes**

The CCC measures the time it takes for cash to flow through the business from purchasing inventory to collecting cash from customers.
