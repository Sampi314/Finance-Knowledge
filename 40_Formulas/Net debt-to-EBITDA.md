# Net debt-to-EBITDA

$$Net\ Debt\ to\ EBITDA\ Ratio = \frac{Net\ Debt}{EBITDA}$$

**Variables**

- $Net\ Debt$ — [[Net debt]] (Short-term debt + Long-term debt - Cash and equivalents)
- $EBITDA$ — Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization (a proxy for operating cash flow).

**Notes**

Used by credit rating agencies and investors to gauge a company's ability to repay ongoing debt obligations.
