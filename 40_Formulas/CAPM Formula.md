# CAPM Formula

$$K_e = rf + \beta (rm - rf)$$

**Variables**

- $K_e$ — Cost of Equity (or Expected Return)
- $rf$ — Risk-Free Rate
- $\beta$ — Beta
- $rm - rf$ — Equity Risk Premium (ERP)

**Notes**

The cost of equity is equal to the risk-free rate plus the product of beta and the equity risk premium.
