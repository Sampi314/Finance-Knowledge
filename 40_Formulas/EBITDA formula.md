# EBITDA formula

$$EBITDA = EBIT + D\&A + \text{Non-recurring charges}$$

**Variables**

- $EBITDA$ — Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization
- $EBIT$ — Operating Income (Earnings Before Interest and Taxes), found on the Income Statement.
- $D\&A$ — Depreciation and Amortization, typically sourced from the Cash Flow Statement.
- $\text{Non-recurring charges}$ — One-time adjustments (e.g., restructuring charges, legal settlements) added back to calculate "Adjusted EBITDA".

**Notes**

Always pull the D&A figure from the Cash Flow Statement, as it may be partially embedded in other line items on the Income Statement.
