# P/E ratio formula

$$ \frac{P}{E} = \frac{\text{Market Capitalization}}{\text{Net Income}} $$

**Variables**

- $P/E$ — Price-to-Earnings ratio, indicating valuation relative to earnings.
- $\text{Market Capitalization}$ — The total market value of a company's outstanding equity.
- $\text{Net Income}$ — The company's total earnings (profit) after all expenses and taxes.

**Notes**

Alternatively, this can be calculated on a per-share basis as Share Price divided by Earnings Per Share (EPS). The ratio is heavily influenced by a company's capital structure due to the deduction of interest expense in arriving at Net Income.
