### **How I made this?**

*   I started with [travel patterns data](http://media.juiceanalytics.com/downloads/travelpatterns.csv)
     Chris shared
*   Then I used [Excel formulas OFFSET()](http://chandoo.org/wp/2008/11/19/vlookup-match-and-offset-explained-in-plain-english-spreadcheats/)
     and [ROW()](http://chandoo.org/img/i/formulas/row.png "Excel Row() Formula")
     and [COLUMN()](http://chandoo.org/img/i/formulas/column.png "Excel Column() formula")
     to rearrange the data in a tabular format (the original format is a matrix)
*   Then I sorted the table on bubble size
*   Now I made a bubble chart with 3 data series, one with bubble sizes >50%, one with 25-50% and the rest
*   I formatted each series and added labels to the first two series
*   Finally made some alignment and bingo
