What is this course?
--------------------

**Power BI is one of the most sought after skill** for data analysts in 2023. In this live cohort based training, I will teach you how to get started with Power BI and create advanced data analysis and reports from your data.

**You will learn:**

*   How to get started and create your first report
*   Setting up data connections & transformations with Power Query
*   Creating measures and calculations with Power Pivot & DAX
*   Visualizing outputs with Power BI
*   Advanced data analysis and automated insights with Power BI
*   Making powerful & insightful dashboards
*   Interactive Power BI reports with Tooltips, Drill-through & Bookmarks
*   Two end to end real world examples

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

**Who is this training for?**

This training is perfect for Power BI beginners & intermediate users. If you work in a data profession (data analyst, reporting analyst or similar) and need to use Power BI on a regular basis, you will find this training _extremely_ valuable.

**How this is training is run?**

This training will be thru a Zoom video session. You will be able to see my presentation & explanations as well as share your audio (and screen if you want) when asking questions.

**What software do I need?**

You need Power BI Desktop software (latest version) to follow along. You also need Zoom to join the class & ask questions.

You can watch the recordings on any video player.

**What topics are covered?**

This training covers Power BI report creation and data analysis at in-depth level. You will get plenty of detail on Power Query, Power Pivot, visual formatting and interactive aspects.

Please refer to Power BI live class topics for more details.

**Do I need any prior skills?**

You don’t need any previous Power BI skills or experience. But you do need basic data skills in platforms such as Excel or SQL. You also need some experience in working with data.

**Can I ask questions?**

The session will be highly interactive. You will have lots of opportunities to share your experiences or ask questions.

**What if I can’t attend part of the training?**

It is _highly_ recommended that you plan to attend all 5 days. While you can watch the recording, you will miss out on the interaction and break-out sessions.
