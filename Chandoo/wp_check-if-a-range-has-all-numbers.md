### Does my range have all numbers from 1 to n?

Let’s say you have a range called **_range_** (duh!).  And you want to check if **range** has all the numbers 1 to n (say n=5) in it, each number appearing only once (no more, no less). You can assume the named ranges **range** and **n** in your formulas.
