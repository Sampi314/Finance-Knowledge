*   Navigation – ctrl+ arrow keys, ctrl+pg up/down
*   Selection – Shift+arrow keys, shift+ctrl+arrow keys, shift+ctrl+8
*   Function keys – F2, F4, F5, F7, F1
*   ALT keys for menus
*   CTRL C, V, X, P, S, O, N, I, B, 5, Z, Y, U
