From Bombay
===========

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

Came to Bombay today morning. Had a wonderful journey. Managed to utilize most of the time by doing masala, sleep and dinner. It was my first time in AC class of trains. Had good fun exploiting the facilities. Somehow managed to lug my baggage out of central station in the morning. Damn, it sure is heavy.

Our hostel (Mackigan Hall or something like that) is very near to chowpatty beach. The location as such is a very beautiful. The hostel hall is 92 years old. Rooms are dirty and dilapidated as i have expected them to be. (in one of the rooms there is a poster saying “what would you expect out of a 92 year old room?) But I am sure I am going to enjoy my stay here. Also this place is very near to my office. So, I dont have to do circus of boarding in/out of locals.

Another good thing about this place is that there are lot of good locations around. I can go experimenting with my new cam. Uploading the photos still remains a problem. There are no decent internet cafes around. May be i will have to do use office net in the after hours to do this stuff 🙂

Will come back tomorrow with more updates.
