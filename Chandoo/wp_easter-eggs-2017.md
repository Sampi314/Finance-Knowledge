It is Easter time, and that means time for another fun Easter Egg hunt in the spreadsheet. For the last 8 years (since 2009), I have been running Easter Egg hunt at Chandoo.org. This year too, I have prepared an _**exciting egg extraction enigma**_ for you. Check it out.

![easter-egg-excel-2017](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/easter-egg-excel-2017.png)

**Can you find all the 5 hidden cells in this workbook?**

First step. [Download](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/egg-in-your-face.xlsx)
 [this workbook](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/easter-eggs-2017.xlsx)
. There are five worksheets, each containing one egg. You need to find the hidden **cell** in each worksheet.

_**One rule:**_

*   You should see the word cell spelled out clearly anywhere on the spreadsheet grid.

**Post your hunt results in the comments**. Post the cell address where you found the results along with process used to find in comments. Hunt them all before they rot.

Good luck.
