**Birth and Early Stages:  
**\> Look up for cool names in [google](http://google.com/)
  
\> Post the newborn pics in [orkut](http://orkut.com/)
. Format them in [Picasa](http://picasa.google.co.in/intl/en/)
. Share them with friends using [Blogger](http://blogger.com/)

**School & College:**  
\> Use [Gtalk](http://www.google.com/talk/)
 to chat with friends. Bye bye bfone!  
\> Goodbye to combined studies, welcome [google groups](http://groups.google.com/)
.  
\> Doubts about what is the latest ninth planet? may be you could try [google answers](http://answers.google.com/)
.  
\> Let your mom watch all those serials while you catch some cool cartoons on [Google Video](http://video.google.com/)
  
\> Dont know where to find the answer. May be you can use some [book search](http://www.google.co.in/books?hl=en)
  
\> Summer project. Just chill!, Use [scholar](http://scholar.google.com/)
 to search for research material  
\> Who writes the dairy anyway. Its cool to have a [blog now](http://blogger.com/)
  
\> Want to findout what your favourite stars upto. May be [entertainment news alerts](http://www.google.com/alerts)
 can help you  
\> Learning foriegn langues but constantly hitting a _cul-de-sac._ Well, you can [translate the content](http://www.google.co.in/language_tools?hl=en)
 in to English and learn.  
\> Look up for which colleges to apply in [Google University Search](http://www.google.co.in/intl/en/options/universities.html)
  
\> Find the shortest route and travel options to school. Use [google locals](http://local.google.com/)
 and [google maps](http://maps.google.com/)
  
\> Know whats cool with latest music. See [google music trends](http://www.google.com/trends/music)
  
\> Planning a fun trip with friends. Do it online without sweat. [Google Transit](http://www.google.com/transit)
  
\> A quick doubt about mercury’s atomic number or the integration of (log x), try [google sms](http://sms.google.com/)

**Marriege & Settling Down:**  
\> Start applying at [google jobs](http://jobs.google.com/)
 \[x\]  
\> Look for the right match in [Google matrimonial](http://matrimonial.google.com/)
 \[x\]  
\> Findout more about her/him in [Orkut](http://orkut.com/)
  
\> Know what he/she up to lately by reading his/her [blog](http://blogspot.com/)
  
\> Visit their [Froogle wishlist](http://froogle.google.com/)
. Findout the most romantic valentine gift  
\> Planning for a house. May be you can ask the world all your doubts. [Google answers](http://answers.google.com/)
. Or you want to see the place before saying yes. [Google Maps](http://maps.google.com/)
. better still, you want to take a walk around the house. You can do sitting in your seat. Watch the [google video](http://video.google.com/)
 of the house.  
\> Worried about where all your money is going? Its high time you started a [google spreadsheet](http://spreadsheets.google.com/)
 and recorded every penny movement.  
\> Track your stock movements and so much more. [Google Finance](http://finance.google.com/finance)
.  
\> Wife nagging everyday about that forgotten mosquito mats or switzerland trip? well, not any more. Write everything in [google notebook](http://www.google.com/notebook/)
.  
\> Tired of installing somuch of software one after another and hence not finding time to [blog](http://blogger.com/)
, well you can use [google pack](http://pack.google.com/intl/en-gb/pack_installer.html?hl=en-gb&gl=in&utm_source=en_gb_IN-et-more&utm_medium=et&utm_campaign=en_gb_IN)
 and save sometime  
\> Crude hits $534895430542 per barrell, laugh reading such lines in your [google news](http://news.google.com/)
, for you have [google ride finder](http://labs.google.com/ridefinder)
 to take you to office.  
\> That 64kbps line taking too much time to load a page. Why not try [google web accelarator](http://webaccelerator.google.com/)
?  
\> Starting your own garage? Start with a webpage first. Try [Google page creator](http://pages.google.com/)
.

**Golden years:**  
\> Wanted to read up all those old news papers that you wish you had read during your ripe years. Well, you can do so at a leisurely pace. [Archive Search](http://news.google.com/archivesearch)
  
\> Poor eye sight bugging you. May be you can use the [google accessible search](http://labs.google.com/accessible/)
.  
\> Worried that they might demote mars to dust before you could take a look at it? time for some [google mars](http://mars.google.com/)
.  
\> Manage your computer better without worrying about the BSoD in Windows2045. [Google Desktop](http://desktop.google.co.in/en/)
