Today we have macroeconomics midterm. For most people here it is a favourite subject and hot discussion topic. But for me it is nothing short of a torture. I dont know whether my mind is not capable of comprehending the intricacies of ;keyenician classical model of expansionary monetary policies’ (even in my worst nightmares, i never wanted to use terminology like this) or is it everybody.  
The entire subject seems like an abstract topic to me. Each time i am getting a new meaning for the same line. Baah. somebody tell me that even he/she cannot understand this crap.
