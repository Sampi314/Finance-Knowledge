And one day…
============

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

And one day even I woke up at 6:15 AM. Mind you, I didn’t sleep at yesterday morning. I slept at my usual 4/5 AM and woke up just now. The blame or praise should not be sent to me. I woke up because everyone else did. We have a photoshoot today. Today is one of those days when all of us dressup like newly weds (which sadly is not an encouraging analogy, but that is later…) and walk up to acad block to “say cheese” and pose for the typical 120 dressed to kill and smiling to win picture.

There were few wishes I had after coming here like,  
– waking up early and be the first one to have breakfast  
– not to eat/drink anything in the night canteen for 3 consecutive days (not including the holidays)  
– checking mail only 2 times a day  
– finishing off with the work by 10

and ofcourse the gold medal (by now you must be knowing my academically challenged status)

Duh!, I can strike off one today. Good progress PHD!
