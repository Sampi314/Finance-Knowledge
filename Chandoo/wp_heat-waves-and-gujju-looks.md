Heat waves and gujju looks
==========================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

After a day or two in on the roads in what could be the worst time of the year with the heat wave on everywhere inside the borders of Hindustan, i am damn tired. Mere walking from one clinic to another clinic for 15 mins is enough to give you a mild sunstroke. Despite the cap and cotton-claddedness i am not left out. The less siad about the water consumption the better it is.

As if to add misery to current state, whenever i walk on the roads strangers are asking me for directions in gujarati :(. Looks like i look like a gujju 😀

Other than the heat and confusion there is nothing much to say or see. Will keep quite for another 2 days. Next blog will be from Dilli.

Tada!
