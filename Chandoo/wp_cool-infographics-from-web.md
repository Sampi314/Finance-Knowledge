[**California Walkability Score Heat Map**  \
![california-walkability-score-heat-map-cool-idea](https://i287.photobucket.com/albums/ll133/pointy-haired-dilbert/charts-of-week/july-24/california-walkability-score-heat-m.jpg)](http://leebyron.com/how/2008/07/13/mapping-walkability-san-francisco/)

Lee Byron took the [Walksocre](http://www.walkscore.com/)
 for california locations and highlighted them on the map to generate this beautiful infographic. \[via [flowingdata](http://flowingdata.com/2008/07/24/mapping-walkability-in-san-francisco/)\
\]

[**Mozilla Firefox vs. Internet Explorer Security Incidents – Cool Square Pie**  \
![firefox vs. internet explorer grid pie chart, simple yet effective](https://i287.photobucket.com/albums/ll133/pointy-haired-dilbert/charts-of-week/july-24/firefox-internet-explorer-security-.jpg)](http://en-us.www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/security/)

This chart is nothing but a square pie chart (or [totally defragged partition chart](http://chandoo.org/wp/2008/07/10/partition-charts-visualization-fun/)
), effective, easy on eye and tells one point as [Seth Godin suggested](http://sethgodin.typepad.com/seths_blog/2008/07/the-three-laws.html)
 🙂 \[via [coolinfographics](http://coolinfographics.blogspot.com/2008/07/when-are-you-at-risk-online.html)\
\]

[**Who owns the land in US?**  \
![who owns land in each of the 50 states : land owned by federal government highlighted](https://i287.photobucket.com/albums/ll133/pointy-haired-dilbert/charts-of-week/july-24/land-owned-by-federal-government-co.jpg)](http://strangemaps.wordpress.com/2008/06/17/291-federal-lands-in-the-us/#comments)

This map shows % of land owned by federal government vs. total land for each state in the US map, pretty cool even though it could be difficult to immediately interpret anything due to varying areas of each state.

[**Fine to you if your pants sag**  \
![fine to you if your butt shows in the saggy pants](https://i287.photobucket.com/albums/ll133/pointy-haired-dilbert/charts-of-week/july-24/saggy-pants-fines-in-detroit.jpg)](http://freep.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20080708/NEWS06/307080009)

This is not entirely a chart, but uses the ideas to tell people how much they will be fined based on their how much their pants sagged. Pretty neat 😀 \[via [Publication Design](http://publish.wordpress.com/2008/07/15/fashion-police/)\
\]

[**Visualizing Large Text Data – The trick is in colors**  \
![visualizing large text data, colors do the trick](https://i287.photobucket.com/albums/ll133/pointy-haired-dilbert/charts-of-week/july-24/visualizing-large-text-data-chart.jpg)](http://www.wired.com/science/discoveries/magazine/16-07/pb_visualizing)

> The biggest challenge of the Petabyte Age won’t be storing all that data, it’ll be figuring out how to make sense of it.

well, we are fortunate to have people like Martin Wattenberg who thrive to get the meaning out of all those petabytes. \[via [FlowingData](http://flowingdata.com/2008/07/21/martin-wattenberg-on-visualizing-large-textual-data/)\
\]
