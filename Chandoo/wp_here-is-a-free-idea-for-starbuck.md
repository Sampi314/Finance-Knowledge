![starbuck coffee cups](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/05/starbucks_cup_size.jpg "starbucks_cup_size")A significant amount of my job work involves drinking copious amount of coffee while looking out the window. Ok, I am kidding 🙂 I wish my job involved all that, but I still drink a cup of coffee every workday (Weekends are obviously reserved for good old [Filter Kaapi](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_filter_coffee)
). Unfortunately I am forced to buy and drink 355 ml (google tells me that is all of [12 Oz.](http://www.google.com/search?q=12oz+in+ml)
) of coffee because that is the minimum size available. As you can see on the left, the cup looks like a small cauldron. Every sip makes me feel guilty (well, may be because I wasnt looking out of window but actually working on a spreadsheet).

So here is a free idea for Starbucks and all other coffee makers. **Why dont you introduce small cup sizes, something around 8 oz?** It promotes healthy life style, gives customers a choice to be indulgent without guilt and makes reasonable business sense.
