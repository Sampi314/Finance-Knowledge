Today I was using [google reader](http://google.com/reader)
 and suddenly it Grred at me saying that there is a pop-up blocker which is not allowing the reader to open a window.

[![Popup blocker error!](https://static.flickr.com/95/228160638_cdfbec40f0.jpg)](http://www.flickr.com/photos/chandoo/228160638/ "Photo Sharing")
  
\[Click on it incase you cant read it clearly, anyways it says: “Grrr! A popup blocker may be….”\]  
  
Hmm! looks like slowly the world will move away from humble **_‘ok’, ‘cancel’_** mode to colloquial ‘_**dude, why the hell you have that pop-up blocker, should I kill it now?’**_, ‘_**Its ok man, just leave that bloody thing on!**_‘ mode.
