) is a utility developed by Microsoft to help you analyze and report gazillion rows of data. It works right inside Excel 2010 and integrates with SQL Server to fetch, analyze and create reports that would knock the socks off your boss.

Now, I have never seen a Microsoft employee do back flips (although [I saw their CEO do a lot of clapping and passionate chanting](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KMU0tzLwhbE)
). So I am eager to see _**Emilie**_ do a few of those, may be record a video and send it our way.

**On a serious note, you should [try the PowerPivot Nerdtastic Quiz](http://www.facebook.com/powerpivot)

_Remember, the contest ends on May 12th. So go ahead and try it._
