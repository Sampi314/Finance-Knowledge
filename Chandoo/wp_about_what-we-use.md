Stuff I Use & Recommend

![What do I use and recommend - Chandoo](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/2023-05-02_08-49-33.jpg)

Why and what is this page?
--------------------------

From a tiny little site in 2004, Chandoo.org grew to be massive resource on all things Excel & Power BI today. Running this site is an exciting thing. Over the years, I have learned to use certain software, hardware, websites to help me maintain this site & my YouTube channel and make people awesome in their work. Now a days, many people ask me, _**“Hey Chandoo, what tool do you use to do that? It looks cool.”**_ This page is my answer to those questions.

To Run Chandoo.org
------------------

*   **Domain names:** I get all my domains from either [NameCheap](https://namecheap.pxf.io/chandoo)
     or [Godaddy](https://godaddy.com/)
    
*   **Webhosting:** I recommend using either [Bluehost](https://bluehost.sjv.io/chandoo)
     or [SquareSpace](https://squarespace.syuh.net/chandoo)
    .
*   **Dedicated Servers:** If your website gets really busy, consider hosting it on a dedicated or virtual private servers. I use [**Knownhost**](http://www.knownhost.com/affiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=802)
     for this.
*   **e-Commerce**: I suggest using [**Shopify**](https://shopify.pxf.io/chandoo)
     for everyone. But if you are a bit tech savvy and don’t mind coding stuff on your own, you can also use [e-Junkie](https://e-junkie.com/)
     & [Stripe](https://stripe.com/)
    .
*   **Website:** I use hand-crafted HTML, [WordPress](https://wordpress.org/)
     and a bit of PHP to run this website. 
*   **WordPress Plug-ins:** To run the website, I use a bunch of wordpress plug-ins. The main ones are [Elementor](https://elementor.com/)
     & [Wishlistmember](https://wishlistmember.com/)
    .
*   **Online Classes:** I reocmmend using **[teachable](https://teachable.sjv.io/chandoo)
    ** to set up and run your online classes.
*   **Outsourcing my work:** Whenever I need a quick task done, I use [upwork](https://upwork.pxf.io/chandoo)
     to hire freelancers. 

To Run YouTube Channel
----------------------

*   **Screen recording Software**: I use **[Techsmith Camtasia](https://techsmith.z6rjha.net/chandoo)
    ** to record & edit most of my videos. 
*   **Video Editing:** Most of my video editing is done with **[Camtasia](https://techsmith.z6rjha.net/chandoo)
    .** I also use [Adobe Premiere Pro](https://www.adobe.com/products/premiere.html)
     to edit some videos.
*   **Cameras:** My primary camera is **[Canon M50](https://amzn.to/40T37ft)
    **. It produces excellent quality images for a reasonable price. I also use [Logitech C920](https://amzn.to/3nhvAxS)
     as my alternative camera.
*   **Mic:** Audio quality is more important than video quality on YouTube. I use **[Blue Yeti mic](https://amzn.to/3nkWSDC)** as my primary audio capture device. I also use [Rode Shotgun mic](https://amzn.to/3LlXHnx)
     & [Rode Wireless Go mics](https://amzn.to/44mZ12b)
     with my camera.
*   **Phone:** Ocasionally I use my phone (Samsung Galaxy S20FE) to shoot videos and record audio. It does a fabulous job at capturing everything clearly. If I am buying a phone today, I would go for the [latest S23 instead](https://amzn.to/3oQ6F4S)
    .

Computers & Stuff
-----------------

*   I use a PC with Intel i5 @2.9GHZ (a bit old now) with 32GB of RAM
*   It has the **[NVIDIA T1000 8GB](https://amzn.to/3LK9ddM)
    ** graphics card. This helps me edit and produce videos quickly.
*   My primary keyboard is a 10 year old Logitech K240. I would [get the new MK240](https://amzn.to/40Uz7Qr)
     when possible. 
*   **Mouse:** With video editing, there is a lot of mouse involvement. So I use **[Logitech MX Master 3 mouse](https://amzn.to/3AOJiva)
    **. It helps me work faster and keeps my hand happy.
*   I also have a backup laptop when I travel. It is **[ASUS TUF Gaming F15](https://amzn.to/44gATOS)
    **. It has a decent memory, latest processor and good graphics card.
*   I use an extremely comfortable **[Secret Lab office chair](https://secretlab.co.nz/)
    **. I can work for hours on this.
