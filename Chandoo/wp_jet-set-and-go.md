Jet, set and go
===============

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 1 comment

  

Its all set to start from tomorrow. Officially our classes start from tomorrow 9:30 am ( for section a it is 9 am ). We have Microeconomics for Managers, Human Behaviour at Work and Quantitative Techniques 1 for tomorrows sessions. The session usually lasts for 70 mins. After each session we will get a 15 min break. Sometimes when your fate is not good, this 15 minute break might be taken out for spillovers. 🙁  
  
Today i spent most of the time wondering why i didn’t have anything to do. But that feeling didn’t last long. After lunch we got 2 assignments and 1 reading to do. Apart from that we had a presentation on IRIS – The Annual Management Festival of IIM Indore. There were 7 committees in IRIS core team. I am planning to apply for Media and Operations clubs. Even though OR is not my strength i would like to learn something from this experience. 🙂  
  
Except some brief patches of reading and activity in the day, rest of the time either i was wasting it BIG TIME or chatting away with friends.  
  
We will be getting our Insti EmailIDs and Identity cards tomorrow. That is all for now. Bye.
