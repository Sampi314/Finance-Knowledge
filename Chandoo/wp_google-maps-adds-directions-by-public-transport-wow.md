Google Maps Directions by Public Transport

Wow, this is awesome. Google maps has added directions by public transport. Sometime back when they integrated public transport timings along with bus-stop / train station icons, it was pretty cool, but adding directions based on bus / train routes is totally awesome. Try it for yourself, [click here to see](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=d&hl=en&geocode=6740432633792723924,40.076360,-83.129880&saddr=5043+Tuttle+Crossing+Blvd+%23+20,+Dublin,+OH+43016+(Mall+At+Tuttle+Crossing)&daddr=200+Hutchinson+Ave,+Columbus,+OH+43235&mra=pe&mrcr=0&dirflg=r&date=07%2F22%2F08&time=11:46am&ttype=dep&noexp=0&noal=0&sort=&sll=40.103155,-83.055782&sspn=0.098607,0.170975&ie=UTF8&z=12&start=0)
