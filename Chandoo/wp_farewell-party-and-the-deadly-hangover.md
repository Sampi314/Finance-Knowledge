Farewell Party and the deadly hangover
======================================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

I just came out from the hangover of our farewell party (it happened day before yesterday).  
\[[Read the welcome party post](http://www.chandoo.org/blog/2004_07_01_modusindoramus_archive.html#108953613849431307)\
\]

It started with a formal dinner hosted at Mashal. After the initial rounds of beer, kebabs, food and excessive consumption of vanilla icecream with chocolate sauce (stop cribbing, I am passionate about food :D) We came back to hostel.

Juniors showed us a slideshow full of memories and all the fun moments we spent here. Good work [deepti](http://dippity.blogspot.com/)
 & amish 🙂 Then our classmates presented a movie they have made on the batch. It was fun watching all the moments of fun.

Soon the party began. Only consumption of alchohol combined with periodic movements of the bodyparts on the melting pot was allowed.

For some strange reason I am no longer unhappy that I am leaving this place. For I am a part of all this no matter where I am. The spirit of PGP2006 shall live on 🙂
