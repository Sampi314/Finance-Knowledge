Fun way to clean up modern art with visualization
=================================================

[Cool Infographics & Data Visualizations](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/cool-infographics/)
 - 4 comments

  

[TED Talks](http://www.ted.com/index.php/talks)
 are a great way to keep your mind nourished. They inspire you, give you a ton of new ideas. I saw this ted talk (embedded below) [Tidying up art by Ursus Wehrli](http://www.ted.com/index.php/talks/ursus_wehrli_tidies_up_art.html)
 and couldn’t resist sharing it with you. It is an entertaining one and I recommend watching it.

In this talk the speaker takes a swipe at modern art by showing us ways to clean up the apparent gibberish in to meaningful bar charts. Take a look at these two examples:

![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/cleaning-up-modern-art.jpg "cleaning-up-modern-art")

![](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/modern-art-visualization.jpg "modern-art-visualization")

This is the perfect example of how visualizations bring order and insights out of data that looks chaotic.
