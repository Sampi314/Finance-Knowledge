What is a Dashboard?
--------------------

Dashboard reports allow managers to get high-level overview of the business and help them make quick decisions. Dashboards are often called as management dashboards or information dashboards or dashboard reports.

Dashboard Do’s and Don’ts:
--------------------------

Dashboard design is a complex process. It is very easy to make mistakes and choose wrong alternatives along way. In this section, you can find practical advice that can be immediately applied to your next dashboard project.

### Should you use Gauges / Speedometer charts in Dashboards?

[Gauge or Speedometer chart](http://chandoo.org/wp/2008/09/09/excel-speedometer-chart-download/)
 is a very popular way to display a single metric in dashboard. In my opinion, they are a bad choice for depicting the status of one metric (or KPI). You can use a better alternative like [bullet chart](http://chandoo.org/wp/dashboard-bullet-graphs-excel/)
 or [thermo-meter chart](https://chandoo.org/wp/thermometer-charts-in-excel-howto/)
 to show the same information with more context. Also, read the discussion on [best charts to depict actual vs. target performance](http://chandoo.org/wp/2009/12/18/charts-to-compare-targets/)
.
