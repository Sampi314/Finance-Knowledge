. No, wait, I **really** love google maps. I think it is a perfect example of passion, intelligence and wit coming together to create software. \[trivia link: [google maps tells you swim across the ocean](http://www.ghacks.net/2007/04/11/google-maps-swim-across-the-atlantic-ocean/)\
\], I have been using maps to find transit directions from my hotel to office and I have noticed something wierd.

**The walking directions are sometimes illogical. See aside.**

From the place where I get off the bus, I need to walk to point B and the maps tells me to walk all the way to some place and return to point B. One reason could be they are using one-way driving instructions on the roads to create walking directions.
