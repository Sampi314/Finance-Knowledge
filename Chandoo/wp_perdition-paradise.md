Perdition & Paradise
====================

[Random](https://chandoo.org/wp/category/uncategorized/)
 - 0 comments

  

**Hell:**

“tuyoooon” moans my mailbox alert sound. I open the box to check what is there now. As it is a saturday I was expecting a certain mail from certain person about certain things. “I am here to screw your happiness” screams the mail from P%^ office. The mail is simply an attachment of the time table for the next one week. The contents of the mail are weird and cruel to say the least and barbarious, dreadful, irritating, lamentable, merciless, outrageous, painful and sadistic to say the most.

“I have 31 classes in the next 7 days. What the hell?” is the first thing to hit my brain. Most of the PGP2s are going through enormous pain as the classes have been going on for the last 40+ days without a break. Not even one holiday since the term started. The funniest part is coming up now with all project submissions and exams lined up in next 2 weeks. God bless.

**Heaven:**

It is confirmed now. I am going to Leh/Ladakh for this vacation with 4 others. This should be a dream coming true. I always wanted to visit kashmir ever since I read “shrinagara yatra” in seventh class I guess. We have planned the journey for 10 days and we will be going on the less travelled Delhi->Jammu->Srinagar->Kargil/Drass->Leh route reach the place. On return journey we are coming by Manali route. Will post _hazaar_ blogs on this in the next 30 days.
