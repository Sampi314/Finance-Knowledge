How geeky are you? Show off your geekness to others with this kickass room decor ideas,

Just click the images to follow up. **live geek, love geek** 😀

### 1\. Lets start with the door. You can experiment with the door locks to create your own puzzle:

[![puzzle-door-look](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/puzzle-door-look.jpg)](http://www.artlebedev.com/everything/defendius/)

### 2\. Now that you are in how geeky would you like to make your sofa? how about a pixel couch?

[![pixcel-sofa](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/pixcel-sofa.jpg)](http://www.yankodesign.com/index.php/2008/03/28/plush-pixels/)

### 3\. Dont fancy a couch, why dont you create your own ball-pit?

[![fill-the-pit-with-balls](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/fill-the-pit-with-balls.jpg)](http://blag.xkcd.com/2007/11/19/growing-up/)

Not sure how much this would cost, you can find out using this [ball-pit-pocket-hole-calulator](http://www.chiliahedron.com/ballroom/)

### 4\. Now that you are sitting, what about those window curtains, would like them ASCII?

[![ascii-curtain](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/ascii-curtain.jpg)](http://www.nsybrandy.nl/html/ZTmV07Gordijnen.html)

### 5\. May be ASCII is a bit too old, why dont you buy a G(O)oogle saree and hang it up as a curtain?

[![google-saree-as-a-curtain](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/google-saree-as-a-curtain.jpg)](http://shop.satyapaul.com/products/690.html)

At 299.98, this Ooogle sari can make great (expensive ;)) curtain 🙂

### 6\. what about the ceiling, isnt it too bland, why not decorate it up with old CDs & DVDs?

[![cd-dvd-ceiling-decor](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/cd-dvd-ceiling-decor.jpg)](http://www.techmanifesto.com/images/geekroom/cd3.jpg)

### 7\. Now that the ceiling is taken care, what about that dull wall paper, why not _mario_\-fy it?

[![mario-wp-room](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/mario-wp-room.jpg)](http://www.geekologie.com/2008/07/super_mario_landscape_art_inst.php)

### 8\. What about the lighting in the room, why the lame lights when you can DIY some ambient lighting?

[![ambient-lighting-diy](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/ambient-lighting-diy.jpg)](http://lifehacker.com/397415/set-up-cheap-ambient-lighting-with-rope-lights)

### 9\. Now lets geekify the furniture, how about a Tetris book shelf?

[![tetris-bookshelf](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/tetris-book-shelf1.jpg)](http://bravespacedesign.com/product_tetrad_flat.php)

### 10\. Finally what is a room without something to eat, why not say hello world with slice of bread?

[![hello-world-bread](https://chandoo.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/hello-world-bread.jpg)](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/article.php/cnctoast)

So what are you waiting for? Go ahead and make your room geekier than ever. Happy Friday 🙂
