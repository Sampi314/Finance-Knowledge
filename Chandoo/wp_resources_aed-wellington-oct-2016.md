What is in this Masterclass & Who should attend?
------------------------------------------------

This masterclass is conducted **only in Wellington, New Zealand**.

*   Advanced Excel & Dashboards – 13 & 14 October (Thursday & Friday), 2016

This 2 day masterclass is aimed at managers, analysts, reporting professionals, executives in sales, marketing, customer service or anyone who wants to learn about Data Analytics, Advanced Excel, Dashboard reporting

**Advanced Excel & Dashboards Masterclass:**

In this masterclass we will be learning,

*   **Day1: Our first dashboard in Excel**
    *   Excel formulas for dashboards
    *   Applying advanced conditional formatting & sparklines for dashboards
    *   Building our first dashboard – hands-on exercise
*   **Day 2: Advanced dashboard skills**
    *   Selecting right charts for any situation
    *   Enhancing dashboards with Excel 2013 & 2016 – sparklines, slicers, timelines, special charts
    *   Customizing Excel charts – top 5 problems
    *   Building our second dashboard – hands-on exercise
    *   Q&A
