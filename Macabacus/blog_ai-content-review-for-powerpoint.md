A PowerPoint Consistency Check Built for Financial Accuracy 
------------------------------------------------------------

Financial professionals searching for an AI tool for pitch books, IC presentations and CIMs are not looking for generic writing help. They need a way to reduce the risk of material mistakes in complex, multi-slide PowerPoint narratives. 

AI Content Review scans your full deck and identifies errors like mismatched revenue, miscalculated valuation, or inconsistent cash flow figures across slides. It also flags unit drift (M/mm/millions), shifting terminology (Adjusted EBITDA versus Adj. EBITDA), and incorrect company or scenario references to keep slides consistent with your firm’s standards. 

Traditional formatting checks cannot catch a discrepancy between slide 7 and slide 47 in a 60-page pitch book. Manual PowerPoint review is slow and error-prone, especially late at night or under tight deadlines. Now with AI auditing capabilities, Deck Check is a guardrail that reads across slides, understands financial context, and surfaces issues before a client sees your deck.
