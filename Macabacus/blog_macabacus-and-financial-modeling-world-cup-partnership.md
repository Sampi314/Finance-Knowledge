**About FMWC**

Based in Latvia, the Financial Modeling World Cup (FMWC) organizes a series of monthly competitions focused on financial modeling and Microsoft Excel. FMWC was founded in 2020 by Andrew Grigolyunovich, CFA, MFM, and promotes financial modeling as a powerful tool for education, training, and competition.

Over the years, FMWC has grown into three standalone global competitions:

*   Financial Modeling World Cup ([FMWC](https://fmworldcup.com/ "https://fmworldcup.com")
    ) – a leading financial modeling competition
    
*   Microsoft Excel World Championship ([MEWC](https://excel-esports.com/ "https://excel-esports.com/")
    ) – a re-invention of Excel as a mainstream esports competition
    
*   Microsoft Excel Collegiate Challenge ([MECC](https://mecc.college/ "https://mecc.college/")
    ) – free Excel education and competition opportunities for students worldwide
