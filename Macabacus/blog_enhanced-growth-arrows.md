**What makes Macabacus’ Growth Arrows better than other solutions?**
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Macabacus’ Growth Arrows adjust dynamically as your data, which gives you a significant advantage over other solutions that often require you to build Growth Arrows as static shapes, resulting in a time-consuming and inflexible process.

Several new features have been added in the latest version of Macabacus to further improve Growth Arrows\*:

#### **Streamlined, Intuitive UI**

A redesigned interface that makes it easier than ever to add and customize growth arrows in your stacked or clustered column charts.

#### **Full Control Over Start and End Periods**

Specify the start and end periods for your growth arrow, giving you more flexibility to represent your data appropriately.

#### **Dynamic, Formula-Driven Arrows**

Unlike tools that hard-code static shapes, we insert arrows as dynamic data series. This method ensures that your charts update automatically with any changes to your data, keeping your visuals current, impactful, and easy to update.

#### **Compatible with non-Macabacus users**

Macabacus’ Growth Arrows are built using native Excel functionality – this means you can share your work with non-Macabacus users of Excel without the nagging risk of compatibility issues.
