Macabacus and Brandfetch Partner to Launch Logo Library, Delivering Instant Access to 22M+ Logos in PowerPoint
==============================================================================================================

_UPDATE: Macabacus clients can now acquire Brandfetch licenses directly through Macabacus, making it easier than ever to access a library of 22M+ brand logos within PowerPoint. Contact your Macabacus representative for more info._

_June 10, 2025 – New York,_ _NY_ – Macabacus, the leading productivity suite for Microsoft 365, has partnered with Brandfetch, the internet’s source for brand logos, to launch Logo Library — a new feature that gives investment banking and consulting teams real-time access to over 22 million brand logos, directly within PowerPoint. 

![](https://macabacus.com/assets/2025/06/macabacus-brandfetch-stacked.png)

_Logo Library_ empowers bankers and other professionals to build tombstones, pitch books, and M&A decks **faster and more accurately** by eliminating the need to manually source, manage, or format logos. With _Logo Library_, users can **instantly insert the latest logos** and **maintain visual polish** across all client-facing materials. 

“The investment banks where I previously worked tediously maintained their own repositories of company logos.  It took their creative teams 24-48 hours to generate a new logo, and many times the repo’s logos were outdated.  Bankers in a rush often ended up sourcing low-quality and inaccurate logos from the internet,” said Ryan MacGregor, Founder and Chief Product Officer of Macabacus.  “Logo Library, powered by Brandfetch, makes millions of high-quality, up-to-date logos immediately available to our users in PowerPoint, and helps our clients focus on their core businesses.” 

“Brandfetch’s integration with Macabacus makes it easy for finance teams to create on-brand content, with fast, reliable access to over 22 million brand logos—accurate, high-res, and ready to drop into any client-facing deck,” said Amin Kasimov, Co-Founder of Brandfetch. 

**Partnership Highlights** 

*   Access up-to-date, high-resolution logos from a live Brandfetch database of 22M+ global brands 

*   Insert and format logos directly in PowerPoint using Macabacus 

*   No integration or custom setup required—connect an existing or new Brandfetch account
