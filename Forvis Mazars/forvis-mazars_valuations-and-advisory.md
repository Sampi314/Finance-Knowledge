Valuation and Advisory Services

Valuations
----------

Forvis Mazars is a significant provider of specialist valuation services across the energy and infrastructure sectors to large infrastructure funds and other clients around the world. Our approach is underpinned by our broad international exposure to energy and infrastructure transactions, and we apply this detailed sector knowledge to our valuation work.

Our expertise covers a broad range of debt and equity investments. For more information please [read more about Valuations](https://www.mazars.co.uk/Home/Services/Financial-Advisory-Services/Global-Infrastructure-Finance/Infrastructure-Valuations)
 on the Forvis Mazars UK website.

Transaction support and advice
------------------------------

Forvis Mazars has built up a strong reputation providing sector-specific transactional advice across multiple markets across the energy and infrastructure sector.

*   Services: financial modelling support through to lead financial advisory
*   Deal types: M&A (buy side and sell side), debt financings, greenfield project development
*   Sub-sectors: particular expertise in the renewable energy, transport and social infrastructure sectors

For more information, please [read more about Forvis Mazars’ transaction support and advisory services](https://www.mazars.com/Home/Services/Financial-advisory-services/Global-Infrastructure-Finance)
