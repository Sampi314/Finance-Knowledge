Forvis Mazars Named Global Model Auditor of the Year at IJGlobal Awards 2025
============================================================================

By [Carlie Hughes](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/author/carlie-hughes/ "Posts by Carlie Hughes")

Tuesday 17th March 2026

We are proud to announce that Forvis Mazars has been awarded **Global Model Auditor of the Year** at the IJGlobal Awards 2025.

This recognition is a significant milestone for our team and reflects the strength of a truly international platform. In addition to the global award, we were also named **Model Auditor of the Year in North America, LATAM, and EMEA**, highlighting the consistency and quality of our work across the world’s leading infrastructure and energy finance markets. The APAC awards are due to be announced in April, and we look forward to seeing the outcome.

### A Global Team Delivering Consistent Excellence

These awards are a testament to the depth of expertise and collaboration across our global network. Whether supporting transactions in mature or emerging markets, our teams apply the same rigorous approach to financial model assurance – ensuring accuracy, transparency, and confidence in every engagement.

### Why This Matters for Our Clients

For our clients, this recognition reinforces what they experience firsthand:

*   **Independent, robust model assurance** on complex transactions
*   **Consistency across jurisdictions**, regardless of where deals are executed
*   **Deep sector expertise** in infrastructure and energy finance
*   **A global team with local insight**, delivering practical and commercial outcomes

In an environment where financial models underpin critical investment decisions, having a trusted and experienced model auditor is essential. These awards validate our ability to deliver that assurance at the highest level.

### Sharing Our Expertise

Alongside our client work, we are committed to sharing the practical experience and methodologies that underpin our approach. Through our [financial model review training course](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/course-schedule/financial-model-review-course/)
, we support professionals and organisations in building stronger, more reliable models and enhancing their internal assurance capabilities.

### Thank You

We would like to thank our clients for their continued trust, and our colleagues across the firm whose dedication and expertise make achievements like this possible.
