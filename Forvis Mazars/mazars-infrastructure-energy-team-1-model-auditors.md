Mazars Infrastructure & Energy team- #1 Model Auditors!
=======================================================

By [Jerome Brice](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/author/jerome-brice/ "Posts by Jerome Brice")

Friday 7th December 2018

Being ranked #1 for the third consecutive year (by Inframationnews) is a great achievement and testament to the hard work and dedication of the Infrastructure & Energy team at Mazars. Having combined forces with Corality in 2016, we are proud to have undertaken more transactions than any other firm in each of the EMEA, Americas and Asia Pac regions, clearly positioning us as a global leader in financial model audit.

Over the 2015-2018 period, Mazars – Infrastructure & Energy have reviewed projects with a total combined value of $100 billion. This is not an error – $100,000,000,000 of infrastructure projects, categorised into at least 7 sub-sectors, with a blend of greenfield and brownfield assets in almost 20 countries and spread across 6 continents!

![Model auditors by deal count & value](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/image001.png)

If you have any specific financial model review requirements or if you are generally interested in learning more about Mazars and how we can best support you and your projects, wherever in the world that may be, please reach out to our Global Model Audit Lead Jerome Brice on [jerome.brice@gif.mazars.com](mailto:jerome.brice@gif.mazars.com)
 for more information.

As a founding partner of Mazars – Infrastructure & Energy over a decade ago, Jerome was instrumental in the launch of Mazars’ financial model review services and he remains actively involved to this day in the strategic leadership, quality assurance and talent development of our best-in-class team.

Jerome will be able to clarify any commercial or process queries you may have as well as introduce you to the best-placed member of our global team to work with you on your project.

We look forward to hearing from you!
