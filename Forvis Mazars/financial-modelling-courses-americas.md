Project finance and financial modelling courses in New York City (NYC) (2021/2022 update)

By [Malena Fuentes Arbones](https://financialmodelling.forvismazars.com/author/malena/ "Posts by Malena Fuentes Arbones")

Thursday 28th October 2021

Since about 2015 New York City has been a regular location for Mazars financial modelling and project finance courses. While the Covid-19 pandemic has limited the possibilities of classroom based financial modelling courses in 2019/20/21, we are now seeing light at the end of the tunnel and have started preparations for the year ahead.

Mazars scheduling of New York City (NYC) financial modelling courses
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Our ambition is to schedule classroom based courses every 2-3 months in New York City (NYC), and the full schedule will be published on our [global financial modelling course schedule page](https://financialmode1.wpengine.com/course-schedule/financial-modelling-for-renewable-energy-projects-us/)
. We are still delivering courses digitally on a monthly basis in case you don’t want to attend in-person courses yet or live far from New York City (NYC). 

> _“Many of our corporate clients who organise annual courses for their teams, send new joiners to our open financial modelling courses in New York. By combining class-room courses with digital financial modelling course, we ensure that we can provide continuous capability development to these organisations. The global schedule ensures that we can delivery consistent financial modelling training course all over the world, which provides a strong financial modelling foundation for global organisations”_
> 
> Malena Fuentes Arbones, Mazars Energy, Infrastructure and Environment
