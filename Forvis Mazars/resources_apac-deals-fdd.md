Webinar by Cindy Cheung (Forvis Mazars China), Julien Herveau (Forvis Mazars Korea) and Akhil Puri (Forvis Mazars India)

Our partners from India, Korea and China share their expertise in navigating local and cross-border transactions.  Gain insights into recent trends, best practices, key risk areas, and strategies to optimise deal success in today’s dynamic market.  Whether you are a corporate executive, investor, or advisor, this is an opportunity to hear from our experts and learn how to navigate the Asia Pacific’s complex market landscape.  

**Learning Points**

*   Benefit from the expertise of experienced professionals to enhance decision-making in corporate, investment, or advisory roles. 
*   Gain insights into recent financial due diligence trends in Asia-Pacific, and their implications for local and cross-border transactions.
*   Learn to recognise common complexities and pitfalls in the financial due diligence process within Asia’s diverse market landscape.
*   Explore best practices and innovative strategies to mitigate risks and ensure robust deal execution.
*   Develop actionable strategies to navigate the challenges of Asia-Pacific’s dynamic markets and achieve successful transaction outcomes.
