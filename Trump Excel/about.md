Welcome to TrumpExcel.com, a trusted resource for Microsoft Excel that helps people learn Excel the right way.

If you love Excel (or even if you’re just trying to survive it at work), you’ve come to the right place.

I started this site back in 2013 because I kept discovering cool Excel tricks and thought, “Man, more people need to know about this!”

What began as me sharing a few tips has grown into something way bigger than I imagined — millions of people now use TrumpExcel website and my [**YouTube channel to learn Excel**](https://www.youtube.com/trumpexcel)
, and honestly, that still blows my mind.

If you are an Excel Spreadsheet enthusiast, I am sure you would find a lot of good stuff here.

A Bit About Me
--------------

Hi, I’m **[Sumit Bansal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sumit-bansal-9b8a9012/)
**.

![Sumit Bansal](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20800'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

That is me presenting an Excel session in one of the Excel conferences in Bulgaria

When I started my career as a financial analyst, Excel became a huge part of my work — and I quickly realized that knowing the right tricks and formulas made all the difference.

The more I learned, the more I realized how powerful Excel could be when you really knew how to use it.

In 2013, I started TrumpExcel as a side project. Every time I learned something new, I’d write a tutorial or make a video about it. I figured if I found it useful, someone else probably would too.

Fast forward to today — I now work on Trump Excel full-time. I spend my days creating tutorials, recording videos, and diving deep into Excel to find the best ways to teach it.

And here’s something cool: Microsoft recognized me as an [Excel MVP (Most Valuable Professional)](https://mvp.microsoft.com/en-us/PublicProfile/5000966?fullName=Sumit%20Bansal)
 for sharing Excel knowledge and helping people learn. It’s a huge honor and honestly validates all those late nights figuring out VBA macros and array formulas!
