I even played fantasy football with a couple of football junkies. I still follow these matches and I still suck at playing it. 🙂

But I am better at making dashboards, and in this tutorial, I will show you how to create an Excel Dashboard that analyses English Premier League games of the 2014-2015 season.

How to use this Excel Dashboard:

*   Use [Scroll bar](https://trumpexcel.com/create-a-scroll-bar-in-excel/)
     to change the week number.
    *   Note that as you change the week number, it shows the games played it that selected week and the ranking of the teams at the end of the week.![EPL Excel Dashboard - Change Week Number with Scroll Bar](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20856%20344'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
*   Double click on the game result cells to get the detail for the game.
    *   As soon as you double click on the cells that have the game results, the match statistics update along with the logo of the clubs. It also shows the total win/loss numbers of both the teams for the entire season. For example, if I double-click on a game of Chelsea, it would show only 3 lost games for Chelsea (in the red color bar in the win/loss chart).![EPL Excel Dashboard - Double click to show game details](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20856%20344'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
*   Click on the help icon to show instructions. Click on it again to hide the instructions.![EPL Excel Dashboard - Click to show help instructions](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20856%20344'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

### Where did the data come from?

*   I got all the data from [www.football-data.co.uk](http://www.football-data.co.uk/englandm.php)
    . It has similar data for other leagues and competitions.
*   I got the logos from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C.)
    .

### What all goes into making this dashboard?

*   [Scroll bar](https://trumpexcel.com/create-a-scroll-bar-in-excel/)
     to change week number.
*   Excel Functions – [INDEX](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-index-function/)
     | [MATCH](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-match-function/)
     | [SUMPRODUCT](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-sumproduct-function/)
     | [ROWS](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-rows-function/)
     | [IFERROR](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-iferror-function/)
    
*   [Excel Table](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-table/)
     and structured references (a must for data-driven dashboards).
*   [Named Ranges](https://trumpexcel.com/named-ranges-in-excel/)
     (this dashboard uses [Picture Lookup technique](https://trumpexcel.com/picture-lookup/)
     to display the relevant icon on user selection).
*   A little bit of [VBA](https://trumpexcel.com/excel-vba/)
    .
