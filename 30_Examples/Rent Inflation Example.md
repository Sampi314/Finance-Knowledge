If rent is $1,000 today (Real Cash Flow) and inflation is 5% per year:

- **Year 1 Nominal Rent:** $1,000 \times (1 + 0.05)^1 = \$1,050$
- **Year 2 Nominal Rent:** $1,050 \times (1 + 0.05)^1 = \$1,102.50$
- **Year 3 Nominal Rent:** $1,000 \times (1 + 0.05)^3 = \$1,157.63$

To maintain the same purchasing power as $1,000 today, you will need to pay $1,157.63 in Year 3.
