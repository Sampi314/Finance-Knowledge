This source provides an overview of traditional and corporate Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs). It details the difference between utility and corporate buyers, explains Virtual PPAs (VPPAs) which don't involve physical power delivery, and discusses how these structures provide stable long-term cash flows to facilitate project financing.
