This source discusses the concept of Adjusted EBITDA, explaining its utility and common pitfalls. It highlights the dangers of using adjusted EBITDA as a proxy for cash flow, especially when management teams manipulate adjustments, using WeWork's "Community Adjusted EBITDA" as a cautionary tale.
