This source provides a comprehensive overview of Net Operating Income (NOI) in the context of commercial real estate. It explains how NOI differs from net income before taxes, outlines the types of expenses included and excluded, and discusses how to normalize expenses to arrive at a standardized NOI figure for property valuation.
