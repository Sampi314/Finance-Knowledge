This article explains the roles of mezzanine debt and preferred equity in LBO capital structures. It details how mezzanine debt sits between senior debt and equity, functioning as subordinated, unsecured financing that typically carries higher yields, PIK interest, and equity kickers. It also covers when PE sponsors use it to maximize leverage and how to correctly model PIK interest and warrants.
