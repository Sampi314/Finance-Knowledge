This video tutorial reviews the intuition behind the Gordon Growth Formula used to calculate terminal value in a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis. It explains how growth in cash flows impacts the present value of an investment and why the formula takes the shape it does by subtracting the growth rate from the discount rate.
