This source outlines what the cost of debt is, its calculation methods (Yield to Maturity and matrix pricing via debt ratings), and the distinction between pre-tax and after-tax cost of debt. It highlights how the tax deductibility of interest expense makes debt relatively cheaper, utilizing the marginal tax rate to find the after-tax figure.
