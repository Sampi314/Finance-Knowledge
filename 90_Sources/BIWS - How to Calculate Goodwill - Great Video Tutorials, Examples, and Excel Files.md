This source provides a practical breakdown of how goodwill is calculated in M&A deals and merger models. It explains the mechanics of using goodwill as an accounting plug to keep the combined balance sheet balanced when buyers pay a premium over the target's equity value, including the impact of asset write-ups and Deferred Tax Liabilities.
