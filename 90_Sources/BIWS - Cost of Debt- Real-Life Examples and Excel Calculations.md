This guide details the real-world application and calculation of the cost of debt, particularly its use in WACC and DCF models. It underscores the transition from a pre-tax cost of debt (which considers actual or implied interest rates based on market value and yield to maturity) to an after-tax cost that accounts for the tax shield on interest.
