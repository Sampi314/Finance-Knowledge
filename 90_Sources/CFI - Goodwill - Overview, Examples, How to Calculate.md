This guide defines goodwill as an intangible asset arising from paying a premium over the fair market value of net assets. It details the step-by-step process of determining book values, adjusting them to fair values, and capturing the residual excess purchase price as goodwill, while also distinguishing accounting goodwill from economic goodwill.
