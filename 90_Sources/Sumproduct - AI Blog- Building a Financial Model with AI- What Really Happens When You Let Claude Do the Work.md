This article details a controlled experiment where Claude (an AI) was prompted to build a three-statement financial model for a brewery. The source touches on cost build-ups briefly, noting that they typically require "Bills of Materials" mapping costs for materials, packaging, and labor, but the article focuses primarily on evaluating AI's capabilities in modeling rather than providing a deep tutorial on cost building methodologies.
