This web site is designed to support "Applied Corporate Finance: A User's Manual". The publisher is John Wiley and Sons. You can navigate the site by either going to individual chapters and getting supporting material by chapter, or by going to the supporting material directly. The overheads and solutions to the problems are stored in pdf format, and you need Adobe Acrobat to read pdf files. (You can download Acrobat Reader directly from the Adobe site.) The spreadsheets are in Microsoft Excel 95 format, and can be used on either a Mac or Windows system. You can read the preface to the book by [clicking here](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/other/preface.htm)
. If you have a first printing of the book, you can click here to get [corrections](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/corrapcf.htm)
 to it. (Sorry!)
