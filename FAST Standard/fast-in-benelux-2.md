FAST in Benelux
===============

FAST in Benelux

31 Jan 13

On January 23rd there were two FAST events held in Benelux. One took place in Rotterdam hosted by Rebel Group, and other in Brussels, hosted by PMV. FAST Standard Organisation Moderation Board members from Deloitte, Mazars, Rebel Group, and F1F9 were joined by bankers, advisers, accountants, academics and financiers from a wide variety of companies and organisations.

This 5 minute film gives a flavour of the discussions that took place around the Standard itself, and the purpose of the independent standards organisation, the FSO.
