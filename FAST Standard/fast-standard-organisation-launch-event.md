FAST Standard Organisation launch event
=======================================

FAST Standard Organisation launch event

5 Dec 12

On November 29th the launch event for the FAST Standard Organisation, or “FSO” took place in London. The FSO is a not for profit standards body established to promote, protect and further evolve the FAST Standard.

In future, changes to the standard will be considered and decided upon by a moderation board made up of experienced modelling professionals. The moderation board currently has members from Deloitte, Grant Thornton, F1F9, Financial Mechanics, Mazars and Rebel.

This 4 minute film gives an overview of the event where speakers from Grant Thornton, Mazars and F1F9 outlined why they have adopted FAST, and how the FSO will operate.
