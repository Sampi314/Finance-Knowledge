Introduction to FAST event in New York
======================================

Introduction to FAST event in New York

22 Mar 13

On April 11th, F1F9 and Vair Training LLC will be co-presenting a free “Introduction to FAST” event in New York, hosted by Baker & McKenzie LLP.

We’re expecting attendees from Bank of America, Linde North America, Siemens and KPMG among others, as well as representatives  from the FAST moderation board.

The details of the free event are below. To register, please send an email to [events@fast-standard.org](mailto:events@fast-standard.org)
 and include your full name and organisation. NB: Space is limited, so RSVP today.

Thursday April 11, 2013  
6:00 PM  
Baker & McKenzie LLP  
452 Fifth Avenue  
New York City 10018

Both F1F9 and Vair are delighted to be able to promote the FAST Standard globally through their respective training courses.

“F1F9 is a financial-training innovator. The FAST modelling methodology that they have been a driving force in developing is one that we have always admired and consistently recommend to our learners,” said Stephen Janes, Managing Director at Vair. “By adopting the Standard in our own course materials, we are ensuring that our students are part of the largest international community of modellers.”
