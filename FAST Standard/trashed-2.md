Financial modelling standard-webinar recording
==============================================

Financial modelling standard-webinar recording

3 Feb 21

Financial modelling standard – webinar recording. Our thanks to Kenny Whitelaw-Jones and Full Stack Modeller for arranging a webinar to discuss Financial Modelling Standards: where are we and what’s next? Andrew Berkley (representing the FAST Standard Organisation) joined Rickard Wärnelid (Mazars), David Lyford-Smith (ICAEW) and Danielle Stein Fairhurst (Plum Solutions) in an engaging and wide-ranging discussion. You can do watch the video [here](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/4pfalhw7lm)
