Reducing spreadsheet risk

16 May 12

Peter Gillon, a Senior Finance Manager with long experience in the out-sourcing sector, discusses how FAST reduces risk and increases collaboration. Peter notes that the advantages of adopting FAST are less for the individual, and more about making collaboration easier and reducing risk where spreadsheet models need to be shared.
