What’s New at CFI | Managing Your Stakeholders
==============================================

March 4, 2025 / 00:21:59 / E84

In this episode of _What’s New at CFI_, Meeyeon and Ryan Spendelow discuss the importance of stakeholder management in various professional settings, especially in the context of remote work.

They explore how stakeholder management has evolved with the rise of virtual teams, the significance of understanding stakeholder expectations, common mistakes finance professionals make, and strategies for managing difficult stakeholders. The conversation emphasizes the broader impact of these skills on career advancement and personal development.
