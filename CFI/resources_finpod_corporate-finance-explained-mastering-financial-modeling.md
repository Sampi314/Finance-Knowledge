How Do Top Finance Teams Build and Use Financial Models To Drive Billion-Dollar Decisions?
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In this episode of Corporate Finance Explained, we break down the key techniques and best practices in financial modeling—a must-have skill for corporate finance professionals.

### **What You’ll Learn:**

*   **The Role of Financial Modeling** – How companies use models for budgeting, valuation, and risk analysis.
*   **Essential Components of a Strong Model** – The importance of historical data, key assumptions, and financial statements.
*   **Best Practices for Financial Modeling** – Keeping models accurate, structured, flexible, and transparent.
*   **Common Mistakes to Avoid** – Overcomplication, poor assumptions, and missing error checks.
*   **Real-World Corporate Finance Examples:**
    *   **Microsoft’s $68.7B acquisition of Activision Blizzard** – Using DCF modeling for valuation.
    *   **Airbnb’s COVID-19 survival strategy** – Forecasting worst-case cash flow scenarios.
    *   **Tesla’s production planning** – Cash burn and supply chain risk modeling.
    *   **Apple’s stock buyback program** – Financial modeling for capital allocation.

### **Who Should Listen?**

Finance professionals in FP&A, investment banking, private equity, corporate development, and CFO roles who want to sharpen their modeling skills and learn from real-world corporate finance case studies.
