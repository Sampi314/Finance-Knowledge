State of Corporate Training in 2022
===================================

Get ready for the shift to online

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published April 13, 2022

Read Time 54 minutes

Join a panel of acclaimed eLearning and financial services thought-leaders as they discuss the current state of training and skill development for finance professionals in 2022.

In this webinar, we’ll share the latest trends in corporate training, uncovered from a survey of hundreds of organizations and finance professionals, including leading global banks, the Big Four accounting firms, and Fortune 500 companies.

If you’re looking for disruptive ways to develop and upskill your team of finance professionals while increasing employee engagement and talent retention, this webinar is a must-attend.

Our panel of industry experts will cover:
-----------------------------------------

*   How organizations are engaging in skills-based training
*   Key behavioral shifts in talent acquisition and retention
*   The impact of online vs. offline learning models
*   How to foster an effective learning culture that fuels growth and efficiency
*   Get your questions answered by online corporate training experts

Meet our panelists
------------------

#### ![](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/Group-676-1-e1650490185847.png)

#### Andrew Loo | VP, Capital Markets

Andrew is VP of Capital Markets at CFI, having joined the firm in 2019 as a subject matter expert for the team that developed the Capital Markets & Securities Analyst (CMSA)® program.

Prior to joining CFI, Andrew spent over 20 years in financial services with considerable experience working in the capital markets in Asia, with a long-standing focus working with the largest fixed income investors globally at leading global investment banks.  He very much enjoys bringing his wealth of knowledge to students of CFI.

#### ![](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/Group-1-e1650490205561.png)

#### Kyle Peterdy | VP, Commercial Banking

Kyle began his career as a professional educator and coach in 2008, working with youth all the way through collegiate student athletes. After transitioning into financial services in 2014, Kyle joined RBC as a loan officer on several commercial banking portfolios; he also served as a mentor to support the training and onboarding of new hires throughout that time.

In 2020, Kyle joined CFI as a subject matter expert, developing and delivering curriculum for the Commercial Banking & Credit Analyst (CBCA)™ program. He loves teaching and designing innovative educational experiences for students around the world.

#### ![](https://cdn.corporatefinanceinstitute.com/assets/Group-2-e1650490165713.png)

#### Anna Talerico | CEO of CFI

Anna Talerico, CEO of CFI, is passionate about the transformational impact of online learning in the corporate setting and helping learners and companies achieve their goals. She is a serial entrepreneur who most recently held the position of COO of Linux Academy, an online training company focused on cloud technologie
