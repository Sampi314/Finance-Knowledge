CFI and FinChat Partner to Globalize the Future of Investment Research
======================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published November 15, 2023

Updated September 5, 2024

Read Time 1 minutes

November 15, 2023 – CFI and [FinChat](https://finchat.io/)
 have partnered to bring the future of investment research, powered by AI to its global audience. 

Corporate Finance Institute (CFI) the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, has partnered with FinChat to provide continued value to its global audience. FinChat is the complete modern investment research platform for global entities, combining an institutional data platform and AI, FinChat saves time researching investment opportunities. 

The partnership represents a strategic alignment of shared values and a commitment to excellence. Both companies are poised to catalyze significant breakthroughs in the finance and investment industry by pooling resources, knowledge, and networks.

“We are thrilled to announce this strategic partnership with FinChat,” said Anna Talerico, CFI CEO, “We continually strive to ensure we are bringing innovative resources to our members and we feel strongly that FinChat does just that.”

**About Corporate Finance Institute (CFI)**

[Corporate Finance Institute](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/)
 (CFI) is the number one rated online provider of finance and banking training, certifications, and productivity tools. With the industry’s leading training library, we empower professionals and teams with job-ready skills and practical resources to succeed in diverse roles within finance and banking. Established in 2016, CFI is trusted by over 2 million individuals and thousands of organizations worldwide.

**About FinChat**

[FinChat](https://finchat.io/)
 is the complete modern investment research platform for global equities. Combining an institutional data platform and AI, FinChat saves you time researching your next great investment idea.
