Cost-benefit analysis (CBA) is more than simple math—it’s the cornerstone of smart business decisions. In this episode of _Corporate Finance Explained_, we dive deep into how companies weigh financial trade-offs, avoid common pitfalls, and use CBA to drive strategy. Learn how leading firms like Amazon, Tesla, Apple, Ford, and Quibi used (or misused) CBA to shape their success—or struggle.
