**How Do Top Companies Manage Their Cost of Capital for Sustainable Growth?**
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

In this episode, we explore how Tesla, Amazon, and Apple optimize their Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) to make smarter financing and investment decisions.

### **What You’ll Learn:**

1.  What is WACC & why it matters in corporate finance
2.  Debt vs. equity financing – How companies balance funding sources
3.  How beta, credit ratings, and market conditions impact WACC
4.  Real-world case studies – How Tesla structures financing & how Apple lowers its WACC
5.  How FP&A teams use WACC in valuation, M&A, and capital budgeting
6.  Proven strategies to reduce WACC and maximize shareholder value
