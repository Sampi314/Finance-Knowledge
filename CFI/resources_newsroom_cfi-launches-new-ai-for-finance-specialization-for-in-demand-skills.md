CFI Launches New AI for Finance Specialization for In-Demand Skills
===================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published January 14, 2025

Read Time 1 minutes

**January 14, 2025**  — Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, is thrilled to announce the launch of its AI for Finance Specialization. This specialization is designed to equip professionals with practical, hands-on skills to leverage AI to streamline financial analysis, automate workflows, and improve decision-making.

CFI Senior VP of Training & Curriculum Ryan Spendelow notes, “We’re seeing a demand for AI content as Finance Professionals begin to navigate the future of AI in their roles. Financial analysis and Excel automation using AI are here, and we at CFI are committed to providing the skills to help professionals navigate this new way of working.” 

Through hands-on learning, participants will delve into AI applications in financial statement analysis, scenario planning, risk assessment, and data-driven dashboard creation. By the end of the program, they will be prepared to excel in finance, banking, and corporate strategy roles that require innovation and technical expertise. 

As part of CFI’s growing portfolio of high-impact training programs, this specialization underscores the organization’s dedication to delivering relevant and practical education for financial professionals. Since its inception, CFI has issued over 3 million course certificates to learners in more than 150 countries and is a trusted leader in financial certifications and professional development.
