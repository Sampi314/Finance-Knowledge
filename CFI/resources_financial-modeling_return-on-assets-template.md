What is Return on Assets (ROA)?
-------------------------------

Return on Assets (ROA) is a financial ratio that measures a company’s profitability relative to its total assets. It shows how effectively a business is using its assets to generate net income over a specific time period. ROA is a valuable tool for comparing performance across companies or tracking your own company’s return over time.

### ROA Formula

#### **ROA = Net Income / Average Total Assets**

Where:

*   **Net Income** = Net profits reported on the income statement
*   **Average Total Assets** = (Beginning Assets + Ending Assets) / 2

A higher ROA indicates better efficiency, while a lower ROA may point to underutilized resources or high direct cost structures.
