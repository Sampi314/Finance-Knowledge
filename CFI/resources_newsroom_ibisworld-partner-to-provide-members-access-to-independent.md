CFI and IBISWorld Partner To Provide Members Access to Independent, Cutting-Edge Industry Information
=====================================================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published January 1, 2024

Read Time 2 minutes

January 1, 2024 – CFI has partnered with IBISWorld, the global leader in industry research, to provide members the benefit of accessing cutting-edge industry information. 

Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, has partnered with IBISWorld to provide continued value to its global audience. IBISWorld’s exceptional team of analysts leverage reliable data, proprietary forecast models, and innovative methodology to provide independent, cutting-edge industry information.

“We appreciate the opportunity to partner with IBISWorld on behalf of our global members,” said Anna Talerico, CFI CEO, “We continually strive to be a valuable resource for modern finance and banking professionals and feel strongly IBISWorld adds to that value.”

This partnership gives users insights into thousands of global industries, enterprises, and economic drivers. IBISWorld’s thoroughly researched and unbiased industry analysis shapes the global business landscape, empowering CFI members to act with confidence and make informed, data-driven strategic decisions.

IBISWorld’s VP, Commercial Banking, Matt Murphy explains “For over 50 years, IBISWorld has helped Banks, Financial Services, and Professional Services firms better understand their clients’ industries and make better business decisions. Given the exceptional education and certification CFI provides those exact same professionals, partnering with them has been a natural fit for our organization. This partnership provides CFI learners with access to the same, real-world industry research many of their employer firms are using at the corporate level and up to date, reliable information to use in their coursework.”

‘s unrivaled online database gives users insights into thousands of industries, enterprises, and economic drivers globally. Having built longstanding relationships with clients all over the world, IBISWorld helps businesses achieve their ideal outcomes by remaining at the forefront of industry research and providing unmatched coverage. IBISWorld’s thoroughly researched and unbiased industry analysis shapes the global business landscape, empowering clients to act with confidence and make informed, data-driven strategic decisions.
