CFI Launches the Accounting for Financial Analysts Specialization
=================================================================

Written by [CFI Team](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/about-cfi/)

Published August 1, 2024

Updated June 12, 2025

Read Time 2 minutes

July 30, 2024 – Corporate Finance Institute (CFI), the #1 rated online provider of finance and banking training and productivity tools, announces the launch of its Accounting for Financial Analysts Specialization, specifically designed to equip financial analysts with the accounting skills necessary to excel in their roles and drive more accurate financial analysis and strategic decision-making.

The Accounting for Financial Analysts specialization addresses a critical need for deeper accounting knowledge among financial analysts. While many professionals possess a solid foundation in finance, this course bridges the gap, delivering an in-depth understanding of financial statements, accrual accounting, revenue recognition, asset management, and liability assessment, among other key concepts. Participants will gain hands-on experience with real-world case studies and industry-relevant exercises that foster a practical, results-driven approach to accounting.

“CFI’s mission has always been to provide finance professionals with the skills and tools they need to advance in their careers,” said Ryan Spendelow, SVP of Training & Curriculum at CFI. “With CFI’s new Accounting for Financial Analysts specialization, we are enabling analysts to strengthen their accounting knowledge—an essential skill set that underpins sound financial analysis.”

The specialization includes the following core modules:

*   **Fundamentals of Financial Accounting** – Establishing a strong understanding of accounting principles and frameworks.
*   **Revenue Recognition and Expense Matching** – Key insights into accrual accounting and how timing affects financial outcomes.
*   **Asset and Liability Management** – Deep dives into the valuation, depreciation, and amortization of assets, alongside liability accounting.
*   **Financial Statement Analysis** – A practical approach to reading, interpreting, and utilizing financial statements for accurate, data-driven decision-making.

This new specialization offers a self-paced, fully online learning experience accessible from anywhere in the world, providing flexibility for busy professionals. Learners who complete the program will earn a specialization certificate in Accounting for Financial Analysts, showcasing their commitment to expanding their expertise and increasing their value in the workplace.

As CFI continues to expand its offerings, this specialization aligns with its commitment to delivering relevant, high-impact training for financial professionals. Since its founding, CFI has issued more than 3,000,000 course certificates to professionals in over 150 countries and continues to be a trusted resource for financial certifications and professional development.
