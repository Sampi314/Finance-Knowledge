# AI multi-language model translation

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/ai-multi-language-model-translation

---

In the [Gridlines modelling team](https://www.gridlines.com/?ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
, we are frequently supporting cross-border transactions. We are at times asked to show each line item in the model in two different languages, often English and a local language.

In the [latest version of the Productivity Pack Macros](https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/financial-modelling-productivity-pack/)
, we have added AI support to translate your model labels automatically. This is only available in Version 25 of the macros and later.

The translation tool passes your text through an online translation engine. You can either use Microsoft Azure Translation or Google translate. See the guidance below on how to sign up for a free Microsoft Azure Translation account.
