# Financial Modelling Handbook now available as an ebook

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/ebook-version-now-available

---

I didn’t intend for the Financial Modelling Handbook to be available digitally.

But having just this morning opened my 100th email requesting an ebook version, I changed my mind. I know that for many of you, cost is an issue, and also delivery times vary around the world.

[So here it is](https://fmhandbook.gumroad.com/l/e-book?ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
.

Same deal as the hardcopy:

*   All the worked example files
*   Exclusive additional online chapters
*   Access to supporting video tutorials.

Just fewer trees destroyed in the process.

The ebook version of the handbook is now available for £49. Thank you to all those of you who asked!

You can [buy the ebook version here](https://fmhandbook.gumroad.com/l/e-book?ref=financialmodellinghandbook.org)
.

Have a great weekend.

Kenny
