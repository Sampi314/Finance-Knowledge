# Can you help me choose the title for Volume 1?

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/can-you-help-me-choose-the-title-for-volume-1

---

The first draft of the book design is done, and we're still on track for publishing by the end of this year. Here's a preview of the cover:

![](https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/content/images/size/w2400/2022/12/cover-the-financial-modelling-handbook-met-def-copy-221122.jpg)

And a preview of how an internal two-page spread will look:

![](https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/content/images/size/w2400/2022/12/2022-12-12_10-29-24.jpg)

We're splitting the material written so far into two volumes. Volume 1 will contain the first three parts of what's written so far, covering model structure and workflow.

The thing is, I'm not sure that "Model structure and workflow" is a very sexy title for Volume 1.

I think that some of these titles may "market" better:

*   Core financial modelling skills
*   Essential financial modelling skills
*   Foundational financial modelling skills

What do you think? What title would draw you to the book?

Thanks so much for your input & support.

Kenny
