# Progress report

**Source:** https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/progress-report-3-2-22

---

We've finished the migration of the site from Substack over to Ghost. It's all-around better; more formatting features, better layout & editing tools.

We have not yet pulled the comments over from the previous site, but that's in progress.

In the meantime, I've added a new section on [Financial Modelling Standards](https://www.financialmodellinghandbook.org/financial-modelling-standards/)
. A few beta readers suggested this - thank you!

I would be very keen to get your feedback on it and the new site overall.

The pace of posting and notifications will increase again now that we're set up on the new site. As ever, if it's too much and not what you signed up for, please opt-out!

Thanks again for your support and encouragement,  
Best,  
Kenny
