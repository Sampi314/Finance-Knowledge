### **Date Centers New Online Course, a Free resource** 

We are busy, creating new sub-sector courses. One such course is on Data Centers.

The first (and free) version of this course will be released online this year. This course will include an overview of what data centers are, types of data centers, an overview of revenue structures, operating costs, and capital costs. We also discuss whether Data Centers are infrastructure or real estate.

This free version won’t include models, but if you have completed one of our project finance modeling courses, you will be in a good place to figure it out.

The free course will be available to everyone for a period of 2 months. During that time, we will produce some more detailed lessons and a financial model which will be included in the paid version of the course. The paid course will be an add-on to our project finance modeling courses. in a similar way to how our mining / critical minerals and battery courses are structured.

### New Advanced Project Finance Debt Modeling Live Stream

We’re excited to launch an expanded version of our Advanced Debt Course, offering deeper instruction, more hands-on modeling, and additional support.

The original course focused on complex project finance debt structures such as leverage constraints, sensitivities, and P50/P90/P99 sizing. The new format now goes further, covering:

*   Summarizing a loan term sheet
*   Sweep-style debt (e.g. Term Loan B, merchant battery debt)
*   Multi-tranche
*   Swaps, DSRAs/DSRFs, and reserve accounts
*   Advanced macros (solving multiple scenarios, data table macros, and how to speed up calculations)
*   Seasonality and mitigants
*   Junior debt and shareholder loans

**Key Enhancements:**

*   Longer format for more modeling, Q&A, and discussion
*   Practical exercises building into a cumulative model
*   Extra homework and feedback for ongoing support

### Online Courses Available

[Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/project-finance-modeling/)

Learn the concepts to develop a financial model related to PPP/P3s for both social and economic infrastructure. The course centers on a hospital PPP and toll road, although other sub-sectors are mentioned

[Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling/)

Learn how to develop a best-practice financial model and optimize it for both debt & equity investors for a renewable energy project finance deal. Using a case study, learn the concepts required to develop a financial model related to wind and solar.

[Battery Storage Financial Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/battery-storage-financial-modeling/)

This course provides an overview of standalone batteries and co-located batteries with wind or solar assets. It is designed to familiarize developers, investors, and lenders with the investment decisions and risks of battery storage projects.

[Mining and Critical Minerals](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/mining-critical-minerals-project-finance-modeling/)

Learn how to develop a financial model for a copper project with a typical mine plan including ore mined, waste mined, input grades, high and low-grade stockpiling, and processing recoveries. Understand the key drivers of mining.

[Tax Equity and Hybrid Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/tax-equity-hybrid-financial-modeling/)

Gain an overview of the tax equity structures commonly used in U.S. renewable energy investments, with a focus on the development of a financial model. Dive deep into tax capital accounts, deficit restoration obligations, stop loss allocations, and HLBV accounting.

[Financial Modeling Fundamentals](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/financial-modeling-fundamentals/)

Building a model from a blank spreadsheet .this course bridges the gap between theoretical learning and practical application. Participants will build a best-practice financial model and will learn the frameworks for evaluating your business and projects, including future cash flows, income statement, balance sheet, and financial statement analysis.

[Introduction to Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/introduction-project-finance-modeling/?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8ni3tZKMG0QR0FZzj0SZ7BUm69vJH48uUUX8xWzavsOtuK-VGeDsuorwYB8wzmiCeZMH-7)

This course is designed to give participants a strong foundation in the core concepts that impact investments and debt structuring in project finance transactions.

It’s ideal for: Beginner modelers, Students and Professionals who don’t build models daily, but need to understand project finance models and concepts to make informed decisions. If you’re a developer, engineer, accountant, or lawyer – this could be the right course for you.
