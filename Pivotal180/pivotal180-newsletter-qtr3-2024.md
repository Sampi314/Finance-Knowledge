**Warning: Major flaw in latest version of Excel!**

There is a serious issue with the latest version of Excel (version 2405). The issue arises when you add new rows in your financial model. The rows may insert properly, but they often don’t, resulting in incorrect row references in formulas or other errors. The only real fix (recommended by Microsoft) at the moment is to roll back to a previous version of Excel (see [here](https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/forum/all/row-insertion-deletion-causes-errors/f39b0a67-4f31-4a42-b2b5-5fea6929a432?utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-_YO4gI-_euGL6zBPZSMY-NhjbLG0qxQWwrreLlApWTqEU0stogdN78hEe52tqDbQfUse3s)
). This will be hard for many people without corporate administration rights. Some Excel users have said that setting calculations to manual does fix the issue. Hopefully Microsoft fixes this ASAP!
