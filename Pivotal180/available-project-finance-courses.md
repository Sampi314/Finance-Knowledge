### Advanced Project Finance Debt Modeling

Advanced course for experienced professionals focusing on sophisticated debt modeling techniques, multiple sizing constraints, refinancing calculations, and complex multi-tranche structures. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Model multiple debt sizing constraints including leverage and P50/P99/P90
*   Structure and size multi-tranche, junior, and mezzanine debt
*   Manage extreme seasonality and apply P90 sweeps for renewables
*   Model bridging loans during construction and refinancing scenarios
*   Resolve circularities from fees and taxes in project finance debt
*   Implement target debt balance structures such as Term Loan B

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/advanced-project-finance-debt-modeling/)

### Project Finance Modeling

Comprehensive course for analysts, associates, and vice presidents seeking advanced project finance modeling skills. Covers complex structures, debt sizing, and real-world case studies across infrastructure sectors. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Build best-practice project finance models for infrastructure projects
*   Analyze DSCR, LLCR, and other key debt sizing metrics
*   Structure equity financing and model cash waterfalls
*   Forecast revenues and costs for social and economic infrastructure
*   Integrate tax, depreciation, and international tax considerations
*   Optimize capital structure for debt sizing and equity returns

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/project-finance-modeling/)

### Introduction to Project Finance Modeling

Perfect for entry-level professionals seeking to enter the project finance sector. This comprehensive course covers fundamental modeling techniques, project finance structures, and essential Excel skills. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Understand core concepts and structures in project finance
*   Build and analyze an annual transaction model for a renewable project
*   Calculate DSCR, WACC, and size debt using different repayment methods
*   Model generation, PPA revenues, and operating costs
*   Apply tax rules, NOLs, and IRA-related credits to project models
*   Perform scenario and sensitivity analysis to assess project viability

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/introduction-project-finance-modeling/)

### Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling

Specialized training focused on renewable energy project finance, covering wind and solar project modeling, power purchase agreements, tax credits, and regulatory considerations specific to clean energy projects. **Key Learning Outcomes:**

*   Build best-practice renewable energy project finance models
*   Analyze DSCR and other key debt sizing and repayment metrics
*   Model generation forecasts, curtailment, and degradation
*   Structure debt, equity, and tax equity financing for wind and solar
*   Apply tax credit rules, NOLs, and IRA provisions in project models
*   Optimize project returns using sensitivity analysis and scenario testing

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling/)
