Self Paced Online
=================

Using videos and downloadable excel templates our online programs take you through how to build a model line by line.

[Contact Us](https://pivotal180.com/contact-us/)

**Our approach emphasizes why things work the way they do and how the multi-faceted pieces fit together in the broader context of a transaction.**

Pivotal180 training not only can significantly improve your best practice modeling skills, but we also have a real emphasis on the concepts and focusing on what’s important to the transaction. Best practice models are critical in project finance. Our courses however are not limited to financial modeling and best practices in Microsoft Excel, we are committed to explaining how the numbers align with underlying financial and engineering concepts, transaction structures, legal documentation, market conditions and risk management approaches.

The training is not just about coding, it teaches students to understand the bigger picture (e.g., contract structures, risks, how to forecast wind) and how to analyze deals. Much of the content of our courses is focused on how to achieve the needs of lenders, developers, and various equity participants. 

The quality and clarity of our online content is truly exceptional, there is simply nothing else like it in the market.
