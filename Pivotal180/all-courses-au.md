Designed Specifically for the Australian Market
-----------------------------------------------

Australia’s project finance and infrastructure landscape has its own structure, conventions, and modelling expectations. Generic global courses rarely cover the debt sizing, tax, regulatory frameworks, and deal structures used across:

*   Australian renewable energy projects
    
*   Toll roads, transport, energy networks, and social infrastructure
    
*   Mining and critical minerals
    
*   State and federal government partnerships
    
*   Local investment, lending and advisory teams
    

Our courses are built by practitioners who have worked on Australian transactions, understand the local modelling standards, and teach using examples directly relevant to Australia’s financial environment.
