We are proud to bring a new and exciting course available to all students, Financial Modeling Fundamentals.

This course bridges the gap between theoretical learning and practical application. Participants will build a best-practice financial model using a real-life case study. You will learn frameworks for evaluating your business and project, including future cash flows, income statement balance sheet and cash flow integration, and financial statement analysis.  Courses Available**
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/project-finance-modeling/)

Learn the concepts to develop a financial model related to PPP/P3s for both social and economic infrastructure. A mining financial model will be added to this course soon. Watch this space!

### [Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling/)

Learn how to develop a best-practice financial model and optimize it for both debt & equity investors for a renewable energy project finance deal. Using a case study, learn the concepts required to develop a financial model related to wind and solar.

### [Introduction to Battery Storage](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/battery-storage-financial-modeling/)

This course provides an overview of standalone batteries and co-located batteries with wind or solar assets. It is designed to familiarize developers, investors, and lenders with the investment decisions and risks of battery storage projects.

### [Mining and Critical Minerals](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/mining-critical-minerals-project-finance-modeling/)

Learn how to develop a financial model for a copper project with a typical mine plan including ore mined, waste mined, input grades, high- and low-grade stockpiling, and processing recoveries. Understand the key drivers of mining, processing, and transport costs

### [Tax Equity and Hybrid Modeling](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/tax-equity-hybrid-financial-modeling/)

Gain an overview of the tax equity structures commonly used in U.S. renewable energy investments, with a focus on the development of a financial model. Dive deep into tax capital accounts, deficit restoration obligations, stop loss allocations, and HLBV accounting. Learn more on emerging tax credit finance structures under the IRA!
