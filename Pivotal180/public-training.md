**PUBLIC TRAINING - Live Streaming** 

Online learning has its advantages, but many clients prefer instructor-led training, with the opportunity for real-time interaction with the instructors. 

*   Line-by-line construction of a model, rather than explanations of a pre-constructed template
*   Instructors who review your work as you do it. One-on-one assistance as needed "office hours"
*   Presentation of finance concepts and modeling techniques with time for Q&A
*   Renewable energy financial modeling is 8 \*3 hours live sessions for 1-2 days a week over a 4–5-week period. This is equivalent to three days training.
*   Tax equity financial modeling is is 7 \* 3 hours live sessions for 1-2 days a week over a 5-6 week period. This is equivalent to two days training.
*   Battery storage is 4 \* 2 hour live sessions for 1-2 days a week over a two week period. This is equivalent to one day of training.
*   The spacing of the classes gives students time to review concepts before the next class and to dive deeper into concepts that are covered in the self-paced online course.
*   12 months of access to our online learning platform for self-paced review and supplemental learning materials
*   Recordings of each live session
*   Participants come from various organizations and class sizes are limited to ~12.
*   Homework is submitted and reviewed to allow us to cover more material over the course
