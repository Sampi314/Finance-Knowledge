Instructor-Led Training
=======================

A new standard of training At Pivotal180, we have redefined the standards for project finance and financial modeling training. Focused exclusively on teaching, institutional and human capacity building is not a side business, it is core.

Training with Pivotal180
------------------------

Our instructor-led training courses are highly customized and developed to address the unique challenges that your team and organization face.

**Customization options:**

Our training programs can be custom tailored to meet the needs of your organization, including:

*   unique case studies and business objectives
*   standard company metrics or presentation styles
*   corporate operating procedures
*   country-specific rules, regulations and tax regimes

**Course Customization Tools**

Our sector focused course customization tools are simple to use and designed to help determine what a potential course might look like for your team. They outline what is available both in terms of subject matter as well as delivery options, including:

*   Class time (important when planning instructor-led training)
*   If there is a modeling component to the lesson
*   If the lesson is available on our online platform

Use the green buttons below to access the course customization tool applicable to your team.

**Training Course Delivery**

The vast majority of our instructor-led courses adopt a blended approach, combining interactive training via live streaming + access to our online learning platform.  Our live streaming financial modeling courses include:

*   Line-by-line construction of a model, rather than explanations of a pre-constructed template
*   Instructors who review your work as you do it
*   One-on-one assistance as needed "office hours"
*   Presentation of finance concepts and modeling techniques with time for Q&A
*   Access to our online learning platform for self-paced review and supplemental learning material

**The live streaming format entails:**

*   2.5-hour sessions, 3 days a week, for 2 or 3 weeks
*   Recordings of each live session, posted daily for review
