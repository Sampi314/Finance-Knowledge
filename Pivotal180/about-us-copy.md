What We Value
=============

[Join our Mailing List](https://pivotal180.com/subscription/)

At Pivotal180, our values shape everything we do. As a specialists in financial training, we are committed to equipping professionals with the skills needed for success in renewable energy financial modelling, project finance training, and financial modelling courses. Our core principles reflect our responsibility to our clients, employees, and the broader global community.

### Social Responsibility

We believe that financial education should empower individuals and organisations to make ethical, informed decisions.

*   We champion diversity, equity, and inclusion in the financial sector. Our project finance courses welcome professionals from all backgrounds, ensuring accessibility and opportunity for all.
*   Work-life balance is essential for long-term success. We offer online training programmes in order to accommodate busy schedules without compromising quality.
*   We demand excellence while recognising that true success also values personal well-being and integrity.
*   We believe that businesses have a responsibility to their employees and to the communities in which they operate.

### Environmental Responsibility

Climate change presents one of the greatest financial and societal challenges of our time. Our renewable energy financial modelling training plays a critical role in advancing sustainable infrastructure investment.

*   We know that rapid adoption of environmentally sustainable infrastructure is critical to our collective future.
*   We equip professionals with the tools to structure and finance renewable energy projects effectively.
*   By training experts in project finance modelling, we help accelerate the adoption of sustainable and climate-resilient financial strategies.
*   We are committed to being part of the solution, ensuring that our training courses promote knowledge and skills that support global environmental goals.

### International Development

Strong financial knowledge is essential for economic independence. Our mission is to provide financial modelling training that empowers professionals worldwide.

*   We support capacity-building in developing nations, reducing dependence on foreign consultants.
*   Our courses help local professionals take the lead in structuring investments and negotiating Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs).
*   We ensure that government institutions and financial professionals have the expertise needed to secure sustainable, long-term economic growth.

### More than modeling

Project finance training is not just about building spreadsheets—it’s about making informed, strategic decisions.

*   Financial models must be grounded in sound assumptions and real-world scenarios.
*   Our courses go beyond numbers to teach critical thinking, risk assessment, and deal structuring for effective project finance execution.
*   We bridge the gap between technical skills and practical application, ensuring that professionals understand the meaning behind the numbers.

### Experience

With real-world expertise, our team brings invaluable insights to every training program.

*   We have raised billions in debt and equity, structured major financial transactions, and served on the boards of public and private companies.
*   Our practical experience ensures that our financial modelling training reflects current industry standards and best practices.
*   We don’t just teach theory—we provide actionable knowledge that professionals can apply immediately in their careers.

Pivotal180 doesn’t just just train professionals; we build future leaders in project finance, renewable energy financial modelling, and financial strategy. Our values drive us to create impactful, career-enhancing courses that empower individuals and organisations to succeed in a complex financial world.
