Project Finance Modeling Courses
--------------------------------

Build the skills to structure, model, and execute renewable energy and infrastructure deals. Our courses combine real transaction experience with industry best practices.

### Tax Equity Essentials

Strategic course for managers, executives, and dealmakers new to tax equity seeking comprehensive understanding of transaction processes and market dynamics. Covers practical deal execution, documentation, and negotiation tactics with battle-tested playbooks from industry practitioners.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/tax-equity-essentials/)

### Advanced Project Finance Debt Modeling

Advanced course for lenders, investors, and advisors ready to go beyond vanilla project finance debt. Covers sophisticated debt structures, leverage constraints, P50/P90/P99 sizing, sweeps, multi-tranche facilities, reserves, and circularities through hands-on modelling.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/advanced-project-finance-debt-modeling/)

### Battery Storage Financial Modeling

Introductory course for developers, investors, and lenders seeking to understand battery storage projects. Covers battery economics, revenue streams, key risks, and evaluation approaches for standalone and co-located assets, using clear explanations and simple financial model examples.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/battery-storage-financial-modeling/)

### Project Finance Modeling

Practical course for finance professionals building or reviewing project finance models. Covers best-practice infrastructure modelling, PPP case studies, debt sizing, equity returns, and key metrics including DSCR and IRR, with real-world applications across infrastructure and resources.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/project-finance-modeling/)

### Introduction to Project Finance Modeling

Perfect for entry-level professionals seeking to enter the project finance sector. This comprehensive course covers fundamental modeling techniques, project finance structures, and essential Excel skills.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/introduction-project-finance-modeling/)

### Renewable Energy Project Finance Modeling

Specialized training focused on renewable energy project finance, covering wind and solar project modeling, power purchase agreements, tax credits, and regulatory considerations specific to clean energy projects.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/renewable-energy-project-finance-modeling/)

### Tax Equity & Hybrid Financial Modeling

Deep-dive tax equity modelling course for U.S. renewable energy projects. Learn how tax capital accounts, depreciation, tax credits, and HLBV accounting drive returns in structures that go far beyond standard project finance.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/tax-equity-hybrid-financial-modeling/)

### Mining & Critical Minerals Project Finance Modeling

Practical course focused on building a mining financial model using a copper project case study. Covers mine plans, grades, stockpiling, recoveries, cost drivers, and how investors and lenders assess mining deals using metrics such as RTR and RLCR.

[Learn More](https://pivotal180.com/course-type/mining-critical-minerals-project-finance-modeling/)

Skills & Outcomes You Can Master
--------------------------------

Build practical expertise in the financial modeling techniques that lenders, developers, and investors use every day, from cash flow projections to complex tax equity structures. Topics you can master include:

### The purpose, structure, risks and opportunities of project finance transactions

### Benefits of debt and how banks size and structure loans for energy and infrastructure deals

### Calculation and use of key ratios used for debt sizing and covenant compliance

### Operational cash flow projections and risks for different revenue and opex contract models

### Construction period modeling with funding drawdowns and contingency reserves

### Impact of depreciation and tax on investor returns, project valuation, and deal structuring decisions

### Structure, sizing and execution of tax equity partnership structures, including capital accounts

### Scenario and sensitivity analysis for risk and resiliency assessment of any project

### Model optimization utilizing goal seek, macros and other tools in Excel

### Best practices for creating, maintaining and reviewing clear, concise and accurate financial models
