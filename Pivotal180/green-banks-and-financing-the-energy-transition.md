Green Banks and Financing the Energy Transition – In Conversation w/Andrew Kessler
==================================================================================

By Fiona Wilson | October 20, 2022

**In Conversation w/Andrew Kessler**
------------------------------------

In this episode of _Pivotal180 in Conversation_, we interview **[Andrew Kessler, President of New York Green Bank](https://greenbank.ny.gov/About/Team)
.** Andrew shares with us the mission of the New York Green Bank and how it’s uniquely designed to catalyze investment in clean energy and climate tech, as well as background on a few of the specific initiatives that New York Green Bank is working on. He also shares his insight about how Green Banks across the US can play a crucial role in the energy transition, and how sharing of information and lessons learned can enhance the efficacy of these institutions.
